## 1. Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi
Consistently ranked among the top 3 engineering colleges in India by NIRF, IIT Delhi needs no introduction. It is the pinnacle of engineering education, offering exceptional faculty, cutting-edge research facilities, and record-breaking placements.

* **NIRF 2025 Ranking:** #2 in Engineering
* **Exams Accepted:** JEE Main + JEE Advanced
* **Average B.Tech Fees:** ₹8.5 Lakhs (4 years)
* **Average Placement:** ₹20.5 LPA
* **Highest Placement:** ₹2.0+ Crores (International)
* **Top Recruiters:** Microsoft, Google, Apple, Tower Research, Jane Street

## 2. Delhi Technological University (DTU)
Formerly known as Delhi College of Engineering (DCE), DTU is a state university highly sought after for its exceptional placements in IT and core engineering sectors. It boasts a massive alumni network and an entrepreneurial culture.

* **Exams Accepted:** JEE Main (Admission via JAC Delhi)
* **Average B.Tech Fees:** ₹8.5 Lakhs (4 years)
* **Average Placement:** ₹18.0 LPA
* **Highest Placement:** ₹82.0 LPA
* **Top Recruiters:** Amazon, Atlassian, Adobe, Palantir, Goldman Sachs

## 3. Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT)
NSUT (formerly NSIT) stands neck-to-neck with DTU. Located in Dwarka, it is particularly famous for its Computer Science, IT, and ECE branches. The coding culture here is top-notch, leading to stellar software placements.

* **Exams Accepted:** JEE Main (Admission via JAC Delhi)
* **Average B.Tech Fees:** ₹8.5 Lakhs (4 years)
* **Average Placement:** ₹17.5 LPA
* **Highest Placement:** ₹1.25 Crores (International)
* **Top Recruiters:** Microsoft, Google, DE Shaw, Amazon, Sprinklr

## 4. Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Delhi
If you are deeply interested in Computer Science, AI, Machine Learning, and research, IIIT Delhi is arguably the best place in Delhi-NCR. Despite being relatively young, it has outpaced many older institutes in coding culture and research output.

* **Exams Accepted:** JEE Main (Admission via JAC Delhi), UCEED (for B.Tech CSSS)
* **Average B.Tech Fees:** ₹18.0 Lakhs (4 years)
* **Average Placement:** ₹20.0 LPA
* **Highest Placement:** ₹51.0 LPA
* **Top Recruiters:** Qualcomm, Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Bloomberg

## 5. National Institute of Technology (NIT) Delhi
NIT Delhi is one of the newer NITs but has rapidly improved its infrastructure and placements. Being located in the capital gives its students a significant location advantage for internships and placements.

* **Exams Accepted:** JEE Main (Admission via JoSAA / CSAB)
* **Average B.Tech Fees:** ₹6.0 Lakhs (4 years)
* **Average Placement:** ₹15.5 LPA
* **Highest Placement:** ₹86.0 LPA
* **Top Recruiters:** Adobe, Microsoft, Amazon, Samsung

## 6. Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW)
IGDTUW is India's premier technical university exclusively for women. The university has seen phenomenal placement records over the last few years, with many top tech giants hiring exclusively from its campus.

* **Exams Accepted:** JEE Main (Admission via JAC Delhi)
* **Average B.Tech Fees:** ₹4.5 Lakhs (4 years)
* **Average Placement:** ₹18.5 LPA
* **Highest Placement:** ₹82.0 LPA
* **Top Recruiters:** Google, Microsoft, Uber, Atlassian, Intuit

## 7. Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI)
A central university with a rich history, Jamia Millia Islamia offers highly subsidized engineering education with excellent ROI. It consistently ranks in the top 20 engineering colleges in the NIRF rankings.

* **Exams Accepted:** JEE Main
* **Average B.Tech Fees:** ₹65,000 (4 years)
* **Average Placement:** ₹9.0 LPA
* **Highest Placement:** ₹25.0 LPA
* **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Wipro, L&T, Siemens

## Top Private Engineering Colleges in Delhi (GGSIPU Affiliated)

Apart from the elite government institutions, Delhi has excellent private colleges affiliated with **Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU)**. Admission to these is primarily through **JEE Main**.

1. **Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology (MAIT):** Known for its strong IT placements and strict academic environment. (Avg Placement: ₹8-9 LPA)
2. **Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology (MSIT):** A close competitor to MAIT, offering great ROI and location advantage in Janakpuri.
3. **Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering (BVCOE):** Located in Paschim Vihar, it offers decent placements in CS and IT branches.
4. **Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology (BPIT):** An emerging college with improving placement records year on year.

## Admission Process & Important Counselling Bodies (2026)

To get into the top engineering colleges in Delhi, you must primarily focus on the **JEE Main** examination. Depending on your rank, you will have to participate in different counselling processes:

1. **JoSAA / CSAB Counselling:** For admission to **IIT Delhi** (requires JEE Advanced) and **NIT Delhi**.
2. **JAC Delhi Counselling:** The most crucial counselling for Delhi students. It grants admission to **DTU, NSUT, IIIT Delhi, IGDTUW, and DSEU**. (Note: 85% of seats are reserved for students who passed their 12th from a school located in Delhi).
3. **GGSIPU Counselling:** For admission to top private colleges like MAIT, MSIT, BVCOE, BPIT, etc., based on JEE Main ranks.

> 💡 **Delhi Quota Advantage:** If you complete your Class 12 from a school situated within the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi, you are eligible for the **85% Delhi Home State Quota** in DTU, NSUT, IIIT Delhi, and IGDTUW. This significantly lowers the cutoff ranks compared to the Outside Delhi (15%) quota.
