### **College Review: IBI Greater Noida**
*   **Corporate Intensive**: Specifically designed as a career-centric PGDM institute in Greater Noida.
*   **Global Exposure**: Known for residential international tours (Dubai/UK) to give students global market perspective.
*   **Mentorship Model**: Focuses on personalized grooming and leadership development.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: **₹50 LPA** (International/Premium Domestic).
*   **Average Package**: **₹21 LPA** (Reported for top-tier roles) | General Average: ₹7-10 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Wipro, KPMG, Deloitte, Flipkart, Haldiram, and HDFC Bank.
*   **Support Cell**: Exceptional focus on getting every student placed in high-growth roles.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Modern Setup**: Air-conditioned classrooms and a tech-integrated learning environment.
*   **Resources**: Digital library, conference halls, and dedicated corporate communication rooms.
*   **Comforts**: On-campus hostels with Wi-Fi and amenities for focus-driven study.
*   **Recreation**: Includes a gym, sports rooms, and student lounges for networking.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Experienced Mentors**: Faculty members from premier institutes with heavy industry experience.
*   **Practical Insights**: Strong emphasis on practical knowledge, live projects, and Harvard-style case studies.
*   **Grooming**: Daily focus on corporate manners, personality building, and leadership drills.

**Final Verdict**: Best for students looking for a specialized, high-intensity B-school experience with a heavy focus on international exposure and premium placements.
