The wait is finally over! The **National Testing Agency (NTA)** has officially declared the results for the **JEE Main 2026 Session 2 (April Attempt)** today, **April 20, 2026**. lakhs of students who appeared for the exam between April 2 and April 9 can now access their final NTA scores and All India Ranks (AIR).

With the declaration of the Session 2 results, NTA has also released the **final cutoff for JEE Advanced 2026**. Only the top 2,50,000 qualifiers will be eligible to appear for the JEE Advanced exam scheduled for next month.

### **How to Check JEE Main 2026 Session 2 Result?**

Candidates can download their scorecards by following these simple steps:

1.  **Visit the Official Website**: Go to [jeemain.nta.nic.in](https://jeemain.nta.nic.in) or [ntaresults.nic.in](https://ntaresults.nic.in).
2.  **Click the Result Link**: On the homepage, look for the link "JEE Main 2026 Session 2 Scorecard Download".
3.  **Login Credentials**: Enter your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth** (or Password).
4.  **Security Pin**: Enter the captcha code shown on the screen.
5.  **Submit & View**: Click on 'Submit' to view your result.
6.  **Download & Print**: Save the PDF scorecard for future reference and counseling purposes.

### **Important Details Mentioned in the Scorecard**
Your JEE Main scorecard is a crucial document. Ensure the following details are correct:
*   Name of the Candidate
*   Application Number & Roll Number
*   Subject-wise NTA Scores (Physics, Chemistry, Maths)
*   Total NTA Score (Percentile)
*   All India Rank (AIR) and Category Rank
*   Qualifying Status for JEE Advanced 2026

### **What Next After JEE Main Results?**

1.  **If You Qualify for JEE Advanced**: Start your rigorous preparation for the IIT entrance. Focus on high-level problem solving and previous year papers.
2.  **If You Don't Qualify for Advanced**: Don't lose hope! Your JEE Main AIR can get you into top **NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs** through JoSAA/CSAB counseling. 
3.  **Explore Private Universities**: Many top-tier private universities like **BITSAT, VITEEE, and Manipal** have their own entrance exams or accept JEE Main scores for direct admission.
