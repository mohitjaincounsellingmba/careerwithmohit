Intellipaat School of Technology (IST) has emerged as a disruptive force in engineering education, offering industry-first B.Tech programs in partnership with leading UGC-recognized universities. Their model focuses on bridging the gap between traditional academics and high-end tech requirements like AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science.

Here is everything you need to know about the Intellipaat B.Tech program for the 2026 academic session:

## Key Highlights & USP

- **Industry-First Model:** While the degree is awarded by a partner university, the curriculum is designed and delivered in collaboration with IIT faculty and industry experts.
- **12-Month Paid Internship:** A core part of the 4-year program is a year-long (sometimes up to 18 months) compulsory internship, allowing students to gain real-world experience before graduating.
- **IIT Mentorship:** Students receive masterclasses and mentorship from professors at top IITs (like IIT Roorkee and IIT Mandi).
- **Hiring Network:** Access to a network of over 3,100+ hiring partners including Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Adobe, and more.

## Placement Statistics (Expected 2026)

The placement ecosystem at Intellipaat is highly robust, leveraging their existing global training network.

- **Highest Package Reported:** ₹30.00 LPA - ₹1.00 Crore (for top performers in global roles)
- **Average Placement Package:** ₹8.00 LPA - ₹12.00 LPA (Estimated for CSE/AI specializations)
- **Top Recruiters:** Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Wipro, Infosys, Adobe, and numerous high-growth startups.
- **Placement Support:** Dedicated career services, mock interviews, and resume-building workshops by industry veterans.

## Admission Process

Admission to the Intellipaat School of Technology is highly selective and involves:

1.  **ISTEE Entrance Exam:** An online test evaluating Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Logical Reasoning.
2.  **Personal Interview:** A technical and behavioral round with the IST admissions committee.
3.  **Eligibility:** Minimum 55% in 10+2 (PCM) from a recognized board.

## Our Verdict: Is it worth it?

**Pros:**
- The **12-month internship** is a game-changer, giving you a massive head start over traditional B.Tech students.
- **Direct exposure** to IIT faculty and industry certifications (Microsoft, IHUB) adds significant value to your resume.
- Focus on **modern tech stacks** (AI/ML/Data Science) ensures high employability.

**Cons:**
- The fees are on the higher side (₹8L-₹17L) compared to standard state universities.
- Being a newer model, the "campus life" is dependent on the partner university's infrastructure.

**Final Recommendation:** If you are a tech-focused student who wants to bypass the "theory-only" trap of traditional engineering and values hands-on industry experience, the **Intellipaat School of Technology** is an excellent choice for B.Tech in 2026.
