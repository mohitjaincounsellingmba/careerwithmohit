## Bihar's Unique Significance in BPSC Examination

Bihar has a deeply rich historical heritage — making the **Bihar Special History** component both intellectually fascinating and intensely important for BPSC aspirants:

### Ancient Bihar History (15–20 Q)
- **Magadh Empire:** Haryanka, Nanda, and Maurya dynasties — Chandragupta Maurya, Ashoka, and the Mauryan empire
- **Patliputra (modern Patna):** Capital of the Maurya and Gupta empires
- **Buddhism in Bihar:** Siddharth Gautama's enlightenment at Bodh Gaya; Nalanda University (world's earliest residential university)
- **Jainism in Bihar:** Mahavira born in Vaishali; Jain Tirthankaras
- **Guptas:** Samudra Gupta, Chandra Gupta II; Bihar's role in the Golden Age of India

### Medieval Bihar History (8–10 Q)
- Bakhtiyar Khilji's invasion, destruction of Nalanda (1193)
- Sher Shah Suri (born in Bihar — Sasaram): Grand Trunk Road, Rupiya currency, administrative reforms
- Mughal administration in Bihar

### Modern Bihar & Freedom Movement (15–18 Q)
- **Champaran Satyagraha (1917):** Gandhi's first major action in India — against British indigo plantation
- **Bihar in 1857:** Kunwar Singh (Jagdishpur's Babu Kunwar Singh — Bihar's most celebrated 1857 warrior)
- **Jayaprakash Narayan:** Bihar Movement (1974), Total Revolution concept
- **Emergency (1975–77):** Bihar's role in resisting Emergency

### Bihar Geography & Economy (10–12 Q)
- **Rivers:** Ganga (crosses Bihar), Gandak, Kosi (sorrow of Bihar), Sone, Punpun
- **Floods:** Bihar is India's most flood-affected state annually (Kosi and Gandak rivers)
- **Agriculture:** Bihar's main crops: Maize, Wheat, Rice, Litchi (Muzaffarpur)
- **Industries:** Barauni Refinery (IOCL), Muzaffarpur Sugar Mills, Silk (Bhagalpur Silk — "Tasar Silk")
- **Divisions and Districts:** 9 Divisions, 38 Districts, 534 Blocks

### Bihar Polity & Administration (8–10 Q)
- **State Assembly:** 243-member Vidhan Sabha (one of India's largest)
- **Bihar CM and Cabinet** (current composition)
- **Panchayati Raj in Bihar:** Structure, reservation, MGNREGA implementation
- **Special Category Status debate**
- **Prohibition in Bihar:** Bihar's alcohol prohibition policy since 2016

## Section-Wise Strategy for BPSC Prelims

- **History (50 Q):** Highest weightage. Split your prep 40% Bihar history + 40% Freedom Movement + 20% Ancient/Medieval India. Use Bipin Chandra's "India's Struggle for Independence" for the freedom movement.
- **General Science (30 Q):** NCERT Class 8–10 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) is your primary source. Focus on practical science — everyday phenomena, diseases, human biology.
- **Current Affairs & GS (70 Q):** 3–6 months national CA + Bihar state CA + basic Polity (Laxmikanth) + Geography. Bihar's annual flood situation, MGNREGA performance, and agricultural statistics are frequently tested.
