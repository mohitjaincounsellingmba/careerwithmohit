**JK Lakshmipat University (JKLU)** in Jaipur (Rajasthan) is a leading private university established in 2011, known for its high-quality faculty (many PhDs from IITs) and industry-aligned technical education. Accredited with **NAAC Grade 'A'**, JKLU has consistently been a preferred choice for students who want a research-oriented engineering experience in the Pink City.

### **JKLU Jaipur Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Selection Base**: Valid scores in **JEE Main 2026**, **CUET UG**, or the university's own merit-based assessment.
2.  **Assessment**: Includes a thorough review of your academic profile and a mandatory **Personal Interview (PI)**.
3.  **Specializations**: B.Tech in CSE (Core), with tracks in AI, Cyber Security, and Data Science.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
JKLU is reachable for aspirants with decent JEE scores or high Class 12 percentages.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | Other Core Engineering |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 70 - 80+ | 60 - 70 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM-English)**| 75% - 85%+ | 65% - 75% |
| **CUET Score** | 80th Percentile+ | 70th Percentile+ |

### **JKLU Jaipur B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
JKLU offers premium facilities at an annual fee comparable to other top-tier Rajasthan private universities.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹2.98 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹11.9 Lakhs - ₹12.5 Lakhs** (for the entire duration).
*   **Additional Charges**: Enrollment, Registration, and Caution Money (~₹30,000 total).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.3 Lakhs to ₹1.8 Lakhs per year (depending on amenities).

### **Placement Review: A Reliable Career Launcher**
JKLU Jaipur is proud of its high placement percentages across circuit branch graduates.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹22.50 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹7.93 LPA to ₹8.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Infosys, Amazon, Deloitte, TCS, IBM, Accenture, Wipro, and Flipkart.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 90% student across all eligible engineering branches.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Highly qualified faculty, modern labs, focus on research and industry-ready skills, and situated in the industrial hub of Jaipur.
**Cons**: Smaller student intake compared to premium private universities in the NCR, relativamente high fee structure for a newer university.
