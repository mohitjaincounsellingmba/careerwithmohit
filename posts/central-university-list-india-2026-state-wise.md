India has **56 Central Universities** established by Acts of Parliament and funded by the Ministry of Education (or other central ministries). These universities are regulated by the **University Grants Commission (UGC)** and offer some of the most affordable, high-quality education in the country.

Most Central Universities now use **CUET UG / CUET PG** scores for admissions. This guide gives you the complete state-wise list, key facts, popular courses, and admission routes.

- **LLM** – Law
- **M.Tech** – Engineering
- **MCA** – Computer Applications

### Research Programs
- **Ph.D.** – All disciplines via UGC-NET/JRF (57 Central Universities offer PhD)

## 💰 Fee Structure at Central Universities

One of the biggest advantages of Central Universities over private colleges is their **heavily subsidised fee structure**:

| Program | Approximate Annual Fee |
|---|---|
| B.A. / B.Sc / B.Com | ₹5,000 – ₹25,000/year |
| B.Tech (BHU, AMU) | ₹30,000 – ₹80,000/year |
| LLB | ₹10,000 – ₹40,000/year |
| M.A. / M.Sc | ₹8,000 – ₹30,000/year |
| MBA | ₹80,000 – ₹1,20,000/year |
| Ph.D. | ₹5,000 – ₹25,000/year |

> **Tip:** Fee waivers and scholarships (like PM-YASASVI, NSP, PMS-SC/ST) are widely available at Central Universities.

## 🤔 FAQs About Central Universities

**Q. How many Central Universities are there in India in 2026?**
There are **56 Central Universities** in India as per UGC records for 2026.

**Q. Which state has the most Central Universities?**
**Delhi** has the maximum – 7 Central Universities.

**Q. Do all Central Universities accept CUET?**
51 out of 56 Central Universities accept **CUET UG** scores. A few have their own entrance tests (like AMU-AT).

**Q. What is the cheapest Central University in India?**
Most Central Universities are very affordable. **CUSB (Bihar), CUO (Odisha), CURAJ (Rajasthan)** have some of the lowest fees – as low as ₹5,000–₹10,000 per year.

**Q. Can I do MBA from a Central University?**
Yes! 7 Central Universities offer MBA: **BHU, AMU, JMI, DU (FMS), UoH, BBAU, Pondicherry University**. Admission is via CAT or CUET PG.

**Q. Is IGNOU a Central University?**
Yes, **IGNOU (Indira Gandhi National Open University)** is a Central University offering distance and online education.
