## 🏛️ The Mumbai Legend: GLC

### 1. Government Law College (GLC), Mumbai
Established in 1855, GLC is the oldest law school in Asia. Its location (near Churchgate) and its legacy of producing Chief Justices of India make it the most prestigious choice for students targeting litigation.
- **Placement Prowess:** Despite being a government college, its location ensures that every top-tier firm in Mumbai recruits from here.
- **Selection:** Extremely high percentile in **MAH CET Law (3-year and 5-year)**.

---

## 🚀 The Corporate Powerhouses: MNLU & NMIMS

### 2. Maharashtra National Law University (MNLU), Mumbai
Though relatively new, MNLU Mumbai has quickly climbed to the top 10 NLU rankings. Located in Powai, it offers a corporate-heavy ecosystem.
- **Why Choose It:** Best for students targeting **In-house Counsel** roles in major corporate houses.

### 3. NMIMS School of Law (Kirit P. Mehta)
A premium law school that benefits from the massive NMIMS brand and infrastructure.
- **Niche:** Powerhouse for **Business and Commercial Law**.
- **Admission Strategy:** Prepare for the **NLAT** (NMIMS Law Test) or provide a high CLAT score.

---

## 🏗️ The Mumbai Internship Culture

For a 2026 Law student, Mumbai offers a distinct edge:
- **Year-long Internships:** Unlike NLUs where internships happen in breaks, Mumbai students (especially at GLC) often intern part-time throughout the year at the High Court or with firms in BKC.
- **Financial hub:** Access to specialized fields like **IBC (Insolvency), SEBI Regulations, and Stock Market Laws**.
- **Maritime Law:** Being a port city, Mumbai is the best place to study Admiralty/Maritime law.

---

## 🏗️ Direct Law Admission in Mumbai 2026

If you miss the MAH CET or CLAT deadlines, several private universities in the greater Mumbai area offer merit-based direct entry:
- **Amity University (Navi Mumbai):** High-end campus with global tie-ups.
- **School of Law, DY Patil (Nerul):** Good brand value and decent placements in the Navi Mumbai and Thane regions.
- **Somaiya University:** A growing law school with strong traditional academic values.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is GLC Mumbai better than NLUs?**
For **Litigation**, GLC is as good as Top 3 NLUs because of its location in South Mumbai. For **Corporate Law placements**, Top 5 NLUs may have a slight brand edge, but GLC remains a top recruiter hub.

**Q2. Does NMIMS Mumbai accept CLAT?**
Yes. NMIMS accepts both **NLAT** and **CLAT** scores for its law programs, but they have their own merit list based on these scores.

**Q3. What is the fee for BA LLB in GLC Mumbai?**
Almost negligible. As a government-aided college, the fees are around ₹7,000 - ₹10,000 per year, making it the best ROI law college in India.

**Q4. Can I get a job in a law firm in BKC after Law?**
Yes. Most Tier-1 law firms are located in Bandra-Kurla Complex (BKC). Students from MNLU, GLC, and NMIMS are the primary candidates for their internships.

**Q5. When is the MAH CET Law 2026 exam?**
The 5-year CET is usually in **May**, and the 3-year CET is in **June**.
