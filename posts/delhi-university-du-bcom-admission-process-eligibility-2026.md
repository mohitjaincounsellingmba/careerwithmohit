## 📋 DU BCom Eligibility Criteria 2026

To even be considered for admission, you must meet DU's absolute eligibility conditions. It's crucial because failure to map your subjects correctly will result in outright rejection.

1. **Educational Qualification:** You must have successfully passed your Class 12 board examination from a single recognized educational board (CBSE, ICSE, or State Boards).
2. **Age Criteria:** There is no minimum or maximum age limit for undergraduate admissions at DU.
3. **Gap Year:** If you took a drop year for preparation, do not worry. A gap year is completely acceptable and does not affect your admission chances.
4. **Subject Mapping Rule (Crucial):** You MUST appear for CUET UG only in those subjects that you have passed in your Class 12 board exams. If a specific subject isn't available in CUET, you need to choose the most closely related subject (with at least a 50% syllabus overlap).

### Required CUET Subject Combinations for B.Com

The subject mapping depends on whether you opt for the regular B.Com (Program) or B.Com (Honours).

#### For B.Com (Hons.)
You can choose either of the following two combinations:
*   **Combination I:** Any 1 Language from List A + Mathematics / Applied Mathematics + Any 2 subjects (out of which at least one should be from List B1).
*   **Combination II:** Any 1 Language from List A + Accountancy / Book Keeping + Any 2 subjects (out of which at least one should be from List B1).

#### For B.Com (Program)
Similarly, there are two alternate combinations available:
*   **Combination I:** Any 1 Language from List A + Any 2 subjects from List B1 + Any 1 subject from either List B1 or List B2.
*   **Combination II:** Any 1 Language from List A + Any 1 subject from List B1 + CUET General Test (Section III).

*(Note: Check the official DU Information Bulletin to review exact lists for A, B1, and B2).*

## 🚀 The Delhi University DU BCom Admission Process 2026

A good CUET score is just half the battle. Here is a step-by-step breakdown of how the actual seat allocation works through DU's Common Seat Allocation System (CSAS).

### Step 1: Register and appear for CUET UG 2026
Your first task is registering on the NTA website for CUET UG. Select the subjects correctly based on the eligibility combinations mentioned above.

### Step 2: Register on the DU CSAS Portal
Once your CUET results are out, you must separately register on the Delhi University CSAS admission portal (*admission.uod.ac.in*). 

### Step 3: Fill Your Preferences (Most Important Step)
On the CSAS portal, you’ll be asked to list the specific programs and colleges you wish to apply for, in order of preference. 
*Pro Tip: Always put your dream colleges (like SRCC and Hindu) at the top, regardless of what you think your CUET score will get you.* For a strategic list, explore our ranking of the [Top CUET UG Colleges in Delhi NCR](/top-cuet-ug-colleges-delhi-ncr).

### Step 4: Seat Allocation
DU will release multiple merit lists based on your CUET scores, category, and the preference list you submitted. 

### Step 5: Accept and Pay
Once allocated a college, you must accept the seat within the given deadline window. Your documents will undergo an online verification by the college, and upon approval, you must pay the admission fee to freeze your seat.
