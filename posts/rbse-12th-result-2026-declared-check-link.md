The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (RBSE) has officially declared the **RBSE Class 12 Result 2026** for all streams—**Science, Commerce, and Arts**—on March 31, 2026. Over 9 lakh students who appeared for the examinations can now access their provisional marksheets online.

This year, the overall pass percentage has seen a significant improvement, with students from the Arts and Science streams leading the way.

### **Where to Check RBSE 12th Result 2026?**
Students can check their results on the following official websites by entering their **Roll Number**:

1.  **[rajresults.nic.in](http://rajresults.nic.in)**
2.  **[rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in](http://rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in)**

### **How to Check RBSE 12th Result 2026 Online?**
Follow these simple steps to download your result:
1.  Visit the official result portal: [rajresults.nic.in](http://rajresults.nic.in).
2.  Click on the link for **"Senior Secondary (Science/Commerce/Arts) - 2026 Result"**.
3.  Enter your **Roll Number** as mentioned on your admit card.
4.  Click on **Submit**.
5.  Your RBSE 12th result will be displayed on the screen.
6.  Download the PDF or take a printout for future reference.
