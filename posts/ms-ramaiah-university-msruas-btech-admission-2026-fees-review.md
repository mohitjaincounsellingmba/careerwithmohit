**MS Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences (MSRUAS)** is a leading private university in Bangalore that offers a wide range of specialized B.Tech programs through its Faculty of Engineering and Technology (FET). While its sister institution, **MSRIT**, remains a traditional powerhouse, MSRUAS is known for its application-oriented curriculum and a more modern campus in Peenya.

If you are looking at the **MSRUAS Bangalore B.Tech 2026 batch**, here is the complete breakdown of admissions, fees, and placements.

### **MSRUAS Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **KCET**: For Karnataka state quota.
2.  **COMEDK**: For All India candidates.
3.  **RUASAT**: The university's own entrance exam for direct admission.
4.  **Management Quota**: For merit-based direct entry.

### **Specialized B.Tech Specializations at MSRUAS**
MSRUAS is unique for its "Applied Sciences" approach, offering branches that are rare in other Bangalore colleges:
*   B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML)
*   B.Tech in Automotive Engineering (Specialization)
*   B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering
*   B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Core)

### **MSRUAS B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
Fees at MSRUAS can vary by branch and placement category.

| Category | Approx. Annual Fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| **KCET Entry** | approx. ₹1.4 Lakhs |
| **COMEDK Entry** | approx. ₹2.5 Lakhs |
| **Management Quota (CSE)** | approx. ₹5.0 Lakhs |

*Total course cost for B.Tech CSE under management quota is approximately ₹20 Lakhs to ₹21 Lakhs.*

### **Placement Review: Industry-Ready Graduates**
MSRUAS focuses heavily on internships and industrial exposure, resulting in solid placement numbers for its students.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹52.00 LPA (Domestic) / ₹24.54 LPA (International).
*   **Average Package (Overall)**: ₹7.00 LPA to ₹8.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, TCS, Infosys, IBM, Google, ABB, Genpact, and Hyundai.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 80% for engineering graduates.

### **MSRIT vs. MSRUAS: The Key Difference**
*   **Focus**: MSRIT is a traditional, research-oriented autonomous college. MSRUAS is an "Applied Sciences" university with a focus on skill-based and vocational learning.
*   **Location**: MSRIT is in Mathikere. MSRUAS has two campuses (Gnanagangothri and Peenya).
*   **Degree**: MSRIT gives a VTU degree (autonomous). MSRUAS is a standalone UGC-recognized university degree.
