**KR Mangalam University (KRMU)** is a leading private university in Gurugram (Sohna Road), established in 2013 and accredited with **NAAC Grade 'A'**. Known for its industry-aligned engineering programs (in collaboration with IBM, Xebia, and Samatrix), KRMU offers focused tracks in Computer Science, AI, and Cloud Computing.

### **KR Mangalam Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Selection Base**: Valid scores in **JEE Main 2026**, **CUET UG**, or the university's own **KREE** (K.R. Mangalam Entrance Exam).
2.  **Assessment**: Includes a Personal Interview (PI) based on your KREE score or national test percentile.
3.  **Specializations**: Flagship B.Tech in CSE (Core), with specialized tracks in Cyber Security, AI & ML, and Data Science.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
KRMU is reachable for aspirants with high Class 12 percentages or decent JEE ranks.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | Core Engineering |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 70 - 80+ | 60 - 70 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM-English)**| 75% - 85%+ | 65% - 75% |
| **KREE Score** | 80th Percentile+ | 70th Percentile+ |

### **KR Mangalam University B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
KRMU offers premium facilities at an annual fee comparable to other top-tier Gurugram private universities.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee (CSE)**: approx. ₹2.30 Lakhs - ₹2.50 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹13.6 Lakhs - ₹15.2 Lakhs**.
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.92 Lakhs per year (AC mandatory for most rooms).

### **Placement Review: A Robust Career Network**
KR Mangalam University is proud of its corporate connectivity along the NCR business belt.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹56.60 LPA (Secured by a graduate with Ferrari).
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹11.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (CSE)**: ₹12.50 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Microsoft India, Amazon, LinkedIn, IBM, TCS, Deloitte, and Genpact.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 85% students across all eligible engineering branches.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: NAAC A Grade, world-class specialized labs with industry tie-ups, 100% placement assistance, and a focus on soft-skill grooming.
**Cons**: Higher hostel fees than traditional institutes, rigorous attendance requirements.
