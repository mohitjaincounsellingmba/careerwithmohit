SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) is one of India's largest private universities, offering a massive array of B.Tech specializations across multiple campuses. For the **2026 admission cycle**, SRM has already commenced its application process through the **SRMJEEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination)**.

If you are an engineering aspirant looking at SRM, here is everything you need to know about the campuses, fees, cutoffs, and the admission timeline.

### **SRMJEEE 2026: Important Exam Dates**
SRM conducts its entrance exam in three phases. Candidates can choose to appear in any or all phases to improve their rank.

| Phase | Application Deadline | Exam Dates (Tentative) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | April 16, 2026 | April 23 – April 28, 2026 |
| **Phase 2** | June 4, 2026 | June 10 – June 15, 2026 |
| **Phase 3** | June 30, 2026 | July 4 – July 5, 2026 |

*Note: The exam is typically conducted in a Remote Proctored Online Mode (RPOM).*

### **SRM Campus Comparison: Which One to Choose?**
SRM has several campuses, but the "KTR" campus remains the most prestigious.
*   **Kattankulathur (KTR), Chennai**: The main 250-acre flagship campus. Best for infrastructure, diversity, and placements.
*   **Ramapuram, Chennai**: Located within the city, highly academic and student-focused.
*   **SRM University AP, Amaravati**: A newer, high-tech campus with a strong focus on research and modern labs.
*   **SRM NCR, Ghaziabad**: Ideal for students from North India looking for the SRM brand locally.

### **SRM B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
Fees at SRM vary based on the campus and the popularity of the branch. **CSE (Computer Science)** is the most expensive across all campuses.

| Campus | CSE Annual Tuition Fee | Other Branches (ME/Civil/ECE) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SRM KTR (Main)** | ₹4.0 Lakhs - ₹4.5 Lakhs | ₹2.5 Lakhs - ₹3.0 Lakhs |
| **SRM Ramapuram** | ₹3.5 Lakhs - ₹3.75 Lakhs | ₹2.0 Lakhs - ₹2.5 Lakhs |
| **SRM University AP** | ₹3.5 Lakhs - ₹4.0 Lakhs | ₹2.5 Lakhs |
| **SRM NCR / Sonepat** | ₹2.5 Lakhs - ₹3.0 Lakhs | ₹1.5 Lakhs - ₹2.0 Lakhs |

*Total 4-year tuition for CSE at the main campus (KTR) is approximately ₹16 Lakhs to ₹18 Lakhs, excluding hostel and mess.*

### **Expected SRMJEEE 2026 Cutoffs (Rank-wise)**
SRM does not release official cutoff lists, but based on previous years' admission data, here are the target ranks for **CSE**:

| Campus | Target Rank for CSE (Core) | Target Rank for CSE Spec. |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **SRM KTR** | Under 2,000 | Under 10,000 |
| **SRM Ramapuram** | Under 15,000 | Under 25,000 |
| **SRM AP** | Under 10,000 | Under 20,000 |
| **SRM NCR** | Under 30,000 | Under 50,000 |

### **Placements Review: The SRM Advantage**
SRM is known for its high-volume placements. The centralized placement cell ensures that students from all campuses can participate in recruitment drives at the main KTR campus.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026**: ₹1.10 Crore (International) / ₹52 LPA (Domestic).
*   **Average Package (CSE)**: ₹8.5 LPA to ₹10.2 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, Microsoft, Google, PayPal, Barclays, and Hyundai.
*   **Mass Recruiters**: TCS, Cognizant, Wipro, and Infosys are frequent visitors.
