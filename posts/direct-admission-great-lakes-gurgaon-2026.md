Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM) Gurgaon is a sister campus of the legendary Chennai school, offering the same academic excellence in India's corporate heartland. Known for its strong industry integration and focus on Analytics, it is a high-priority college for Delhi-NCR aspirants. For students looking for **Direct Admission in Great Lakes Gurgaon 2026**, here are the facts on how to secure your seat.

## Why Great Lakes Gurgaon is a High-Growth Choice?

- **Strategic Location**: Located in Gurgaon, placing students right next to MNC headquarters like Google, Pepsi, and BCG.
- **Analytics Focus**: Recognized as one of the best campuses for students pursuing Business Analytics and AI in management.
- **Placements**: The average package for the 2025 batch was approximately **₹11.6 LPA** (PGDM) and **₹15.8 LPA** (PGPM).

## Is there a Management Quota in Great Lakes Gurgaon?

As per official guidelines, Great Lakes Gurgaon is a strictly merit-based institution. However, students often use "Direct Admission" keywords to find the institutional selection process:

### 1. Cycle-Based Direct Shortlist
Great Lakes follows a 4-cycle admission process. 
- **Direct Advantage**: Applying in **Cycle 1 (January deadline)** gives you a significant advantage in terms of lower competitive pressure and early scholarship opportunities.

### 2. Profile-Based Admission (Diversity Quota)
The institute actively seeks students with diverse backgrounds (engineers vs. non-engineers, female candidates, and students from different states). If you fit a specific diversity profile, you can secure admission even with a CAT/XAT score in the **80-85 percentile range**.

### 3. NRI & Sponsored Seats
Similar to the Chennai campus, there is a provision for NRI students and candidates sponsored by corporate entities. These seats are filled based on profile strength and GMAT scores.

## Fee Structure for 2026
The total academic fee for the two-year PGDM at Great Lakes Gurgaon is approximately **₹18 Lakhs to ₹19 Lakhs**. The ROI is excellent given the package-to-fee ratio in the Gurgaon region.

## Steps to Apply for 2026

1. **Verify Entrance Score**: Ensure you have a score in CAT, XAT, GMAT, or CMAT.
2. **Detailed Application**: Great Lakes values your graduation GPA and extra-curriculars heavily.
3. **Interview Preparation**: Focus on your "Analytical Mindset" and knowledge of current industry trends.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Does Great Lakes Gurgaon offer direct admission?
Official direct admission (donation-based) is not available. All admissions are via the merit portal based on entrance scores and profile assessment.

### 2. What is the cutoff for Great Lakes Gurgaon?
For the PGDM program, the expected cutoff is usually around the **80-85 percentile** in CAT or XAT.

### 3. Is Gurgaon campus as good as Chennai?
Yes, both campuses share the same curriculum and placement support, with the Gurgaon campus offering a slight edge in terms of Corporate proximity in NCR.

### 4. When are the application deadlines for 2026?
Cycle 1 usually ends in **January**, with subsequent rounds ending in March or April.
