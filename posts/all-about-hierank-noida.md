### **College Review: Hierank Business School**
*   **Boutique Experience**: Known for its specialized focus and smaller batch sizes, allowing for individual attention.
*   **Connectivity**: Strategically located in Sector 62, Noida, which is a massive IT and corporate hub.
*   **Affordable Quality**: Provides a good management education experience with a focus on value and industrial readiness.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Achieves a steady placement record for its students every year.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹8 - 12 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: **₹4 - 5.5 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Tech Mahindra, HCL, Axis Bank, and various E-commerce startups.
*   **Support Cell**: The placement team is known for providing multiple opportunities to students across various sectors.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Modern Workspace**: Features air-conditioned classrooms and a tech-integrated learning environment.
*   **Library**: A functional library with access to major management journals and databases.
*   **Digital Connectivity**: High-speed internet across the campus to support online research.
*   **Cafeteria**: A decent cafeteria area that serves as a hub for student interaction and networking.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Approachable Staff**: Faculty members are noted for being very supportive and helping students with doubt-clearance.
*   **Practical Edge**: Focuses on assignments and projects that reflect current market demands.
*   **Industrial Visits**: Regular sessions and visits to Noida-based companies to provide real-world insights.

**Final Verdict**: A good option for students seeking a centrally located B-school in Noida with affordable fees and decent ROI.
