Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), popularly known as IP University, is the go-to destination for B.Tech aspirants in Delhi-NCR. With 85% seats reserved for Delhi candidates, it offers some of the best ROI (Return on Investment) engineering colleges after IIT Delhi and DTU/NSUT.

In this guide, we break down the **top IPU B.Tech colleges, their 2025-2026 expected cutoffs (JEE Main ranks), fee structures, and placement records.**

While there are over 20+ affiliated engineering colleges under GGSIPU, the "Top 5" consistently dominate in terms of placements and cutoffs:

## Admission Process — How to Apply?

1. **JEE Main Exam:** You MUST appear for JEE Main Session 1 or Session 2.
2. **IPU Registration:** Register on the official portal [ipu.admissions.nic.in](https://ipu.admissions.nic.in/) and pay the ₹1,500 application fee.
3. **Choice Filling:** During the counseling process, you list your preferred colleges and branches.
4. **Counseling Rounds:** There are 3 main rounds + 1 Sliding Round + Spot Rounds.
5. **Seat Allotment:** If allotted, pay the part-academic fee (₹60,000 approx) to confirm your seat.

## FAQs — IPU B.Tech Admissions 2026

**Q1. Can I get IPU B.Tech without JEE Main?**
No. Except for B.Tech Biotechnology, all other engineering admissions in IPU are strictly based on JEE Main ranks.

**Q2. What is the fee structure for IPU B.Tech?**
USICT (Main Campus) has lower fees (~₹80k - 1L per year). Private colleges like MAIT/MSIT have fees ranging from ₹1.4L to ₹1.6L per year.

**Q3. Is 85% reservation only for Delhi residents?**
The reservation is for students who completed their **Class 12th from a school located in Delhi**, regardless of their permanent residence.

**Q4. Which is better: MAIT or MSIT?**
MAIT is generally ranked higher for its coding culture and placement packages, but MSIT is often preferred for its discipline and central location in Janakpuri.
