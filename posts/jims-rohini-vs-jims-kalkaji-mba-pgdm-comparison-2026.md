When it comes to PGDM in Delhi, the "JIMS" brand is a staple. However, with multiple campuses, students often find themselves torn between **JIMS Rohini (Sector 5)** and **JIMS Kalkaji**. While JIMS Rohini is often marketed as the "flagship," a closer look at 2026 trends reveals significant reasons why many toppers are now shifting their preference towards the Kalkaji campus.

### **The JIMS Rohini 'Reality Check': Reasons to Reconsider**
While JIMS Rohini holds a high NIRF rank, there are several "pain points" that current aspirants must consider before locking in their ₹10.8 Lakh investment:

#### **1. Zero Campus Life & Massive Congestion**
JIMS Rohini (Sector 5) is notorious for its lack of a traditional "campus." It operates out of a single standalone building in a crowded residential area. With hundreds of students across PGDM, MCA, and other courses, the institute feels more like a coaching center than a premier B-school. If you're looking for open green spaces or sports facilities, Rohini will deeply disappoint you.

#### **2. The 'Mass Placement' Trap**
JIMS Rohini boasts a highest package of ₹24 LPA, but students often complain that this is an outlier. A significant chunk of the batch gets placed in service-heavy roles or sales profiles with heavy variable components. For the average student, the actual "in-hand" salary often hovers around the ₹6-7 LPA mark, despite the high average reported.

#### **3. Location Disadvantage**
Being at the edge of North Delhi, Rohini is miles away from the corporate nerve centers of Gurgaon and South NCR. This affects the frequency of "last-minute" corporate guest lectures and live project opportunities, which are much easier to access for students in South Delhi.

---

### **Why JIMS Kalkaji is Gaining the Edge**
Increasingly, students focused on **Finance and IB (International Business)** are choosing JIMS Kalkaji over Rohini. Here’s why:

#### **1. The South Delhi Corporate Hub**
Located near Okhla and Nehru Place, JIMS Kalkaji students have a massive geographical advantage. Corporate leaders from Gurgaon and Noida find it much more convenient to visit Kalkaji for seminars and recruitment drives than traveling to the far end of North Delhi.

#### **2. Industry Exposure & Live Projects**
Kalkaji has established stronger ties with MNC brokers and financial consultants in the South Delhi area. Their PGDM-IB (International Business) program is arguably one of the best in the private category, offering better exposure to global trade modules.

#### **3. Better Faculty Interaction**
Due to a slightly smaller intake compared to the massive Rohini pool, students at Kalkaji often report better mentoring and personalized guidance during the placement season.

---

### **Admission Cutoffs 2026**
*   **JIMS Rohini**: Requires **75+ percentile** in MAT/CAT.
*   **JIMS Kalkaji**: Accepts students at **60-70 percentile**, making it a high-ROI "hidden gem" compared to the inflated cutoffs of Rohini.

### **Final Verdict: Where Should You Go?**
*   **Choose JIMS Rohini** ONLY if you are purely brand-driven and don't care about campus environment or location.
*   **Choose JIMS Kalkaji** if you want a **professional corporate environment, better location advantage,** and a more balanced student-to-faculty ratio.

### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**
**1. Is JIMS Rohini better than JIMS Kalkaji?**
On paper, Rohini has a higher NIRF rank. However, for placement quality in South Delhi corporates and better industry access, Kalkaji is often preferred by serious aspirants.

**2. Is the PGDM from JIMS equivalent to an MBA?**
Yes, both campuses offer AIU-approved PGDM programs that are equivalent to an MBA degree for government jobs and higher studies.

**3. Does JIMS Rohini have a playground?**
No, JIMS Rohini is a standalone building with no playground or open sports facility on its specific PGDM campus.
