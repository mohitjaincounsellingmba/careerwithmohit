## Tier 1 — Top Rated Engineering Colleges in Noida

### 1. JIIT Noida — Jaypee Institute of Information Technology
JIIT is consistently Noida's #1 private engineering college for CSE and IT placements. Located in Sector 62 (the "Silicon Valley of Noida"), its proximity to tech companies makes it highly coveted.

- **Courses:** CSE, IT, ECE, Biotechnology, Physics (dual-degree options)
- **Admission:** JEE Main merit + JIIT's own direct admission process
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.1 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹8–12 LPA | Highest: ₹60+ LPA (Amazon, Adobe, Microsoft)
- **Top Recruiters:** Amazon, Adobe, Microsoft, Qualcomm, Samsung, Infosys
- **Best For:** Students targeting a software product company career

### 2. Shiv Nadar University, Greater Noida
Backed by HCL's founder, Shiv Nadar University is one of India's most research-intensive private universities with a liberal engineering curriculum and excellent faculty.

- **Courses:** CSE (AI/ML, Data Science), ECE, Mechanical, Chemical, Civil
- **Admission:** SNUAT (own entrance) / JEE Main / Board Merit
- **Annual Fee:** ₹3.5 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹8–14 LPA
- **Best For:** Students targeting research, US higher education, or product roles

### 3. Bennett University, Greater Noida
Times Group-backed Bennett has modern infrastructure, startup incubators, and strong placement support for CSE, ECE, and Data Science branches.

- **Courses:** CSE (core, AI/ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity), ECE, Mechanical
- **Admission:** JEE Main / SAT / CUET / Board Merit (direct)
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.2 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹8–11 LPA | Highest: ₹45 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS Digital, Wipro Elite, Deloitte, EY, Infosys

## Tier 2 — Good Value Engineering Colleges in Noida

### 4. GL Bajaj Institute of Technology & Management, Greater Noida
GL Bajaj is one of AKTU's most placement-active engineering colleges — particularly known for producing the highest number of placed students across the AKTU network year after year.

- **Admission:** AKTU UPTAC Counselling / Direct (15% management quota)
- **Annual Fee:** ₹95,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Capgemini, HCL

### 5. NIET — Noida Institute of Engineering & Technology, Greater Noida
One of Greater Noida's oldest and most established AKTU-affiliated colleges with a strong alumni base across Noida's IT belt.

- **Admission:** AKTU UPTAC | **Fee:** ₹1.0 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

### 6. JSS Noida — JSS Academy of Technical Education
Affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), JSS offers an IP University degree which is highly prized among Delhi NCR employers.

- **Admission:** IPU CET / JEE Main | **Fee:** ₹1.4 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA

### 7. GNIOT — Greater Noida Institute of Technology
A solid, affordable AKTU-affiliated college popular among budget-conscious students seeking a functional engineering education with decent placement support.

- **Admission:** AKTU UPTAC | **Fee:** ₹90,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–6 LPA

### 8. IILM University, Greater Noida
A growing private university with a focus on industry-aligned curriculum and reasonable fees. Good direct admission option.

- **Admission:** Direct / JEE Main | **Fee:** ₹1.2 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

## Direct Admission in Noida B.Tech Colleges 2026

Most AKTU colleges reserve 15% of seats for management quota — available after UPTAC is complete or in parallel if seats remain:
- JIIT has a small number of direct merit seats
- Bennett, Shiv Nadar, Amity, IILM offer rolling direct admissions based on 12th marks

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Noida 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Noida?**
JIIT Noida is the top private engineering college in Noida for CSE and IT. Shiv Nadar University and Bennett University in Greater Noida follow closely for premium education.

**Q2. What is the AKTU cutoff for GL Bajaj Greater Noida CSE?**
In recent years, the AKTU state merit rank for GL Bajaj CSE in OC category has ranged between 25,000–50,000. Seats are also available via direct admission.

**Q3. Is JIIT better than Amity University Noida for B.Tech?**
Yes, JIIT consistently outperforms Amity in CSE/IT placements (avg ₹8–12 LPA vs ₹5–8 LPA) and has a more focused tech campus culture. Amity has a larger campus and more diverse programmes.

**Q4. Can I get B.Tech in Noida without JEE Main?**
Yes. Bennett University, Amity University, IILM, and several AKTU colleges accept students without a JEE score through board merit or direct admission for management quota seats.

**Q5. What is the fee for B.Tech at JIIT Noida?**
JIIT charges approximately ₹2.1 Lakhs per year. Total B.Tech programme cost is around ₹8.5–₹9 Lakhs.
