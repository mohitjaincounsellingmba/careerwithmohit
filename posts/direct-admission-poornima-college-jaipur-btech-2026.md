### **Is There Direct Admission or Management Quota at Poornima College?**

**Yes — Poornima College does have a Management Quota.** As per Rajasthan state norms:

- **85% of seats** are filled through **REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process)** — the centralized state counselling.
- **15% of seats** are reserved under the **Management Quota**, filled directly by the institution.

This 15% management quota is the legitimate "direct admission" route available at Poornima College. These seats are filled based on:
- 10+2 marks (Physics + Maths + English)
- First-come, first-served basis after basic eligibility is met
- Direct contact with the college admissions office

> **Important**: Management quota seats must still meet the minimum eligibility criteria. Any agent promising a seat without eligibility compliance is committing fraud. Always get your admission letter directly from the college.

---

### **Admission Process 2026 — Step-by-Step**

**Route 1: REAP Counselling (85% Seats)**
1. Register on the REAP portal (opens around June 2026).
2. Fill in JEE Main score or 12th marks.
3. Fill college and branch preferences during counselling.
4. Seat allotment based on merit and preference.
5. Report to Poornima College with original documents.

**Route 2: Management Quota (15% Seats)**
1. Visit or call Poornima College admissions directly (+91-99285 55222).
2. Submit application with 12th marksheet and ID proof.
3. Eligibility verification by the college.
4. Fee deposit to confirm the seat.

---

### **Eligibility Criteria for Poornima College B.Tech 2026**

- Passed 10+2 with **minimum 45% aggregate** (General) / **40%** (SC/ST).
- Compulsory subjects: **Physics and Mathematics**.
- One of the following: Chemistry / Biology / Computer Science / Biotechnology.

*No JEE Main score is mandatory for management quota seats — 12th marks alone are sufficient.*
