## Tier 1 — Top Private Universities in Dehradun

### 1. UPES Dehradun — University of Petroleum and Energy Studies
UPES is Dehradun's most career-focused private university, known for specialised programmes in oil & gas, aviation, power, cybersecurity, and data science. Strong industry connections with ONGC, GAIL, TCS, and Airbus.

- **Courses:** CSE (Specialised: Cybersecurity, Data Science, DevOps, Blockchain), ECE, Mechanical (Energy/Aerospace), Chemical (Oil & Gas), Civil, Power Engineering
- **Admission:** UPESEAT / JEE Main Merit / Board Merit (direct)
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.5 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹5–10 LPA | Highest: ₹30+ LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, ONGC, Airbus, Wipro, Deloitte, EY, DRDO

### 2. Graphic Era University, Dehradun
Graphic Era is one of India's fastest-growing deemed universities (NAAC A grade), popular for CSE, ECE, and AI/ML programmes with a strong placement cell.

- **Courses:** CSE (AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity), ECE, Mechanical, Civil
- **Admission:** GEEAT (Graphic Era Entrance) / JEE Main / Board Merit
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.8 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA | Highest: ₹28 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL, Capgemini

### 3. graphic Era Hill University
Sister institution to Graphic Era, Hill University offers a more affordable option in Dehradun/Bhimtal campuses with decent placement support.

- **Admission:** GEEAT / Direct | **Fee:** ₹1.4 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

### 4. DIT University, Dehradun
A private university with NAAC B++ accreditation, strong for CSE and IT placements. An affordable alternative with a focus on emerging technology programmes.

- **Admission:** UASET / Direct / Board Merit | **Fee:** ₹1.5 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

**UASET (Uttarakhand State Admission Entrance Test)** manages engineering admissions to UASET-participating colleges:

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Dehradun 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Dehradun?**
UPES Dehradun is the top private university in Dehradun for specialised engineering programmes (petroleum, energy, cybersecurity). Graphic Era University is the best overall private option for CSE/ECE.

**Q2. Is UPES Dehradun good for B.Tech placements?**
Yes. UPES has strong industry ties particularly in oil & gas (ONGC, GAIL), aerospace (Airbus), and IT (TCS). Average packages range from ₹5–10 LPA depending on specialisation.

**Q3. Can I get B.Tech in Dehradun without JEE?**
Yes. UPES, Graphic Era, DIT, Quantum, and COER all accept students via their own tests or board merit without requiring JEE Main.

**Q4. Is Dehradun a good city for engineering education?**
Yes — lower living costs, clean environment, and proximity to Delhi make Dehradun a practical choice for quality private engineering education at affordable fees.

**Q5. What is the fee for B.Tech at Graphic Era University Dehradun?**
Graphic Era charges approximately ₹1.8 Lakhs per year. Total B.Tech programme cost is around ₹7–7.5 Lakhs.
