### **Important Tips for Checking Board Results**
1.  **Keep Hall Ticket Ready**: You will always need your Roll Number, Roll Code, or Date of Birth.
2.  **Wait for the Link**: On result day, sites are very slow. Refresh every 5-10 minutes.
3.  **Provisional Marksheet**: The online result is a provisional marksheet. The original must be collected from your school.
4.  **Verification**: If you are unhappy with your marks, all boards provide a 10-15 day window for "Scrutiny" or "Re-evaluation" after results are out.
