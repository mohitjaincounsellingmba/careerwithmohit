## Placement Review: What to Expect?

### 1. The ROI Champions (JBIMS, SIMSREE, PUMBA)
These colleges are famous for their incredibly low fees (₹1.3L to ₹6L for 2 years) and high average packages. **JBIMS** continues to be the top choice for Finance and Investment Banking. **PUMBA** is the best choice for students looking for opportunities in the manufacturing and IT hub of Pune.

### 2. Corporate Connect (Welingkar, SIES, Chetana)
Private autonomous institutes like **Welingkar** provide excellent infrastructure and a very wide recruiter base. Their PGDM programs often have better industry exposure than the university-affiliated MMS programs, though the fees are higher (₹13L - ₹15L).

### 3. Regional Leaders
Colleges like **IMRT Nasik** or **Tirpude Nagpur** are great for students who want to build a career in their home region. Placements here are usually in sales, operations, and local banking sectors.

## The CAP Admission Process 2026

It is important to remember that MAH MBA CET has **no fixed passing marks**. You simply need a non-zero score to participate in the **Centralized Admission Process (CAP)**.

1. **Registration:** Sign up on the State CET Cell website after results.
2. **Verification:** Get your documents verified at an FC center.
3. **Merit List:** Check your state and all-India rank.
4. **Option Entry:** Carefully fill your college preferences (Option Form).
5. **Allotment:** Seats are allotted in 3 rounds (CAP 1, 2, and 3).
