## 🏛️ The Hyderabad Icons: Osmania & Gandhi

### 1. Osmania Medical College (OMC)
One of the oldest and most prestigious medical institutes in India. OMC is known for its incredible surgery departments and its association with the Osmania General Hospital.
- **Top Choice:** For students targeting high-volume clinical exposure in South India.

### 2. Gandhi Medical College (GMC)
A close second to Osmania, GMC is heritage-rich and famous for its modern hospital block and excellent placement of its graduates in top UK and US hospitals.

## 🚀 The Hyderabad Advantage (Technology & ADR)

For a 2026 medical student, Hyderabad offers:
- **Med-Tech Hub:** Many global pharmaceutical and medical technology firms (like Dr. Reddy’s) have their R&D centers in Hyderabad, offering unique clinical research opportunities.
- **Super-Specialty Density:** Hyderabad has a higher concentration of private super-specialty hospitals (Apollo, KIMS, AIG) per square mile than most other Indian cities.
- **Telangana Bond Policy:** Compared to many North Indian states, Telangana has a more reasonable service bond policy for government medical students.

## 🏗️ Telangana NEET Counselling (TSCHE) 2026

The **Kaloji Narayana Rao University of Health Sciences (KNRUHS)** conducts the admissions.
- **85% Local Quota:** Reserved for Telangana and Andhra Pradesh graduates (as per presidential order).
- **Management Category (Cat-B):** Hyderabad has an "Open Category" in private medical colleges where students from any state can apply, making it a hotspot for North Indian students seeking high-quality private medicine.

---

## 🏗️ Direct MBBS Admission in Hyderabad 2026

Direct admission in Hyderabad private colleges (like Apollo or Mallareddy) is done through the "Management Category" (B and C category seats) in state counselling.
- **Process:** You MUST be NEET-qualified. Application is through the KNRUHS website.
- **Fees:** Range from ₹12 Lakhs to ₹28 Lakhs per year depending on the college and category.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is Osmania better than AIIMS?**
In terms of **Pure Clinical Patient Variety**, Osmania is as good as any AIIMS. However, AIIMS has a better **National Resume Brand** and integrated PG entrance advantages.

**Q2. Does Hyderabad have any Deemed medical colleges?**
Unlike Mumbai or Bangalore, Hyderabad’s medical colleges are primarily affiliated with KNRUHS (State Health University). However, new private universities like Woxsen and Mahindra are entering the medical space—always check for 2026-batch BCI approval.

**Q3. What is the fee for MBBS in Osmania Medical College?**
Almost negligible. Tuition fees are around ₹15,000 to ₹25,000 per year, but the hostel and mess charges are separate.

**Q4. Can a student from Delhi get a seat in Apollo Hyderabad?**
Yes. You can apply for "Management Category B" seats in private medical colleges through the KNRUHS portal. These seats are open to all Indian citizens.

**Q5. When is the Telangana Medical admission 2026 starting?**
Counselling registrations typically open in **July** after the national MCC counselling cycles start.
