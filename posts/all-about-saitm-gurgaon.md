### **College Review: SAITM Gurgaon**
*   **Campus Scale**: One of the larger campuses in the Gurgaon region (22 acres) with a focus on both Tech and Management.
*   **Holistic Growth**: Balanced emphasis on academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.
*   **Industry Connect**: Regular corporate sessions to keep students updated on market trends.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: **₹40 LPA** (Global Opportunities).
*   **Average Package**: **₹6 - 8 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Infosys, TCS, Wipro, HDFC Bank, Deloitte India, and Paytm.
*   **Support Cell**: Provides active placement assistance and mandatory internship opportunities.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Massive 22-Acre Campus**: State-of-the-art infrastructure with a focus on modern design.
*   **Digital Learning**: Wi-Fi enabled classrooms with smart boards and high-end projectors.
*   **Library & Labs**: A massive library and well-equipped communication and computer labs.
*   **Residential Life**: High-quality separate hostels for boys and girls with 24/7 power and security.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Expert Blend**: Mix of experienced academic professors and industry veterans.
*   **Practical Focus**: Faculty members are noted for clarifying doubts through practical business scenarios.
*   **Student Prep**: Focused on making students "Industry Ready" through simulated workshops.

**Final Verdict**: A great choice for students who prefer a large campus environment and are looking for a mix of solid placements and high-quality infrastructure.
