Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) has important news for all postgraduate (PG) aspirants. If you are aiming for their prestigious **MBA, MCA, or M.Tech** programs, the university has extended the online registration deadline and confirmed the window for the Common Entrance Test (CET) 2026.

## IPU CET 2026 Important Dates (PG)

| Event | Date |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Last Date to Apply (Extended)** | **April 11, 2026** |
| **IPU CET 2026 Exam Window (PG)** | **April 25 – May 17, 2026** |
| **MCA / M.Tech Admit Card** | **3rd Week of April 2026** |
| **MBA Admissions (through CAT/CMAT)** | **Counseling starts June 2026** |

## Registration Window Extended
GGSIPU has extended the registration deadline for the 2026-27 session. Candidates can now submit their online applications until **April 11, 2026**. This extension is vital for candidates who are appearing for or have appeared for national-level tests but still wish to be considered for one of the 80+ colleges affiliated with IPU.

👉 **[Register Now on Official IPU Admissions Portal](https://ipu.admissions.nic.in)**

## Major Postgraduate Admissions under IPU CET
The university follows a specific hierarchy for admissions into its PG programs:

1. **MBA Admissions:**
   - Primary: Based on **CAT 2025** scores.
   - Secondary: Based on **CMAT 2026** scores.
   - Tertiary: Based on **IPU CET (Paper Code 101)** if seats remain vacant.
   - *Note: Candidates must register on the IPU portal by April 11 even if applying through CAT.*

2. **MCA Admissions:**
   - Primary: Based on **NIMCET 2026** scores.
   - Secondary: Based on **IPU CET (Paper Code 105)**.

3. **M.Tech Admissions:**
   - Primary: Based on **GATE** (if qualified).
   - Secondary: Based on **IPU CET** for non-GATE candidates.
