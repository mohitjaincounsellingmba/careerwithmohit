Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) is currently conducting the **IPU CET 2026** for various postgraduate (PG) programs. Since the entrance tests are spread across several dates from April 25 to May 17, 2026, the university is releasing **admit cards in a phased manner**.

If your exam is scheduled for the upcoming weekend (**May 9, 10**) or next week (**May 16, 17**), ensure you download your hall ticket at least 48-72 hours before the test.

## How to Download Your IPU CET PG Admit Card
Follow these steps to get your hall ticket from the official CET portal:

1.  Visit the official admissions portal at **[cet.ggsipu.ac.in](https://cet.ggsipu.ac.in/)**.
2.  Click on the link for **"CET 2026 Examination Portal"**.
3.  Log in using your **Application Number** and **Password** (or Date of Birth).
4.  Navigate to the 'Admit Card' tab on your dashboard.
5.  Check your exam date, timing, and venue details. Download and print 2 color copies.

> **Note:** If your admit card is not yet visible, don't panic. It usually appears 3-4 days before your specific course's CET date. Keep checking the portal daily.

## Major Postgraduate Admissions under IPU CET
The university follows a specific hierarchy for admissions into its PG programs:

1. **MBA Admissions:**
   - Primary: Based on **CAT 2025** scores.
   - Secondary: Based on **CMAT 2026** scores.
   - Tertiary: Based on **IPU CET (Paper Code 101)** if seats remain vacant.
   - *Note: Candidates must register on the IPU portal by April 11 even if applying through CAT.*

2. **MCA Admissions:**
   - Primary: Based on **NIMCET 2026** scores.
   - Secondary: Based on **IPU CET (Paper Code 105)**.

3. **M.Tech Admissions:**
   - Primary: Based on **GATE** (if qualified).
   - Secondary: Based on **IPU CET** for non-GATE candidates.
