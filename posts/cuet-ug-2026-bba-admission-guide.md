## 📊 CUET 2026 Exam Pattern for BBA

To be eligible for BBA at top universities like Delhi University, you usually need to follow a specific combination:

| Section | Mandatory / Optional | Focus for BBA |
|---|---|---|
| **Section IA (Language)** | **Mandatory** | English (High accuracy needed)|
| **Section II (Domain)** | **Mandatory** | **Mathematics / Applied Math** (Crucial for DU)|
| **Section III (General Test)**| **Mandatory** | Logical Reasoning & Quant Aptitude|

*Note: For BMS/BFIA at DU, Math in CUET is mandatory. For BBA at other universities like Jamia or BHU, you may only need English and the General Test.*

## 🚀 CUET 2026 Preparation Strategy

### 1. The General Test (Section III)
This is the determining factor for BBA. Unlike boards, this is about **Speed**.
- **Quant:** Practice 8th-10th level math (Speed Math).
- **Logical Reasoning:** Focus on Syllogisms, Series, and Blood Relations.
- **GK:** Stay updated with business news (Top CEOs, Mergers, Acquisitions).

### 2. Domain Choice (Mathematics)
If you are targeting **Shaheed Sukhdev (SSCBS)**, you cannot ignore Mathematics. The level is based on Class 12 NCERT. Even if you aren't great at Math, you must pick it in CUET to remain eligible for DU-BMS.

### 3. Language Proficiency
English is not just about reading; it's about **Vocabulary and Synonyms/Antonyms**. High scores here are needed since the competition for BBA is extremely intense (0.1 percentile difference causes a rank shift of 1000+).

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. can I get BBA through CUET without Math?**
Yes. While **Delhi University** requires Math for BMS/BFIA, several other universities like **B.R. Ambedkar (AUD), Jamia Millia, and BHU** do not mandate Math in CUET for their BBA programs.

**Q2. is CUET mandatory for all BBA colleges in 2026?**
No. CUET is for **Central and participating Private Universities**. Autonomous colleges like Christ and Symbiosis still have their own entrance exams.

**Q3. What is the safe bucket of marks for SSCBS in CUET?**
For a General category student, you generally need a score of **600+ out of 650** (depending on the normalization of that year) to secure a seat in the first list.

**Q4. Does Jamia Millia Islamia have BBA placements?**
Yes. Jamia is one of the best ROI colleges in India. Fees are very low, and placements in firms like KPMG and Amazon have been reported.

**Q5. When is the CUET 2026 exam?**
Typically held in **May/June 2026** across multiple shifts.
