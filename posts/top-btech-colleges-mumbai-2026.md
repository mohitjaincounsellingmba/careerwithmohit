## Tier 1 — IIT & Government Elite

### 1. IIT Bombay (IITB)
IIT Bombay is India's #1 ranked engineering institution (NIRF 2024) and consistently among Asia's best universities. Its placements and alumni network are unmatched in the country.

- **Courses:** B.Tech in CSE, EE, ME, Chemical, Civil, Aerospace, Engineering Physics + Dual Degrees
- **Admission:** JEE Advanced → JoSAA Counselling
- **JEE Advanced CRL Cutoff (CSE):** ~100 | ECE: ~700 | ME: ~3,000
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.5 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹22–35 LPA | Highest: ₹3.67 Cr (international, Microsoft Japan 2024)
- **Top Recruiters:** Google, Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, McKinsey, Qualcomm, DE Shaw

### 2. VJTI Mumbai (Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute)
VJTI is Mumbai's most prestigious state government engineering college — often called the "IIT of Mumbai" among government institutions. Heavily oversubscribed via MHT CET.

- **Courses:** CSE, IT, Mechanical, Electronics, Civil, Production
- **Admission:** MHT CET + CAP Counselling (Maharashtra)
- **MHT CET Score Needed (CSE, Mumbai cutoff):** 135+/150
- **Annual Fee:** ₹25,000–₹35,000
- **Avg Package:** ₹7–12 LPA | Top: ₹35 LPA (Microsoft, PwC)

### 3. SPIT — Sardar Patel Institute of Technology
Consistently ranked among Mumbai's top government-aided colleges. Strong alumni base in banking, fintech, and product companies.

- **Admission:** MHT CET / JEE Main | **Fee:** ₹30,000/yr
- **Avg Package:** ₹6–10 LPA

## Tier 2 — Top Private Engineering Colleges in Mumbai

### 4. KJ Somaiya Institute of Technology
One of Mumbai's most reputed private engineering institutions with a strong CSE + Data Science ecosystem and booming startup culture.

- **Courses:** CSE, AI/ML, Data Science, ECE, ECS, Mechanical, Civil
- **Admission:** MHT CET / JEE Main / CUET + Merit
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.1 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹8–15 LPA | Highest: ₹45 LPA (Goldman Sachs)
- **NAAC Rating:** A+

### 5. NMIMS MPSTME (Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies — School of Technology)
Part of the NMIMS Deemed University, MPSTME offers excellent industry connections particularly in fintech, banking tech, and AI.

- **Courses:** B.Tech CSE (specialisations: AI, Data Science, Blockchain), ECE, Mechanical
- **Admission:** NMIMS NPAT exam
- **Annual Fee:** ₹4.2 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹8–14 LPA

### 6. DJ Sanghvi College of Engineering (DJSCE)
One of the most sought-after aided colleges in Mumbai, known for electronics, computer science, and strong industry links.

- **Admission:** MHT CET + CAP | **Fee:** ₹35,000/yr
- **Avg Package:** ₹6–10 LPA

### 7. Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC)
TSEC is Bandra-based and consistently ranked in Mumbai's top 10, especially popular for IT and CSE.

- **Admission:** MHT CET | **Fee:** ₹35,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–9 LPA

### 8. Vidyalankar Institute of Technology (VIT Mumbai)
Not to be confused with VIT Vellore. VIT Mumbai is located in Wadala and is well-regarded for practical training, labs, and placement support.

- **Admission:** MHT CET / Direct Quota | **Fee:** ₹1.4 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA

## Direct Admission in Mumbai B.Tech Colleges 2026

If your MHT CET score is below the cutoff, management quota (15% of seats) is available at private unaided colleges like KJ Somaiya, NMIMS MPSTME, VIT Mumbai, and Fr. CRCE.

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 60% in PCM in Class 12
- Valid score in any engineering entrance exam (or board merit)
- DTE Maharashtra registration

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Mumbai 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Mumbai?**
IIT Bombay is the #1 ranked engineering college in India. For state-level government colleges, VJTI Mumbai is the gold standard. Among private colleges, KJ Somaiya and NMIMS MPSTME are highly regarded.

**Q2. What MHT CET score is needed for VJTI Mumbai CSE?**
Typically, a percentile of 99.5+ or a raw score above 135/150 is needed for VJTI CSE in open category. Competition is extremely high.

**Q3. What is the fee for B.Tech in Mumbai private colleges?**
Government-aided college fees are very low (₹25,000–₹50,000/yr). Private unaided colleges like KJ Somaiya charge ₹1.5–₹4.5 Lakhs per year.

**Q4. Can I get B.Tech admission in Mumbai without MHT CET?**
Yes — NMIMS MPSTME uses NPAT, and private colleges can admit students via management quota based on JEE Main rank or Class 12 merit.

**Q5. Does Mumbai have any B.Tech colleges accepting CUET UG scores?**
A few private colleges have started accepting CUET scores as part of their merit evaluation. Check individual college admission portals for the latest updates.
