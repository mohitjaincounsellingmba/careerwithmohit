The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (RBSE) has officially declared the **RBSE Class 10 Result 2026 today, March 24, 2026, at 1:00 PM**. Over 10 lakh students who appeared for the examinations held in February can now access their scores online.

The results were announced in a press conference held at the RBSE headquarters in Ajmer by the Board Secretary, **Gajendra Singh Rathore**, and Rajasthan Education Minister, **Madan Singh Dilawar**.

### **RBSE 10th Result 2026: Official Websites to Check**
Due to the heavy volume of traffic, the official portals may experience slow loading times. Students are advised to use the following verified links:

1.  **[rajresults.nic.in](http://rajresults.nic.in)**
2.  **[rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in](http://rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in)**

### **How to Check RBSE 10th Result 2026 Online?**
Follow these simple steps to download your provisional marksheet:

1.  **Visit Official Portal**: Go to [rajresults.nic.in](http://rajresults.nic.in) or [rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in](http://rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in).
2.  **Locate Link**: Click on the link "Secondary (Class 10) - 2026 Result".
3.  **Enter Logins**: Enter your **Roll Number** as mentioned on your RBSE admit card.
4.  **Submit**: Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button.
5.  **Download**: Your RBSE 10th result will appear on the screen. Download the PDF and take a printout for future reference.
