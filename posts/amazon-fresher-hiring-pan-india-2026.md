### **Key Job Roles for Freshers**

#### **1. Software Development Engineer (SDE-1)**
- **Eligibility**: B.E/B.Tech/M.E/M.Tech in CS/IT or related fields.
- **Skills**: Strong DSA, Problem Solving, Java/Python/C++, Object-Oriented Design.
- **Focus**: Building scalable backend systems and customer-facing features.

#### **2. Cloud Support Associate (AWS)**
- **Eligibility**: Any Graduate with a strong interest in Networking and Cloud.
- **Skills**: Linux/Windows Administration, Troubleshooting, Basics of AWS/Azure.
- **Focus**: Helping global customers solve technical issues on the AWS platform.

#### **3. Operations Manager (Area Manager)**
- **Eligibility**: MBA/PGDM from Tier-1/Tier-2 colleges.
- **Skills**: People Management, Logistics, Process Improvement, Leadership.
- **Focus**: Managing Amazon's massive fulfillment centres and delivery networks.

### **Amazon Selection Process 2026**
The recruitment process typically consists of 4-5 rounds:
1. **Online Assessment (OA)**: Coding challenges (for SDE) and Psychometric tests.
2. **Technical Interview 1**: Data Structures, Algorithms, and System Design.
3. **Technical Interview 2**: Coding and Project discussions.
4. **Bar Raiser Round**: Focus on Amazon's **16 Leadership Principles**.
5. **HR Round**: Culture fit and compensation discussion.

### **Important Tips for Success**
- **Master Leadership Principles**: Amazon hires based on its Leadership Principles (Customer Obsession, Ownership, Bias for Action, etc.). Use the **STAR method** to answer behavioral questions.
- **Resume Optimization**: Ensure your resume highlights your technical projects, internships, and any open-source contributions.
