### **📊 JEE Main 2026 Marking Scheme (NTA)**

It is essential to understand the marking scheme used by the National Testing Agency (NTA) to calculate your raw score out of 300.

| Response Type | Marks Awarded |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Correct Answer** | +4 Marks |
| **Incorrect Answer** | -1 Mark |
| **Unattempted Question** | 0 Marks |
| **Numerical Value Questions (NVQ)** | +4 for Correct / -1 for Incorrect |

**Raw Score Formula:**  
`Total Score = (Total Correct × 4) - (Total Incorrect × 1)`
