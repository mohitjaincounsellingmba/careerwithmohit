Following the successful completion of the **NEET UG 2026** exam on May 3, the National Testing Agency (NTA) is preparing to release the official provisional answer keys and recorded responses. Millions of medical aspirants are now eagerly waiting to calculate their estimated scores before the official results.

Here is the latest update on the NEET UG 2026 answer key release timeline and the process to raise objections.

## 1. NEET UG 2026 Answer Key: Key Dates
While the NTA has not announced a fixed date, historical trends suggest that the provisional key is released within 7-10 days of the exam.

| Event | Tentative Date |
| :--- | :--- |
| **NEET UG 2026 Exam Date** | May 3, 2026 (Completed) |
| **Provisional Answer Key Release** | **Expected May 8 – May 12, 2026** |
| **Objection Window** | 2-3 days after release |
| **Final Answer Key & Result** | June 2026 |
| **Official Website** | [exams.nta.ac.in/NEET](https://exams.nta.ac.in/NEET) |

## 2. How to Download NEET 2026 Answer Key & OMR Sheet
Once the NTA activates the link, you can download your specific set's answer key by following these steps:

1.  Visit the official NEET portal: **[exams.nta.ac.in/NEET](https://exams.nta.ac.in/NEET)**.
2.  Click on the link **"NEET UG 2026 Answer Key & OMR Challenge"**.
3.  Log in using your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth/Password**.
4.  Your recorded OMR sheet and the provisional answer key for your question paper code will be displayed.
5.  Download the PDF for score calculation.

## 3. How to Challenge the Provisional Answer Key?
If you find any discrepancy in the official key, you can challenge it within the stipulated window:
- **Fee:** You must pay a non-refundable processing fee of **₹200 per question** challenged.
- **Process:** Upload supporting documents or justifications for your claim on the NTA portal.
- **Review:** Experts will review all challenges, and if valid, a **Final Answer Key** will be prepared.

## 4. Predicting Your NEET 2026 Score
To estimate your marks:
- **Correct Answer:** +4 Marks
- **Incorrect Answer:** -1 Mark
- **Unattempted:** 0 Marks
