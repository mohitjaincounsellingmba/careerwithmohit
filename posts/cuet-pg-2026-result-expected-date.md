The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) PG 2026 has concluded, and the wait for the results is often the most stressful period for students. Whether you are aiming for Delhi University, BHU, or TISS, your CUET PG scorecard will determine your seat in the 2026 academic session.

### **⏱️ CUET PG 2026: Official Timeline (Expected)**

Based on NTA's historical data from 2024 and 2025, here is the anticipated schedule for the post-exam phase:

*   **Provisional Answer Key**: 3rd Week of April 2026
*   **Answer Key Challenge Window**: 4-5 Days after release
*   **Final Answer Key Release**: Last Week of April 2026
*   **CUET PG 2026 Result Declaration**: **First Week of May 2026 (Expected: May 4 - May 7)**

---

### **🔍 How to Check CUET PG 2026 Result?**

Once the NTA activates the link, follow these steps to download your marksheet:

1.  **Official Portal**: Visit [exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/).
2.  **Login Section**: Click on the 'CUET PG 2026 Scorecard' link.
3.  **Enter Details**: Input your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth**.
4.  **Verification**: Enter the security pin (captcha).
5.  **View Scorecard**: Your raw score and percentile for each subject will be displayed.
6.  **Download**: Save the PDF and take multiple printouts for counseling sessions.

---

### **⚠️ What if the Result is Delayed?**

Occasionally, results may be delayed due to a high volume of answer key challenges. If the result isn't out by the first week of May, keep an eye on the official NTA Twitter (X) handle for real-time updates.

---

### **🚀 Next Steps After Results**

Once your scores are in, the real work begins:
1.  **Register for Counseling**: You must apply separately on the portal of each university you are interested in (e.g., DU CSAS, BHU Counseling).
2.  **Check Cutoffs**: Compare your raw score with previous years' trends.
3.  **Document Verification**: Keep your 10th, 12th, and Graduation marksheets ready.
