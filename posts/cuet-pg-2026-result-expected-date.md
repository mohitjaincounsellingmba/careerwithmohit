The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) PG 2026 has concluded, and thousands of students are now waiting with bated breath for their results. ### **CUET PG 2026: Expected Result Date**
Based on official patterns from the National Testing Agency (NTA) and trends from previous years (2024 and 2025), the **CUET PG 2026 Results are expected to be declared in the first week of May 2026.**

*   **Expected Date**: May 2, 2026 - May 6, 2026
*   **Final Answer Key**: Typically released 2-3 days before the final result.
*   **Scorecard Validity**: Valid for the 2026-27 academic session only.

### **Expected Dates for Major Programs:**
The results for all programs are usually released simultaneously. However, the counseling for individual programs starts at different times:

1.  **Management (MBA/PGDM)**: Results in early May; Counseling starts by late May.
2.  **Computer Applications (MCA)**: Results in early May; Counseling varies by university.
3.  **Engineering (M.Tech/M.E.)**: Results in early May; Focus on GATE-based vs CUET-based seats.
4.  **Science & Arts (M.Sc/M.A.)**: Results in early May; Massive competition for DU, BHU, and JNU.

### **How to Check CUET PG 2026 Result?**
The NTA will host the result on its specialized portal. Follow these steps to ensure you get your scorecard without any technical glitches:

1.  **Visit Official Website**: Go to [exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/).
2.  **Click on Login**: Find the 'CUET PG 2026 Scorecard' link on the homepage.
3.  **Enter Credentials**: You will need your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth** (or Password).
4.  **Security Pin**: Enter the CAPTCHA/Security Pin exactly as shown.
5.  **Submit & View**: Once logged in, your detailed scorecard showing percentiles and raw scores will be displayed.
6.  **Download & Print**: Save a PDF copy and keep at least 3-4 printouts for the physical counseling rounds.
