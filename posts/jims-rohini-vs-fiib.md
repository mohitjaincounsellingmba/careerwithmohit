Choosing the right B-School in Delhi NCR can be overwhelming given the number of options. For students looking at institutes with fees under ₹10 Lakhs, **Jagan Institute of Management Studies (JIMS) Rohini** and **Fortune Institute of International Business (FIIB) Delhi** are two of the most popular choices. 

Both institutes offer robust PGDM programs and have a long-standing legacy in management education. However, when we dive deep into the data—especially ROI, corporate exposure, and average placements—certain clear winners emerge. In this detailed comparison for the 2026 intake, we break down why **FIIB Delhi holds a strong strategic advantage** for MBA/PGDM aspirants.

## 🏛️ 1. JIMS Rohini Overview

JIMS Rohini is a well-established name in North Delhi with a reputation for solid academic discipline and consistent placement records spanning many years.

### ✅ What works for JIMS Rohini?
* **Established Legacy:** Highly regarded in the North Delhi region.
* **100% Placement Record:** Ensures that all eligible students get placed.
* **Top-Tier Recruiters:** Companies like Amazon, Deloitte, PhonePe, and McKinsey frequently visit the campus.

### ❌ Things to Consider:
* **Higher Cost:** The fee structure (~₹9.25 Lakhs) is slightly higher compared to direct competitors in similar tiers.
* **Location Limitations:** Situated in Rohini, it lacks the immediate proximity to the major corporate hubs of South Delhi or Gurgaon.

---

## 🏛️ 2. FIIB Delhi Overview

FIIB has built a stellar reputation as a highly career-centric institute focusing on International Business, Marketing, and Finance. Its location in South Delhi provides a massive strategic edge over its peers.

### ✅ What makes FIIB Stronger?
* **Superior ROI:** With lower fees (~₹8.95 Lakhs) and a higher average package (₹8.75 LPA), FIIB delivers a distinctly better Return on Investment.
* **South Delhi Corporate Connect:** Being located in Okhla Phase III gives students unparalleled, direct access to the corporate headquarters of Gurgaon, Noida, and South Delhi for live projects, internships, and guest lectures.
* **Global Outlook & Pedagogy:** FIIB's curriculum is heavily tailored toward global market strategies, making its graduates highly sought after by MNCs.
* **Better Average & Highest Packages:** FIIB outperforms in both the average (₹8.75 LPA vs ₹8.30 LPA) and highest package (₹25.00 LPA vs ₹24.00 LPA) metrics.

### ❌ Things to Consider:
* **Compact Campus:** Being an urban, corporate-styled setup in South Delhi, the campus is vertical and compact.

## 🔍 Detailed Comparison

### 💼 Placements and ROI
When it comes to the numbers that matter most, **FIIB takes a clear lead.** 
* The average package at FIIB is **₹8.75 LPA**, beating JIMS Rohini's ₹8.30 LPA. 
* Considering FIIB's slightly lower fee structure (₹8.95L vs ₹9.25L), the **Return on Investment (ROI) is mathematically superior at FIIB**. Students taking education loans will find FIIB's financial dynamic much more favorable.

### 🏙️ Location Advantage
Location plays a massive role in a B-School's ability to pull top recruiters and provide live industry exposure. 
* **FIIB** is situated in South Delhi, adjacent to major corporate hubs, making it effortless for industry leaders to visit the campus and for students to secure premium internships.
* **JIMS Rohini** is located in North Delhi, which, while well-connected, is farther away from the epicenter of corporate operations in the National Capital Region.

### 📚 Curriculum & Specializations
While both colleges offer rigorous PGDM programs:
* **FIIB** has a distinct edge in **Marketing and International Business** due to its rich industry connections and a curriculum designed around global market trends. They also boast Bloomberg terminals for Finance students.
* **JIMS Rohini** offers a disciplined academic environment but follows a slightly more traditional approach.
