## 💡 Quick Tips for MAH MBA CET Aspirants

- **Target High Percentile**: Top colleges like JBIMS and PUMBA often require 99+ percentile.
- **DTE CAP Round**: All admissions through MAH CET are processed via the Centralized Admission Process (CAP) rounds conducted by DTE Maharashtra.
- **ROI Factor**: Government colleges have significantly lower fees compared to private ones, offering excellent Return on Investment.
