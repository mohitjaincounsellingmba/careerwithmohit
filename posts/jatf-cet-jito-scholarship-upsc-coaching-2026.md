Preparing for the UPSC Civil Services Examination (IAS, IPS, IRS) or State Public Service Commissions (SPSC) is a rigorous and expensive journey. Recognizing this, the **Jain International Trade Organisation (JITO)**, through its **JATF (JITO Administrative Training Foundation)** wing, offers an incredible scholarship and coaching program to empower students from the Jain community.

If you are a Jain aspirant dreaming of breaking into the Indian bureaucracy, the **JATF CET (Common Entrance Test) 2026** is your golden ticket to receiving top-tier, free, or highly subsidized coaching.

---

## What is the JATF CET & PACE Program?
JATF conducts a nationwide **Common Entrance Test (CET)** every year. The objective is to identify bright, dedicated aspirants from the Jain community and sponsor them through the **PACE (Public Administration and Civil Services Enhancement)** project.

Instead of a traditional cash scholarship, meritorious students selected through this exam are provided with **heavy financial sponsorship for coaching fees** at some of India's premier UPSC coaching institutes (primarily in hubs like Delhi and Pune).

### **What Facilities Are Provided?**
To ensure aspirants face zero hurdles during their preparation, JATF provides comprehensive support:
*   **Coaching Sponsorship:** Partial or 100% funding for UPSC/SPSC coaching at top partner institutes.
*   **Hostel & Accommodation:** Access to JATF's specialized hostels equipped with premium study environments and libraries.
*   **Mentorship:** Direct guidance and mentorship from serving IAS, IPS, and IRS officers who belong to the Jain community.
*   **Test Series:** Free or subsidized access to standard prelims and mains test series.

---

## Eligibility Criteria for JATF CET 2026
To apply for the JATF CET 2026 exam, aspirants must fulfill the following criteria:

*   **Community Requirement:** The applicant must be a follower of Jain principles.
*   **Educational Qualification:** The candidate must be a graduate from a recognized university. Students currently in the **final year of their graduation** are also eligible to apply.
*   **Age Limit:** The preferred age bracket is typically between **20 to 28 years**. 
*   **Special Preference:** Graduates from premier national institutes (IITs, IIMs, NITs, NLUs) or those holding professional degrees (MBBS, CA) may be given extra weightage during the interview stage.
*   **Restriction:** Candidates who have previously availed of JATF hostel/coaching facilities via previous CET exams are generally not eligible to reappear.

---

## The Selection Process
The scholarship is highly competitive. The selection is strictly merit-based and follows a two-stage process:

1.  **Written Examination (CET):** An objective screening test covering General Studies (History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Current Affairs) and CSAT (Aptitude, Reasoning, Comprehension)—similar to the actual UPSC Prelims pattern.
2.  **Personal Interview:** Candidates who clear the written test are invited for a personal interview to gauge their psychological suitability, dedication, and administrative aptitude.

---

## How to Apply for JATF CET 2026
1.  **Track Official Announcements:** JATF usually opens registrations for the CET a few months before the actual exam date. Keep a close watch on the official website: [**www.jatf.in**](https://www.jatf.in).
2.  **Submit Online Form:** Complete the application form, upload your degree certificate/final year marksheet, and pay the nominal exam registration fee.
3.  **Secure Recommendation:** Some applications may require signatures or recommendations from a local JITO chapter official.
