## Option 3 — Direct Admission Based on Class 12 Marks (No Exam!)

The simplest route: Management Quota / Board Merit Seats at private AICTE-approved colleges.

**How it works:**
1. Class 12 with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths/CS) — minimum 60% aggregate
2. Contact college admission office directly or through a verified counsellor
3. Submit documents + pay seat booking fee
4. No entrance exam required at all

**Colleges offering direct admission:**
- Bennett University (Greater Noida)
- Amity University (Noida, Lucknow, Jaipur)
- Shoolini University (Himachal Pradesh)
- Quantum University (Roorkee)
- UPES Dehradun
- GL Bajaj (15% management quota, AKTU)
- Chandigarh University (Mohali)
- VGU Jaipur, JECRC Jaipur
- Manipal University Jaipur

## FAQs — B.Tech Without JEE 2026

**Q1. Is B.Tech admission valid without JEE Main?**
Yes, completely valid. AICTE recognises all routes — state exams, private exams, and management quota admissions — as legally valid for B.Tech degrees.

**Q2. Which good college gives B.Tech without JEE?**
VIT Vellore, SRM, Manipal MIT, BITS Pilani (BITSAT), Chandigarh University, Amrita University, and Bennett University all offer admission through their own processes without JEE.

**Q3. Can I get NIT without JEE Main?**
No. NITs, IITs, IIITs, and GFTIs admit students exclusively through JEE Main/Advanced via JoSAA. These colleges cannot be accessed without a JEE score.

**Q4. What is the minimum marks in Class 12 for direct B.Tech admission?**
Most private colleges require a minimum of 60% aggregate in PCM in Class 12 for direct admission (management quota). Some accept students with 45–50% for their lowest-priority branches.

**Q5. Is VITEEE harder than JEE Main?**
VITEEE is considerably easier than JEE Main in terms of difficulty level. It is a good alternative for students who want a quality engineering college without the intense JEE preparation grind.
