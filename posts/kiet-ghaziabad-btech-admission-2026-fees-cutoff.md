**KIET Group of Institutions** in Ghaziabad is consistently ranked among the top private engineering colleges in Uttar Pradesh. Affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and accredited with NAAC Grade 'A+', KIET is a major hub for tech placements in the NCR region.

If you are looking at **KIET B.Tech 2026 admissions**, here is your detailed breakdown of the process, cutoffs, and fees.

### **KIET Admission Process 2026**
1.  **Entrance Exam**: You must appear for **JEE Main 2026**.
2.  **UPTAC Counselling**: Apply for the Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counselling (UPTAC) based on your JEE Main AIR.
3.  **Direct Admission**: KIET offers merit-based direct admission for specific seats (contact the college during the application window).

### **Expected JEE Main Cutoffs 2026 (UPTAC Ranks)**
KIET has some of the most competitive cutoffs among AKTU-affiliated colleges.

| Branch | General (All India Rank) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Computer Science (CSE)** | 95,000 - 1,25,000 |
| **AI & Machine Learning** | 1,30,000 - 1,50,000 |
| **Information Tech (IT)** | 1,40,000 - 1,75,000 |
| **Electronics (ECE)** | 3,50,000 - 4,50,000 |

### **KIET Fee Structure 2026**
KIET offers a balanced fee structure with significant scholarship opportunities for meritorious students.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹1.12 Lakhs.
*   **First-Year Total (with Misc. Charges)**: ₹1.53 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Cost**: **₹4.5 Lakhs - ₹4.8 Lakhs**.
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.2 Lakhs to ₹1.7 Lakhs per year (AC/Non-AC).

### **Placement Review: Global Success**
KIET stands out in Ghaziabad for its massive placement numbers and occasional high-value international offers.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹1.78 Crore (International offer for IT student).
*   **Highest Domestic Package**: ₹60.0 LPA to ₹90.0 LPA.
*   **Average Package**: ₹5.44 LPA to ₹6.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Infosys (bulk recruiter), TCS, Wipro, Amazon, Adobe, and Cisco.
*   **Placement Rate**: Above 86% across all eligible engineering branches.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Excellent ROI, strong bulk placement ties, good infrastructure, and ranked 8th among leading private universities by R-World.
**Cons**: Mandatory attendance policies (typical of AKTU), location on the NH-58 can be busy.
