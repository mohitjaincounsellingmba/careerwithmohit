## Tier 1 — Premier Government Institutions in Kolkata

### 1. Jadavpur University (JU)
Jadavpur University is West Bengal's crown jewel of engineering education — one of India's finest government universities, producing world-class engineers, researchers, and civil servants.

- **Courses:** CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, Printing, Food Technology
- **Admission:** WBJEE rank
- **WBJEE Cutoff (JU CSE, General):** Typically rank 1–150
- **Annual Fee:** ~₹40,000 (government-subsidised)
- **Avg Package:** ₹6–12 LPA | Highest: ₹50+ LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Amazon, Cognizant, IBM, Goldman Sachs

### 2. IIEST Shibpur (Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology)
One of India's oldest engineering institutes (est. 1856), IIEST has central govt funding and produces excellent mechanical, civil, and CS engineers.

- **Admission:** JEE Main (JoSAA) + WBJEE
- **Annual Fee:** ~₹1 Lakh
- **Avg Package:** ₹6–10 LPA
- **Best For:** Core engineering (Mech, Civil, Electrical) and GATE aspirants

## Tier 2 — Top Private Engineering Colleges in Kolkata

### 3. Heritage Institute of Technology (HIT), Kolkata
Consistently Kolkata's top private engineering college, affiliated with MAKAUT (formerly WBUT), offering excellent CSE and ECE placements in IT companies.

- **Admission:** WBJEE | **Fee:** ₹1.4 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–9 LPA

### 4. Techno India University, Salt Lake
Located in Kolkata's Salt Lake tech hub (home to TCS, Wipro, Infosys campuses), Techno India offers IT-focused education with strong proximity to placement opportunities.

- **Admission:** WBJEE / Direct | **Fee:** ₹1.0 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

### 5. RCC Institute of Information Technology (RCCIIT)
Affiliated with MAKAUT and popular for CSE, IT, and ECE with a consistent placement record in Kolkata's IT sector.

- **Admission:** WBJEE | **Fee:** ₹85,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

### 6. JIS University, Kolkata
A growing private university offering engineering in CSE, ECE, Mechanical, and Civil with a rising placement record and modern campus facilities.

- **Admission:** WBJEE / Direct | **Fee:** ₹1.0 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

## WBJEE 2026 — West Bengal Engineering Admissions

**WBJEE (West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination)** is the state-level entrance exam for B.Tech/B.E. admissions in West Bengal.

- **Authority:** West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB)
- **WBJEE 2026 Exam Date:** April 2026 (expected)
- **Eligibility:** 10+2 with PCM, minimum 45% aggregate
- **Colleges Covered:** Jadavpur University, IIEST Shibpur, Heritage, Techno India, and 100+ colleges
- **JEE Main accepted at:** IIEST Shibpur (via JoSAA)

## Direct Admission in Kolkata B.Tech Colleges 2026

Private colleges in West Bengal allow management quota admission post-WBJEE:
- **Eligible colleges:** Techno India, JIS University, Sister Nivedita, and smaller MAKAUT colleges
- **Requirement:** 12th PCM with 45%+ aggregate
- **Timing:** July–August 2026 (after WBJEE counselling rounds)

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Kolkata 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Kolkata?**
Jadavpur University is Kolkata's #1 engineering college and among India's top government institutions. For private colleges, Heritage Institute of Technology is Kolkata's best-regarded option.

**Q2. What WBJEE rank is needed for Jadavpur University CSE?**
A WBJEE rank in the top 150 (General category) is typically required for Jadavpur University CSE — one of the most competitive cutoffs in India.

**Q3. Is IIEST Shibpur better than private colleges in Kolkata?**
Yes. IIEST Shibpur's central government funding, historical alumni network, and GATE performance make it far superior to private colleges for core engineering.

**Q4. Can I get B.Tech in Kolkata without WBJEE?**
JEE Main qualifiers can apply to IIEST Shibpur through JoSAA. Private colleges like Techno India and JIS University accept students via direct admission without WBJEE.

**Q5. What is the fee for B.Tech at Jadavpur University?**
Jadavpur University charges approximately ₹40,000 per year — one of the lowest fees for a top-quality engineering education in India.
