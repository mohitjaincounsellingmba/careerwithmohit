## What is the RRB ALP Examination Process?

The ALP selection process has multiple stages:

1. **CBT 1 (Screening):** 75 questions in 60 minutes — this is the mock test we offer.
2. **CBT 2 (Main):** 175 questions in 150 minutes — includes trade-specific Part B
3. **CBAT (Computer-Based Aptitude Test):** For ALP role only (not Technician)
4. **Medical Examination:** Fitness test with vision standards

CBT 1 is purely a screener — only top candidates (15x vacancies) move to CBT 2.

## Section-Wise Strategy

- **Mathematics (20 Q):** Class 10 level — BODMAS, Percentages, Averages, Time & Work, Ratio, Simple & Compound Interest, Mensuration basics. Very scoring for a well-prepared candidate.
- **General Intelligence / Mental Ability (25 Q):** Coding-Decoding, Number/Letter Series, Analogy, Venn Diagrams, Non-Verbal Reasoning (mirror image, embedded figures). Practice 15 minutes daily.
- **General Science (20 Q):** NCERT Class 8–10 Science. Focus on Physics (Newton's laws, Electricity, Light), Chemistry (Acids/Bases, Metals/Non-metals, Carbon), and Biology (Cells, Plants, Digestion, Diseases).
- **Current Affairs (10 Q):** Last 6 months — Railway expansions, government schemes, sports, science technology news. Less weightage but can't be ignored.

- **Focus on Science:** Most science questions come directly from NCERT Class 8–10. Revise all 3 years of Science — it is your biggest differentiator in CBT 1.
