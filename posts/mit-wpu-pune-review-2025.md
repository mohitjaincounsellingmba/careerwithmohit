MIT-WPU (MIT World Peace University) is one of the most prominent private universities in Pune. Known for its massive Kothrud campus and holistic "Peace Studies" integrated management curriculum, it attracts students from all over India.

### **Quick Highlights (2025 Batch):**
*   **Avg Placement**: ₹6.23 LPA (Median: ₹5.50 LPA)
*   **Highest Package**: ₹51.36 LPA (Recent International Offer)
*   **Placement Rate**: ~78.5%
*   **Entrance Exam**: CAT / XAT / NMAT / CMAT / MAH CET / MIT-WPU CET

### **Why Choose MIT-WPU?**
*   **Global Exposure**: Strong international tie-ups and semester exchange programs.
*   **World-Class Infrastructure**: The Kothrud campus is legendary for its academic facilities, labs, and student activity centers.
*   **Holistic Development**: The "Peace Study" curriculum focuses on ethics and spirituality alongside core management subjects.

### **Fee Structure:**
The total fee for the two-year MBA program is approximately **₹9.0 - ₹12.0 Lakhs**, depending on the specialization. It is a solid choice for students seeking a premium campus experience and diverse career opportunities.
