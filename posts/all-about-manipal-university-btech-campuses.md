**Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE)** and the broader Manipal Educational Group stand as a behemoth in Indian private engineering education. Choosing “Manipal” for B.Tech isn't just about one college; it’s about choosing between four distinct campuses spread across the country, each possessing unique strengths, fee brackets, and placement profiles.

If you are planning to appear for the **Manipal Entrance Test (MET) 2026** or exploring direct admissions, this guide breaks down everything you need to know about all four Manipal B.Tech campuses.

---

## 1. Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT), Main Campus
Located in the bustling university town of Manipal, Karnataka, the main campus (MIT Manipal) is highly prestigious and incredibly sought-after. It frequently rivals top-tier NITs and premium institutes like BITS and Thapar.

- **Admission Process:** Strictly via the highly competitive **MET (Manipal Entrance Test)**. There is no direct "management quota" admission for general category B.Tech seats without attempting the entrance test or falling under strict NRI quotas.
- **Top Courses:** Computer and Communication Engineering (CCE), Computer Science Engineering (CSE), and Information Technology (IT).
- **Fees:** The 4-year tuition fee for CS-related branches is around **₹18 Lakhs to ₹20 Lakhs**.
- **Placements (2026 Est.):** Phenomenal. The highest packages routinely breach the **₹50+ LPA** threshold, with the average package for CSE hovering around **₹14 - ₹16 LPA**.

## 2. Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT), Bengaluru
The Bengaluru campus is the newest major addition to the MAHE umbrella, specifically tailored to capitalize on the profound IT ecosystem of "India’s Silicon Valley".

- **Admission Process:** Completely tied with MIT Manipal through the unified **MET counselling process**.
- **Specializations:** Focuses predominantly on future-ready domains like AI & Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, and Electronics.
- **Fees:** Very similar to the Manipal main campus, standing at approximately **₹18 - ₹20 Lakhs** for engineering tuition.
- **Placements:** Being located in Yelahanka, Bengaluru, provides immense internship opportunities. Placements are typically pooled with the main campus, giving students an identical platform to secure tier-1 IT jobs.

## 3. Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ)
Located in Dehmi Kalan, Jaipur, **MUJ** operates as a separate state university but carries the profound Manipal legacy. It features one of India's most breathtaking, modern campus infrastructures and an elite socio-cultural student life.

- **Admission Process:** Admissions occur via **MET**, direct board marks evaluation (for select branches), and Management Quota. 
- **Top Courses:** CSE (AI & ML), CSE (Data Science), Information Technology.
- **Fees:** The total B.Tech tuition fee costs around **₹16 - ₹18 Lakhs**, with luxury hostel options pushing total expenses up.
- **Placements:** Growing exceptionally fast. Top coders secure packages above **₹40 LPA** with Amazon, Microsoft, and Dell. Average CSE placements sit comfortably at **₹8 - ₹10 LPA**.
- **Direct Admission Info:** Since MUJ operates slightly differently, students with lower MET scores often seek premium branch entries here. ## 4. Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology (SMIT)
Situated on the serene banks of the river Teesta in Majitar, Sikkim, **SMIT** is the oldest off-campus institute of the Manipal group. It provides a peaceful, pollution-free, and highly disciplined academic environment.

- **Admission Process:** Unlike MAHE, SMIT accepts a wider variety of entrance scores, including **MET, JEE Main, and Sikkim Manipal Entrance Test (SMIT Online Test)**.
- **Fees:** Considerably more affordable than the other three campuses. B.Tech tuition is around **₹12 - ₹14 Lakhs** for four years.
- **Placements:** Core IT recruiters like TCS, Infosys, and Cognizant recruit in mass. Average placements generally range between **₹5 - ₹6 LPA**, but dedicated students secure higher independent offers.

## Which Manipal Campus Should You Choose?

1. **Choose MIT Manipal** if you secure a top rank in MET and desire a purely historic, competitive, and legendary alumni network (Satya Nadella is a notable alumnus).
2. **Choose MIT Bengaluru** if you specifically want to leverage start-up and local IT internships in India's leading tech city.
3. **Choose MUJ (Jaipur)** if you missed the main campus cutoff for CSE, want spectacular infrastructure, or are looking for management/[direct admissions to B.Tech Colleges in India](/blog/best-btech-cse-colleges-delhi-ncr-direct-admission-2026).
4. **Choose SMIT Sikkim** if you're seeking a budget-friendly Manipal tag with gorgeous terrain and a calm atmosphere away from city distractions.
