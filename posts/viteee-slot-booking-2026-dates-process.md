The Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) has officially commenced the **VITEEE Slot Booking 2026** process. Candidates aiming for B.Tech admissions across VIT campuses must use the Online Test Booking System (OTBS) to select their preferred exam date, time, and test city. 

Since slots are allotted strictly on a **first-come, first-served basis**, it is highly recommended to complete the booking process as early as possible.

Here is a complete guide to navigating the OTBS portal, important dates, and the step-by-step process to download your admit card.

---

## 📅 Important Dates for VITEEE 2026

Keep these crucial deadlines in mind for a smooth examination process:

*   **Slot Booking Starts:** April 17, 2026
*   **Slot Booking Deadline:** April 19, 2026 (5:00 PM)
*   **VITEEE 2026 Exam Dates:** April 28 to May 3, 2026

*Note: If you do not book a slot by the deadline, the VIT administration will automatically allocate a slot for you based on availability, which cannot be changed later.*

---

## 💻 What is OTBS?

**OTBS** stands for the **Online Test Booking System**. It is the official web portal created by VIT that empowers candidates to personalize their examination schedule. Through OTBS, you can choose:
1. Your preferred Test City
2. The exact Date of the examination (between April 28 – May 3)
3. Your preferred Time Slot (Morning, Afternoon, etc.)

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step VITEEE Slot Booking Process

Follow these steps to successfully book your slot and generate the admit card:

### Step 1: Visit the Official OTBS Portal
Head over to the official website: [viteee.vit.ac.in](https://viteee.vit.ac.in) and click on the "VITEEE Slot Booking / OTBS" link.

### Step 2: Login to Your Dashboard
Enter your registered credentials:
*   **Application Number**
*   **Password** (Check your registered email or SMS if you forgot it)
*   Security Captcha (if present)

### Step 3: Select Your Preferences
Once logged in, navigate to the ‘Book a Seat’ section.
*   Choose your **Test City** from the dropdown menu.
*   Select your preferred **Test Date** and **Test Slot**.
*   The system will display the real-time availability of seats for the chosen slot in a green/red color code.

### Step 4: Confirm Your Booking
Review your selection carefully. **Once a slot is booked, it cannot be modified or changed under any circumstances.** 
Click on 'Confirm' to lock in your exam schedule.

### Step 5: Download the e-Admit Card
Immediately after confirmation, your **VITEEE 2026 Admit Card** will be generated. You can download and print it. The admit card will also be simultaneously mailed to your registered email address. 

*Always carry a clear, printed copy of your admit card to the examination center along with a valid photo ID proof.*

## 🎯 Pro-Tips for VITEEE 2026 Candidates

1.  **Be Quick:** Popular slots (especially morning slots on weekends) fill up within hours. Log in immediately to secure your preferred timing.
2.  **Stable Internet:** Ensure you have a stable internet connection while booking to avoid timeout failures.
3.  **Cross-Check Details:** Verify your personal details on the admit card. Discrepancies should be reported to the VIT admissions office immediately.
4.  **Take a Mock Test:** Familiarize yourself with the CBT (Computer Based Test) environment before the actual exam.
