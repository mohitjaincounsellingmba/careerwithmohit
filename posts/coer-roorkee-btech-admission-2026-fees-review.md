**COER University** (formerly known as the College of Engineering Roorkee - CORE), established in 1998, is one of the oldest and most respected private engineering colleges in Uttarakhand. Located near Roorkee (the hub of world-class technical education), COER is known for its academic heritage and strong placement record.

### **COER Roorkee Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Selection Base**: Valid scores in **JEE Main 2026** percentile.
2.  **Merit Route**: Based on Class 12 PCM marks (minimum 45% aggregate).
3.  **Counselling**: Admissions are primarily through state-level counselling based on JEE merit.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
COER is accessible to candidates with decent JEE scores or high Class 12 percentages.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | Other Core Engineering |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 65 - 75+ | 50 - 60 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM)** | 75% - 85%+ | 65% - 75% |

### **COER University B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
COER offers a balanced fee structure for its high-quality technical programs.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹1.68 Lakhs - ₹2.26 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹4.52 Lakhs - ₹9.29 Lakhs** (for the entire duration).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹90,000 to ₹1.25 Lakhs per year.

### **Placement Review: A Reliable Career Launcher**
COER has a dedicated training and placement cell that prepares students for the corporate world.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹44.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹5.50 LPA to ₹6.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Adobe, Amazon, Wipro, Microsoft India, Mahindra, TCS, and HCL.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 95% for all engineering graduates.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Long-standing academic heritage, strong placement record, focus on internships and industrial exposure, and located in the technical hub of Roorkee.
**Cons**: Limited research facilities compared to premium private universities, mandatory attendance requirements.
