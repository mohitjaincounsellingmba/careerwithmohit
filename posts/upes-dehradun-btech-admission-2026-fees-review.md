**UPES Dehradun**, officially the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, is an globally recognized private university in Uttarakhand. Established in 2003, it has grown from a specialized energy institute into a multidisciplinary powerhouse (Accredited NAAC Grade 'A').

If you are an engineering aspirant looking at the **UPES B.Tech 2026 batch**, here is the complete breakdown of admissions, fees, and placements.

### **UPES Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Entrance Route (UPESEAT)**: The primary admission route for 70% of the seats.
2.  **JEE Main Route**: For candidates with a valid JEE Main 2026 percentile (typically 60-80 percentile range).
3.  **Board Merit**: Direct admission for students with 80%+ marks in 10+2 PCM.
4.  **CUET / SAT**: Accepted scores for specific specializations.

### **Expected Cutoffs 2026 (General Category)**
UPES is known for its rigorous entrance process, particularly for its specialized Computer Science and Energy branches.

| Branch | UPESEAT Rank (Expected) | JEE Main Percentile (Expected) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computer Science (CSE)** | 1 - 2,500 | 75 - 85+ |
| **CSE (Mathematics & Computing)** | 1 - 1,200 | 80 - 90 |
| **Energy Engineering** | 3,000 - 6,000 | 60 - 75 |
| **Aerospace / Mechanical** | 2,500 - 5,000 | 65 - 78 |

### **UPES B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
UPES offers specialized education at a premium cost, but includes extensive industry certifications and global exposure.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹3.5 Lakhs - ₹4.5 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹15.5 Lakhs - ₹20.9 Lakhs** (CSE is the most expensive).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹2.0 Lakhs to ₹2.5 Lakhs per year (depending on amenities).

### **Placement Review: A Leading Career Hub**
UPES has a dedicated placement cell with nearly 100 recruiters visiting the campus every year.
*   **Highest Package (CSE-2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹52.00 LPA (Domestic).
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹8.66 LPA.
*   **Average Package (CSE)**: ₹9.5 LPA to ₹11.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Shell, Reliance, Amazon, Microsoft India, Schlumberger, and TCS.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently around 95% for eligible graduates.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Global leader in energy and petroleum engineering, spectacular infrastructure, strong industry-aligned curriculum, and beautiful Himalayan campus.
**Cons**: Higher fee structure than other private universities, relatively high competition for the flagship CSE-AI specialization.
