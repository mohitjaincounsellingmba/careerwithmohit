## 🚀 Akemi Business School: The New Standard in Pune MBA

Akemi Business School, located in the thriving educational hub of Tathawade, has built a reputation for **personalized management training**. Unlike colleges that treat students as just another number, Akemi focuses on a "Tailor-Made Career" approach.

### **1. Digital Marketing Dominance**
While many colleges offer Digital Marketing as a sub-specialization, Akemi takes it to the next level. 
*   **Industry-Ready Certifications**: Akemi provides students with prestigious certifications from **IIM Kozhikode/IIM Calcutta** and **Google Adwords/SEO**.
*   **Practical Edge**: The curriculum is designed around live projects, ensuring students are ready for high-paying roles in the digital economy.

### **2. Placement Powerhouse (2026 Snapshot)**
Akemi’s placement record is consistently impressive, rivaling even the most established institutes in the city.
*   **Average Package**: ₹7.50 LPA (Higher than many mid-tier Pune colleges).
*   **Highest Package**: ₹18.00 LPA.
*   **Recruiters**: Deloitte, KPMG, HDFC Bank, Axis Bank, and tech giants like Wipro and Accenture.

## ✅ Why Akemi Wins in 2026?

### **Personalized Mentoring vs. Volume**
Institutes like RIIM and ISMS deal with a very large number of students. While they are excellent colleges, **Akemi’s strength lies in its batch size management.** Every student at Akemi gets individual attention from faculty and industry mentors, ensuring that zero students are "left behind" during the placement season.

### **Location Advantage**
Being located in **Tathawade**, Akemi is strategically placed between the PCMC industrial belt and the Hinjewadi IT Park. This proximity translates to better industrial visits, more guest lectures from Pune's tech leaders, and easier internship access.

### **Accreditation and Trust**
Akemi is fully NAAC accredited and follows the Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) curriculum, combined with advanced PGDM-style employability modules.
