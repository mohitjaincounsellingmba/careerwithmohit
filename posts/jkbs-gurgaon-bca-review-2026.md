While JK Business School (JKBS) is predominantly known for its PGDM and BBA programs, its **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)** has been gaining traction among students looking for a solid foundation in IT with a professional management touch. Affiliated with Gurugram University, the JKBS BCA program offers a blend of technical academic rigor and corporate readiness.

In this **JKBS Gurgaon BCA Review 2026**, we analyze the fee structure, placement trends, and the unique assessment-based selection process to help you decide if it’s the right fit for your IT career.

---

## 🏫 JKBS Gurgaon BCA: Overview
The BCA program at JKBS is designed for students who want a degree that is recognized by the state (via Gurugram University) while benefiting from a private B-school's corporate culture. The campus life at JKBS is more disciplined and professional compared to traditional degree colleges.

### **Key Highlights**
*   **Affiliation:** Gurugram University (A State University of Haryana).
*   **Campus:** 10-acre green campus with dedicated computer labs and high-speed labs.
*   **Pedagogy:** Focus on logical reasoning and management concepts along with core coding.

## 🎯 BCA Curriculum & Skills
The curriculum follows the Gurugram University guidelines but is enhanced by JKBS through:
*   **Professional Skill Drills:** Focus on communications and resume building.
*   **Technical Workshops:** Guest sessions on Python, AI, and Web Development.
*   **Management Integration:** Basics of business operations to prepare students for leadership roles in tech.

---

## 📈 Placement Review (2025-26)
Placements for BCA students are facilitated by the Corporate Resource Centre. While the highest packages are often seen in the PGDM segment, BCA students secure steady entry-level roles.

*   **Average Package:** **₹3.0 - ₹5.5 LPA**
*   **Highest Package (BCA):** Reported in the range of **₹7 - 8 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters for IT:** TCS, Wipro, JK Tech, Genpact, and local tech startups.
*   **Key Advantage:** Small batch sizes in BCA often result in more personalized attention during the placement season.

---

## 📝 Admission Process 2026
JKBS does not offer direct admission on marks alone. They follow a holistic selection process:

1.  **Eligibility:** 10+2 from a recognized board with minimum 45-50% marks (Maths is often a requirement).
2.  **Application:** Online via the official JKBS website.
3.  **Selection Rounds:**
    *   **WAT (Written Aptitude Test):** To check your written expression.
    *   **MSG (Management Simulation Game):** To check logical thinking.
    *   **PI (Personal Interview):** To assess your career goals and confidence.

**Pros:**

**Cons:**
