This is the most honest, data-backed comparison of **SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST, KTR)** vs **Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT, Vellore)** for B.Tech 2026 admissions.

## 💰 2. Fee Structure Comparison

### VIT Vellore – Category-Based Fee System
VIT has a unique sliding-scale fee model based on your VITEEE rank. The better your rank, the lower your fee.

| Category | VITEEE Rank (CSE) | 4-Year Total Fee |
|---|---|---|
| **Category 1** | Under 1,500 | ₹7.80 Lakhs |
| **Category 2** | 1,500 – 5,000 | ₹11.90 Lakhs |
| **Category 3** | 5,000 – 15,000 | ₹16.20 Lakhs |
| **Category 4** | 15,000 – 30,000 | ₹17.80 Lakhs |
| **Category 5** | Above 30,000 | ₹19.80 Lakhs |

> **Key Point:** If you crack VITEEE in the top 1,500, VIT Vellore (Cat 1) at ₹7.8L total is one of the **best ROI deals** in private engineering.

---

### SRM KTR – Fixed Fee by Branch

| Branch | Annual Fee | 4-Year Total |
|---|---|---|
| **CSE (Core)** | ₹4.0L – ₹4.5L | ₹16L – ₹18L |
| **CSE with AI/ML/DS** | ₹4.25L – ₹5.0L | ₹17L – ₹20L |
| **ECE / EEE** | ₹2.5L – ₹3.0L | ₹10L – ₹12L |
| **Mechanical / Civil** | ₹2.0L – ₹2.5L | ₹8L – ₹10L |

> **Key Point:** SRM charges a flat fee regardless of merit. The only way to reduce fees is via SRM's internal scholarship test.

**Fee Verdict:** 🏆 **VIT wins clearly** – if you rank well in VITEEE, you can get CSE at nearly half the cost of SRM.

## 🎓 3. Admission Process

### VIT Vellore
- Entrance: **VITEEE** (online, 125 MCQs)
- No management quota / no direct admission
- Slots booked via online portal based on rank
- **No donations, no backdoor entry**

### SRM Institute (KTR)
- Entrance: **SRMJEEE** (3 phases across the year)
- Also accepts **JEE Main / Board marks** for some seats
- Has **management quota** (direct admission without exam)
- Management quota CSE fee: ₹4.5L – ₹5L/year

**Admission Verdict:** 🏆 **VIT is more transparent and merit-based.** SRM offers flexibility with management quota, which is useful if your JEE/SRMJEEE score is borderline.

**Placements Verdict:** 🏆 **VIT has a slight edge** due to lower student intake and stronger coding culture. But SRM's placement numbers are respectable and comparable for core IT roles.

## 🏫 5. Campus Life & Culture

### VIT Vellore
- Fully Flexible Credit System (**FFCS**) – choose your own faculty and timetable
- 220+ student clubs and technical societies
- **Strict hostel rules** – curfew, limited outing permissions
- Very diverse student body (students from 50+ countries)
- Technical events: Gravitas (National Tech Fest), Riviera (Cultural Fest)
- Strong **coding culture** – multiple ACM ICPC regional qualifiers every year

### SRM KTR
- Fixed timetable, more structured academic calendar
- Massive, lively campus – like a mini-city
- **More relaxed hostel rules** compared to VIT
- Vibrant social life – lots of events, fests, and activities
- Strong **startup and entrepreneurship** ecosystem
- Tech Fest: Aaruush; Cultural Fest: Milan
- Active industry collaborations with Samsung, IBM, Bosch

**Campus Life Verdict:** 🏆 **SRM for social life; VIT for academic freedom via FFCS.** Depends entirely on what you value more.

## 🤔 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q. Is VIT better than SRM for placements?**
VIT has a slight edge in average package (₹9.23 LPA vs ₹8.5 LPA) and coding culture, but both are comparable for mass IT placements.

**Q. Which has better infrastructure – SRM or VIT?**
Both are world-class. VIT is larger (360 acres) and more self-contained. SRM has the advantage of being near Chennai.

**Q. Can I get direct admission at VIT without VITEEE?**
No. VIT does **not** offer management quota. Admission is strictly through VITEEE rank.

**Q. Is SRM CSE worth it at ₹16–18 lakhs?**
Yes, if you're looking at SRM KTR specifically and have realistic career goals in IT. The placement record justifies the investment for most students.

**Q. Which campus is better – SRM KTR or SRM AP?**
**KTR (Chennai)** is the flagship and best campus of SRM. SRM AP (Amaravati) is a newer, smaller campus with fewer companies visiting.
