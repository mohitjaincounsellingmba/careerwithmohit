## Tier 1 — Government & Autonomous Institutions

### 1. PEC University of Technology (Punjab Engineering College), Chandigarh
PEC is Chandigarh's most prestigious government engineering institution — a deemed university with strong faculty, excellent infrastructure, and deep placement roots in IT, defence (DRDO, HAL), and manufacturing.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Aerospace, Production, Metallurgy
- **Admission:** JEE Main → JoSAA Counselling
- **JEE Main Cutoff (PEC CSE, General 2025):** ~8,000 CRL
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.5 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹7–12 LPA | Highest: ₹40+ LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** Amazon, Microsoft, TCS, L&T, DRDO, Samsung

### 2. UIET — University Institute of Engineering & Technology, Panjab University
Part of the prestigious Panjab University, UIET is Chandigarh's top government-aided engineering college. A Panjab University degree carries great respect in North India.

- **Admission:** JEE Main / Board Merit (admissions routed via Punjab govt)
- **Annual Fee:** ₹60,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA

## Tier 2 — Top Private Universities near Chandigarh

### 3. Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, Patiala
Only 45 minutes from Chandigarh, Thapar is consistently ranked among India's top 5–10 private engineering universities. A must-consider for high-scoring JEE students who miss IIT/NIT cutoffs.

- **Courses:** CSE (AI/ML, DS), ECE, Chemical, Mechanical, Biotechnology, Mathematics & Computing
- **Admission:** JEE Main merit-based direct admission
- **Annual Fee:** ₹4.0 Lakhs
- **Avg Package (CSE):** ₹10–18 LPA | Highest: ₹60+ LPA (Sprinklr, Uber India)
- **Top Recruiters:** Microsoft, Sprinklr, Goldman Sachs, TCS Digital, Uber

### 4. Chandigarh University (CU), Mohali
The fastest-growing private university in North India with 30,000+ students, multiple campuses, and a massive placement cell that annually places 5,000+ students. CU is a good mass-placement option.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, ME, Civil, Aerospace, Data Science, Gaming, Cloud
- **Admission:** CUCET / JEE Main / Board Merit (Direct admission available)
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.5 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹5–9 LPA | Highest: ₹42 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Wipro, Infosys, HCL, Cognizant

### 5. Chitkara University, Rajpura (Mohali)
Chitkara is a fast-rising private university known for its industry-integrated programmes, IBM and Microsoft partnerships, and affordable fees.

- **Courses:** CSE (AI, Data Science, Cloud), ECE, Mechanical, Civil
- **Admission:** Chitkara CUCET / JEE / Board Merit
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.0 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹5–9 LPA

### 6. CGC Jhanjeri (Chandigarh Group of Colleges), Mohali
One of Mohali's most popular engineering colleges with affordable fees and decent CSE/ECE placement support.

- **Admission:** JEE Main / Board Merit | **Fee:** ₹1.0 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

## Punjab Engineering — State Admission Process 2026

- **Government Colleges (PEC, GNDEC, NIT Jalandhar):** Via JEE Main → JoSAA or Punjab state counselling (PTUSDL)
- **Private Colleges:** Via CUCET (Chandigarh University), Chitkara own test, or direct board merit admission

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Chandigarh 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Chandigarh?**
PEC Chandigarh (Punjab Engineering College) is the top government institution. Thapar University (Patiala, 45 mins away) is the top private option for high-quality education and placements.

**Q2. Is Chandigarh University good for B.Tech?**
Yes, CU is a good option if you want mass placements (₹5–9 LPA average) and a large campus. However, for quality of education and brand value, PEC and Thapar are superior.

**Q3. What JEE Main rank is needed for PEC Chandigarh CSE?**
A JEE Main CRL rank under 8,000 (General category) is typically required for CSE at PEC Chandigarh.

**Q4. Is Thapar University worth the high fees?**
Yes, if you're targeting software product companies. Thapar's ROI is excellent — ₹16 Lakhs total fees vs. ₹10–18 LPA average salary is a strong return.

**Q5. Can I get direct admission in Chandigarh engineering colleges?**
Yes — Chandigarh University, Chitkara, CGC, and Rayat Bahra offer direct admissions based on board marks without requiring a JEE score.
