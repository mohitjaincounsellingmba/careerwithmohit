## 🏛️ Tier 1: The Lucknow Foundations

### 1. Lucknow University (LU)
The gold standard for computer applications in UP. Their BCA degree is highly valued by government sectors and MNC mass recruiters like Infosys.
- **Selection:** LUVET Entrance Test.
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Infosys, HCL, Wipro.

### 2. Amity University, Lucknow
The most "Corporate-Ready" campus in the city. Known for its global infrastructure and massive placement drives.
- **USP:** Industry-aligned labs and frequent guest lectures from HCL tech leads.
- **Admission:** Merit-based + Personal Interview.

### 3. BBD University (Babu Banarasi Das)
A household name in Lucknow for technical education. They have one of the largest batches and consistent placement success in North India.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is BCA from Lucknow University good?**
Yes. For government jobs and traditional mass recruitment (TCS/Wipro), Lucknow University is very highly regarded.

**Q2. Does Amity Lucknow require Maths for BCA?**
Generally, they require Mathematics or Computer Science at the 12th level. However, they also offer "Bridge Courses" for students from non-maths backgrounds.

**Q3. What is the average fee for BCA in Lucknow?**
Government-affiliated colleges cost around ₹30,000 to ₹50,000 per year. Private colleges range from ₹80,000 to ₹1.5 Lakhs per year.

**Q4. Can I get a job in Noida/Gurgaon after BCA in Lucknow?**
Yes. Lucknow is part of the same "Hindi-Belt" corporate recruitment circuit. Companies that visit Noida habitually visit Lucknow for talent.

**Q5. When is the LU BCA admission 2026 starting?**
Registrations usually start in **April/May**. The entrance test (LUVET) is typically held in June.
