## 🏛️ The Icons: Symbiosis & ILS

### 1. Symbiosis Law School (SLS), Pune
Consistently ranked among the top 10 law schools (NLU and non-NLU) in India. SLS Pune is essentially the gateway to corporate law firms in Mumbai and Delhi.
- **Top Recruiters:** Khaitan & Co, J. Sagar Associates, ICICI Bank.
- **Admission Strategy:** Score high in **SLAT** and have a strong current affairs background for the PI-WAT round.

### 2. ILS Law College (Indian Law Society)
A heritage institution that has produced Supreme Court judges and top legal luminaries. Known for its massive 150-acre green campus and strict litigation-focused pedagogy.
- **Selection:** High percentile in the **Maharashtra Law CET (3-year or 5-year)** is mandatory for both Maharashtra and Outer-Maharashtra category students.

## 🚀 The Pune Advantage: Corporate vs Litigation

For a 2026 Law student, Pune offers two distinct paths:
- **The Litigation Route:** Colleges like **ILS** and **DES Shri Navalmal** are powerhouses for students targeting judicial services or independent practice at the Bombay High Court.
- **The Corporate Route:** **SLS Pune** and **Bharati Vidyapeeth** are preferred by students seeking roles in law firms and multinational corporations (MNCs).

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is Pune better than Mumbai for Law?**
**Pune** is a better "student city" with a self-contained campus lifestyle. **Mumbai** is better for students who want to clear internships in the Financial District (BKC/Fort) every single day.

**Q2. Does SLS Pune accept CLAT?**
No. Symbiosis Law School Pune strictly requires you to appear for the **Symbiosis Law Admission Test (SLAT)**.

**Q3. What is the fee for LLB in ILS Pune?**
ILS is a grant-in-aid institution. The annual fees are very affordable, ranging from ₹35,000 to ₹50,000 per year for Maharashtra domicile students.

**Q4. Can I get direct admission in Pune Law colleges?**
Most reputable colleges in Pune follow the **MAH Law CET** centralized process. However, some private universities like MIT-WPU and various autonomous bodies offer an Institutional Quota for direct admission.

**Q5. When is the MAH CET Law 2026 exam?**
The 5-year CET is usually in **May**, and the 3-year CET is in **June**.
