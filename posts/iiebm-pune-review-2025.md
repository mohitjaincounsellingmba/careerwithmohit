IIEBM (Indus Business School) is one of Pune's most established PGDM institutes, known for its rigorous academic standards and a unique focus on ERP training through SAP integration.

### **Quick Highlights (2025 Batch):**
*   **Avg Placement**: ₹7.95 LPA (General) | ₹10.25 LPA (SAP Batch)
*   **Highest Package**: ₹24.00 LPA (Domestic) | ₹34.00 LPA (SAP Batch)
*   **Placement Rate**: ~95%+
*   **Entrance Exam**: CAT / XAT / MAT / CMAT / ATMA / GMAT

### **The IIEBM Advantage:**
*   **SAP Certification**: Its PGDM + PGPERP (SAP) program is a major differentiator, making students highly job-ready for specialized consulting roles.
*   **Military-Grade Discipline**: Focuses heavily on grooming, ethics, and professional conduct.
*   **Corporate Network**: Over 300+ companies visit every year, including giants like Deloitte, PepsiCo, and Mahindra Finance.

### **Fee Structure:**
The total tuition fee is **₹9.15 Lakhs** for PGDM and **₹11.9 Lakhs** for the specialized SAP integrated program. If you are looking for a tech-heavy management career, IIEBM is a standout choice.
