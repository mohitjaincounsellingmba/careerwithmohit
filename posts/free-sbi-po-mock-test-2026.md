## What Makes SBI PO Tougher Than IBPS PO?

SBI PO is notoriously harder than IBPS PO for several critical reasons:

- **Higher Cutoffs:** SBI's section-wise and overall cutoffs are typically 5–8 marks higher than IBPS PO.
- **Complex Reasoning:** Puzzles in SBI PO often involve multi-variable, multi-layered constraint problems that require 10–15 minutes per set.
- **RC Difficulty:** SBI PO reading comprehension passages are longer and cover abstract or editorial-style business and finance topics.
- **Surprise Patterns:** SBI frequently changes the question format within sections without notice, requiring candidates to be highly adaptable.

## Section-Wise Strategy for SBI PO Prelims

- **English (30 Q, 20 min):** Don't spend more than 8 mins on RC. Prioritize grammar, error detection, and cloze tests first.
- **Quantitative Aptitude (35 Q, 20 min):** SBI DI sets are complex. Solve arithmetic questions first (5–6 quick ones), then dedicate 10+ minutes to DI.
- **Reasoning Ability (35 Q, 20 min):** Puzzles dominate. Attempt 2 puzzles max — choose the simpler ones. Finish with coding-decoding and inequality quickly.
