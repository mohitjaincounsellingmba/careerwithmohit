## Section-Wise Strategy for LIC AAO Prelims

- **Reasoning (35 Q, 20 min):** High-level Puzzles, Seating Arrangements, Syllogism, Input-Output, Inequality. Difficulty is IBPS PO-equivalent. Attempt easier questions first — aim for 28+ correct.
- **Quantitative Aptitude (35 Q, 20 min):** Data Interpretation (2–3 sets), Number Series (5 Q), and Arithmetic (10–12 Q). Aim for 28+ correct.
- **English (30 Q, 20 min — Qualifying):** Only need 12/30 (40%) to qualify. Do not spend too much time here; save your energy for Reasoning and Quant.

## LIC AAO Specializations in Mains

LIC AAO Mains tests you on a General Awareness section **with heavy insurance and financial market content**. Depending on your specialization applied, Mains may include:
- **Generalist (General):** Insurance & Financial Market Awareness
- **IT:** Computer Science, Software Development
- **Chartered Accountant (CA):** Accounting, Finance, Auditing
- **Actuarial:** Statistics, Probability, Actuarial Science
- **Legal:** Labour Laws, Contract Law, Corporate Law

Most candidates apply for the **Generalist** track, which requires the most familiar exam preparation.

## LIC AAO Mains Insurance Awareness — Key Topics

- **Life Insurance Basics:** Term plan, ULIP, Endowment, Whole Life, Money-Back plans
- **IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India):** Regulations, guidelines, recent circular changes
- **LIC Products and History:** Founded 1956, Bima Gold, Jeevan Anand, LIC Kanyadan Policy
- **General Financial Markets:** Mutual Funds, Stock Market, Banking Regulations
- **International Insurance Bodies:** IAIS, insurance penetration ratios across countries

## Pro Tips for LIC AAO 2026

- **Don't Waste Time on English:** Since English is only qualifying, aim for 13–14 correct answers (just above 40%) and invest most of your 60 minutes in Reasoning and Quant.
- **Reasoning Puzzle Strategy:** Unlike simple clerk-level exams, LIC AAO reasoning is PO-level. Practice 2 full puzzle sets + Syllogism + Inequality daily for 6 weeks.
- **Insurance Awareness from Day 1:** Start reading about LIC products, IRDAI regulations, and insurance current affairs immediately — this is the key differentiator in Mains.
- **Apply for Interview Prep Early:** LIC AAO has a Personal Interview round after Mains. Start personality development and interview practice early.
