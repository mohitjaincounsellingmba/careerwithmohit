**Vivekananda Global University (VGU)**, established in 2012 and accredited with **NAAC Grade 'A+'**, is one of the most prominent private multidisciplinary universities in Jaipur (Rajasthan). Known for its industry-aligned engineering programs (especially in CSE and Artificial Intelligence), VGU has established itself as an affordable and high-growth destination for engineering aspirants.

### **VGU Jaipur Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Entrance Route**: Based on scores in **JEE Main 2026**, **CUET UG**, or the university's official entrance exam **VGUCET**.
2.  **Merit Route**: Based on 10+2 marks (minimum 45% aggregate in PCM).
3.  **Specializations**: Flagship B.Tech in CSE (Core), with tracks in AI, Cyber Security, and Cloud Computing.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
VGU is accessible to students with middle-range JEE scores or decent Class 12 percentages.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | Other Core Engineering |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 60 - 70+ | 50 - 60 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM)** | 70% - 80%+ | 60% - 70% |
| **VGUCET Score** | 75th Percentile+ | 65th Percentile+ |

### **VGU Jaipur B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
VGU offers a competitive fee structure compared to other premium private universities in Jaipur.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee (CSE Core)**: approx. ₹2.03 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹6.1 Lakhs - ₹8.7 Lakhs** (for the entire duration).
*   **Registration/Misc**: approx. ₹25,000 (one-time).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.0 Lakhs to ₹1.5 Lakhs per year (depending on amenities).

### **Placement Review: Industry-Ready Success**
Vivekananda Global University has a strong placement division that focuses on product-based and service-oriented companies.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹46.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹4.60 LPA to ₹6.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Infosys, Google, Deloitte, Wipro, IBM, PayTm, and Oracle.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently around 80% student placement in tech branches.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: NAAC A+ Grade, excellent lab infrastructure for AI and Cyber Security, affordable fee structure for a premium university, and situates students in Jaipur's growing tech hub.
**Cons**: Average packages for core branches like Mechanical or Civil are lower than CSE, relatively high academic pressure for final placement targets.
