The Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT), established by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, is the premier institute for learning International Business in India. With campuses in Delhi and Kolkata, it is legendary for its global outlook and stellar placements. If you are targetting **Direct Admission in IIFT for 2026**, it is essential to understand the specific merit-linked pathways available for non-regular applicants.

## Why IIFT is the Ultimate Destination for International Business?

- **Specialized MBA (IB)**: The most respected program in India for a career in global trade, logistics, and international marketing.
- **Top placements**: For the 2025 batch, the average package touched **₹29.1 LPA**, with several students securing overseas offers.
- **Global Connections**: Strong links with global trade organizations and a vast alumni base across the world.

## The Truth About Direct Admission at IIFT

IIFT is a government-undertaking and is strictly merit-centric. It does not have a private management quota. Here are the valid routes for direct entry in 2026:

### 1. Foreign National / NRI Quota
IIFT reserves a dedicated number of seats for Foreign Nationals and NRI candidates. 
- **Entrance Score**: These candidates must apply with a valid **GMAT score**.
- **Process**: Selection is based on the GMAT score, academic profile, and a dedicated interview round. This allows international applicants to secure a seat without appearing for the CAT.

### 2. Corporate Sponsored Seats (Executive Program)
IIFT offers executive courses where professionals with 3+ years of experience can be sponsored for the program, providing a more direct entry pathway for experienced corporate individuals.

### 3. Institutional Selection for 2026
For Indian resident students, the only way into IIFT for 2026 is through the **CAT (Common Admission Test)**.
- **Historical Change**: Note that IIFT has discontinued its own entrance exam (IIFT exam) and now accepts CAT scores for its flagship MBA-IB program. 
- **Merit List**: Shortlisting is strictly based on CAT scores (usually 95+ percentile), academic performance, and PI performance.

## Fee Structure for 2026
The total academic fee for the MBA-IB program at IIFT is approximately **₹21 Lakhs to ₹23 Lakhs**. For Foreign/NRI students, the fees are structured differently in USD ($).

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is there a management quota in IIFT?
No, IIFT is a government institute and does not offer donation-based management quota seats.

### 2. How can I get direct admission in IIFT?
Direct admission is only available for **Foreign Nationals and NRIs** through the GMAT entry route.

### 3. Does IIFT accept CAT scores?
Yes, for the 2026-28 batch, IIFT will accept **CAT 2025** scores for its flagship MBA (International Business) program.

### 4. What is the average package of IIFT?
The latest average package for the MBA-IB batch is approximately **₹29.1 LPA**.
