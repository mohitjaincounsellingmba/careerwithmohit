## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Can NRIs apply through CAT for IIFT?
NRIs who are residing in India at the time of the Common Admission Test (CAT) can apply through the general merit route. However, those residing abroad are eligible to apply through the GMAT route.

### 2. Is there a Management Quota in IIFT?
No. IIFT is a government-regulated autonomous institution. All seats, including those for NRIs and Foreign Nationals, are filled strictly on merit (GMAT score + Profile + Interview).

### 3. What documents are required for the NRI category?
You will typically need a valid Passport, proof of NRI status (e.g., Parent's working visa/PR), and an official GMAT score report from GMAC.
