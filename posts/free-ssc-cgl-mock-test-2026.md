## Section-Wise Strategy for SSC CGL Tier 1

- **English:** Attempt first — it's the fastest-scoring section. Focus on error spotting, fill-in-the-blanks, and synonyms/antonyms.
- **General Awareness:** Static GK (polity, history, science) + current affairs from the last 6 months. Attempt all questions since you either know it or you don't.
- **Quantitative Aptitude:** Focus on Arithmetic topics — Percentage, Ratio, Profit & Loss, Time & Work. Skip complex geometry if stuck.
- **Reasoning:** Coding-decoding, series, analogy, and syllogism are high-scoring and relatively quick.
