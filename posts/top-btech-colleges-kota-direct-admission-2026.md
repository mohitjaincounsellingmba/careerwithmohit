## 1. IIIT Kota (Indian Institute of Information Technology)
The **Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kota (IIITK)** is the most prestigious engineering institute in the city. It is an Institute of National Importance and currently operates from its permanent campus in Ranpur, Kota.

*   **Admission Process:** Strictly through **JEE Main** ranks.
*   **Counseling:** Conducted by **JoSAA/CSAB**.
*   **Top Branches:** CSE (Computer Science) and ECE (Electronics & Communication).
*   **Why Choose IIIT Kota:** Exceptional brand value, high-tier placements (Avg package ₹12-15 LPA), and a strong network of Silicon Valley recruiters.

## B.Tech Counseling Process in Kota

If you're targeting colleges in Kota, you'll likely go through one of these two major counseling bodies:

### 1. JoSAA / CSAB (For IIIT Kota)
The **Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA)** handles admissions for all IIITs, NITs, and IITs. You must appear for JEE Main and register on the JoSAA portal to secure a seat at IIIT Kota.

### 2. REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process)
For **RTU Kota** and private colleges like **MIT**, the primary admission route is **REAP**. 
*   **Priority 1:** JEE Main Score.
*   **Priority 2:** 12th Board Percentile (for those who didn't appear for JEE).
*   **Priority 3:** Diploma in Engineering (for Lateral Entry).
