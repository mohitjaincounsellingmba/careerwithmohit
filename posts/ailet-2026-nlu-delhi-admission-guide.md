While CLAT is the gateway to 24 NLUs, **AILET (All India Law Entrance Test)** is the exclusive gateway to just one: **NLU Delhi**. Ranked consistently among the top 3 law schools in India, NLU Delhi has a legacy of producing elite judicial and litigation professionals.

Because the number of seats is extremely limited (approx. 110-120), AILET is often considered **tougher than CLAT** due to its unpredictable logical reasoning and high difficulty level. This guide provides a definitive **AILET 2026 preparation strategy**.

## 📝 AILET 2026 Exam Pattern (The 3-Section Format)

NLU Delhi changed its pattern recently. There are no separate "Legal Reasoning" or "Maths" sections. The entire exam (150 marks) is divided into three parts:

### 1. English Language (50 Marks)
- High-level comprehension and vocabulary.
- Direct grammar questions are rare; it's all about context and speed.

### 2. Current Affairs & GK (30 Marks)
- Focused on the last 12 months.
- High frequency of "Legal Current Affairs" (Bills, Judgments, Apppointments).

### 3. Logical Reasoning (70 Marks)
- **The Decider:** This section now includes elements of Legal Logic embedded within logical puzzles.
- It tests your ability to identify patterns, draw conclusions, and apply logic to complex scenarios.

## 🚀 AILET vs. CLAT: Strategic Differences

| Feature | CLAT | AILET |
|---|---|---|
| **Total Marks** | 120 | 150 |
| **Duration** | 2 Hours | 2 Hours (Much tighter) |
| **Difficulty** | Logic-based | Speed & Accuracy based |
| **Legal Sec** | Pure Legal Reasoning | Mixed with Logic |
| **Maths** | Yes (10-12 marks) | NO |

**Pro Tip:** If you are "Maths-phobic," AILET is a great opportunity as it has zero mathematics. However, you must compensate with incredible English and Logical speed.

## 🏗️ 3 Tips for AILET 2026 Success

1.  **Prioritize Logical Reasoning:** Since 70 marks are from logic, this is where you win or lose the seat. Solve puzzles from GMAT and LSAT level books to stay ahead.
2.  **Focus on NLU Delhi Specials:** Review the "NLU Delhi Newsletter" and recent faculty research topics. NLU Delhi sometimes subtly includes themes from their own focus areas in the GK section.
3.  **Mock Drill (The 90-Minute Bar):** While the exam is 120 minutes, aim to finish your mocks in **90-100 minutes**. AILET is notorious for having "Extra Long" logic passages that consume time.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. can I use CLAT scores for NLU Delhi?**
No. NLU Delhi **does not accept CLAT scores**. You must appear for the AILET exam separately.

**Q2. Is AILET open to all categories?**
Yes. Seats are reserved for SC, ST, OBC, PWD, and EWS categories as per government regulations.

**Q3. Does NLU Delhi have a 3-Year LLB course?**
NLU Delhi traditionally offered only the 5-year Integrated BA LLB. However, they have recently introduced a **multi-disciplinary** approach—always check the latest 2026 brochure in August.

**Q4. What is the cutoff for NLU Delhi?**
For the General category, a score of **85-95 out of 150** is usually required to stay in the safe zone, but this varies significantly based on paper difficulty.

**Q5. When is the best time to start AILET prep?**
Ideally, you should start along with CLAT prep (around **12 months before exam**). Since the syllabus overlaps by 80%, you just need to adjust your logic speed for AILET in the final 3 months.
