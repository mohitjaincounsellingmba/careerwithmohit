### **College Review: IILM University**
*   **Institutional Legacy**: With over 30 years of excellence, IILM is a well-established name in management education.
*   **Dynamic Learning**: Focuses on liberal education mixed with technical management skills.
*   **Multi-Campus**: Advantage of campuses in New Delhi (Lodhi Road), Gurugram, and Greater Noida.

### **Placement Review (2025 Batch)**
*   **Gurugram Campus**: Reported **100% placements** with a highest package of **₹26 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: ₹7 - 8.6 LPA (depending on the campus and branch).
*   **Top Recruiters**: Deloitte, KPMG, E&Y, PwC, Google, and Amazon.
*   **Global Reach**: Strong alumni network helping students secure roles in global consulting firms.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Lush Green Campuses**: All campuses (especially Gurugram and Greater Noida) are known for their greenery and maintenance.
*   **Library**: Extensive collection of books, digital resources, and peaceful reading zones.
*   **Digital Prep**: High-speed Wi-Fi and updated computer labs across all sites.
*   **Cafeteria**: Great medical facilities and diverse student-friendly cafes on campus.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Blended Learning**: Faculty members use a mix of traditional lectures and modern digital tools.
*   **Supportive Staff**: Highly educated teachers with significant industry and academic experience.
*   **Practical Edge**: Strong focus on workshops, fieldwork, and case study discussions.

**Final Verdict**: IILM is a safe and prestigious choice for students who want a balanced MBA experience with reliable placement support and a strong alumni network.
