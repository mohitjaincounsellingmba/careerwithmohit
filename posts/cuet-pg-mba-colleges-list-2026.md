### **📍 How to Apply to These Colleges?**
Unlike CAT, the CUET PG admission process is **not centralized**. Here is what you need to do:

1.  **Check CUET PG Score Card**: Once results are out, check your raw score and percentile.
2.  **Individual Registration**: You must visit the **official website of each university** separately and fill out their counseling forms.
3.  **Merit List**: Boards like DU (CSAS) or BHU will release their own merit lists based on your CUET scores.
4.  **Group Discussion (GD) & Personal Interview (PI)**: Top tier colleges like TISS, BHU, and JNU will conduct a second round of selection for shortlisted candidates.
