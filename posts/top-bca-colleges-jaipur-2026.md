## 🏛️ Tier 1: The Jaipur Leaders

### 1. Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ)
The most premium BCA program in Rajasthan. MUJ offers a state-of-the-art campus and an industry-aligned curriculum that is highly respected by global MNCs.
- **Top Recruiters:** Microsoft, Amazon, Infosys, Dell.
- **USP:** Massive campus life and incredible global exchange opportunities.

### 2. University of Rajasthan (UniRaj)
For those looking for high brand value at almost zero fees. Being a state university, it is highly respected across North India for government jobs and MCA admissions.

### 3. JECRC University
A name synonymous with engineering in Rajasthan. Their BCA program benefits from the same centralized placement cell that works with hundreds of IT companies.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is BCA from Jaipur worth it?**
Yes. Jaipur is the #1 educational hub of Rajasthan. If you plan to work in North India or target the nearby Gurgaon IT hub, Jaipur is an excellent starting point.

**Q2. Does Manipal Jaipur require Maths for BCA?**
No, MUJ often accepts students from all streams for BCA, provided you have a high percentage in your 12th Board results.

**Q3. What is the average package for BCA in Jaipur?**
For top private universities (Manipal/JECRC), expect ₹4L to ₹6L. For other colleges, packages range from ₹2.5L to ₹4L.

**Q4. Can I get a job in Gurgaon/NCR after doing BCA in Jaipur?**
Absolutely. Many Jaipur universities have dedicated placement drives in Gurgaon/Noida due to their close geographic proximity.

**Q5. When is the University of Rajasthan BCA admission starting?**
Usually in **June/July**, following the RBSE and CBSE board result declarations.
