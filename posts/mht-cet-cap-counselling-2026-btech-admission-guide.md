**MHT-CET CAP (Centralised Admission Process) 2026**, conducted by the State CET Cell, Government of Maharashtra, is the official gateway for engineering admissions in over 300+ government and private colleges in Maharashtra. Known for legendary institutes like **COEP Pune** and **VJTI Mumbai**, MHT-CET is a top choice for students nationwide.

### **MHT-CET CAP 2026 Important Dates (Tentative)**
| Event | Expected Date |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Counselling Registration** | June 2026 |
| **Final Merit List Release** | July 2026 |
| **Choice Filling (CAP Round 1)**| July 2026 |
| **Round 1 Allotment Results** | August 2026 |
| **Reporting to College** | August 2026 |

### **Eligibility & Admission Criteria**
*   **Entrance Exam**: 
    *   **Maharashtra State Candidates**: Must have a valid **MHT-CET 2026** score (PCM).
    *   **All India Candidates**: Can apply using their **JEE Main 2026** score (priority) or MHT-CET 2026 score.
*   **Quota**: 
    *   **Maharashtra Domicile**: 85% of total seats are reserved for Maharashtra state students.
    *   **All India**: 15% seats in private unaided institutions are open for non-Maharashtra candidates.

### **Counselling Fee & Seat Acceptance**
*   **Registration Fee**: 
    *   General/OMS/Ladakh Migrants: ₹800.
    *   Reserved/Maharashtra PWD: ₹600.
    *   NRI/OCI/PIO: ₹5,000.

### **Step-by-Step CAP Counselling Procedure**
1.  **Online Registration**: Visit `cetcell.mahacet.org` and register with your application details.
2.  **Document Verification**: All documents (10+2 marks, CET/JEE scorecard, Domicile, Category) are verified online or at a designated Facilitation Centre (FC).
3.  **Merit List Release**: A provisional merit list is released first, followed by the final merit list. Your "State Merit Number" determines your allotment priority.
4.  **Choice Filling (Option Entry)**: Fill and lock your college/branch preferences for CAP Round 1.
5.  **Seat Allotment**: Check your allotment result online.
6.  **Seat Acceptance**: Use the "Freeze" if satisfied or "Not-Freeze" if you want a better seat in next CAP rounds. Report to the allotted college within the deadline.

**Pro Tip for MHT-CET 2026**:
Maharashtra has many autonomous institutes (COEP, VJTI, ICT) which have their own academic calendar and very high placement reputations. If your rank is good, prioritize these even if they are outside your home district.
