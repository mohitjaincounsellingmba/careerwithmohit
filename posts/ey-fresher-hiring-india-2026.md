### **Key Job Roles for Freshers**

#### **1. Technology Consultant / Associate Software Engineer**
- **Eligibility**: B.E/B.Tech/MCA/M.Sc (CS/IT).
- **Skills**: Java, Python, SQL, Cloud Fundamentals (Azure/AWS).
- **Focus**: Implementing digital solutions and IT infrastructure for global clients.

#### **2. Tax Associate / Audit Associate**
- **Eligibility**: B.Com/M.Com/BBA/MBA (Finance).
- **Skills**: Strong accounting principles, Excel, Analytical thinking.
- **Focus**: Assisting in financial audits and corporate tax filings.

#### **3. Data Analyst**
- **Eligibility**: Any graduate with strong mathematical and analytical skills.
- **Skills**: SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Excel.
- **Focus**: Interpreting complex data sets to help businesses make informed decisions.

### **EY Selection Process 2026**
The recruitment process is structured into 3-4 key stages:
1.  **Online Assessment**:
    - **Aptitude**: Quantitative, Logical, and Verbal reasoning.
    - **Technical MCQ**: Domain-specific questions.
    - **Situational Judgment Test (SJT)**: Behavioral assessment.
2.  **Technical Interview**: Deep dive into your projects, internships, and core technical skills.
3.  **HR/Partner Interview**: Focuses on cultural fit, communication, and long-term career goals at EY.

### **How to Apply?**
1. Visit the **[EY Careers India](https://www.ey.com/en_in/careers)** page.
2. Select "Early Career" or search for "Associate" roles.
3. Use filters like "India" and your preferred city.
4. Upload your resume and complete the application form.

### **Preparation Strategy for EY**
- **Focus on SJT**: EY places high value on how you handle real-world workplace scenarios. Practice Situational Judgment Tests.
- **Communication is Key**: Being a client-facing firm, EY prioritizes candidates with excellent verbal and written English.
- **Understand EY Values**: Research the "Building a better working world" mission and align your answers with their core values.
