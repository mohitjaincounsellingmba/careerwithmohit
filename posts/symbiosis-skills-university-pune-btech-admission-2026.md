**Symbiosis Skills and Professional University (SSPU)** in Pune is recognized as Maharashtra's first "Skill Development University." Unlike traditional engineering colleges that focus solely on academic theory, SSPU is designed to create **industry-ready** professionals through a unique B.Tech model that integrates mandatory internships every year.

If you are an engineering aspirant looking at **SSPU Pune B.Tech 2026 admission**, here is the complete breakdown of the process, fees, and the "Skill-based" approach.

### **SSPU Admission Channels 2026**
SSPU conducts its own admission process and accepts multiple entrance exams:
1.  **SEET (Symbiosis Engineering Entrance Test)**: The university's official entrance exam.
2.  **JEE Main / MHT-CET**: Valid for all B.Tech seats.
3.  **CUET-UG**: Accepted for specific specializations.

### **SEET 2026: Important Exam Dates**
*   **Application Commencement**: Expected in February 2026.
*   **Registration Deadline**: March 29, 2026.
*   **Final Rounds**: April - June 2026.

### **B.Tech Specializations at SSPU**
*   B.Tech in Computer Science & IT (Cyber Security / Data Science)
*   B.Tech in Mechatronics Engineering
*   B.Tech in Automobile Engineering
*   B.Tech in Logistics & Supply Chain Management

### **SSPU B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
SSPU offers a high-value skill-based education, with fees covering extensive laboratory work and industry certification.

| Fee Component | Approx. Annual Amount |
| :--- | :--- |
| **B.Tech Tuition Fee** | ₹2,90,000 |
| **Total 4-Year Tuition (Approx.)** | **₹11.5 Lakhs - ₹12.0 Lakhs** |
| **Hostel & Mess (Optional)** | ₹1.8 Lakhs - ₹2.0 Lakhs |

*Total course cost including premium hostel facilities can reach up to ₹19 Lakhs over four years.*

### **Placement Review: Industry-Ready ROI**
The unique advantage of SSPU is its **Placement Assistance** and mandatory on-the-job training.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹12.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹5.50 LPA.
*   **Average Package (CSE/IT)**: ₹6.5 LPA to ₹7.5 LPA.
*   **Key Recruiters**: Mahindra & Mahindra, Tata Motors, Amazon, Reliance, and various high-tech MSMEs.
*   **Internship Rate**: 100% (mandatory for all students every year).

### **Why Choose SSPU Pune?**
*   **Practical Exposure**: 70% of the curriculum is practical/hands-on, ensuring zero "gap" between college and industry.
*   **Annual Internships**: Students work in actual companies for 2 months every year, leading to better PPOs (Pre-Placement Offers).
*   **Campus Facilities**: World-class labs for Mechatronics and Automobile engineering, supported by industrial partners.
