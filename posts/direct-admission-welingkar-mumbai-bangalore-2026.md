Welingkar Institute of Management (WeSchool) is a top-ranked institute in Mumbai and Bangalore, famous for its innovation-led curriculum and corporate connections. For the 2026-28 batch, WeSchool offers **Direct Admission** through Management and NRI quotas for aspirants who want to secure their career in a premier B-school.

## Why WeSchool is a Top Choice?

Welingkar is known for its specialized PGDM programs that cater to emerging industry needs:
- **PGDM Specializations**: E-Business, Research & Business Analytics, Retail, Healthcare, and Media.
- **Mumbai Campus**: Located in Matunga, it offers massive networking opportunities in India's financial capital.
- **Bangalore Campus**: Situated in Electronic City, perfect for students eyeing roles in Tech and E-commerce.
- **Placements**: Average packages are around **₹12 LPA to ₹12.5 LPA**.

## Welingkar Direct Admission: How it Works

Unlike traditional "donation" seats, Welingkar's management quota is a structured process that prioritizes profiles alongside merit.

### 1. Management Quota Seats
Welingkar reserves approximately 15% to 20% of its total seats for the Management/Institutional category. These seats are filled based on academic performance and entrance scores in CAT, XAT, CMAT, ATMA, or GMAT.

### 2. NRI Quota Admission
Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and their wards can apply under a separate quota. This is a profile-based process that requires valid documentation and meeting the university's base eligibility.

### Fee Structure 2026
The regular PGDM fee at Welingkar is approximately **₹14 Lakhs to ₹15 Lakhs** for two years. For Management/NRI category seats, there is usually an additional institutional fee which varies depending on the specialization and campus.

## Selection Strategy for 2026

To secure a seat at Welingkar directly:
1. **Entrance Exam**: Ensure you have a valid score in any national-level exam. Even if your percentile is moderate (70-80), you are eligible for the management category.
2. **Personal Interview**: WeSchool places heavy weightage on your passion for innovation and "Design Thinking."
3. **Early Application**: Since these seats are limited, applying in the early cycles (January-February) is critical.
