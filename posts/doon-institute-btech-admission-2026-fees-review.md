**Doon Institute of Engineering and Technology (DIET)**, located in the scenic surroundings of Dehradun (Rishikesh), is a prominent constituent of the Doon Group of Institutions. Affiliated with Uttarakhand Technical University (UTU) and approved by AICTE, DIET is known for providing quality technical education at an affordable price point compared to premium private universities in the region.

### **DIET Dehradun Admission Process 2026**
1.  **Selection Base**: 85% of seats are filled through the **Uttarakhand Technical University (UTU)** centralized counselling based on **JEE Main 2026** ranks.
2.  **Quota**: 50% seats are reserved for Uttarakhand domicile students, and 35% for All India candidates.
3.  **Management Quota**: 15% seats are available for direct admission based on JEE Main merit and 10+2 marks.

### **B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
One of the biggest advantages of DIET is its highly affordable fee structure, making it a great choice for budget-conscious students.
*   **Total Course Fee**: approx. ₹3.2 Lakhs - ₹3.5 Lakhs (for the entire 4-year duration).
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹75,000 - ₹85,000.
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹60,000 to ₹80,000 per year.

### **Placement Review: Preparing Industry-Ready Graduates**
DIET focuses on skill development and career preparation through its dedicated placement cell.
*   **Placement Rate**: approx. 70% for technical branches.
*   **Average Package**: ₹3.00 LPA to ₹4.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: TCS, Infosys, HCL, JBM Group, Munjal Showa, and Eicher.
*   **Training**: Includes resume building, group discussions, and aptitude training for final year students.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Highly affordable fees, scenic and peaceful campus, supportive faculty, and affiliation with a reputed state technical university.
**Cons**: Average packages are lower than premium private universities, infrastructure for advanced research labs is basic compared to high-fee colleges.
