### **Sector-Wise Placement Trends:**
*   **Consulting & Finance**: NDIM has a strong footprint in the consulting domain, with the "Big 4" (Deloitte, KPMG, EY, PwC) frequently visiting for Risk Advisory and Financial Analyst roles.
*   **Marketing & Sales**: FMCG giants like Nestlé, ITC, and Asian Paints offer lucrative roles in channel management and brand strategy.
*   **IT & E-commerce**: Tech leaders like Amazon, Genpact, and Wipro actively recruit for data analytics and product management profiles.

### **The NDIM Edge:**
*   **Corporate Mentorship**: NDIM is known for its strong industry-linked curriculum and over 250+ corporate mentors who guide students for final interviews.
*   **Location Advantage**: Being located in the heart of Delhi, students have year-round access to live projects and internships in the NCR region.
