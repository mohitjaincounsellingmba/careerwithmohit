**JSS Academy of Technical Education (JSSATE)** in Noida is widely considered the #1 engineering college affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU). Located in Sector 62, Noida, JSS is a premium destination for AKTU aspirants, known for its academic heritage, high-quality faculty, and spectacular results in the university rank lists.

If you are looking at **JSS Noida B.Tech 2026 admissions**, here is your detailed breakdown of the process, cutoffs, and fees.

### **JSS Noida Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Entrance Exam**: You must appear for **JEE Main 2026**.
2.  **UPTAC Counselling**: Primary route for 85% of the seats through AKTU's official counselling.
3.  **Direct Admission**: Available for merit-based candidates under the institutional quota (contact the college for their separate application).

### **Expected JEE Main Cutoffs 2026 (UPTAC Ranks)**
JSS usually has the tightest cutoffs among all private AKTU-affiliated colleges.

| Branch | General (All India Rank) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Computer Science (CSE)** | 70,000 - 90,000 |
| **Information Tech (IT)** | 90,000 - 1,20,000 |
| **Electronics (ECE)** | 1,80,000 - 2,50,000 |
| **Electrical Engineering** | 3,50,000 - 5,00,000 |

### **JSS Noida B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
JSS offers a relatively balanced fee structure, especially given its location and placement outcomes.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹1.38 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Cost**: **₹4.9 Lakhs - ₹5.5 Lakhs**.
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹96,000 to ₹1.10 Lakhs per year (depending on room type).

### **Placement Review: Consistent & Strong**
JSS stands out in Noida for its high placement percentages and strong recruiter relationships.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹57.00 LPA.
*   **Highest Domestic Package**: ₹35.0 LPA to ₹47.0 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹6.0 LPA to ₹8.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, TCS, Infosys, IBM, Adobe, and Mercedes-Benz.
*   **Placement Rate**: Above 87% students placed in tech branches.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Best AKTU placement record, highly respected brand name, centralized location in Noida (Sector 62), and strong alumni presence.
**Cons**: Small campus compared to government universities, strict attendance and academic policies of AKTU, and high demand leading to high cutoffs.
