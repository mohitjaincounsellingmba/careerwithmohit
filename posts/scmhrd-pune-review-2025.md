SCMHRD (Symbiosis Centre for Management and Human Resource Development) is a premier institute renowned specifically for its Human Resources (HR) program, often ranked as one of the best in Asia. However, its Business Analytics and Infrastructure Management programs are also gaining significant traction.

### **Quick Highlights (2025 Batch):**
*   **Avg Placement**: ₹21.47 LPA
*   **Highest Package**: ₹35.90 LPA
*   **Placement Rate**: ~95%+
*   **Entrance Exam**: SNAP (97+ Percentile)

### **Why Choose SCMHRD?**
*   **HR Powerhouse**: If your goal is a career in HR, SCMHRD is a Tier 1 choice alongside XLRI and TISS.
*   **Niche Specializations**: Offers unique programs in Infrastructure Management and Business Analytics that are highly valued by consultants.
*   **Hinjewadi Advantage**: Located in the heart of Pune's IT hub (Hinjewadi), providing excellent industry connect.

### **Fee Structure:**
The total academic fee for the flagship MBA program is approximately **₹22.0 Lakhs**. Given the median package is close to ₹20 LPA, it remains one of the most prestigious high-ROI choices in the Symbiosis family.
