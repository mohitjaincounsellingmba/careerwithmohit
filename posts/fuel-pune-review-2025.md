FUEL Business School (Friends Union for Energising Lives) is a unique institution that evolved from a massive social skill-building organization. It is known for its focus on grooming students from diverse socioeconomic backgrounds for elite corporate roles.

### **Quick Highlights (2025 Batch):**
*   **Avg Placement**: ₹5.50 - ₹6.50 LPA
*   **Highest Package**: ₹12.00 LPA+
*   **Fees (Total)**: ₹5.5 - ₹8.0 Lakhs
*   **Entrance Exam**: CAT / MAT / CMAT / MAH CET / FUEL Entrance

### **Why FUEL Pune?**
*   **Skill-First Approach**: They focus on futuristic skills like Data Analytics, Python for Business, and Digital Marketing as core parts of the PGDM.
*   **Corporate Mentorship**: Students get mentored by senior leaders from companies like Capgemini, LinkedIn, and Oracle.
*   **Modern Infrastructure**: A new-age campus designed to promote collaborative learning and innovation.

### **Fee Structure:**
With a total two-year fee ranging from **₹5.5 Lakhs to ₹8.0 Lakhs**, FUEL is an excellent choice for students looking for high-quality instruction with a mission-driven approach.
