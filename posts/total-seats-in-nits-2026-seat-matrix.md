### **Special Seat Quotas in NITs**
Unlike IITs, NITs have a **Home State Quota** system:
*   **Home State Quota (50%)**: Seats reserved for candidates belonging to the state where the NIT is located.
*   **Other State Quota (50%)**: Seats open to candidates from all other states and Union Territories.

### **NIT Admission 2026: JoSAA and CSAB**
Admission to all NITs is conducted through **JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority)** for the first 6 rounds, followed by **CSAB (Central Seat Allocation Board)** for the vacant seats if any remain.
