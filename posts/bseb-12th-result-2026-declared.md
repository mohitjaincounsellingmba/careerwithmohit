### **How to Check Bihar Board 12th Result 2026?**
Follow these simple steps to access your Inter result:

1.  **Visit Official Portal**: Go to [results.biharboardonline.com](https://results.biharboardonline.com).
2.  **Locate Link**: Click on "Intermediate Annual Examination Result 2026".
3.  **Enter Logins**: Provide your **Roll Code** and **Roll Number**.
4.  **Solve Captcha**: Enter the arithmetic sum displayed on the screen.
5.  **Submit & View**: Click on the 'View' button to display your marksheet.
6.  **Download**: Save the PDF and keep a printed copy for college admissions.
