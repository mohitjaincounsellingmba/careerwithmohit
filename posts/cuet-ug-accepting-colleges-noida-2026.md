Noida, the "Electronic City" of Uttar Pradesh, is a part of the National Capital Region (NCR) and a global outsourcing hub. With some of the most specialized and high-tech private universities in India, it offers diverse opportunities for those appearing for the **Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG 2026**.

If you are a student targeting **Noida for the 2026-27 session**, here are the top institutions you should consider using your CUET scores.

---

### **1. Amity University, Noida**
One of the most expansive and high-tech private universities in India.
*   **Popular Degrees:** B.Tech, BBA, B.Com, BA, B.Sc.
*   **USP:** World-class infrastructure and high-tier placements.

### **2. Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT, Noida)**
While technical, JIIT offers several multidisciplinary tracks through various entrance routes including CUET scores for specific programs.
*   **Programs:** Integrated B.Tech, BBA, B.Sc.
*   **USP:** A top-tier institute known for its high academic standards in technology.

### **3. Noida International University (NIU)**
Located in Greater Noida East, this university offers multidisciplinary education.
*   **Programs:** BA (Hons), B.Com (Hons), B.Sc, B.Tech.
*   **USP:** Global exposure and a large international student community.

### **4. ICFAI University Noida (Specific Programs)**
Known for its management education legacy.
*   **Programs:** BBA, B.Com (Hons).
*   **USP:** Research-led teaching and corporate interface.
