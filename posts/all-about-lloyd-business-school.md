### **College Review: Lloyd Business School**
*   **Industrial Tie-ups**: Famous for its specialization collaborations with giants like IBM for Supply Chain and Business Analytics.
*   **Modern Prep**: Focused on providing "Next-Gen" skills including data-driven decision making.
*   **Campus Culture**: Offers a highly professional environment that mirrors a modern corporate office.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: **₹24.8 - 25 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: **₹7.22 LPA** (For PGDM students).
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently exceeds **95%** for eligible students.
*   *Top Recruiters**: Accenture, Big Basket, ITC Limited, Genpact, and HCL.
*   **Corporate Scale**: Over 240 companies participate in the recruitment cycle.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Premium Blocks**: Well-maintained classrooms and labs with high-speed internet.
*   **Specialized Labs**: Includes a dedicated **IBM Lab** for data analytics and supply chain research.
*   **Library**: A comprehensive resource center for all management subjects.
*   **Hostel Excellence**: residential facilities described by students as feeling like "5-star hotels" with superior food and services.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Highly Qualified**: Majority of faculty members hold PhDs and have relevant industry experience.
*   **Innovative Methods**: Use of digital simulations and industry-led workshops to explain concepts.
*   **Student Support**: known for being accessible and dedicated to building student portfolios.

**Final Verdict**: Best for students who want a tech-heavy MBA with strong industrial certifications and premium residential life in Greater Noida.
