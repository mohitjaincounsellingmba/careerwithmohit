Kolkata, the "Cultural Capital" of India, has long been a center for academic excellence. With the **CUET PG 2026** score becoming a primary admission criterion for many postgraduate programs, Kolkata’s top universities have integrated the CUET score into their admission process.

If you are looking for **MBA, MCA, or MSc programs in Kolkata**, here is your detailed guide to institutions accepting CUET PG scores.

---

### **1. Visva-Bharati University (Santiniketan, Near Kolkata)**
Technically located in Santiniketan, Visva-Bharati is the top choice for any CUET PG aspirant in West Bengal.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MSc, MA (Economics).
*   **Admission Process:** Entirely based on CUET PG scores followed by their own counseling portal.
*   **Why Choose Visva-Bharati?** Low fees, central university tag, and exceptionally high-quality teaching standards.

### **2. Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Educational and Research Institute (RKMVERI) (Kolkata/Belur)**
Known for its academic rigor and holistic postgraduate programs.
*   **Top Programs:** MSc in Environment Management, MSc in Rural Development.
*   **Admission Process:** They consider CUET PG scores as part of their eligibility criteria for several of their PG programs.

### **3. Amity University (Kolkata)**
Amity Kolkata is known for its modern infrastructure and diverse PG courses in management and information technology.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA, MSc.
*   **Admission Process:** They consider various national scores including CUET PG for several of their postgraduate departments.

### **4. Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) (Associated Colleges)**
While MAKAUT has its own entrance for many courses, some affiliated private colleges in Kolkata use CUET PG scores for admissions to management and engineering postgraduate programs.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA.

---

### **Why Kolkata for PG?**
*   **Academic Heritage:** Kolkata has a deep-rooted academic heritage, offering a rich learning environment for postgraduate research and management studies.
*   **Affordability:** Compared to Mumbai or Bangalore, living costs in Kolkata are significantly lower, making it a budget-friendly option for students from across India.
*   **Rich Culture & Lifestyle:** Studying in Kolkata provides a uniquely intellectual atmosphere with access to some of the country’s oldest libraries and research centers.
