Foundation for Organisational Research and Education (FORE) School of Management, located in the prominent Institutional Area of South Delhi, is a top-ranked B-school known for its academic excellence and robust corporate linkages. For the 2026-28 batch, **Direct Admission in FORE School of Management** is a popular topic for students eyeing high-ROI careers in the capital.

## Why FORE School of Management is a Top Delhi B-School?

- **Prime Location**: Located in South Delhi, providing unmatched access to corporate events and networking.
- **Academic Rigor**: Known for its data-driven decision-making curriculum and global immersion programs.
- **Placements**: The average package for the latest batch arrived at **₹15.4 LPA**, with top global brands as frequent recruiters.

## Is there a Management Quota in FORE School of Management?

FORE School of Management follows a strictly merit-linked admission policy, but here is how the alternative seat categories function for 2026:

### 1. Institutional Seats (Merit-Category)
FORE reserves a specific number of seats as Institutional Category seats. 
- **The Process**: These are filled based on a combination of your entrance score and academic profile.
- **Cutoff Reality**: While the General CAT/XAT cutoff is usually 85+ percentile, institutional applicants with an **80 percentile** and a strong academic track record can secure admission during the interview rounds.

### 2. NRI / Foreign National Quota
Candidates from the NRI category follow a profile-based selection. This is a direct pathway where a valid **GMAT score** is often the primary evaluation tool instead of CAT.

### 3. Diversity Group Selection
FORE actively encourages students from across India and from different academic streams (Arts, Commerce, Science). This diversity focus often leads to direct shortlists for candidates who bring unique perspectives to the classroom.

## Fee Structure for 2026
The total academic fee for the two-year program at FORE School of Management is approximately **₹18 Lakhs to ₹20 Lakhs**. This includes the global immersion costs and top-tier facility access.

## Selection Process (2026 Batch)
Even for institutional seats, the standards are high:
1. **Entrance Exam**: CAT or XAT is mandatory for Indian applicants.
2. **Business Case Discussion**: FORE uses a unique selection tool focusing on your analytical ability.
3. **Personal Interview**: Focuses on your leadership qualities and career goals.

Direct admission is available through the **Institutional Merit category** and the **NRI quota**, which focus on a holistic profile evaluation alongside entrance scores.

No, FORE School of Management accepts only **CAT, XAT, and GMAT** scores for its PGDM admissions.

### 3. What is the average salary at FORE Delhi?
The average package for the recent batch is approximately **₹15.4 LPA**.

### 4. Is freshers friendly?
Yes, FORE has a balanced mix of freshers and students with professional work experience, offering a diverse learning environment.
