The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has officially announced the **Bihar Board Class 10 Result 2026 today, March 29, 2026, at 1:15 PM**. Over 16 lakh students who appeared for the Matric examinations held in February can now access their marks and download the provisional marksheet online.

The results were declared in a press conference by the BSEB Chairman, **Anand Kishor**, at the board's headquarters in Patna.

### **BSEB 10th Result 2026: Official Websites to Check**
To avoid technical glitches due to high traffic, the board has hosted the results on multiple official portals. Use the following verified links to check your score:

1.  **[results.biharboardonline.com](https://results.biharboardonline.com)**
2.  **[matricbiharboard.com](https://matricbiharboard.com)**

### **How to Check Bihar Board 10th Result 2026 Online?**
Students can follow these simple steps to access their results:

1.  **Visit Official Portal**: Go to [results.biharboardonline.com](https://results.biharboardonline.com).
2.  **Locate Link**: Click on the link "Annual Secondary Examination Result 2026".
3.  **Enter Credentials**: You will need to enter your **Roll Code** and **Roll Number** (refer to your admit card).
4.  **Solve Captcha**: Enter the arithmetic sum as shown on the screen.
5.  **Submit**: Click on 'View' or 'Search' to see your result.
6.  **Download**: The scorecard will appear. Download the PDF and take a printout for future reference.

Ensure that the following details on your provisional result are correct. If you find any discrepancies, contact your school or the BSEB helpdesk immediately:

*   Roll Code and Roll Number
*   Registration Number
