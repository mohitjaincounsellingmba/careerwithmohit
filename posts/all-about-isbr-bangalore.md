Located right in the heart of Electronic City—India's biggest IT hub—the International School of Business & Research (ISBR) Bangalore leverages its strategic location to offer excellent corporate exposure. It is a highly sought-after B-School for students aiming to build careers in IT, Analytics, and Business Development.

Here is the ultimate guide to ISBR Bangalore for 2026 admissions:

## Key Highlights

- **Establishment:** 1990
- **Programs Offered:** 
  - PGDM (AICTE Approved, NBA Accredited)
  - MBA (Affiliated to Bangalore University)
  - Global PGDM (with international study trip)
- **Legacy & USP:** Over 3 decades of educational legacy. The biggest USP is its location in Electronic City Phase 1, placing students right next to the headquarters of Infosys, Wipro, and TCS.

## Admission Details

- **Entrance Exams Accepted:** CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, NMAT, KMAT, PGCET
- **Expected Cut-off (Percentile):**
  - MAT/CMAT: 70 - 75+
  - CAT/XAT: 60+
  - NMAT: 160+ Score
- **Total Fees:** ₹8.50 Lakhs to ₹12.00 Lakhs (varies between standard PGDM and Global PGDM modules)

## Specializations Offered

- Business Analytics (Highly in demand)
- Marketing
- Finance
- Human Resource Management
- Information Technology / Systems
- Supply Chain & Logistics

## Placement Statistics (2025-2026)

Thanks to its proximity to tech giants and startups, ISBR brings excellent profiles to the campus.

- **Highest Placement Package:** ₹14.00 LPA
- **Average Placement Package:** ₹7.50 LPA
- **Lowest Placement Package:** ₹5.00 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** Infosys, Wipro, Dell, Biocon, Hexaware, PwC, Flipkart, Mindtree, and Decathlon.

## Student Reviews & Verdict

**What Students Say:**
- **Pros:** Unbeatable location giving easy access to live projects and internships. Strong international exchange programs and guest lectures by CEOs from neighboring tech parks.
- **Cons:** The ROI is slightly lower for the premium Global PGDM module. Competition for top Finance roles can be tough, as the campus is heavily skewed towards Tech/IT and Marketing recruiters.

**Our Verdict:** ISBR Bangalore is a safe and reliable bet if you want to start your career in the IT capital of India. Those interested in Business Analytics and Digital Marketing will benefit immensely from the college ecosystem. It is an ideal college for steady, secure career growth.
