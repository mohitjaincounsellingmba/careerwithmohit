**GD Goenka University (GDGU)**, located in the corporate hub of Gurugram (Sohna Road), is a premier multidisciplinary private university. Accredited with **NAAC Grade 'A+'**, it is recognized for its industry-integrated engineering programs and specialized tracks in AI, Machine Learning, and Robotics.

### **GD Goenka Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Entrance Route**: Based on scores in **JEE Main 2026**, **CUET UG**, or the university's own **GATA** (Goenka Aptitude Test for Admission).
2.  **Merit Route**: 10+2 marks (minimum 50-60% aggregate in PCM).
3.  **Specializations**: B.Tech Hons in CSE is the highlight, with industry tie-ups for niche tracks.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
GD Goenka is accessible to merit-based candidates with decent JEE scores or high 12th percentages.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | Core Engineering |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 75 - 85+ | 65 - 75 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM)** | 80% - 90% | 70% - 80% |
| **CUET Score** | 80th Percentile+ | 75th Percentile+ |

### **GD Goenka University B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
GDGU offers premium facilities at an annual fee comparable to other top-tier Gurugram private universities.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee (CSE)**: approx. ₹2.75 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Cost**: **₹11.1 Lakhs - ₹13.3 Lakhs** (for Aerospace/CSE).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.5 Lakhs to ₹2.5 Lakhs per year (AC mandatory for most rooms).

### **Placement Review: A Reliable Career Launcher**
The proximity to Cyber City and Udyog Vihar gives GD Goenka students a significant edge during internships and final placements.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹41.20 LPA.
*   **Highest Domestic Package (Overall)**: ₹35.0 LPA.
*   **Average Package (Engineering)**: ₹8.50 LPA.
*   **Average Package (Overall)**: ₹5.50 LPA to ₹6.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Accenture, Genpact, Deloitte, Siemens, Berger, and Wipro.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: NAAC A+ accreditation, excellent location in Gurugram, high focus on management and soft-skill integration, and industry-ready engineering labs.
**Cons**: Hostel and mess charges are relatively high, campus environment is corporate-centric rather than traditional academic.
