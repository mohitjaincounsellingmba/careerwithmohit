The **CUET PG 2026 examination concluded on March 27, 2026**, for millions of postgraduate aspirants across India. Now that the exams are over, the next big milestone is the release of the **Provisional Answer Key and individual Response Sheets** by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

If you are waiting to calculate your raw marks and check your correct answers, here is the latest expected timeline and procedure.

### **📅 CUET PG 2026: Response Sheet Expected Date**
Based on NTA’s current pattern of releasing answer keys within 3–5 days of the last exam, here is the anticipated schedule:

| Event | Expected Date |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Exam Concluded** | March 27, 2026 |
| **Response Sheet & Provisional Key** | **March 31 – April 2, 2026 (Expected)** |
| **Answer Key Challenge Window** | April 2 – April 4, 2026 |
| **Final Answer Key Release** | 3rd Week of April 2026 |
| **CUET PG 2026 Result Declaration** | 1st Week of May 2026 |

*Note: The NTA usually releases the notification on their official website [exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/) late in the evening.*

### **🖥️ How to Download CUET PG 2026 Response Sheet & Key?**
Once the link is officially active, candidates can follow these steps:

1.  **Visit Official Website**: Go to [exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/).
2.  **Login Section**: Click on "Login" or "Candidate Activity" tab.
3.  **Enter Credentials**: You will need your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth** (or Password).
4.  **View Response Sheet**: Click on the 'View/Challenge Answer Key' or 'View Response Sheet' button.
5.  **Download PDF**: Your original responses (what you marked) and the NTA’s provisional answers will be displayed. Save this PDF immediately for future reference.

### **💰 How to Challenge the CUET PG 2026 Answer Key?**
If you find a discrepancy in the NTA’s provisional answer key, you have the right to challenge it:
*   **Challenge Fee**: ₹200 per question challenged (Non-refundable).
*   **Requirement**: You must upload supporting documents (textbook references or solved proofs) for your claim.
*   **Result**: If the challenge is accepted, NTA will update the answer in the **Final Answer Key**, and marks will be awarded to all candidates in that shift.

### **📊 Calculating Your Raw Score**
By tallying your response sheet with the provisional key, you can calculate your raw marks using this formula:
*   **Correct Answer**: +4 Marks
*   **Incorrect Answer**: -1 Mark
*   **Unattempted**: 0 Marks
