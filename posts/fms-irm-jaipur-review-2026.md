The **Institute of Rural Management (FMS IRM), Jaipur**, established in 1994, is a highly regarded business school offering rigorous management and rural management programs. It is known for its strong corporate connections and legacy of excellence in PGDM education.

If you are considering FMS IRM Jaipur for your management studies, here is a detailed review covering the fee structure, placement stats, and entrance exams.

---

### **Quick Highlights**

*   **Average Package**: ₹7.00 - ₹7.50 LPA
*   **Highest Package**: ₹20.00 - ₹22.00 LPA
*   **Total Tuition Fees**: ~₹9.50 Lakhs
*   **Flagship Programs**: PGDM (General) & PGDM (Rural Management)
*   **Approved By**: AICTE, Ministry of HRD

### **Placements at FMS IRM Jaipur**

FMS IRM boasts an impressive track record of providing 100% placement assistance. More than 250 reputed companies visit the campus for recruitment annually.

*   **Top Package**: Between ₹20 LPA to ₹22 LPA
*   **Average Package**: ₹7.5 LPA
*   **Top Recruiters**: Ernst & Young (EY), AMUL, MRF, Coca-Cola, Berger Paints, PwC, Ujjivan Finance, Reliance, and Flipkart.

The Rural Management (PGDM-RM) students also get lucrative offers from top microfinance firms, FMCG rural divisions, and developmental banks.

---

### **Admission Process & Cutoffs**

FMS IRM shortlists candidates on the basis of national-level management entrance exams.

*   **Accepted Exams**: [CAT](/blog/all-about-cat-exam), [MAT](/blog/all-about-mat-exam), XAT, [CMAT](/blog/all-about-cmat-exam), and ATMA.
*   **Expected Cutoffs**: 
    *   **CAT/XAT**: ~80 Percentile
    *   **MAT/ATMA**: ~75-80 Percentile

After shortlisting, candidates must clear the Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds. A candidate's past academic record and soft skills play a significant role in final selection.
