## What to Study for Phase 1

### General Awareness (80 Marks — Critical!)
- RBI policies, monetary tools (Repo Rate, CRR, SLR, MSF)
- SEBI regulations, IRDAI, NABARD, NHB
- Union Budget, Economic Survey highlights
- International economics (IMF, World Bank, WTO)
- Banking sector current affairs (last 12 months)
- Historical RBI governors and landmark policy decisions

### Reasoning (60 Marks)
- High-difficulty puzzles, seating arrangements (circular, linear)
- Syllogisms, Input-Output, Blood Relations
- Data Sufficiency and Logical Reasoning sets

### English (30 Marks)
- Reading Comprehension (editorial-level topics on finance and economy)
- Para Jumbles, Error Detection, Fill in the Blanks
- Vocabulary in context (banking and legal terms)

### Quantitative Aptitude (30 Marks)
- Data Interpretation (complex sets — bar + line combo graphs)
- Arithmetic (Percentage, SI/CI, P&L, partnerships)
