## 🏛️ Why UOK is the Best Pick for "Budget" Aspirants?
The University of Kota (UOK) is a state university that caters to students who want a quality degree without the burden of heavy education loans.
*   **Lowest Fees:** At roughly ₹25,000 for the entire MBA course, the ROI is mathematically unbeatable.
*   **Government Recognition:** A degree that is valid for all State and Central government jobs (UPSC, Banking, State PSC).
*   **Academic Rigor:** Strong emphasis on the traditional theoretical foundation of management.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Are placements good at University of Kota?
While UOK doesn't match the high packages of private B-schools, it consistently places students in regional banking, logistics, and retail roles with a median package of **₹2.1 - ₹3.0 LPA**.

### 2. Can I get direct admission in UOK MBA?
Admission is usually based on merit or CMAT scores. It's a great option for locals or those planning to prepare for competitive exams alongside their MBA.

### 3. Is the UOK MBA valid for PhD?
Yes, UOK is a UGC-recognized state university. Its MBA degree is fully valid for pursuing a PhD, NET/JRF, or any further academic degree globally.
