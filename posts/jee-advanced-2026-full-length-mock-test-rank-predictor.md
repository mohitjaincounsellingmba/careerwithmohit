## Subject-Wise Focus Areas
To score high in this mock test and the actual exam, focus on these critical chapters:

### 1. Physics: The Concept King
Focus on **Mechanics**, **Electrodynamics**, and **Modern Physics**. Don't ignore experimental measurements; IITs love testing your error analysis skills.

### 2. Chemistry: The Score Booster
**Physical Chemistry** (Thermodynamics, Kinetics) and **Organic Mechanisms** (GOC, Carbonyls) are the high-weightage zones. For a quick revision, check our **[60 Most Important Questions for JEE Advanced](/blog/jee-advanced-important-questions-2026)**.

### 3. Mathematics: The Rank Decider
**Calculus** and **Algebra** (Complex Numbers, Matrices) are always tough. Practice analytical geometry (3D) as it often carries straightforward but multi-step questions.

## Pro-Tips for Your Mock Attempt
* **Skip if Unsure:** Negative marking in JEE Advanced can be brutal (-1 or -2). If you aren't 100% sure about a multi-correct option, don't guess.
* **Partial Marking Strategy:** In MSQs (Multiple Select Questions), if you know two options are correct but are unsure about the third, mark only the two. You’ll get partial marks!
* **Start with Your Strength:** Whether it's Chemistry or Math, starting with your strongest subject builds momentum.
