### **🔍 How to Check Your CUET PG 2026 Response Sheet?**
Follow these simple steps to download your PDF:

1.  **Visit Official NTA Site**: Go to [exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-PG/).
2.  **Click on Login**: Select the 'CUET PG 2026 Login' or 'Candidate Activity' tab.
3.  **Enter Credentials**: Login using your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth/Password**.
4.  **View Response Sheet**: Click on the button labeled **"View/Challenge Answer Key"**.
5.  **Download PDF**: Your response sheet (with question IDs and your marked options) will appear. Download and save it immediately.

---

### **🚩 How to Challenge the CUET PG 2026 Answer Key?**
If you believe a particular answer marked by NTA is incorrect, you can challenge it:
*   **Challenge Fee**: ₹200 per question challenged (Non-refundable).
*   **Evidence**: You must upload a scanned copy of supporting documents (Textbooks, research papers, etc.).
*   **Result of Challenge**: If the expert committee finds your challenge valid, the final answer key will be updated, and marks will be awarded to all affected candidates.

### **📊 Calculate Your Raw Marks**
Use the following marking scheme to calculate your score:
*   **Correct Option**: +4 Marks
*   **Incorrect Option**: -1 Mark
*   **Unattempted**: 0 Marks

**Formula**: `(No. of Correct × 4) - (No. of Incorrect × 1)`
