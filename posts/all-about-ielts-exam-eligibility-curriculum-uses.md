## 2. Academic vs. General Training: Which one for you?

*   **IELTS Academic:** Mandatory for students applying for undergraduate or postgraduate programs abroad (UK, USA, Canada, Australia).
*   **IELTS General Training:** Required for immigration (PR) to Canada (Express Entry), Australia, and New Zealand, or for work-related training.

---

## 3. What is IELTS One Skill Retake (OSR)?
Introduced recently, the OSR is a game-changer for 2026 aspirants. If you achieve your target bands in three sections but fall short in one (e.g., Writing), you can retake **only that specific section** within 60 days of your original test date.

*   **Eligibility:** Only available for Computer-Delivered IELTS.
*   **Benefit:** Saves time and the stress of re-sitting the entire 3-hour exam.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. What is the minimum IELTS score for Canada Study Visa in 2026?
Most Canadian universities (under the SDS category) require an overall band of **6.5 with no less than 6.0 in any individual module**. For top-tier universities, aim for a 7.0+ overall.

### 2. Is passport mandatory for IELTS registration?
Yes, a valid, unexpired **Passport** is the only accepted identification for IELTS registration and entry to the test center in India.

### 3. How many times can I take the IELTS?
There is no limit. You can take the IELTS as many times as you want until you achieve your desired band score. With the new OSR, you can even fix a single-module score without a full retake.
