**Shoolini University of Biotechnology and Management Sciences**, located in the scenic hills of Solan (Himachal Pradesh), is one of India's most research-driven private universities. Established in 2009, it is accredited by **NAAC Grade 'A+'** and is globally recognized for its focus on innovation, research outputs, and high-tech engineering specializations.

### **Shoolini Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Entrance Route**: Based on scores in **JEE Main 2026**, **CUET UG**, or the university's official entrance exam **SU-SAT**.
2.  **Merit Route**: Based on 10+2 marks (minimum 55% to 60% aggregate in PCM).
3.  **Specializations**: Flagship B.Tech in CSE, with tracks in Cloud Computing, AI, and Big Data.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
Shoolini is accessible to students with high Class 12 percentages or decent national exam scores.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | Other Core Engineering |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 65 - 75+ | 55 - 65 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM)** | 75% - 85%+ | 60% - 70% |
| **SU-SAT Score** | 80th Percentile+ | 70th Percentile+ |

### **Shoolini University B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
Shoolini offers specialized education with high-value research labs and industry certifications.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee (CSE)**: approx. ₹2.53 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹10.1 Lakhs - ₹11.5 Lakhs** (for the entire duration).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.4 Lakhs to ₹1.6 Lakhs per year (depending on room configuration).
*   **Scholarships**: Generous tuition fee waivers up to 100% for high achievers in JEE and Board results.

### **Placement Review: Industry-Ready Success**
Shoolini University focuses heavily on internships and industrial exposure, resulting in solid placement numbers.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹45.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹8.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (UG Batch Overall)**: ₹4.50 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Cipla, IBM, TCS, Accenture, Amazon, Tata, and Cognizant.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 90% for engineering and biotechnology graduates.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Global world ranking focus (QS and NIRF Top 100), high research investment, spectacular Hill campus, and industry-oriented certifications included in the curriculum.
**Cons**: Mandatory attendance policies, relatively high hostel and mess charges.
