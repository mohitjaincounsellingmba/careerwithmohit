The National Testing Agency (NTA) is preparing to release the **NEET UG 2026 Admit Card** for the upcoming medical entrance examination. Aspirants aiming for MBBS, BDS, and other medical courses can soon download their hall tickets from the official NTA portal.

The NEET UG 2026 exam is a crucial step for medical aspirants in India, and carrying a valid admit card to the examination center is mandatory. Here is everything you need to know about the admit card release date, the download process, and important exam day guidelines.

### **📅 NEET UG 2026: Important Admit Card Dates**

The NTA generally releases the admit card about a week before the examination. Here are the expected dates for NEET UG 2026:

| Event | Date |
| :--- | :--- |
| **NEET 2026 Exam Date** | May 3, 2026 |
| **Admit Card Release Date** | April 27, 2026 (10:00 AM) |
| **Official Website** | [neet.nta.nic.in](https://neet.nta.nic.in) |

### **🖥️ How to Download the NEET 2026 Admit Card?**

Once the NTA activates the link, candidates can follow these simple steps to securely download their NEET hall tickets:

1. **Visit Official Website**: Navigate to the official NTA NEET portal at [neet.nta.nic.in](https://neet.nta.nic.in).
2. **Find the Link**: On the homepage, look under the "Candidate Activity" or "Latest News" section for the link titled **"NEET UG 2026 Admit Card"**.
3. **Login**: Click on the link. You will be redirected to a login page.
4. **Enter Credentials**: Carefully input your **Application Number**, **Date of Birth** (or Password), and the **Security Pin** (Captcha) displayed on the screen.
5. **Submit & Download**: Click on the "Submit" button. Your admit card will be displayed on the screen.
6. **Print the Hall Ticket**: Review all the details printed on the admit card. Download the PDF and take a clear, color printout to carry on exam day.

### **⚠️ Crucial Details to Check on Your Admit Card**

After downloading, meticulously check the following details. If you spot any discrepancies, contact the NTA immediately:
- Candidate's Name and Roll Number
- Father's/Mother's Name
- Photograph and Signature (Must be clear and match the application)
- **Exam Center Name and Address**
- **Reporting Time** and Gate Closing Time
- Exam Date and Time (May 3, 2026)

### **🎒 What to Carry to the Exam Center?**

To ensure a smooth entry into the examination hall, remember to carry these mandatory items:
*   A printed copy of the **NEET UG 2026 Admit Card** (preferably in color) with a passport-size photograph affixed in the designated space.
*   One additional **Passport-size Photograph** (same as uploaded in the application form) to be pasted on the attendance sheet.
*   A valid, original **Government Photo ID proof** (e.g., Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License).
*   PwD certificate (if applicable).

**Prohibited Items**: Do not carry electronic devices, mobile phones, calculators, digital watches, or any metallic items/jewelry into the exam hall, as strict frisking measures will be in place.
