**National Institute of Technology (NIT) Delhi** is one of the fastest-growing NITs in the country. Initially operating from a temporary campus, it has now moved into its permanent, state-of-the-art facility in Narela, Delhi. Its proximity to the tech hub of the capital and its consistent climb in NIRF rankings (Ranked #65 in 2025) make it an elite choice for engineering aspirants.

### **NIT Delhi Admission Process 2026**
1.  **Entrance Exam**: You must secure a high rank in **JEE Main 2026**.
2.  **Counselling**: Register for **JoSAA 2026** (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) for seat allotment.
3.  **HS vs OS**: 50% of the seats are reserved for students from the **Delhi (Home State)** region.
4.  **CSAB Special Rounds**: Vacant seats are filled through the Central Seat Allocation Board (CSAB) in late July/August.

### **NIT Delhi B.Tech Fees 2026**
NIT Delhi follows the standard fee structure for all National Institutes of Technology.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹1.25 Lakhs (General/OBC).
*   **Total 4-Year Cost**: **₹6.4 Lakhs - ₹7.0 Lakhs** (including hostel).
*   **Fee Waiver**: 100% Tuition Fee waiver for SC/ST/PwD and families with income < ₹1 Lakh. 2/3rd waiver for income between ₹1L to ₹5L.

### **Placement Review: A Tech Destination**
Despite its smaller campus, NIT Delhi outcomes are on par with some of the older NITs.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹62.00 LPA (reached in 2024, competitive trends for 2026).
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹17.72 LPA.
*   **Average Package (CSE)**: ₹18.87 LPA+.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Google, Flipkart, Morgan Stanley, D.E. Shaw, Oracle, Adobe, and Salesforce.
*   **Placement Rate**: 100% for Computer Science (CSE) students.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Excellent ROI, world-class faculty, prime location in Delhi, perfect for product-based company roles.
**Cons**: Permanent campus is still undergoing construction of some blocks, smaller student intake compared to older NITs.
