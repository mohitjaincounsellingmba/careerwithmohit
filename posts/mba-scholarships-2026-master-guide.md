Pursuing an MBA from a premier institute is a significant investment, often costing between ₹15L to ₹30L. However, several corporate giants and trusts offer generous scholarships to support meritorious and financially deserving students. 

Here is your master list of **MBA Scholarships for the 2026-2027 academic session**.

---

### **1. IDFC First Bank MBA Scholarship**
This is one of the most popular need-based scholarships for students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds.

*   **Scholarship Amount**: ₹2 Lakhs (₹1 Lakh per year).
*   **Eligibility**:
    *   Must be an Indian national.
    *   Enrolled in the 1st year of a full-time MBA/PGDM program at a selected institution.
    *   Gross annual family income must be **less than ₹6 Lakhs**.
    *   Age: 35 years or younger.
*   **Application Process**:
    1.  Apply online via the [IDFC First Bank Website](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/about-us/csr/mba-scholarship) or Buddy4Study.
    2.  Upload income proof, admission letter, and previous academic mark sheets.
    3.  Shortlisted candidates undergo a verification process.
*   **Website**: [idfcfirstbank.com](https://www.idfcfirstbank.com/about-us/csr/mba-scholarship)

---

### **2. Aditya Birla Scholarship**
A highly prestigious merit-based award for students at top-tier B-schools.

*   **Scholarship Amount**: ₹3 Lakhs per annum.
*   **Eligibility**:
    *   Students of **IIM Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Kolkata, Lucknow, Indore, Kozhikode, and XLRI Jamshedpur**.
    *   Only the **top 20 students** from each of these institutes (based on entrance ranks) are invited to apply.
    *   Selection is based on academic excellence, leadership traits, and essays.
*   **Application Process**:
    1.  Eligible students are notified by the Dean of their respective institute.
    2.  Download the form from the [Official Portal](http://www.adityabirlascholars.net/).
    3.  Submit the form with supporting docs; finalist interviews are held in Mumbai/Online.
*   **Website**: [adityabirlascholars.net](http://www.adityabirlascholars.net/)

---

### **3. OPJEMS (O.P. Jindal Engineering & Management Scholarships)**
Promotes academic excellence and leadership among the youth.

*   **Scholarship Amount**: ₹1,50,000 for Management students.
*   **Eligibility**:
    *   First-year students: Selection based on entrance exam ranks at participating institutes (IIMs, FMS, XLRI, SPJIMR, etc.).
    *   Second-year students: Based on CGPA from the 1st year.
    *   Participating institutes include top 16-20 B-schools in India.
*   **Application Process**: 
    1.  Candidates are nominated by their respective colleges.
    2.  Nominated students appear for an online test and a personal interview.
*   **Website**: [opjems.com](https://www.opjems.com/)

---

### **4. Reliance Foundation Postgraduate Scholarship**
Focuses on nurturing talent in future-ready fields, including management specializations in tech and AI.

*   **Scholarship Amount**: Up to ₹6 Lakhs for the duration of the program.
*   **Eligibility**:
    *   Indian citizens enrolled in 1st year full-time PG programs.
    *   Specializations in **Business Analytics/AI/IT Management** are preferred.
    *   Undergraduate CGPA of 7.5 or above.
    *   Must clear the Reliance Foundation Aptitude Test.
*   **Application Process**:
    1.  Apply online at the [Reliance Foundation Portal](https://www.reliancefoundation.org/scholarships).
    2.  Appear for the mandatory online aptitude test.
    3.  Final selection based on test scores and interviews.
*   **Website**: [reliancefoundation.org](https://www.reliancefoundation.org/scholarships)

---

### **5. J.N. Tata Endowment (Loan Scholarships)**
For those looking to pursue their **MBA overseas** (International B-schools).

*   **Scholarship Amount**: Loan amount up to ₹10 Lakhs (interest-free if repaid on time).
*   **Eligibility**:
    *   Indian graduates (min. 60% marks) looking to study abroad.
    *   Age: Under 45 years.
    *   Admission to the foreign university must be secured before final selection.
*   **Application Process**:
    1.  Online application opens in January and closes in March (for September intake).
    2.  Appear for an online Thinking Skills Test.
    3.  Final Interview with an eminent panel of experts.
*   **Website**: [jntataendowment.org](https://www.jntataendowment.org/)

---

### **Quick Checklist for 2026 Applicants:**
*   **Update Documents**: Keep your Income Certificate, ITRs, and Graduation Degree ready.
*   **Track Deadlines**: Most scholarships open between **January and June**.
*   **Focus on Essays**: Scholarships like Aditya Birla and Reliance value your vision and leadership potential as much as your marks.
