## Top Central Universities Accepting CUET PG 2026

Central Universities offer incredibly high ROI due to their nominal fee structures and excellent academic reputation. Here are the elite Central Universities participating in CUET PG:

### 1. Delhi University (DU)
DU transitioned all its postgraduate admissions (including M.A, M.Com, M.Sc, and LLM) to CUET PG. Getting a PG degree from DU opens doors to academics, civil services, and top corporate roles.

### 2. Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi
BHU is highly sought after for its **MBA and MCA** programs through CUET PG. The FMS-BHU MBA program has excellent placements with extremely low tuition fees.

### 3. Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi
JNU accepts CUET PG for its MCA program, MA in International Relations, Economics, and specialized Language programs. It remains one of the top-ranked universities in the NIRF rankings.

### 4. University of Hyderabad (UoH)
Known for excellence in Sciences and Management, UoH is a premier destination for M.Sc, MCA, and MBA candidates applying through CUET PG.

### 5. Pondicherry University
A popular destination for MBA, M.Sc, and MCA programs, offering a great campus life and solid placement assistance.

## The TISS Factor in CUET PG (Tata Institute of Social Sciences)

One of the biggest game-changers for CUET PG is the inclusion of **TISS (Tata Institute of Social Sciences)**.
TISS discontinued its own exam (TISSNET) and now uses **CUET PG (Paper Code: COQP12 - General MBA)** for admission into its flagship Master of Arts in Human Resources Management and Labour Relations (MA HRM & LR) program in Mumbai, which rivals top IIMs in terms of placements.

*If you want to enter the HR domain, TISS via CUET PG is the holy grail.*

## Admission Process for CUET PG Colleges

1. **Check Subject Codes:** MBA uses **COQP12**, MCA uses **SCQP09**. Ensure you appear for the correct paper.
2. **Score Declaration:** Download your CUET PG Scorecard from the NTA website.
3. **University-Specific Application:** You must visit the website of BHU, DU, TISS, etc., individually and fill out their PG admission form using your CUET PG application number and score.
4. **Counseling/Interview:** Some institutes (like TISS) will conduct a secondary round consisting of a Personal Interview (PI) before final selection.
