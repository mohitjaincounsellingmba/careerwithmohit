The Institute of Business Management and Research (IBMR) Gurgaon positions itself as a stepping stone into the corporate world of Delhi NCR. Known for its affordable fee structure and practical approach, it attracts students looking for budget-friendly MBA options in a prime location.

Here is the complete breakdown of IBMR Gurgaon for 2026 admissions:

## Key Highlights

- **Establishment:** 1999
- **Programs Offered:** 
  - MBA (Affiliated to MDU Rohtak)
  - PGPM (Industry integrated program)
- **Legacy & USP:** Over two decades of presence in the educational sector. Its primary USP is providing a highly cost-effective management degree right in the heart of Gurgaon's corporate sector.

## Admission Details

- **Entrance Exams Accepted:** CAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA, CMAT
- **Expected Cut-off (Percentile):**
  - MAT/ATMA/CMAT: 50 - 60+ (IBMR relies heavily on the subsequent Group Discussion and Personal Interview rounds)
- **Total Fees:** ₹4.50 Lakhs – ₹6.50 Lakhs (Approx. depending on the exact MBA/PGPM combination)

## Specializations Offered

- Marketing
- Finance
- Human Resource Management
- Information Technology
- International Business

## Placement Statistics (2025-2026)

IBMR leverages its proximity to Udyog Vihar and Cyber City to place its students in mid-level corporate roles.

- **Highest Placement Package:** ₹12.50 LPA
- **Average Placement Package:** ₹5.50 LPA - ₹6.00 LPA
- **Lowest Placement Package:** ₹4.00 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** Justdial, ICICI Securities, Axis Bank, Genpact, Berger Paints, and various SMEs in the Delhi NCR region.

## Student Reviews & Verdict

**What Students Say:**
- **Pros:** Highly affordable fees making the ROI quite reasonable. The faculty is supportive, and the location in Gurgaon provides ample opportunities for off-campus internships and job hunting.
- **Cons:** The campus is small and operates more like a standalone building. Placements are largely concentrated in sales, business development, and core operational roles rather than high-end consulting or analytics.

**Our Verdict:** IBMR Gurgaon is a solid choice if you are strictly bounded by a budget of under ₹7 Lakhs and want an MBA degree primarily to kickstart a career in Sales, Marketing, or HR in the Delhi NCR region.
