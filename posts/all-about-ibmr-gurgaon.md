### **College Review: IBMR Gurgaon**
*   **Strategic Location**: Situated in Gurgaon, providing great opportunities for industrial visits and live corporate projects.
*   **Diverse Courses**: Offers MBA and PGDM programs with various value-added certifications.
*   **Practical Emphasis**: Focuses on "Out of Class" learning through clubs and industrial interactions.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: **₹21 LPA** (NCML) | **₹20.50 LPA** (Standard Chartered).
*   **Average Package**: **₹5.8 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Mashreq Bank, Standard Chartered, and various regional leaders.
*   **Diversity**: Companies from various sectors like BFSI and Logistics recruit from the campus.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Green Campus**: A dedicated 3-acre green campus that creates a calm learning environment.
*   **Standard Facilities**: Includes basic labs, a library, smart classrooms, and a functional cafeteria.
*   **Connectivity**: High-speed internet across campus for research and online learning.
*   **Campus Vibes**: While compact, it offers all necessary amenities for a modern B-school experience.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Approachable Staff**: Faculty members are known to be very supportive and helpful to students.
*   **Activity-Based**: the curriculum focuses on practical experience through industrial visits and various student-run clubs.
*   **Management Support**: The administrative management is responsive to student feedback and placement needs.

**Final Verdict**: A good option for students looking for a reasonably priced MBA/PGDM in Gurgaon with decent placement support.
