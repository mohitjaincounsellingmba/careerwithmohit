## 🏦 Bihar Student Credit Card (2026-28) — Key Facts

- **Loan Amount**: Up to **₹4 Lakhs** (Covers tuition, hostel, books, and even a laptop).
- **Interest Rate**: **0% (Interest-Free)** for all students (Male, Female, Transgender).
- **Collateral & Guarantor**: **Not Required** (The Bihar Government acts as your guarantor).
- **Repayment Period**: 
    - Up to 7 years (84 installments) for loans up to ₹2 Lakhs.
    - Up to 10 years (120 installments) for loans above ₹2 Lakhs.
- **Repayment Starts**: 1 year after the course completion or 6 months after securing a job.

## 📋 How to Apply (Step-by-Step for 2026-28)

1.  **Online Registration**: Fill out the application on the [Official MNSSBY Portal](https://www.7nishchay-yuvaupmission.bihar.gov.in).
2.  **Documents Upload**: Upload digital copies of your Aadhaar, 10th/12th marksheets, and **College Admission Letter**.
3.  **DRCC Visit**: Carry original documents to your district DRCC office for physical verification.
4.  **Disbursement**: Funds are directly transferred to the college bank account semester-wise.

## 💡 Important "DRCC 2026" Tips

- **Laptop Allowance**: You can get an additional amount (up to **₹35k**) sanctioned for a laptop if requested in the bonafide.
- **Top-Up Loans**: If your MBA fee is ₹10 Lakhs, DRCC provides ₹4 Lakhs. You can apply for the remaining ₹6 Lakhs through other education loans.
- **Course Approval**: Ensure your course is **AICTE/UGC** approved.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is the Bihar Student Credit Card really interest-free in 2026?
Yes! As per the September 2025 update, the interest rate is **0% for all applicants** for the 2026-28 batch.

### 2. Can I apply for DRCC if I am studying outside Bihar?
Yes. The scheme is designed for residents of Bihar to pursue higher education anywhere in India, provided the college/course is recognized.

### 3. What if I don't get a job after my MBA?
You can submit a self-declaration of unemployment to the DRCC to extend your grace period.

### 4. How long does the verification process take?
Usually **45 to 60 days** from the date of your DRCC office visit.
