## Tier 1 — MNIT Jaipur (Government Excellence)

### 1. Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Jaipur
MNIT Jaipur is Rajasthan's premier government engineering institution and one of India's top NITs. Getting into MNIT CSE is a dream for thousands of Rajasthan JEE aspirants.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, Architecture
- **Admission:** JEE Main → JoSAA Counselling
- **JEE Main Cutoff (CSE, General 2025):** ~3,500 closing rank
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.7 Lakhs
- **Avg Package (CSE):** ₹10–14 LPA | Highest: ₹60+ LPA (Microsoft, Amazon)
- **Top Recruiters:** Amazon, TCS, Wipro, Infosys, L&T, PwC

## Tier 2 — Top Private Engineering Colleges in Jaipur

### 2. Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ)
Part of the Manipal Group with the same brand quality as MIT Manipal, MUJ is Jaipur's top private engineering college with excellent industry connections and a strong placement cell.

- **Courses:** CSE (AI/ML, Data Science, IoT), ECE, Mechanical, Civil, Biotechnology
- **Admission:** MU OET / JEE Main / Board Merit
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.3 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹6–10 LPA | Highest: ₹42 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Cognizant, Infosys, Wipro, KPMG

### 3. JECRC University, Jaipur
JECRC has emerged as Jaipur's most rapidly growing private engineering university with a strong industry-academia interface, particularly in CSE and ECE.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, Data Science & AI
- **Admission:** REAP / JEE Main / Direct Merit
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.2 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA | Top: ₹25 LPA
- **NAAC Grade:** A

### 4. Poornima College of Engineering, Jaipur
One of Rajasthan's premier autonomous engineering colleges with NAAC accreditation and consistent placements. Popular for CSE and Mechanical.

- **Admission:** REAP / Direct | **Fee:** ₹1.1 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

### 5. SKIT — Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Jaipur
A government-aided institution affiliated with RTU Kota, SKIT is highly regarded for discipline, results, and affordable fees.

- **Admission:** REAP | **Fee:** ₹1.0 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

### 6. JKLU — JK Lakshmipat University, Jaipur
Backed by JK Group, JKLU offers a liberal, research-focused engineering curriculum with small batches and personalised mentoring. Strong for students targeting R&D and higher education abroad.

- **Admission:** Direct / JEE Main / Board Merit | **Fee:** ₹2.5 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹6–10 LPA

### 7. VGU — Vivekananda Global University, Jaipur
A fast-growing university with NAAC B++ accreditation, affordable fees, and an industry-linked curriculum. Good option for budget-conscious students.

- **Admission:** REAP / Direct | **Fee:** ₹1.1 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

## REAP Counselling 2026 — Rajasthan B.Tech Admissions

**REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process)** is the state-level centralised counselling for B.Tech admission to private engineering colleges in Rajasthan.

- **Authority:** Board of Technical Education, Rajasthan (BTER)
- **Eligibility:** 10+2 with PCM, minimum 45% aggregate
- **Seats Covered:** Private engineering colleges across Rajasthan
- **NIT Jaipur (MNIT):** Via JoSAA only — not REAP

## Direct Admission in Jaipur B.Tech Colleges 2026

If your REAP rank isn't enough for your desired college, management quota is available at JECRC, Poornima, SKIT, VGU, and most private colleges:
- Minimum 60% in PCM in Class 12
- No mandatory entrance exam score for many colleges
- Seats available: typically 15–30% of total intake

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Jaipur 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Jaipur?**
MNIT Jaipur is the top government engineering college. Among private colleges, Manipal University Jaipur is the highest-ranked, followed by JECRC University.

**Q2. What JEE Main rank is needed for MNIT Jaipur CSE?**
For the general category, a JEE Main rank below 3,500 (CRL) is typically required for CSE at MNIT Jaipur.

**Q3. Can I get B.Tech admission in Jaipur without JEE?**
Yes. All private colleges in Jaipur accept students through REAP (based on 12th board marks) or via direct admission for management quota seats.

**Q4. What is the fee for B.Tech in Jaipur private colleges?**
Private engineering college fees in Jaipur range from ₹90,000 to ₹2.5 Lakhs per year depending on the institution.

**Q5. Is Manipal University Jaipur better than JECRC for B.Tech?**
Manipal University Jaipur has a stronger brand, better placement packages, and superior infrastructure. JECRC is a more affordable alternative with decent placements for budget-conscious students.
