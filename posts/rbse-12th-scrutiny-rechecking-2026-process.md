The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (RBSE) has declared the Class 12 results for Science, Commerce, and Arts. While thousands have cleared the exams with flying colors, some students may feel that their marks do not reflect their true performance. 

If you are dissatisfied with your scores, you can apply for **Scrutiny (Retotalling)** to ensure your answer sheets were evaluated correctly. Here is everything you need to know about the RBSE 12th Rechecking process 2026.

### **What is RBSE Scrutiny/Rechecking?**
It is important to note that "Scrutiny" primarily involves:
*   Checking for any **totalling errors** in marks.
*   Ensuring that **all questions** have been marked by the examiner.
*   Verifying if marks were correctly carried forward from the inner pages to the front cover.

---

### **Important Dates for RBSE 12th Scrutiny 2026**
The window for scrutiny applications opens immediately after the result declaration.

| Event | Tentative Date |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Result Declaration** | March 31, 2026 |
| **Start of Scrutiny Application** | April 1, 2026 |
| **Last Date (Without Late Fee)** | April 10, 2026 |
| **Last Date (With Late Fee)** | April 15, 2026 |

*Note: Always check [rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in](https://rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in) for official notifications.*

---

### **RBSE 12th Scrutiny Fees 2026**
To apply for rechecking, students must pay a prescribed fee per subject:

*   **Normal Fee**: ~INR 300 per subject (if applied within the first 10 days).
*   **Late Fee**: ~INR 600 per subject (if applied after the first deadline).
*   **Mode of Payment**: Online via Net Banking, Debit/Credit Card, or UPI.

---

### **How to Apply for RBSE 12th Rechecking Online?**
The application process is entirely digital. Follow these steps to submit your form:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal**: Go to [bseronline.com](https://bseronline.com) or the main RBSE website.
2.  **Find the Scrutiny Link**: Look for the link "Scrutiny 2026 Registration".
3.  **Registration**: New users must register using their **Roll Number**, **Class**, and **Date of Birth**.
4.  **Verification**: You may need to provide an OTP sent to your registered mobile number.
5.  **Subject Selection**: Select the subjects you want to send for scrutiny.
6.  **Upload Documents**: You might be asked to upload a scanned copy of your admit card or ID proof.
7.  **Payment**: Pay the required fee online.
8.  **Confirmation**: Print the confirmation page and keep the **Application ID** safe for tracking.

### **Points to Keep in Mind**
*   **No Re-evaluation**: RBSE typically offers scrutiny (retotalling), not a full re-evaluation (re-checking of every answer) unless specifically notified.
*   **Result Outcome**: Marks may increase, decrease, or remain the same after scrutiny. This will be considered your final score.
