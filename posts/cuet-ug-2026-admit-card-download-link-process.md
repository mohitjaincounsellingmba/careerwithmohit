The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially released the **CUET UG 2026 Admit Card** on **May 5, 2026**. This is a critical update for millions of aspirants appearing for the Common University Entrance Test starting from **May 11, 2026**.

If you haven't downloaded your hall ticket yet, do it immediately to check your specific exam slot, timing, and venue details.

## 1. CUET UG 2026 Exam Schedule & Important Dates
The NTA has finalized the schedule. The exams will be conducted between May 11 and May 31. 

**URGENT:** NTA has also opened a correction window for candidates who have been allotted far-away centers. The deadline to request a change is **May 7, 2026 (11:50 PM)**.

## 2. How to Download CUET UG 2026 Admit Card?
Follow these simple steps to download your hall ticket once the link is activated by the NTA:

1.  **Visit the Official Portal:** Go to [cuet.nta.nic.in](https://cuet.nta.nic.in).
2.  **Find the Link:** Click on the "CUET (UG) 2026 Admit Card Download" notification on the homepage.
3.  **Login Credentials:** Enter your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth/Password**.
4.  **Enter Security Pin:** Type the captcha shown on the screen.
5.  **Download and Print:** Your admit card will appear on the screen. Download the PDF and take 2-3 color printouts for the exam day.

---

## 3. Important Details to Verify in Your Admit Card
Once you download the file, ensure the following details are correct:
*   **Candidate Name and Photo:** Should match your registration.
*   **Subject Combination:** Verify the subjects and languages you opted for.
*   **Exam Center Address:** Check the specific school/college allotted in your city.
*   **Reporting Time:** Strictly follow the gate closing time mentioned.

**Note:** If there is any discrepancy in your admit card, contact the NTA Help Desk immediately at 011-40759000 or email at cuet-ug@nta.ac.in.

---

## 4. Mandatory Documents to Carry on Exam Day
Candidates will not be allowed to enter the exam hall without these:
1.  **Printed Admit Card** (with Self-Declaration form filled).
2.  **Original Photo ID Proof** (Aadhar Card/PAN Card/Voter ID/Passport).
3.  **Passport Size Photograph** (same as uploaded in the application).
4.  **Transparent Water Bottle** and a Blue/Black Ballpoint Pen.

After the exams, the focus will shift to university preferences and counseling. Colleges like Delhi University, BHU, and JNU will start their own CSAS portals for seat allocation.
