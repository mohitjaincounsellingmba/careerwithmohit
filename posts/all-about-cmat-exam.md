The Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is a national-level entrance examination facilitated by the Government of India. It is highly popular among students because it is generally considered easier than CAT and XAT, and its scores are universally accepted by all AICTE-approved management institutions across the country.

Here is the complete overview of the CMAT exam for 2026 admissions:

## Exam Overview & Conducting Body

- **Exam Name:** Common Management Admission Test (CMAT)
- **Conducting Body:** National Testing Agency (NTA)
- **Frequency:** Once a year (Typically held in April or May)
- **Mode of Exam:** Computer Based Test (CBT)

## Exam Pattern & Total Marks

CMAT is a pure test of accuracy rather than breakneck speed.

- **Total Duration:** 180 Minutes (3 Hours)
  - *Note: There are no sectional time limits, allowing you to move freely across sections.*
- **Total Questions:** 100 Questions
- **Total Marks:** 400 Marks
- **Question Types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
- **Marking Scheme:**
  - +4 marks for every correct answer
  - -1 mark for every incorrect answer

## Sectional Breakdown

CMAT divides its 100 questions equally across five strict sections (20 questions each):

1. **Quantitative Techniques and Data Interpretation:** 20 Questions (80 Marks)
2. **Logical Reasoning:** 20 Questions (80 Marks)
3. **Language Comprehension:** 20 Questions (80 Marks)
4. **General Awareness:** 20 Questions (80 Marks)
5. **Innovation & Entrepreneurship:** 20 Questions (80 Marks) - *This is a unique section testing business acumen and startup knowledge.*

## Expected Cut-offs (Percentile & Score)

Because the difficulty level is lower, top percentiles require very high raw scores (usually 300+ out of 400).

- **JBIMS (Jamnalal Bajaj), Mumbai & SIMSREE:** 99.9+ Percentile (~330+ Score)
- **Great Lakes Chennai, GIM Goa, K.J. Somaiya:** 98.0+ Percentile (~280+ Score)
- **BIMTECH, Welingkar, NIBM Pune, PUMBA:** 90.0+ to 95.0+ Percentile (~250+ Score)
- **Tier-2/Tier-3 AICTE Colleges:** 70.0+ to 85.0+ Percentile

## Top Colleges Accepting CMAT Scores

Nearly 1,000+ AICTE-approved B-Schools accept CMAT. The top tier includes:

- JBIMS (Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management Studies), Mumbai
- SIMSREE (Sydenham Institute), Mumbai
- Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM), Chennai
- GIM (Goa Institute of Management)
- K. J. Somaiya Institute of Management, Mumbai
- PUMBA (Pune University Department of Management Sciences)
- NIBM (National Institute of Bank Management), Pune
- Welingkar Institute of Management (WeSchool), Mumbai/Bangalore
- BIMTECH (Birla Institute of Management Technology), Greater Noida
- IFMR Graduate School of Business (Krea University)
