**RV University (RVU)** is a newer addition to the Bangalore academic landscape, established in 2020 by the same trust that manages the iconic **RV College of Engineering (RVCE)**. While RVCE remains the traditional flagship, RV University offers a more flexible, interdisciplinary, and research-focused B.Tech (Hons) program.

If you are looking at the **RVU Bangalore B.Tech 2026 batch**, here is the complete breakdown of admissions, fees, and the "RVU vs. RVCE" dilemma.

### **RV University (RVU) Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **RVU-ET (Entrance Test)**: The primary admission route for All India students.
2.  **KCET**: For Karnataka state quota.
3.  **JEE Main / SAT**: For direct admissions and scholarships.
4.  **Management Quota**: Direct entry for merit-based candidates.

### **B.Tech (Hons) Computer Science Specializations**
RVU is heavily focused on current industry trends, and its B.Tech curriculum includes:
*   B.Tech (Hons) in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
*   B.Tech (Hons) in Cybersecurity
*   B.Tech (Hons) in Data Science & Cloud Computing

### **RV University B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
Fees at RVU can vary depending on the mode of entry.

| Category | Approx. Annual Fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| **KCET Entry** | approx. ₹1.5 Lakhs |
| **JEE Main / RVU-ET** | approx. ₹5.30 Lakhs |
| **Management Quota** | approx. ₹10.5 Lakhs |

*Hostel and transportation fees are extra, with on-campus facilities being state-of-the-art but limited in capacity.*

### **Placement Review: The RV Brand Advantage**
The biggest USP of RV University is its **shared placement ecosystem** with RVCE. Students at RVU frequently get opportunities to participate in the same recruitment drives as RVCE students.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹24.00 LPA (Initial reports for the newest batches).
*   **Average Package (CSE)**: ₹6.00 LPA to ₹8.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Microsoft, Google, TCS, Samsung, Infosys, KPMG, and Deloitte.

### **RVCE vs. RV University: Which is Better?**
*   **Ranking & Legacy**: RVCE is an established 60-year-old brand. RVU is a 5-year-old modern university.
*   **Curriculum**: RVCE follows the traditional VTU/autonomous structure. RVU offers a more flexible "major-minor" credit system.
*   **Cutoffs**: RVCE requires top 500 ranks. RVU is accessible to students with ranks in the 7,000 to 15,000 range.
