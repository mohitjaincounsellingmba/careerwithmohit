## 🏛️ The Capital Giants: NLU Delhi & DU Faculty of Law

### 1. NLU Delhi (National Law University Delhi)
Dedicated exclusively to NLU Delhi, the **AILET** exam is the gatekeeper here. This is arguably the best "litigation and judiciary" focused college in the country.
- **Why Choose It:** If you want to work in the Supreme Court or clear the Judicial Services (Judgeship), NLU Delhi is the gold standard.

### 2. Faculty of Law, University of Delhi (DU)
The #1 ROI champion in India for the **3-Year LLB** (post-graduation). It has produced more Supreme Court judges and top politicians than any other institute in India.
- **Admission Strategy:** 100% focus on **CUET PG**.

---

## 🚀 The Corporate Leaders: Jindal & Amity

### 3. Jindal Global Law School (JGLS), Sonipat
While technically in Haryana, JGLS is a part of the integrated NCR ecosystem. It is India's most plush and globally connected Law school.
- **USP:** Incredible Moot Court facilities and mandatory internships with Tier-1 firms.

### 4. Amity Law School, Noida (ALSN)
A massive brand with deep-rooted industry ties. Amity Noida is known for its excellent moots and a very active legal aid clinic.
- **Admission:** Mostly merit-based or through national law test scores like CLAT.

## 🏗️ IP University (GGSIPU) Hub

For those looking for high ROI and a strong urban Delhi presence, IP University affiliated colleges are the best bet:
- **VIPS (Vivekananda Institute):** Known for its academic discipline and top-tier moots.
- **JIMS Kalkaji/Rohini:** Solid options for students targeting corporate law firms in Gurgaon and Okhla.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is Law in Delhi better than Bangalore?**
Delhi is better for **Litigation and Judiciary** due to the presence of the Supreme Court. Bangalore is slightly better for **Corporate Law** brand value (NLSIU factor).

**Q2. Does VIPS (IP University) accept CLAT?**
Yes. In 2026, admission to BCA/Law in IP University is primarily through **CLAT UG** scores, with IPU CET as a secondary backup.

**Q3. Can I get direct admission in Law NCR colleges?**
Yes. Private universities in Noida and Gurgaon like Bennett, Galgotias, and GD Goenka offer merit-based direct admission. GGLS and VIPS do NOT have management quota.

**Q4. What is the fee for LLB in Delhi University?**
Almost negligible. DU is a central university, so the fees are around ₹5,000 - ₹8,000 per year, but the entrance (CUET PG) is one of the toughest in India.

**Q5. When is the AILET 2026 exam?**
AILET is usually held on the **second Sunday of December**.
