The National Testing Agency (NTA) has officially released the **JEE Main 2026 Session 2 Admit Card** today. Candidates who registered for the April session can now download their hall tickets from the official NTA website. 

As per the latest notification, the admit cards are currently available for candidates scheduled to appear for the examination on **April 2 and April 4, 2026**. Admit cards for the remaining dates (April 5, 6, 7, and 8) will be released in a phased manner.

| **Exam Dates** | April 2 – April 9, 2026 |
| **Answer Key Challenge Window** | 2nd Week of April 2026 |
| **JEE Main 2026 Result Declaration** | Last Week of April 2026 |

### **🖥️ How to Download JEE Main 2026 Admit Card?**
Candidates can follow these simple steps to download their hall tickets:

1.  **Visit Official Website**: Go to [jeemain.nta.nic.in](https://jeemain.nta.nic.in) or [exams.nta.ac.in/JEE-MAIN/](https://exams.nta.ac.in/JEE-MAIN/).
2.  **Login Section**: Click on the link "Download Admit Card for JEE (Main) 2026 Session 2".
3.  **Enter Credentials**: You will need your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth** (or Password).
4.  **Security Pin**: Enter the security pin (captcha) displayed on the screen.
5.  **Download Hall Ticket**: Your admit card will appear on the screen. Verify all details (Name, Photo, Exam Center, Shift) and download the PDF.
6.  **Print**: Take a clear color printout of the admit card (specifically the first page).

### **⚠️ Important Instructions for Exam Day**
*   **Validity Check**: Ensure that your admit card has a clearly visible **QR Code and Barcode**.
*   **Mandatory Documents**: You MUST carry a printed copy of the admit card and a valid **Photo ID proof** (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or Passport) to the exam center.
*   **Reporting Time**: Reach the exam center at least 60-90 minutes before the scheduled shift to complete the frisking and registration process.
*   **Prohibited Items**: Electronics, mobile phones, calculators, and jewelry are strictly prohibited inside the exam hall.
