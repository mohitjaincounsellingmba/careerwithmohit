## ₹0 Fee — IIT Education Through Fee Waivers

**The biggest secret about IITs:** Students from economically weaker families pay **zero tuition fees** at IITs.

### IIT Fee Waiver Scheme:
| Parental Annual Income | Tuition Fee |
|---|---|
| Below ₹1 Lakh | ₹0 (complete waiver) |
| ₹1–5 Lakhs | ₹0 (complete waiver) |
| ₹5–9 Lakhs | 2/3rd reduction |
| Above ₹9 Lakhs | Full ₹2.5 Lakhs/year |

> This means a student from a weaker economic background who scores well in JEE Advanced pays **absolutely nothing for one of the world's best engineering degrees** — while getting ₹20+ LPA placement.

Additional IIT scholarships: MCM Scholarship (Merit-cum-Means), INSPIRE Fellowship for science branches.

## FAQs — Lowest Fee B.Tech Colleges India 2026

**Q1. Which is the cheapest good engineering college in India?**
Jadavpur University at ₹40,000/year and VJTI Mumbai at ₹25,000/year offer some of the best quality engineering education at near-zero fees. IITs are also effectively free for economically weaker families.

**Q2. Do IIT students really pay zero fees?**
Yes. IIT students from families with annual income below ₹5 Lakhs pay zero tuition fees. They are also eligible for hostel fee waivers and additional scholarships.

**Q3. What is the cheapest state government engineering college?**
VJTI Mumbai charges approximately ₹25,000/year — among the cheapest quality government engineering colleges in India.

**Q4. Can I get B.Tech through education loan?**
Yes. Education loans for B.Tech are available at 8.5–10% interest from nationalised banks (SBI, Bank of Baroda, PNB). Moratorium period covers entire study duration + 1 year.

**Q5. Are there free B.Tech colleges through scholarship for SC/ST students?**
Yes. SC/ST students at government colleges receive full fee waivers funded by state governments. At central government institutions (IITs, NITs), SC/ST students also get lower fees and additional scholarships.
