### **College Review: Mangalmay Institute**
*   **Established Brand**: A well-reputed group of institutions in Greater Noida with decades of experience in management and tech education.
*   **Placement Driven**: Heavily focused on securing early placements for its MBA and PGDM students.
*   **Knowledge Hub**: Located in Knowledge Park, surrounded by a high density of educational and industrial units.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Achieved a high overall placement rate of **93.70%** in 2024.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹37 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: **₹9.60 LPA** (For the top 25% students).
*   **Median Package**: **₹3.6 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: LG, Infosys, Asian Paints, IBM, HCL, and Genpact.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Well-Maintained Campus**: Features Wi-Fi enabled classrooms and modern seminar halls.
*   **Tech Stack**: Modern computer laboratories with the latest administrative software.
*   **Library**: A well-stocked library with a vast collection of management books and research journals.
*   **Amenities**: Includes standard hostel facilities (on-campus) and a functional multi-cuisine canteen.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Supportive Staff**: Highly qualified and knowledgeable faculty members known for their helpful nature.
*   **Guidance Focused**: Teachers provide significant support for both academic syllabus and final-year project work.
*   **Updated Curriculum**: Course structure is regularly reviewed to include current market trends.

**Final Verdict**: A solid option for students who want a balanced MBA experience with strong placement packages for top performers in the Greater Noida region.
