SVKM's Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) is one of India's most respected deemed-to-be universities. With its flagship Mumbai campus consistently ranked among the top 25 B-Schools in the country and an expanding footprint across 8 cities, NMIMS is a powerhouse for MBA, PGDM, and professional education.

Here is a detailed, campus-by-campus breakdown covering fees, placements, cutoffs, specializations, rankings, and real student reviews for 2026 admissions:

---

## Key Highlights & Legacy

- **Establishment:** 1981 (Mumbai Campus)
- **University Status:** Deemed-to-be University (UGC recognized)
- **Accreditation:** AACSB Accredited (School of Business Management, Mumbai) | NAAC A+ Grade
- **NIRF Ranking 2025 (Management):** 24th (Mumbai Campus)
- **Other Rankings:**
  - India Today 2024: 8th for MBA
  - The Week 2024: 10th for MBA
  - IIRF 2025: 5th for MBA
  - Financial Times MiM 2023: 83rd Globally (SBM Mumbai)
- **Entrance Exam:** NMAT by GMAC (Primary), some campuses also accept CAT/MAT/CMAT scores

### 1. NMIMS Mumbai (Vile Parle West, Mumbai – The Flagship)

**Fee Structure (2-Year MBA):**
- Total Tuition Fees: ₹25.50 Lakhs – ₹27.00 Lakhs
- Hostel Fees: ₹2.50 Lakhs – ₹3.50 Lakhs per year (optional)
- All-In Cost (with hostel): ₹30.00 Lakhs – ₹32.00 Lakhs

**Placement Statistics (2024-2025):**
- Highest Package: ₹67.70 LPA
- Average Package (MBA Core): ₹25.13 LPA
- Average Package (MBA HR): ₹18.64 LPA
- Top 10% Average: ₹40.60 LPA
- PPOs: 37% of MBA Core batch
- 168 recruiters participated; 72 were first-time recruiters
- Top Recruiters: Goldman Sachs, Wells Fargo, CRISIL, Accenture, Deloitte, KPMG, HDFC Bank, ITC, Aditya Birla Capital, American Express

**Specializations Offered:**
- Finance
- Marketing
- Human Resource Management
- Operations & Supply Chain Management
- Business Analytics
- Information Technology & Systems
- International Business
- Digital Transformation
- Pharmaceutical Management
- Entrepreneurship

**Student Reviews:**
- **Pros:** Unmatched brand value in western India. Alumni network is legendary (CXOs across BFSI, FMCG, Pharma). Mumbai location = massive internship & networking opportunities. AACSB accredited. Consistently the top non-IIM B-School in Maharashtra.
- **Cons:** Very high fees (₹27 Lakhs+). Hostel availability is limited. Intense competition for top placements within the batch.

**Our Verdict:** NMIMS Mumbai (SBM) is a clear Tier-1 B-School. If you score 209+ on NMAT and clear the GDPI, this is a no-brainer pick — especially for Finance and Marketing roles. The ₹25 LPA average package justifies the investment.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1. Is NMIMS Mumbai better than newer IIMs?**
In terms of placements and industry reputation, NMIMS Mumbai (SBM) competes with IIM Udaipur, IIM Trichy, and IIM Kashipur. The ₹25 LPA average package is comparable, and NMIMS has a far stronger alumni network in BFSI and Pharma sectors.

**Q2. Does NMIMS accept CAT scores?**
NMIMS primarily accepts NMAT by GMAC scores. Some newer campuses may consider CAT/CMAT/MAT scores, but NMAT remains the primary entrance exam for all MBA programs.

**Q3. Is the NMIMS degree a PGDM or MBA?**
NMIMS being a deemed university, offers an MBA degree (not PGDM). This is an advantage for students seeking government jobs or higher education, as MBA degrees have easier equivalence recognition.

**Q4. Are placements common across all NMIMS campuses?**
NMIMS runs a partially centralized placement system. Students from newer campuses (Shirpur, Chandigarh, Indore) can participate in placement drives at the Mumbai campus, which significantly broadens their recruiter exposure.
