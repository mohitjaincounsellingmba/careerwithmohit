## 🏫 GD Goenka School of Management: Overview
The School of Management at GD Goenka University is known for its industry-aligned curriculum and a focus on holistic student development. Being part of a 60-acre lush green campus, BBA students here enjoy a vibrant life alongside peers from Law, Tech, and Design.

### **Key Highlights**
*   **Location:** Sohna Road, Gurgaon (Delhi NCR).
*   **Accreditation:** Recognized by UGC; member of prestigious international associations.
*   **Infrastructure:** Modern classrooms, high-tech labs, and one of the best sports complexes in North India.

## 📈 Placement Review (2025-26 Batch)
Placements for BBA students have shown a steady upward trend. The university's Corporate Resource Centre (CRC) is active in bringing both startups and MNCs to the campus.

*   **Average Package:** **₹5.0 - ₹5.5 LPA**
*   **Highest Package (Management):** **₹35 LPA** (Projected/Reported)
*   **Top Recruiters:** Deloitte, Amazon, Genpact, Bosch, Wipro, Axis Bank, and Nestle.

Compared to other private universities in Gurgaon, the ROI is decent, especially if you actively participate in the university's corporate grooming sessions.

## 📝 Admission Process 2026
Admissions for the 2026 cycle are currently open. The selection process is more comprehensive than just marks:

1.  **Eligibility:** 10+2 with a minimum of 50-60% from a recognized board.
2.  **Entrance Exam:** 
    *   **CUET:** Valid CUET scores are accepted.
    *   **GATA:** If you don't have a CUET score, you must appear for the **Goenka Aptitude Test for Admission (GATA)**.
3.  **Personal Interview (PI):** A mandatory interaction to assess the candidate's communication skills and career intent.
