The **ICFAI Business School (IBS)** network is one of the largest and most prominent management education ecosystems in India. With 9 strategically located campuses, IBS ensures that high-quality business education and strong corporate linkages are accessible to students across the country.

Admissions to IBS campuses are primarily facilitated through the **IBSAT** exam. However, scores from CAT, XAT, NMAT, and GMAT are also accepted.

If you are evaluating IBS as your management alma mater, this complete guide breaks down all 9 campuses—from the flagship IBS Hyderabad to the regional powerhouses—covering their latest fees, average packages, and IBSAT cutoffs.

---

## The Flagship Campus

### 1. IBS Hyderabad
IBS Hyderabad is the crown jewel of the ICFAI network. It is a massive, sprawling campus spanning over 91 acres and boasts AACSB accreditation (a hallmark of excellence in business education globally).
*   **Programs:** MBA (Full-time) 
*   **Total Fees (2026-28):** ₹17.01 Lakhs
*   **Expected IBSAT Cutoff:** 75 - 85 Percentile
*   **Average Package:** ₹9.85 LPA
*   **Highest Package:** ₹21.00 LPA (Domestic) / ₹41.40 LPA (International)
*   **Top Recruiters:** Deloitte, Axis Bank, TCS, EY, HDFC
*   **The Verdict:** If you clear the IBSAT with a strong score, IBS Hyderabad should be your undisputed first choice. The sheer scale of the alumni network and the number of companies visiting for placements is unmatched.

These campuses are situated in India's biggest corporate hubs, providing students with excellent live-project and fast-track placement opportunities.

## Admissions Breakdown & IBSAT Exemptions

The admission process is uniform across the campuses and broadly involves two stages:
1.  **Entrance Exam:** Clearing the IBSAT or presenting valid scores from other national-level exams.
2.  **Selection Process:** Qualified candidates are called for Group Discussion (Micro Presentation) and Personal Interviews.

### Who is exempt from IBSAT?
Candidates with the following minimum test scores (from 2023 onwards) can directly apply for the selection process:
*   **CAT:** 60+ Percentile
*   **XAT:** 60+ Percentile
*   **GMAT:** 500+ Score
*   **NMAT by GMAC:** 150+ Score

*(Note: For the massive ₹2 Lakhs per student merit scholarship, the cutoffs are substantially higher—for e.g., CAT 90+ percentile, XAT 80+ percentile, or NMAT 225+).*

## Final Verdict
If you are looking at IBS, **IBS Hyderabad** remains the absolute gold standard with its beautiful campus and massive alumni depth. However, from a pure Return on Investment (ROI) perspective, both **IBS Mumbai** and **IBS Gurgaon** are spectacular underdogs. At approximately half the tuition fees of the flagship campus, they deliver average packages nearly 80% as strong as Hyderabad!
