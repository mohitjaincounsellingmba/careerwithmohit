## Understanding the Tiers

### Tier 1 — IITs (Indian Institutes of Technology)

India has **23 IITs** offering B.Tech programs. Admission is through **JEE Advanced**, which is attempted by roughly 1.5 lakh JEE Main qualifiers every year — making IIT admission extremely competitive.

**Key IITs and their JEE Advanced cutoffs (CRL — Open Category, CSE 2025):**

| IIT | JEE Advanced CSE Closing Rank (2025) |
|---|---|
| IIT Bombay | ~100 |
| IIT Delhi | ~120 |
| IIT Madras | ~150 |
| IIT Kanpur | ~210 |
| IIT Kharagpur | ~350 |
| IIT Roorkee | ~450 |
| IIT Guwahati | ~700 |
| IIT Hyderabad | ~1,200 |
| IIT Jodhpur | ~3,000 |
| IIT Bhubaneswar | ~4,500 |

> **Annual Fee:** ₹2.5 Lakhs (identical at all IITs thanks to central govt. funding)
> **Fee waiver:** Students with parental income < ₹5 Lakhs per year are eligible for full tuition waiver

## How to Choose Your B.Tech College — 5 Key Criteria

1. **JEE Rank vs College Tier:** Map your JEE Main/Advanced rank to realistic college options using the JoSAA cutoff data.
2. **Branch Over Brand (sometimes):** CSE at a lower-ranked NIT often outperforms other branches at a higher-ranked NIT in placements.
3. **Placement Records:** Check the actual number of students placed vs total batch size — not just the highest package.
4. **Location & Industry Proximity:** Hyderabad and Bangalore NITs/private colleges benefit from proximity to IT hubs.
5. **Fee vs ROI:** IITs are highly cost-effective. Private universities charging ₹8–12 Lakhs total for a ₹8–12 LPA job offer a marginal ROI — weigh carefully.

## FAQs — Top B.Tech Colleges in India 2026

**Q1. Which is the #1 engineering college in India 2026?**
IIT Madras has been ranked #1 by NIRF for 6 consecutive years (2019–2024). IIT Delhi and IIT Bombay follow closely.

**Q2. What JEE rank is needed for IIT Bombay CSE?**
Approximately CRL rank under 100 in JEE Advanced is needed for CSE at IIT Bombay in the open category.

**Q3. Which NIT is best for computer science?**
NIT Tiruchirappalli (NIT-T) is consistently the top NIT for CSE, followed by NIT Surathkal and NIT Warangal.

**Q4. Is BITS Pilani better than NITs?**
For CSE and electronics in private sector placements, BITS Pilani (Pilani campus) is generally considered comparable to top NITs and better for software roles. NITs may have an edge in government sector jobs and GATE performance.

**Q5. What are the total B.Tech seats in IITs, NITs, and IIITs?**
- IITs: ~16,000 seats | NITs: ~23,000 seats | IIITs: ~7,000 seats
