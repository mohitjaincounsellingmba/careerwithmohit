Pune Business School (PBS) is part of the PCET group (Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust), which has a massive legacy of placements in Engineering. Now, PBS is bringing that same placement muscle to the management world.

### **Quick Highlights (2025 Batch):**
*   **Avg Placement**: ₹7.40 LPA
*   **Highest Package**: ₹27.00 LPA
*   **Placement Rate**: 97% (Batch of 2024-25)
*   **Entrance Exam**: CAT / XAT / MAT / CMAT / MAH CET

### **Why Choose PBS Pune?**
*   **The PCET Legacy**: Benefit from the trust established by one of Pune's most successful education groups over 30 years.
*   **Placement Focus**: Their centralized placement cell is one of the most active in Maharashtra, attracting tech and consulting giants.
*   **Modern Curriculum**: Strong emphasis on Data Analytics, AI in Business, and Digital transformation.

### **Fee Structure:**
While specific 2026 fees are being finalized, the current structure is highly competitive for the Pune market. PBS is ideal for students who want a strong placement-oriented brand without the overly high fees of ultra-premium B-schools.
