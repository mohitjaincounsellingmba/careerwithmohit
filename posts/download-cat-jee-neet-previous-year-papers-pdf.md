## How to Effectively Use PYQs in Your Preparation

To get the most out of these papers, follow this 3-step approach:

### 1. The Mock Environment
Don't just solve questions one by one. Sit in a quiet room, set a timer for the exact exam duration, and solve the full paper without interruptions.

### 2. Deep Analysis
Once done, don't just check the score. Analyze:
-   Which section took the most time?
-   Were your mistakes due to lack of concept or silly errors?
-   Which "traps" did you fall into?

### 3. Topic-wise Revision
If you missed three questions on *Arithmetic* or *Organic Chemistry*, go back to your notes and revise those specific topics before attempting the next paper.
