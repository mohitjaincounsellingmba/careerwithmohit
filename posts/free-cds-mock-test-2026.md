The CDS exam comprises three papers: **English, General Knowledge**, and **Elementary Mathematics** (for IMA, INA, AFA — not required for OTA). Our mock focuses on the challenging **Elementary Mathematics** paper that eliminates a large portion of candidates.

## Elementary Mathematics — Detailed Syllabus

### Arithmetic (40 Questions)
- **Number Systems:** Natural numbers, integers, rational/irrational numbers, decimals, percentages
- **Unitary Method:** Time-Distance, Time-Work, Pipes-Cisterns
- **Simple & Compound Interest:** Formulae and application
- **Profit, Loss & Discount:** Trade and bank discounts
- **Ratio & Proportion:** Direct and inverse variation
- **Averages:** Simple average, weighted average

### Algebra & Geometry (40 Questions)
- **Basic Algebra:** Polynomials, linear/quadratic equations, simultaneous equations
- **Matrices:** Basic operations, determinants (2×2, 3×3)
- **Coordinate Geometry:** Lines, circles, distance formula, section formula
- **Basic Geometry:** Properties of triangles, circles, quadrilaterals, congruency and similarity

### Trigonometry & Statistics (20 Questions)
- **Trigonometry:** Ratios, identities, height and distance problems
- **Statistics:** Mean, Median, Mode, Variance, Standard Deviation, basic probability

## SSB Preparation — Don't Ignore It!

The CDS written exam is just the first filter. The **Services Selection Board (SSB)** is the true battleground — a 5-day assessment that evaluates:
- **Officer Like Qualities (OLQs):** Leadership, courage, teamwork, adaptability
- **Psychological Tests:** Thematic Apperception Test (TAT), Word Association Test (WAT), Situation Reaction Test (SRT)
- **Group Tasks:** Group Discussion, Group Planning Exercise, Group Obstacle Race
- **Personal Interview:** Detailed discussion on your background, motivation, current affairs

**Only 30–40% of SSB candidates are recommended** after this gruelling process. Start developing your personality alongside your written exam preparation.

## Pro Tips for CDS 2026

- **NCERT Class 9–10 Math is Sufficient:** Unlike JEE, CDS Math requires only Class 10 proficiency. Be thorough — don't leave any chapter.
- **Triangles and Circles are High-Value:** Geometry carries significant weightage. Master congruency, Pythagoras, circles, and triangle properties.
- **Speed and Accuracy Balance:** With 120 minutes for 100 questions, you have more comfortable time than SSC/IBPS. Use it to double-check tricky geometry answers.
- **Apply for Both CDS I & II:** UPSC conducts two CDS exams per year (typically Feb and Sept). Apply for both!
- **Start SSB Prep Alongside Written:** Reading books like "SSB Cracker" before the written exam helps you understand the full journey.
