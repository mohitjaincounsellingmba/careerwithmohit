Jaipur, the "Pink City," has emerged as a major education hub in North India, particularly for those looking for high ROI and world-class private university infrastructure. With its rich heritage and modern tech centers, Jaipur offers a balanced environment for those appearing for the **Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG 2026**.

If you are a student targeting **Jaipur for the 2026-27 session**, here are the top institutions you should consider using your CUET scores.

---

### **1. Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ)**
Known for its massive campus and highly industry-aligned curriculum.
*   **Popular Degrees:** B.Tech, BBA, B.Com, B.Sc (Hons).
*   **USP:** World-class infrastructure and top-tier placements.

### **2. JECRC University**
A hub for technical and professional education in Jaipur.
*   **Programs:** B.Tech, B.Sc, Design, Liberal Arts.
*   **USP:** Focus on employability and practical learning.

### **3. Jaipur National University (JNU Jaipur)**
One of the oldest and largest private universities in Rajasthan.
*   **Courses:** BA, B.Sc, B.Com, Professional Diploma.
*   **USP:** Multi-disciplinary environment.

### **4. Vivekananda Global University (VGU)**
Focuses on design, agriculture, and modern engineering specializations.
*   **Programs:** B.Des, B.Sc Agriculture, BBA, BA J&MC.
*   **USP:** Focus on research and entrepreneurship.

### **5. Poornima University**
Known for its engineering and architecture focus.
*   **Programs:** B.Arch, B.Tech, B.Sc, B.Com.
*   **USP:** Strong discipline and result-oriented academic culture.

---

### **Why Jaipur for Your Undergraduate Degree?**
*   **Affordability:** Low cost of living compared to Delhi, Mumbai, or Bangalore.
*   **Growth:** Emerging as a tech and startup hub.
*   **Heritage:** A culturally rich and secure environment for students.
