### **How the 'Free Counselling' Trap Works**
If a consultant isn't charging **YOU**, they are definitely charging the **COLLEGE**. This means their "expert advice" is biased toward the college that pays the highest commission, not the one that offers you the best career.

1.  **The Distraction**: They will tell you that a college with a ₹1.5 Lakh commission has "amazing corporate connections" even if its actual average package is only ₹5 LPA.
2.  **The Pressure**: They will use phrases like "Only 2 seats left" or "Direct admission closes today" to force you into a high-commission college before you can check the real placement reviews.
3.  **The Fake Reviews**: Many consultants control "Student Review" groups on Telegram and WhatsApp to suppress the truth about bad placements.
