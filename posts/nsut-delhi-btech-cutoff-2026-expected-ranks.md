**Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT)**, formerly known as NSIT, is one of the most prestigious engineering institutions in India. Located in Dwarka, New Delhi, NSUT is renowned for its exceptional placement records, especially in the tech and software sectors. Along with DTU and IIIT Delhi, it forms the "Golden Triangle" of engineering education in the national capital.

### **NSUT Delhi B.Tech Admission Process 2026**
Admissions to NSUT are conducted through the **Joint Admission Committee (JAC) Delhi** counselling process.
1.  **JEE Main**: Students must appear for **JEE Main 2026** and secure a valid All India Rank (AIR).
2.  **JAC Delhi Registration**: Register online at the official JAC Delhi portal.
3.  **Choice Filling**: Select NSUT and your preferred branches (CSAI, CSE, IT, ECE, etc.).
4.  **Seat Allotment**: Seats are allotted based on AIR and category-wise quotas.

### **NSUT Expected Cutoffs 2026 (General Category)**
Cutoffs for NSUT are divided into two main quotas: **Delhi Region (85%)** for students who passed Class 12 from Delhi, and **Outside Delhi Region (15%)**.

### **NSUT Delhi B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
NSUT offers a high ROI (Return on Investment) with a fee structure that is competitive compared to private universities.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹2.29 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Cost**: **₹9.5 Lakhs - ₹10.5 Lakhs** (including miscellaneous charges).
*   **Hostel Fees**: approx. ₹40,000 - ₹50,000 per semester.

### **Placement Highlights: The "Dwarka" Advantage**
NSUT is a magnet for top-tier tech companies. Its placement statistics often rival the top IITs.
*   **Highest Package**: ₹1.2 Crore+ (International) / ₹64 LPA (Domestic).
*   **Average Package (CS/IT branches)**: ₹18 LPA - ₹24 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Adobe, Goldman Sachs, Uber, and Tower Research.
*   **Strong Alumni Base**: NSUTians are present in almost every major tech giant and successful startup globally.

### **NSUT vs DTU vs IIIT Delhi: Which one to choose?**
*   **NSUT**: Best for those focused on software placements and a relatively more balanced campus life.
*   **DTU**: Known for its massive 160+ acre campus and a very strong "Brand Name" in North India.
*   **IIIT Delhi**: Best for students interested in research and a more rigorous, specialized tech curriculum.

### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**
**1. What is the difference between CSE and CSAI at NSUT?**
CSAI (Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in AI) has a more focused curriculum on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning compared to the core CSE.

**2. Is there any management quota in NSUT Delhi?**
No, NSUT does not have any management quota. Admissions are strictly based on JEE Main ranks through JAC Delhi counselling.

**3. What is the cutoff for EWS/OBC/SC/ST at NSUT?**
Cutoffs for reserved categories are much higher (relaxed). For example, SC candidates from Delhi often get branches under 1.5 Lakh rank.
