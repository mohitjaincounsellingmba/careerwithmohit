## Section-Wise Strategy

- **English (30 Q, 20 min):** Cloze test, reading comprehension, and error detection dominate. Aim to attempt 25+ questions with high accuracy. RC passages are typically 250–350 words.
- **Quantitative Aptitude (35 Q, 20 min):** DI sets (tables, bar graphs, pie charts) carry high weightage. Solve DI first, then arithmetic, then miscellaneous. Target 25+ attempts.
- **Reasoning Ability (35 Q, 20 min):** Puzzles and Seating Arrangements are the most important topics — often 15–20 questions come from these. Aim for 26+ attempts.

## Pro Tips for IBPS PO 2026 Success

- **Never Ignore Sectional Cutoffs:** Even if your overall score is high, failing to meet the minimum in one section means instant rejection.
- **DI Mastery is Key:** Data Interpretation sets can make or break your Quant score. Practice 2–3 new DI sets daily.
- **Practice Under Time Pressure:** Always attempt mocks within the exact time limits. Never extend your time during practice sessions.
- **Review Error Patterns:** Track which Reasoning puzzle types slow you down the most and solve those exclusively every alternate day.

## What Comes After Prelims?

The IBPS PO Mains consists of **200 questions in 3 hours** with an added reasoning+computer section. Candidates who score well in Prelims and Mains are called for a Group Discussion (GD) + Personal Interview (PI) round. The final merit list is based on Mains + Interview combined scores.
