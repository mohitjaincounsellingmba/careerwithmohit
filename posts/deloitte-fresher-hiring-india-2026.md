### **Key Job Roles for Freshers**

#### **1. Analyst Trainee (Technology)**
- **Eligibility**: B.E/B.Tech (CS, IT, ECE, EEE), MCA.
- **Skills**: Basics of Programming (C/C++/Java/Python), DBMS, SDLC.
- **Focus**: Application development, system integration, and tech consulting.

#### **2. Associate Analyst (Consulting/Risk)**
- **Eligibility**: BBA, BCA, B.Sc, B.Com, MBA graduates.
- **Skills**: Analytical problem solving, Excel, Business communication.
- **Focus**: Business process modeling, risk assessment, and market research.

#### **3. Junior Analyst (Audit & Assurance)**
- **Eligibility**: Graduates with Finance or Accounting backgrounds.
- **Skills**: Financial reporting basics, attention to detail, MS Office.
- **Focus**: Supporting global audit teams with data validation and compliance checks.

### **Deloitte Selection Process 2026**
Deloitte’s hiring process is known for being rigorous yet structured:
1.  **Online Registration**: Applying through the official Deloitte NLA portal or your college TPO.
2.  **Online Assessment (NLA)**:
    - **Aptitude**: Quantitative, Logical, and Verbal.
    - **Technical MCQ**: CS fundamentals and coding snippets.
    - **Situational Judgment**: Workplace behavioral assessment.
3.  **Technical Interview**: Discussion on core subjects, projects, and case-study based problem solving.
4.  **Partner/Director Interview**: A final leadership round to check your fit with Deloitte’s culture and values.

### **How to Apply?**
1. Visit the **[Deloitte India Students Portal](https://www2.deloitte.com/in/en/careers/students.html)**.
2. Look for the **"National Level Assessment"** link if applying off-campus.
3. Create your profile and ensure your academic details match your marksheet.
4. Upload a clean, one-page resume highlighting skills and internships.
