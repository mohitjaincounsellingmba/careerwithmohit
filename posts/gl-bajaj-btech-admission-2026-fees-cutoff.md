**GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management (GL Bajaj)** is one of the most prominent private engineering colleges in Greater Noida. Affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and accredited with NAAC Grade 'A+', GL Bajaj has consistently been ranked among the top engineering colleges in Uttar Pradesh for its modern infrastructure and strong placement volume.

### **GL Bajaj Admission Process 2026**
1.  **Entrance Exam**: Compulsory to appear in **JEE Main 2026**.
2.  **Counselling**: Admission is through the **UPTAC** (Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counselling) process.
3.  **Direct Admission**: Certain seats are available for direct admission based on 10+2 marks and overall merit (separate application on college portal).

### **GL Bajaj B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
GL Bajaj is known for offering a high-tech environment at a standardized price.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹1.50 Lakhs - ₹1.61 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Cost**: **₹4.2 Lakhs - ₹4.5 Lakhs**.
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.0 Lakhs to ₹1.25 Lakhs per year (depending on amenities).

### **Placement Review: A Leading Career Hub**
GL Bajaj has a strong placement cell that attracts many MNCs and startups from the NCR region.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹40.00 LPA to ₹45.0 LPA.
*   **Average Package (CSE/IT)**: ₹6.0 LPA to ₹8.5 LPA.
*   **Average Package (Overall)**: ₹5.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Capgemini, Cognizant, and Bosch.
