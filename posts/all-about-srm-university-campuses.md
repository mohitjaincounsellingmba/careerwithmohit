SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) is one of the largest and most sought-after private engineering universities in India. Known for its massive infrastructure, diverse student body, and strong placement cell, SRM attracts lakhs of applicants every year.

Here is a detailed breakdown of the B.Tech program across all major SRM campuses for 2026:

## Key Highlights & Legacy

- **Establishment:** 1985 (as SRM Engineering College); granted deemed university status in 2002.
- **Legacy & USP:** One of the most globally connected private universities in India, offering numerous Semester Abroad Programs (SAP) with institutions like MIT, Stanford, and UC Berkeley.
- **Campuses:**
  - **Kattankulathur (KTR), Chennai** (The Main & Best Campus)
  - **Ramapuram, Chennai**
  - **Vadapalani, Chennai**
  - **Tiruchirappalli (Trichy)**
  - **SRM AP (Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh)**
  - **SRM Delhi-NCR (Modinagar)**

## Admission Details

- **Program Offered:** B.Tech (AICTE Approved, NAAC A++ Accredited)
- **Entrance Exam Accepted:** SRMJEEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination), JEE Main (for limited seats)
- **Expected Cut-off (SRMJEEE Rank for Computer Science - KTR Campus):**
  - Core CSE: Top 2,000 - 3,000 Rank
  - CSE with Specializations: Top 5,000 - 10,000 Rank
  - *Note: Cutoffs for Ramapuram and AP campuses are more relaxed.*
- **Total Fees (Approximate for 4 Years):**
  - CSE & IT Branches: ₹14.00 Lakhs - ₹18.00 Lakhs
  - Core Branches (Mechanical/Civil): ₹10.00 Lakhs - ₹12.00 Lakhs

## Key Specializations Offered (B.Tech)

- Computer Science and Engineering (Core)
- CSE with Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML)
- CSE with Data Science
- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Mechanical Engineering
- Aerospace Engineering
- Biotechnology

## Placement Statistics (2025-2026)

The SRM Career Centre conducts massive centralized placement drives for all campuses at the main KTR campus.

- **Highest Placement Package:** ₹1.10 Crores (International) / ₹52.00 LPA (Domestic)
- **Average Placement Package:** ₹7.50 LPA - ₹8.50 LPA (Higher for CSE branches)
- **Lowest Placement Package:** ₹3.50 LPA - ₹4.00 LPA (Mass recruitment level)
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Cognizant, Wipro, Infosys, Amazon, Microsoft, Google, PayPal, Barclays, and Hyundai.

## Student Reviews & Verdict

**What Students Say:**
- **Pros:** The KTR campus is practically a small city with world-class facilities, massive libraries, and incredible tech fests (like Aaruush). The diverse peer group and networking opportunities are phenomenal. Highly accessible Semester Abroad Programs.
- **Cons:** The student intake is massive, meaning you have to fight hard to stand out during placements. Fees are on the higher side compared to state government colleges.

**Our Verdict:** Prioritize the **KTR (Main) Campus** specifically for Computer Science or ECE. While the fees are high, if you work hard and maintain a high GPA, the opportunities provided by SRM's corporate ties and international relations are truly unmatched among private institutions.
