The **Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP) 2026** is the centralized state-level counselling held for admission into B.Tech/B.E. and B.Arch programs in Rajasthan. Conducted by the **Centre for Electronic Governance (CEG), Rajasthan**, REAP is the gateway to all top government and private engineering colleges in the state, including the prestigious MBM Jodhpur and RTU Kota.

Unlike other states, Rajasthan does not conduct its own entrance exam. Admissions are based purely on your national-level performance.

### **Admission Priority & Eligibility**
REAP allocates seats based on a strict priority ladder:
1.  **Priority 1**: Candidates with a valid **JEE Main 2026** score (AIR).
2.  **Priority 2**: Candidates with **10+2 (PCM) Merit** (if seats remain vacant after JEE allotment).

**Basic Eligibility**:
*   **Educational**: Must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, and one optional subject (Chemistry/Bio/CS).
*   **Minimum Marks**: 45% aggregate in PCM (40% for SC/ST/OBC of Rajasthan).
*   **Reservation**: 85% of total seats are reserved for **Rajasthan Domicile** students. 15% are open for **All India** candidates.

### **REAP 2026 Registration Fee**
*   **Application Fee**: ₹590 (₹500 + 18% GST).
*   **Nature**: Non-refundable, payable online through Credit/Debit card or Net Banking.

### **Step-by-Step REAP Counselling Process**
1.  **Online Registration**: Visit the official REAP portal and fill in your personal and academic details.
2.  **Payment**: Pay the ₹590 registration fee to activate your choice filling.
3.  **Choice Filling**: This is the most critical step. Arrange your preferred colleges and branches in decreasing order of preference.
4.  **Seat Allotment**: CEG will release allotment lists based on your merit and the choices you filled.
5.  **Reporting**: If allotted a seat, you must physically report to the college to verify documents and pay the admission fee.

**Essential Documents for Reporting**:
*   REAP 2026 Allotment Letter
*   JEE Main Scorecard 2026
*   Class 10 & 12 Marksheets
*   Domicile Certificate (for Rajasthan students)
*   Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
*   Medical Fitness Certificate

**Choice Filling Tips for REAP 2026**:
Always put the top government colleges (MBM/RTU/CTAE) in your first few choices, even if your rank is slightly lower. You can always get upgraded in the upward movement rounds.
