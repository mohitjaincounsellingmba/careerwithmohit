## 🏛️ The Hyderabad Crown: NALSAR

### 1. NALSAR University of Law
Consistently ranked #2 or #3 in India, NALSAR is a powerhouse of legal innovation. It is famous for its flexible curriculum and for having the most active student-run research centers in the country.
- **Top Recruiters:** Linklaters, Herbert Smith Freehills, Khaitan & Co.
- **Admission Strategy:** Secure a top-100 rank in **CLAT UG** to guarantee a seat here.

---

## 🚀 Private Leaders: Symbiosis & ICFAI

### 2. Symbiosis Law School, Hyderabad (SLS-H)
Part of the Symbiosis family, this campus operates with the same high-tier placement cell that works across Pune and Noida.
- **Why Choose It:** Best for students targeting **Corporate Law firms and International Moots**.

### 3. Faculty of Law, IFHE (ICFAI University)
One of the most trusted private brands in South India. ICFAI is known for its rigorous case-study-based approach to legal education.
- **Admission:** They have their own entrance test, **ILSAT**, and also accept high LSAT scores.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is NALSAR better than NLSIU Bangalore?**
They are 1A and 1B. NLSIU has a slightly higher overall brand legacy, but NALSAR is often preferred for its **flexible credits and better student-life balance**.

**Q2. Does Symbiosis Hyderabad offer the same placements as Pune?**
While the placement cell is centralized, the top law firms still prefer the SLS Pune campus for their flagship hires. However, SLS Hyderabad graduates are highly sought after by mid-sized firms and Hyderabad-based MNCs.

**Q3. What is the fee for Law in Osmania University?**
As a state university, the fees are very affordable, around ₹15k - ₹25k per year for the 3-year and 5-year courses through TS LAWCET.

**Q4. Can I get a job at T-Hub as a Law student?**
Yes. T-Hub is India's largest startup incubator. Many startups there need part-time legal interns for IP filing and founder agreements.

**Q5. When is the TS LAWCET 2026 exam?**
Usually held in **June**. Applications typically open in March.
