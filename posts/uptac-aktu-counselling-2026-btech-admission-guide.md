**UPTAC (Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counselling) 2026**, conducted by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), is the gateway to engineering seats in over 750+ colleges across Uttar Pradesh. Whether you are aiming for government institutes like IET Lucknow or premier private colleges in Noida, UPTAC 2026 is your mandatory path.

### **Eligibility & Admission Criteria**
*   **Entrance Exam**: A valid **JEE Main 2026** score is compulsory for B.Tech admissions.
*   **Merit**: Allotment is based on your All India Rank (AIR) in JEE Main.
*   **Domicile**: 
    *   **UP Domicile**: Candidates who studied in UP or whose parents are domiciled in UP are eligible for 100% seats.
    *   **Outside UP**: Eligible for 15% seats in private colleges and the All India Quota.

### **Registration Fee & Seat Acceptance**
*   **Registration Fee**: ₹1,000 (Non-refundable).
*   **Seat Acceptance Fee**: 
    *   General/OBC: ₹20,000.
    *   SC/ST: ₹12,000.

### **Step-by-Step Counselling Procedure**
1.  **Online Registration**: Log in at `uptac.admissions.nic.in` using your JEE Main Application number.
2.  **Document Upload**: Upload high-resolution scans of your 10+2 marksheets, JEE Rank Card, Domicile, and Category certificates.
3.  **Online Verification**: AKTU officials will verify your documents online. You will receive an "OK" status or a query if something is missing.
4.  **Choice Filling**: Once verified, you can fill unlimited choices of college and branches. Always put government colleges at the top.
5.  **Seat Allotment**: If allotted, you must choose to **Freeze** (keep the seat) or **Float** (try for a better college in the next round).

**Pro Tip for UPTAC 2026**:
In the first round, if you don't get your dream branch in a top college, always choose 'Float'. This keeps your current seat safe while giving you a chance to upgrade as others withdraw.

### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**
**1. When will UPTAC 2026 registration start?**
Usually starts in June or July after the result declaration of JEE Main Session 2.

**2. What is the registration fee for UPTAC?**
The fee is ₹1,000 for all candidates.

**3. Can outside-UP students apply for government seats in AKTU?**
No, government college seats are reserved for students with UP domicile. Outside state students are eligible for private college seats.
