### **College Review: Sushant University**
*   **Established Brand**: Formerly Ansal University, it has a long-standing reputation in the Gurgaon educational landscape.
*   **Academic Diversity**: Houses multiple schools (Management, Design, Tech), offering a rich interdisciplinary environment.
*   **Corporate Hub**: Located in a prime area of Gurgaon, facilitating easy industry visits and networking.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: **₹14.10 LPA** (2025).
*   **Average Package**: **₹7.30 - 8.20 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: KPMG, Adobe, HCL, Berger, ITC, and PwC.
*   **CRC Strength**: A dedicated Corporate Resource Center (CRC) focuses on 100% placement assistance.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **14-Acre Campus**: Modern infrastructure including specialized labs and smart classrooms.
*   **Library**: A massive collection of over 40,000 volumes across diverse management domains.
*   **Sports**: dedicated spaces for both indoor and outdoor activities to ensure physical wellness.
*   **Residential**: Separate on-campus hostels for boys and girls with standard amenities.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **High Qualifications**: A large number of faculty members hold Ph.D. degrees.
*   **Supportive Culture**: Teachers are known to be friendly, approachable, and encouraging.
*   **Current Insights**: Curriculum focus on real-life industry examples and regular corporate seminars.

**Final Verdict**: Best for students who want a well-rounded university experience with strong placement linkages and a supportive academic culture.
