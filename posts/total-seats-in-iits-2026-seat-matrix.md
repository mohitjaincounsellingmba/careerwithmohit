### **Category-wise Seat Reservation in IITs**
The seats in IITs are reserved as per the Government of India norms:
*   **General (Open)**: 40.5%
*   **OBC-NCL**: 27%
*   **SC**: 15%
*   **ST**: 7.5%
*   **EWS**: 10%
*   **PwD**: 5% horizontal reservation in each category.

### **JoSAA 2026 Seat Matrix: What to Expect?**
The **Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA)** handles the counselling for all 23 IITs. For 2026, candidates must qualify for **JEE Advanced** to be eligible for these seats.
