Strategically located in the heart of India's capital, the **New Delhi Institute of Management (NDIM)** is a popular choice for MBA aspirants seeking strong corporate linkages and practical industry exposure. Known for its unique dual-specialization model, NDIM offers a robust PGDM curriculum declared equivalent to an MBA by the AIU.

If you are targeting admission for the 2025-2026 session, here is a complete, bulleted breakdown of everything you need to know about NDIM Delhi.

---

## 🏛️ Establishment & Legacy
* **Established:** 1992.
* **Legacy:** Backed by the non-profit "Society for Employment and Career Counselling," NDIM has over three decades of experience in management education.
* **Accreditations:** Approved by AICTE, accredited by NBA, and its PGDM is declared equivalent to an MBA by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU).
* **Location Edge:** Being in New Delhi provides students with extensive opportunities for corporate networking, live projects, and internships.

## 📚 Academic Programs Offered
NDIM's flagship PGDM program allows students to customize their careers through a flexible curriculum:
* **PGDM (General):** The core 2-year full-time program.
* **PGDM Online:** A flexible, specialized program suited for working professionals.

## 🎓 Unique Dual Specializations
Unlike many B-schools that restrict students to one major, NDIM allows candidates to choose **two major specializations** from highly relevant fields:
* Marketing
* Finance
* Human Resource Management (HRM)
* Logistics & Supply Chain Management
* Information Technology (IT)
* Business Analytics (including AI/Machine Learning)
* Digital Marketing
* Media Management & Communication
* International Business (IB)
* Production & Operations Management
* Innovation, Entrepreneurship, and Venture Development

## 💰 Fee Structure (2025-2026)
* **Total Program Fee:** Approximately ₹12.75 Lakhs to ₹13.85 Lakhs for the entire two-year PGDM.
* **Installments:** The fee is typically split (e.g., ~₹6.40 Lakhs in the first year and ~₹6.35 Lakhs in the second year).
* **Online PGDM Fee:** Highly affordable at approximately ₹1.2 Lakhs.
* **Hostel:** Approximately ₹1.08 Lakhs per year (or ~₹9,500/month).

## 📝 Entrance Exams Accepted
NDIM accepts a wide array of national and state-level management entrance test scores:
* **CAT** (Common Admission Test)
* **XAT** (Xavier Aptitude Test)
* **MAT** (Management Aptitude Test)
* **CMAT** (Common Management Admission Test)
* **ATMA** (AIMS Test for Management Admissions)
* **GMAT** (Graduate Management Admission Test)

## 🎯 Expected Cut-Offs (2025/2026 Admissions)
NDIM employs a composite score matrix for admissions, giving weightage to academic performance, work experience, GD/PI performance, and entrance scores.
* **CAT:** ~70+ Percentile
* **XAT:** ~68-70+ Percentile
* **MAT / CMAT / ATMA:** ~70-75+ Percentile

## 💼 Placement Records & Recruiters (Recent Batches)
NDIM boasts a very active corporate resource center and frequent 100% placement records for participating students.

### Salary Statistics (2024/2025 Data)
* **Highest Package:** ~₹24.00 LPA 
* **Average Package:** consistently between ₹9.50 LPA and ₹10.00 LPA.
* **Median Package:** ~₹9.50 LPA (NIRF 2025 data).
* **Lowest / Base Package:** Starting roles hover around ₹4.00 LPA to ₹5.00 LPA.

### Top Recruiters
NDIM has massive corporate linkage, bringing over 250+ companies to campus, including:
* **Consulting & Audit:** Deloitte, EY, PwC, KPMG.
* **Tech & IT:** Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Capgemini, TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Tech Mahindra.
* **BFSI:** ICICI Bank, HDFC, Bajaj.
* **FMCG & Telecom:** HUL, Colgate, Bisleri, Reliance Jio, Airtel.

## 🗣️ Student Reviews & Verdict
Here is a balanced look at what current students and alumni generally share about the NDIM experience:

### What Students Love:
* **Dual Specialization:** The ability to major in two distinct fields (e.g., Marketing + Business Analytics) makes students highly versatile in job interviews.
* **Industry Interface:** NDIM is deeply praised for bringing in leading industry executives for guest lectures and mentoring.
* **Placements:** A vast array of top-tier brands visit the campus, ensuring strong job prospects.
* **Location Factor:** The Delhi location provides unmatched access to corporate headquarters and start-up hubs.

### Things to Consider:
* **ROI Factor:** While the average package is solid (~₹10 LPA), the ~₹13 Lakhs fee means the initial Return on Investment is moderate for those who secure base packages.
* **Infrastructure:** Being a city-based campus, space can feel constrained compared to massive university plots.

**Final Verdict:** If you are seeking vast corporate exposure in Delhi, practical mentoring by industry leaders, and the flexibility of a dual specialization, NDIM is an incredibly strong, AICTE-approved alternative to traditional university MBAs.
