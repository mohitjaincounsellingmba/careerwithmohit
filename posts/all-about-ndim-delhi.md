### **College Review: NDIM Delhi**
*   **Industry Recognition**: NDIM is widely recognized as one of the best private B-schools in India, consistently ranked among the top by NIRF and Business India.
*   **Brand Value**: Known for its 100% placement legacy and strong corporate connections.
*   **Location Advantage**: Situated in Tughlakabad, it offers great access to the South Delhi corporate hub.

### **Placement Review (2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Successfully maintained a 100% placement record for the 2024-26 batch starting as early as September.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹23.80 LPA** (Domestic).
*   **Median Package**: **₹9.5 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, BlackRock, Wipro, and Colgate Palmolive.
*   **Sector Highlights**: Marketing and Finance remain the strongest hiring domains.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Digital Classrooms**: Spacious, air-conditioned rooms equipped with high-end audio-visual aids.
*   **Library**: A vast repository of physical and digital journals, case studies, and research papers.
*   **Campus Life**: Green surroundings with modern auditoriums and clean, dedicated indoor sports zones.
*   **Hostel**: Reliable hostel facilities available with good security and amenities.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Expert Insight**: Highly experienced faculty with a mix of academic rigor and industry exposure.
*   **Supportive Environment**: Teachers are noted for being approachable and helping with live research projects.
*   **Case-Based Learning**: The curriculum emphasizes real-world role-plays and industry-oriented assignments.

**Final Verdict**: NDIM is a powerhouse for Marketing and Finance aspirants seeking a high ROI and a strong corporate network in the heart of Delhi.
