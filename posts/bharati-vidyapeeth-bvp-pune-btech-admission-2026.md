**Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering (BVDU COE)** in Pune is one of the oldest and most respected private engineering colleges in Maharashtra. Known for its affordable fee structure (compared to other premium private universities) and high-quality faculty, Bharati Vidyapeeth remains a top choice for students looking for a solid ROI in Pune.

If you are an engineering aspirant looking at **Bharati Vidyapeeth B.Tech 2026 admission**, here is the complete breakdown of the process, cutoffs, and fees.

### **Bharati Vidyapeeth Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **BVP CET (Entrance Test)**: The primary route for all B.Tech seats across India.
2.  **JEE Main / MHT-CET**: Used for seat allocation in specific quotas and female-only colleges.
3.  **Institutional Quota (15% Seats)**: Merit-based direct admission for eligible candidates.

### **BVP CET 2026: Important Exam Dates**
The BVP CET is an All-India computer-based test conducted by the university.
*   **Application Deadline**: Expected in May 2026.
*   **Exam Dates**: Expected in June 2026.
*   **Counselling**: Begins in July 2026.

### **Expected Cutoffs 2026 (General Merit)**
The competition for Computer Science (CSE) and Information Technology at BVP Pune is intense.

| Branch | BVP CET Rank (Expected) | MHT-CET Percentile (Expected) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computer Science (CSE)** | 1 - 800 | 80.0 - 85.0 |
| **Information Tech (IT)** | 800 - 1,500 | 78.0 - 82.0 |
| **Electronics (ECE)** | 1,500 - 3,500 | 75.0 - 80.0 |
| **Mechanical Engineering** | 4,000 - 6,000 | 65.0 - 75.0 |

### **Bharati Vidyapeeth B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
BVUCOE is one of the more economically friendly options among Pune's premium private institutions.

| Fee Component | Approx. Annual Amount |
| :--- | :--- |
| **CSE Tuition Fee** | ₹1,45,000 - ₹1,65,000 |
| **Other Engineering Branches** | ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,40,000 |
| **Total 4-Year Cost (Approx.)** | **₹6.5 Lakhs - ₹8.5 Lakhs** |

*Hostel fees at Bharati Vidyapeeth range from ₹90,000 to ₹1.1 Lakhs per year.*

### **Placement Review: Consistent & Reliable**
Bharati Vidyapeeth has a dedicated placement cell that ensures high placement rates for all its circuit branch graduates.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹28.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹5.15 LPA to ₹6.5 LPA.
*   **Median Salary (BE/BTech)**: ₹7.2 Lakhs.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, Emirates, HSBC, TCS, Capgemini, Accenture, and Cognizant.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 85% for eligible graduates.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Highly affordable fees, strong faculty, well-maintained labs, situated in a safe and vibrant part of Pune (Katra-Kothrud area).
**Cons**: High emphasis on attendance and academic discipline, smaller campus compared to universities like MIT-WPU.
