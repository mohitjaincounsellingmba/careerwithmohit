The **Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi** is consistently ranked as the #2 engineering institute in India (NIRF 2025). Located in the heart of South Delhi (Hauz Khas), it is the ultimate dream for millions of JEE aspirants. Known for its rigorous academics, world-class research, and a billionaire-producing alumni network, IIT Delhi is the pinnacle of engineering education in India.

### **IIT Delhi Admission Process 2026**
1.  **JEE Main**: First, you must rank in the top 2.5 Lakh candidates in **JEE Main 2026**.
2.  **JEE Advanced**: You must appear for **JEE Advanced 2026** and secure a top All India Rank (AIR).
3.  **JoSAA Counselling**: Seats are allotted purely based on your JEE Advanced AIR during the JoSAA rounds.
4.  **Reporting**: Physical reporting and fee payment at the Hauz Khas campus.

### **IIT Delhi B.Tech Fees 2026**
IIT Delhi offers a premium education at a government-subsidized rate.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹2.0 Lakhs (General/OBC).
*   **Total 4-Year Cost**: **₹8.5 Lakhs - ₹9.5 Lakhs** (including hostel).
*   **Fee Waiver**: 100% Tuition Fee waiver for SC/ST/PwD. Full or partial waivers for students with annual family income below ₹5 Lakhs.

### **Placement Review: Global Opportunities**
IIT Delhi placements are legendary, often featuring the highest international packages in India.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026**: ₹2.0 Crore+ (International) / ₹1.0 Crore (Domestic).
*   **Average Package (UG)**: ₹21.90 LPA.
*   **Median Salary**: ₹20.00 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Google, Microsoft, Oracle, BCG, Apple, American Express, and top PSUs like IOCL and BPCL.
*   **Startup Culture**: IIT Delhi has produced more unicorn founders than any other institute in India.

### **Review & Campus Life**
**Pros**: Global brand recognition, arguably the best alumni network in Asia, vibrant fests (Rendezvous & Tryst), and prime location in South Delhi.
**Cons**: Extremely high academic pressure (competition with the brightest minds), some older hostel infrastructure.
