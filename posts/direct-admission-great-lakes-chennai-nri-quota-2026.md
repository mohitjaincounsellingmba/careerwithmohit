Great Lakes Institute of Management (GLIM) Chennai is a top-tier B-school famous for its "Global Mindset with an Indian Heart." Led by the legacy of Dr. Bala V. Balachandran, it is a premier destination for both freshers (PGDM) and experienced professionals (PGPM). For the 2026-28 batch, **Direct Admission in Great Lakes Chennai** is achievable through the NRI and profile-category seats.

## Why Great Lakes Chennai is a Tier-1 Destination?

- **Global Outlook**: The institute focuses on data science, machine learning, and emerging tech.
- **Top Faculty**: A mix of international visiting professors and industry experts.
- **Placements**: The average package for the 2025 batch was approximately **₹15.1 LPA** for PGDM and **₹17.3 LPA** for PGPM.

## Understanding Direct Admission at Great Lakes Chennai

Great Lakes maintains a strictly merit-based admission portal, but here is how the specific quotas function for 2026:

### 1. NRI / OCI / PIO Quota
For candidates living abroad or with NRI parents, Great Lakes offers a dedicated admission route. 
- **Entrance**: A valid GMAT or GRE score is typically preferred for this category.
- **Profile Weights**: Heavily focused on your global exposure and academic background.

### 2. Profile-Based Institutional Entry
While Great Lakes officially denies a "management quota" for local students, they follow a comprehensive **Score Card system** where:
- **CAT/XAT/GMAT (90+ preferred)** is one component.
- **Past Academics, Diversity, and Work-Ex** form the other major part. If your profile is stellar, you can get a direct shortlist in early cycles even with a slightly lower test score.

### 3. Industry Sponsored Category
Corporate professionals sponsored by their companies for the 1-year PGPM program have a faster, more direct selection process.

## Fee Structure for 2026
The total academic fees for the two-year PGDM at Great Lakes Chennai is approximately **₹21 Lakhs to ₹23 Lakhs**. For the NRI category, the fees are usually USD-denominated.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is there a management quota in Great Lakes Chennai?
Great Lakes does not have a traditional donation-based management quota. Admission is through merit cycles using CAT, XAT, GMAT, or CMAT scores.

### 2. How can I get direct admission in GLIM Chennai?
The most direct routes are the **NRI quota** (via GMAT) or the **Industry Sponsored seats** for experienced professionals.

### 3. What is the average package for Great Lakes Chennai?
The latest average package is around **₹15.1 LPA** for freshers (PGDM).

### 4. Which entrance exams are accepted for 2026?
They accept **CAT 2025, XAT 2026, GMAT, and CMAT 2026**.
