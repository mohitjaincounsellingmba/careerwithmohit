## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is there a Management Quota in Great Lakes Chennai?
No. Great Lakes follows a strict merit-based policy. However, candidates with exceptional profiles (strong work-ex, international exposure, or high academics) may receive a direct shortlist in early admission cycles.

### 2. Can I apply to Great Lakes Chennai with a GMAT score?
Yes, Great Lakes highly values GMAT scores for both its 1-year PGPM (for professionals with 2+ years exp) and 2-year PGDM (for freshers/early professionals).

### 3. What is the difference between PGPM and PGDM?
PGPM is a fast-track **1-year MBA equivalent** for those with at least 24 months of work experience. PGDM is a traditional **2-year program** designed for fresh graduates and those with less than 2 years of experience.
