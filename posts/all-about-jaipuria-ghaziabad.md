### **College Review: Jaipuria Institute, Ghaziabad**
*   **Prime Location**: Situated in Indirapuram, Ghaziabad, offering excellent connectivity to Delhi, Noida, and the wider NCR.
*   **Trusted Brand**: Part of the nationally acclaimed Jaipuria Group, known for high academic and moral standards.
*   **Corporate Readiness**: One of the most preferred destinations in Ghaziabad for students seeking professional grooming.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Successfully maintains a high placement record for its PGDM students.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹12 - 18 LPA** (Domestic).
*   **Average Package**: **₹5 - 7 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Deloitte, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Amazon, and various FMCG leaders.
*   **Role Diversity**: known for placing students in diverse roles like Digital Marketing, Supply Chain, and Finance Analytics.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Modern Block**: Features fully air-conditioned smart classrooms and seminar halls.
*   **Resources**: A well-stocked library and high-speed Wi-Fi across the management buildings.
*   **Tech Integrated**: Computer labs equipped with the latest software used in the management industry.
*   **Amenities**: Includes decent indoor sports facilities, a cafeteria, and a professional auditorium for events.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Experienced Staff**: Faculty members with a mix of academic research and corporate tenures.
*   **Supportive Culture**: Known for an approachable faculty that helps students with industry research and live projects.
*   **Case-Study Focus**: Heavy emphasis on delivering concepts through real-world business scenarios and simulations.

**Final Verdict**: Best for students looking for a reputable brand name and reliable placement support in the Ghaziabad region.
