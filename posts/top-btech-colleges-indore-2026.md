## Tier 1 — IIT Indore & Government Institutions

### 1. IIT Indore
IIT Indore has established itself as one of India's promising new IITs with a modern campus, strong research programmes, and rapidly improving placement records.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, EE, ME, Civil, Mathematics & Computing, Physics, Astronomy
- **Admission:** JEE Advanced → JoSAA
- **JEE Advanced CSE Cutoff (General 2025):** ~6,500 CRL
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.5 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹14–20 LPA | Highest: ₹1 Cr+ (international)
- **Top Recruiters:** Amazon, Microsoft, DE Shaw, Qualcomm, Goldman Sachs

### 2. IET DAVV Indore (Institute of Engineering & Technology, DAVV)
IET DAVV is Indore's most prestigious state government engineering college, part of Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya. Known for excellent academic quality and affordable fees.

- **Courses:** CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil
- **Admission:** JEE Main merit + RYCET (MP counselling)
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.0 Lakh | **Avg Package:** ₹5–9 LPA

### 3. SGSITS Indore (Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science)
One of Indore's oldest autonomous engineering institutions with strong alumni presence in industry and government.

- **Admission:** RYCET | **Fee:** ₹90,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Indore 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Indore?**
IIT Indore is the top engineering college. Among government colleges, IET DAVV is the most prestigious state institution. For private colleges, Acropolis and Medicaps are well-regarded.

**Q2. What JEE Advanced rank is needed for IIT Indore CSE?**
A General category JEE Advanced rank of approximately 6,500 CRL is needed for CSE at IIT Indore.

**Q3. Can I get B.Tech in Indore without JEE?**
Yes. All private colleges and SGSITS accept students through RYCET (based on 12th marks) or direct admission without requiring JEE.

**Q4. Is IET DAVV Indore good for placements?**
Yes. IET DAVV has a strong alumni network and consistent placements in IT companies. Being a government autonomous college, it offers excellent value for fees paid.

**Q5. What is the fee for B.Tech at IIT Indore?**
IIT Indore charges approximately ₹2.5 Lakhs per year, identical to all other IITs under central government funding.
