## 💡 How Management Quota Works in Mumbai?

Management quota seats are reserved by the institute for direct admissions. These seats follow their own merit-cum-interview process and often have a different fee structure. 

### Why Opt for Direct Admission?
- **Guaranteed Placement**: Access the same premium recruiters as regular merit students.
- **Save a Year**: Don't wait for another attempt if your scores were borderline.
- **Profile-Based Selection**: Colleges value your work experience and academic background over a single 3-hour exam score.

> **Warning:** Always verify the legality and AICTE status of the management quota seats before paying any fee. Only work with verified admission partners.
