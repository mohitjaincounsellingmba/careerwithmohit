Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Research and Entrepreneurship Education (SIMSREE), Mumbai, is legendary for offering the best ROI in India after FMS Delhi. For the 2026 admissions cycle, students often ask about **Direct Admission or Management Quota** at SIMSREE. As a government institute, SIMSREE has a unique, merit-focused approach to institutional seats.

## Why SIMSREE is Exceptional?

- **Lowest Fees**: The total fees for the MMS program is around **₹1.36 Lakhs** for two years!
- **High Placements**: Average CTC for the 2024 batch reached **₹15.19 LPA**, with top recruiters like Barclays, JP Morgan, and Godrej.
- **Mumbai Advantage**: Located at Churchgate, just like JBIMS, it offers unparalleled corporate interaction.

## Is there Direct Admission at SIMSREE?

It is important to understand the hierarchy of seats to avoid misinformation:

### 1. Management Quota (Donation-Based)
**Does not exist.** SIMSREE is a government institute under the Maharashtra State Government. It does not accept donations or offer "backdoor" entry.

### 2. Institute-Level Seats (Against-CAP Rounds)
After the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) rounds are completed, any vacant seats are filled through a "Direct Institutional Round." 
- **The Catch**: You still need to be in the merit list. These seats go to students with high percentiles (typically 99.5+ for SIMSREE) who didn't get allocated in CAP rounds.

### 3. NRI / OCI / PIO Quota
SIMSREE reserves seats for NRI candidates. These are profile-based but still require a valid entrance score. This is the only "Direct" path for students outside the regular merit pool.

## MMS vs PGDM at SIMSREE (2026)

- **MMS (Master of Management Studies)**: Affiliated with Mumbai University. Most competitive.
- **PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management)**: Autonomous program. Often has a slightly different merit structure but maintains the same placement pool.

## Fee Structure for 2026
Regular fees for open category: **₹68,000 per year** (Total ₹1.36L). 
NRI/Special category fees are higher as per government directives.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Can I get direct admission in SIMSREE through management quota?
No, SIMSREE does not have a management quota. Admission is 100% merit-based via the CAP rounds.

### 2. What CET score is needed for SIMSREE?
Usually, a percentile of **99.93+** is required for the General Home University category to secure an MMS seat.

### 3. Does SIMSREE accept CMAT or CAT?
Yes, SIMSREE accepts CAT and CMAT scores for the All India (AI) Quota seats.

### 4. What is the average package for SIMSREE Mumbai?
The current average package is approximately **₹15.19 LPA**.
