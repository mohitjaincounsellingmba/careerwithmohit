Choosing the right MBA specialization is one of the most important decisions of your career. The companies that hire you, the roles they offer, and the salary they pay — all depend on your specialization.

In this guide, we break down the **top recruiters, average packages, and key skills required** for every major MBA/PGDM specialization in 2025-26.

---

## 💰 1. Finance

**Average Package**: ₹10 – ₹28 LPA (IIMs/FMS go up to ₹1 Crore+)

### Top Recruiters
- **Investment Banking**: Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley, Citibank, Barclays
- **Consulting**: McKinsey, BCG, Bain (CFO tracks), Deloitte, KPMG, EY, PwC
- **BFSI**: HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Kotak Mahindra, Axis Bank, SBI Capital
- **Asset Management**: HDFC AMC, SBI Mutual Fund, Nippon AMC, Mirae Asset
- **FinTech**: Razorpay, PhonePe, Zerodha, Groww, Paytm, BharatPe
- **NBFCs**: Bajaj Finance, Muthoot Finance, Shriram Finance
- **Insurance**: LIC, HDFC Life, ICICI Prudential, SBI Life

### Skills Required
- **Financial Modelling & Valuation** (DCF, LBO, M&A)
- **Excel & Power BI** (Advanced)
- **CFA / FRM** (Additional certification — highly valued)
- **Financial Statement Analysis**
- **Risk Management & Derivatives**
- **Corporate Finance & Treasury**
- **Bloomberg / Reuters Terminal** (for IB roles)
