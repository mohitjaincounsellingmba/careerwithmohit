The **National Testing Agency (NTA)** has officially declared the **CUET PG 2026 Results today, April 24, 2026**. Candidates who appeared for the Common University Entrance Test (Postgraduate) can now download their scorecards from the official website.

This year, the exam was conducted for **157 subjects** in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode across various centers in India and abroad. The results will now be shared with the participating universities for their respective counseling and admission processes.

### **How to Check CUET PG 2026 Result Online?**
Follow these simple steps to download your official NTA scorecard:

1.  **Visit Official Portal**: Go to [exams.nta.nic.in/cuet-pg/](https://exams.nta.nic.in/cuet-pg/).
2.  **Click Result Link**: Find and click on the "CUET PG 2026 Scorecard" link on the homepage.
3.  **Enter Login Credentials**: Enter your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth** (or Password).
4.  **Submit Details**: Enter the Security Pin and click "Submit".
5.  **Download Scorecard**: Your CUET PG result will be displayed. Download the PDF and take a printout for the counseling process.

### **What After CUET PG 2026 Result?**
The NTA's role ends with the declaration of results. The next steps involve:
*   **University-wise Cutoffs**: Participating universities like DU, BHU, JNU, and TISS will release their own cutoff lists.
*   **Counseling Registration**: You must register separately on the admission portals of your preferred universities.
*   **Document Verification**: Ensure you have your CUET PG scorecard, admit card, and academic certificates ready.
