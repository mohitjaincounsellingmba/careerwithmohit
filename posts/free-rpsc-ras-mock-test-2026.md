## The Rajasthan Special Component — Your Biggest Differentiator

The **Rajasthan Arts, History & Economy** section (50 questions, 50 marks) is unique to RPSC RAS and is what separates serious Rajasthan-specific aspirants from those who only prepare standard UPSC GS material.

### Rajasthan History (15–18 Q)
- **Ancient:** Pre-historic sites (Kalibangan, Ahar), Jainism in Rajasthan, Gurjara-Pratiharas
- **Medieval:** Rajputana kingdoms, major battles (Haldighati 1576, Khanua 1527, Samel 1544), Akbar-Prithviraj Chauhan relationship, Swatantryaveer Savarkar's Rajasthan connection
- **Modern:** 1857 revolt in Rajasthan, Bijolia Peasant Movement, Arjunlal Sethi, Kesorilal Bapoora
- **Integration of Rajputana States into India (1947–1950)**

### Rajasthan Economy (12–15 Q)
- **Agriculture:** Bajra, Jowar, Guar; Indira Gandhi Nahar Pariyojana; Irrigation projects
- **Industry:** Cement (Rajasthan = India's largest producer), Textile, Mining (Zinc at Rampura Agucha; Silver at Zawar; Marble at Makrana)
- **Tourism:** Heritage palaces, Forts, Desert Tourism; Rajasthan Tourism's tagline
- **MSME and Economic Development:** RUDA, District Industries Centers

### Rajasthan Arts & Culture (12–15 Q)
- **Folk Music:** Maand (Gavari Devi), Baul, Langha-Mangniyar tradition
- **Folk Dances:** Ghoomar, Kalbelia (UNESCO Intangible Heritage), Chari, Kathputli, Terah Taali
- **Literature:** Veli Krisan Rukamni ri (Prithviraj Rathore), Rajasthani language recognition movement
- **Festivals:** Teej, Gangaur, Pushkar Fair (world's largest camel fair), Urs at Ajmer Sharif
- **Painting Schools:** Kishangarh School (Bani Thani), Jaipur School, Bundi-Kota School, Mewar School

## Section-Wise Strategy for RPSC RAS Prelims

- **Rajasthan Special (50 Q):** This is 1/3 of the paper. A Rajasthan aspirant must target 40+ marks here. NCERT is insufficient — use RPSC-specific Rajasthan GK books.
- **Indian History & Polity (50 Q):** Standard UPSC preparation material — Laxmikanth for Polity, Bipin Chandra + Spectrum for Modern History.
- **Mental Ability (20 Q):** Standard reasoning (analogies, syllogism, series) and basic arithmetic. Quick marks if practiced — target 16/20.
- **Science & Current Affairs (30 Q):** 6 months of national current affairs + basic science (NCERT Class 8–10). Target 22+.

## Pro Tips for RPSC RAS 2026

- **Rajasthan GK Books:** Use local Rajasthan publishers' GK books (LDC material, Rajasthan GK by Lucent special edition) alongside standard IAS prep.
- **Rajasthan Government Portal:** Rajasthan.gov.in regularly publishes cultural and development news — a direct source for exam questions.
- **Maintain a Rajasthan Current Affairs Notebook:** Note every important Rajasthan event — inaugurations of development projects, new policies by the state government, honours to Rajasthani personalities.
