Ghaziabad, part of the National Capital Region (NCR), is a major industrial and educational hub. With its proximity to Delhi, it offers numerous opportunities for those appearing for the **Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG 2026**.

If you are a student targeting **Ghaziabad for the 2026-27 session**, here are the top institutions you should consider using your CUET scores.

---

### **1. Mewar University**
A private university with its registered office in Ghaziabad and campus in Chittorgarh, accepting CUET scores.
*   **Programs:** B.Tech, BBA, B.Sc, B.Com.
*   **USP:** Multi-disciplinary education and research focus.

### **2. IMS Ghaziabad (University Courses Campus)**
A premier institute in Ghaziabad, known for its undergraduate management and technology programs.
*   **Programs:** BBA, BCA, B.Sc (Hons) Biotechnology, B.Sc (Hons) Microbiology.
*   **USP:** Solid placement record and corporate interfaces.

### **3. KIET Group of Institutions (Specific Programs)**
A top-tier technical institute that considers CUET scores for specific multidisciplinary tracks.
*   **Programs:** B.Pharm, BBA, BCA.
*   **USP:** High placement rates and excellent infrastructure.

### **4. IPEM Group of Institutions**
Offers a wide variety of management and computer application courses.
*   **Programs:** BBA, BCA, B.Com, B.Ed.
*   **USP:** Focus on professional ethics and practical skills.

### **5. SRM University (NCR Campus, Modinagar/Ghaziabad)**
Part of the SRM family, accepting CUET for various liberal arts and professional courses.
*   **Programs:** BBA, B.Com, BA, B.Sc.
*   **USP:** World-class curriculum and great placement facilities.

### **Why Ghaziabad for Your Undergraduate Degree?**
*   **Proximity to Delhi:** Benefit from the NCR advantage without the high living costs of New Delhi.
*   **Industrial Belt:** Ghaziabad and neighboring areas are heavy on manufacturing and IT industries.
*   **Academic Cluster:** Access to a large network of professional and engineering colleges.
