## 🎓 Eligibility Criteria for NIMCET 2026

Before applying, ensure you meet the following baseline requirements:
1. **Academic Qualification:** Candidates must have a B.Sc. / B.Sc. (Hons) / BCA / BIT of minimum 3 years full-time with Mathematics/Statistics as one of the subjects **OR** B.E./B.Tech. from a recognized university.
2. **Minimum Marks:** At least **60% marks** or **6.5 CGPA** on a 10-point scale for General/OBC candidates (55% or 6.0 CGPA for SC/ST/PwD).
3. **Nationality:** Must be an Indian Citizen.

## 📝 How to Apply for NIMCET 2026: Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to submit a flawless application:
1. **Registration:** Visit the official website [nimcet.admissions.nic.in](https://nimcet.admissions.nic.in) and create a login ID.
2. **Fill Information:** Enter personal details, academic scores, and choice of test centers (you can select up to 3).
3. **Upload Documents:** You will need a recent passport-size photograph, signature, and category certificates (if applicable).
4. **Fee Payment:** Pay the application fee (₹2,500 for Open/OBC and ₹1,250 for SC/ST/PwD) through online mode.
5. **Print Confirmation:** Download and print the application form for future reference during counseling.

## 🚀 Last Minute Preparation Tips

With the exam scheduled for **June 6, 2026**, you have roughly six weeks left:
- **Focus on Mathematics:** 50 out of 120 questions are from Math. This section has the highest weightage (600 out of 1000 marks).
- **Master Logical Reasoning:** 40 questions test your analytical skills. Practice previous year NIMCET papers to understand the logic patterns.
- **Computer Awareness:** Don't ignore the 10 questions on basic computer fundamentals and binary arithmetic—these are easy marks!
- **Mock Tests:** Take at least two full-length mock tests per week to build endurance for the 2-hour window.
