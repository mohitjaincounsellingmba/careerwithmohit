**Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology (MSIT)** is a top-ranked engineering college in Delhi, affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). Located in Janakpuri (West Delhi), MSIT is known for its academic discipline, high faculty standards, and excellent connectivity to the Delhi Metro.

If you are aiming for **MSIT B.Tech 2026 admissions**, here is your guide to the cutoffs, fees, and campus placements.

### **MSIT Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **JEE Main Air (85% Seats for Delhi)**: Primary admission route through GGSIPU online counselling. 
2.  **JEE Main AIR (15% Seats for All India)**: For candidates outside Delhi.
3.  **Management Quota**: 10% seats filled directly based on 10+2 marks and JEE Main participation.

### **Expected JEE Main Cutoffs 2026 (Closing Ranks)**
MSIT usually follows closely behind MAIT in the preference list of IPU aspirants.

| Branch | Delhi Region (Home State) | All India (Outside Delhi) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computer Science (CSE)** | 40,000 - 60,000 | 25,000 - 35,000 |
| **Information Tech (IT)** | 55,000 - 75,000 | 35,000 - 45,000 |
| **Electronics (ECE)** | 85,000 - 1,15,000 | 60,000 - 75,000 |
| **Electrical (EEE)** | 1,20,000 - 2,20,000 | 90,000 - 1,10,000 |

### **MSIT Fee Structure 2026**
MSIT is one of the most affordable private engineering options in the NCR.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹1.15 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Cost**: **₹5.6 Lakhs - ₹6.5 Lakhs**.
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹65,000 to ₹80,000 per year (non-AC).

### **Placement Review: Consistent & Strong**
MSIT has a dedicated placement cell that ensures students from CSE and IT branches are placed in reputed tech giants early in their final year.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹50.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹7.5 LPA to ₹12.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Google, Adobe, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, and Deloitte.
*   **Placement Rate**: approx. 85% for circuit branches.

### **MSIT vs. MAIT: The Verdict**
*   **Connectivity**: MSIT (Janakpuri) wins due to its location on the Blue Line Metro.
*   **Academics**: MSIT is considered stricter with attendance and internal marking.
*   **Campus Life**: MAIT (Rohini) has a slightly larger campus and more student societies.
