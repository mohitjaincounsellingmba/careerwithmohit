## 📝 Eligibility & Admission Process

Securing a "Direct Admission" seat usually involves a streamlined process:

*   **Academic Eligibility**: Minimum 50% marks in Graduation (any stream).
*   **Entrance Exams**: While CAT/CMAT scores are preferred, many colleges accept students based on their own entrance tests (like JET, LPUNEST) or via **Management Quota**.
*   **Personal Interview (PI)**: The most critical stage. Colleges assess your communication skills and interest in the digital domain.
*   **Application**: You must apply through the college's official portal or a verified admission partner.
