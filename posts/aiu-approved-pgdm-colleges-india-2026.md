Many students are often confused between MBA and PGDM. While both are postgraduate management courses, a PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management) is only considered equivalent to an MBA if it is **AIU (Association of Indian Universities)** approved. 

This equivalence is crucial for students who wish to pursue higher education abroad (like a PhD) or apply for government jobs in India. In this guide, we list the top AIU-approved PGDM colleges across major Indian cities with their 2025-26 placement and fee stats.

## Conclusion: Why Should You Care About AIU Approval?

If you plan to settle for a corporate job in India, AICTE approval is enough. However, if you have aspirations for:
1.  **Ph.D. or Higher Education:** Most universities require an MBA-equivalent certificate from AIU.
2.  **Public Sector (PSU) Jobs:** Many government agencies require an MBA degree specifically.
3.  **Global Recognition:** AIU approval makes your credentials more portable across international borders.
