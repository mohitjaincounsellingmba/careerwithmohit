Many students are often confused between MBA and PGDM. While both are postgraduate management courses, a PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management) is only considered equivalent to an MBA if it is **AIU (Association of Indian Universities)** approved. 

This equivalence is crucial for students who wish to pursue higher education abroad (like a PhD) or apply for government jobs in India. In this guide, we have categorized the top AIU-approved PGDM colleges into Tiers to help you make an informed decision.
