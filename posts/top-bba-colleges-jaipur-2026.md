Jaipur, the "Pink City," is rapidly emerging as a significant educational hub in North India. With modern campuses and a growing corporate presence, it offers excellent opportunities for students pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA).

Here is our list of the **Top BBA Colleges in Jaipur for 2026**.

---

## 🏛️ Top BBA Institutes in Jaipur

### 1. Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ)
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹1.5 - 2.0 Lakhs (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹4 - 6 LPA (Highest ₹10 LPA+)
- **Entrance Exam:** MET / Merit-based
- **USP:** Premium campus, global brand, and specialized BBA tracks in Analytics and Marketing.

### 2. Jaipur National University (JNU)
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹70,000 - 90,000 (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹3.5 - 5 LPA
- **Entrance Exam:** JNU Combined Entrance Test
- **USP:** Affordable fees with a strong focus on practical vocational training.

### 3. Amity University Rajasthan, Jaipur
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹1.2 - 1.8 Lakhs (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹4 - 6 LPA
- **Entrance Exam:** Amity Entrance / Interview
- **USP:** Hi-tech infrastructure and strong corporate connectivity with firms like KPMG and EY.

## 🚀 Admission Outlook 2026
Jaipur is an excellent choice for students who want a balanced lifestyle with quality education. Most colleges here accept scores from national-level exams or conduct their own entrance tests. Be sure to check the eligibility criteria for **Maths** which is often required by premium institutes like Manipal.
