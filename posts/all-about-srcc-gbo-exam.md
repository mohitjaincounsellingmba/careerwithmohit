Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), affiliated with Delhi University, is renowned as the top commerce college in Asia. The SRCC GBO (Global Business Operations) program is an exclusive post-graduate diploma strictly equivalent to an MBA in International Business.

If you are looking for an extremely high-ROI management degree (fees under ₹3.2 Lakhs) located on the premier Delhi University North Campus, the SRCC GBO exam is your direct ticket.

Here is the complete overview of the SRCC GBO entrance test for 2026 admissions:

## Exam Overview & Conducting Body

- **Exam Name:** SRCC GBO (Global Business Operations)
- **Conducting Body:** Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), University of Delhi
- **Frequency:** Once a year (Typically in February or March)
- **Mode of Exam:** Computer Based Test (CBT)

## Exam Pattern & Total Marks

The SRCC GBO exam tests your fundamental quantitative and analytical skills under strict time limits.

- **Total Duration:** 120 Minutes (2 Hours)
- **Total Questions:** 160 Questions
- **Total Marks:** 640 Marks
- **Question Types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
- **Marking Scheme:**
  - +4 marks for every correct answer
  - -1 mark for every incorrect answer

## Sectional Breakdown

The 160 questions are distributed evenly across four sections of 40 questions each:

1. **English Comprehension and Language Ability:** 40 Questions (160 Marks)
2. **Quantitative Ability (QA):** 40 Questions (160 Marks)
3. **Logical Ability (LA):** 40 Questions (160 Marks)
4. **Data Interpretation (DI):** 40 Questions (160 Marks)

## Expected Cut-offs (Marks)

Since the GBO program only has 90 highly-coveted seats (distributed across categories), the competition is incredibly fierce. SRCC evaluates purely on Marks, not Percentiles.

- **General Category:** 400 - 425+ Marks (out of 640)
- **OBC Category:** 360 - 380+ Marks
- **SC Category:** 290 - 310+ Marks
- **ST Category:** 200 - 220+ Marks
- *Note: Candidates clearing the CBT cutoff must then face an intense Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) round on the SRCC campus.*

## Top Colleges Accepting SRCC GBO Scores

Unlike CAT or MAT, the SRCC GBO exam is a **single-college entrance examination**. 

- **Valid strictly for:** The Post Graduate Diploma in Global Business Operations (PG Diploma in GBO) exclusively at **Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), New Delhi.**
- **Placements:** The GBO program acts identically to a premier B-School. SRCC guarantees massive corporate involvement owing to its legendary brand name. Average packages hover around ₹12.50 LPA, with top recruiters like American Express, EY, KPMG, and Gartner.
