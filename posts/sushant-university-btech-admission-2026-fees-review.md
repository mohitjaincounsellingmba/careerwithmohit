**Sushant University (formerly Ansal University)** in Gurugram (Sector 55), established in 2012, is a premier multidisciplinary private university known for its excellence in design, architecture, and technology. Accredited with **NAAC Grade 'A'**, Sushant is one of the most respected brands in Gurugram's educational landscape.

### **Sushant University Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Selection Base**: Valid scores in **JEE Main 2026**, **CUET UG**, or the university's own **SUEE** (Sushant University Entrance Exam).
2.  **Merit Route**: Based on Class 12 PCM marks (minimum 50% aggregate).
3.  **Specializations**: B.Tech in CSE (Core), with specialized tracks in AI & ML, Cyber Security, and Digital Manufacturing.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
Sushant is reachable for aspirants with decent Class 12 percentages or JEE ranks.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | AI / Cyber Security |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 70 - 80+ | 75 - 85 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM-English)**| 75% - 85%+ | 80% - 90% |
| **SUEE Score** | 80th Percentile+ | 85th Percentile+ |

### **Sushant University B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
Sushant offers premium facilities at an annual fee comparable to other top-tier Gurugram private universities.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee (CSE Hons)**: approx. ₹2.45 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹9.90 Lakhs - ₹10.5 Lakhs**.
*   **Total with Other Charges**: **₹12.0 Lakhs - ₹13.0 Lakhs**.
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.6 Lakhs to ₹2.2 Lakhs per year (AC mandatory for most rooms).

### **Placement Review: Professional Success**
Sushant University has a dedicated placement cell with strong ties to corporate giants.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹26.50 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹8.20 LPA.
*   **Average Package (Top 10%)**: ₹8.50 LPA+.
*   **Top Recruiters**: KPMG, Adobe, Google, Wipro, Mahindra, Canon, and HCL.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 85% for circuit branches.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Excellent infrastructure, strong focus on design-thinking in engineering, world-class labs, and premium location on Golf Course Road.
**Cons**: Higher fee structure than traditional institutes, smaller engineering intake compared to some of its NCR competitors.
