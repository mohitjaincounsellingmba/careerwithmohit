Delhi NCR is widely considered the legal heart of India. With the Supreme Court, the Delhi High Court, and hundreds of national and international law firms headquartered here, law students in this region have a distinct "internship advantage" that other cities simply cannot match.

Whether you are a high school student aiming for a 5-year integrated degree or a graduate looking for a 3-year LLB, the 2026-27 admission cycle offers several premier paths.

---

## 🏛️ Premier Government Law Institutions

These institutions are the hardest to get into but offer the highest brand value and most affordable fee structures.

| College Name | Popular Program | Entrance Exam | Average Placement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NLU Delhi** | 5-Year BA LLB | **AILET** | ₹14.5+ LPA |
| **Faculty of Law, DU** | 3-Year LLB | **CUET PG** | ₹8.0+ LPA |
| **Jamia Millia Islamia** | 5-Year BA LLB | **JMI Entrance** | ₹6.5+ LPA |
| **USLLS, GGSIPU** | 5-Year Integrated | **CLAT** | ₹7.0+ LPA |

**Note for DU Aspirants:** Admission to the Faculty of Law (North, South, and LC1/LC2) is strictly via **CUET PG 2026**. This is a 3-year program specifically for bridge-builders who already have a graduation degree.

---

## 🎓 Best Private Law Colleges (Corporate Exposure)

Private law schools in NCR have invested heavily in mock courtrooms, legal aid clinics, and global faculty.

### 1. Jindal Global Law School (Sonipat)
Consistently ranked as India's #1 private law school, JGLS offers a curriculum that is at par with international universities. Admission is typically via **LSAT-India** or **CLAT** scores.

### 2. Lloyd Law College (Greater Noida)
Lloyd is a favorite for students who want a rigorous academic environment focused on litigation. Their "Moot Court" culture is one of the strongest in the region, helping students prepare for real-world legal battles.

### 3. Symbiosis Law School (SLS, Noida)
SLS Noida offers the "Symbiosis" brand advantage with a curriculum deeply integrated with corporate law. Located in the heart of Sector 62, it provides easy access to the Noida corporate belt.

### 4. VIPS (IP University)
Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies is the top choice for students appearing for IPU counselling. It is known for its discipline and strong alumni presence in the Delhi High Court.

---

## ⚖️ 5-Year vs. 3-Year: Which is for you?

*   **5-Year Integrated (BA/BBA/B.Com LLB):** Choose this if you have just finished Class 12. It saves one year and is designed for a early career in Law. Target: **CLAT** or **AILET**.
*   **3-Year LLB:** Choose this if you have already completed your graduation (B.A., B.Com, B.Tech, etc.). Target: **CUET PG (for DU/BHU)**.

---

## 📊 Admission Checklist for 2026

1.  **CLAT 2026:** December 2025/Jan 2026. (For NLUs and major private colleges).
2.  **AILET 2026:** Specifically for NLU Delhi.
3.  **CUET PG:** For Faculty of Law, DU (Register early 2026).
4.  **Portfolio:** Many private colleges look for moot court participations or debate certificates during interviews.
