### **College Review: NIET Greater Noida**
*   **Technical Edge**: leverage strong engineering roots to provide a technology-intensive MBA experience.
*   **Industry Partnerships**: Known for labs and centers of excellence built with partners like Microsoft and Amazon.
*   **Placement Focus**: One of the highest volume placement institutes in the NCR region.

### **Placement Review (2025 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: Up to **₹50 LPA** (International/Tech-Integrated roles).
*   **Average Package**: **₹4 - 6 LPA**.
*   **Placement Rate**: Claims a solid **90% placement rate** across its management programs.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, TCS, Wipro, ICICI Bank, and various high-growth startups.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Modern Block**: A dedicated management block with air-conditioned classrooms and interactive smart tech.
*   **Innovation Labs**: Access to world-class research and computing facilities.
*   **Digital Library**: Extensive collection with full digital access for off-campus research.
*   **Residential**: Reliable hostels with Wi-Fi, hygienic food, and 24-hour medical assistance.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Experienced Staff**: Highly qualified teachers who are proactive in updating the curriculum.
*   **Practical Learning**: Heavy focus on case studies, live projects, and industry-synced assignments.
*   **Supportive Environment**: Known for being responsive to student feedback and placement needs.

**Final Verdict**: Best for students who want a tech-forward MBA in a fast-paced environment with high-volume placement opportunities.
