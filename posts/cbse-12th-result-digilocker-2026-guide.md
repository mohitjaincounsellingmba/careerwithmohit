### **The 6-Digit Security PIN: What is it?**
To ensure student data privacy, CBSE provides a unique **6-digit security PIN** to every student. This PIN is mandatory to activate your DigiLocker account for accessing board results.

**How to get the PIN?**
*   The PIN is NOT generated by students.
*   It is provided by the CBSE directly to your **School Principal**.
*   **Action**: Contact your school's administrative office or class teacher to collect your unique security PIN immediately.

---

### **Step-by-Step: How to Activate DigiLocker for CBSE Results**

You should complete the account confirmation process *before* the result day to avoid last-minute technical glitches.

1.  **Visit the Activation Portal**: Go to [cbseservices.digilocker.gov.in/activatecbse](https://cbseservices.digilocker.gov.in/activatecbse).
2.  **Start Confirmation**: Click on the "Get Started with Account Confirmation" button.
3.  **Select Your Class**: Choose **Class XII** from the options.
4.  **Enter Details**: Provide your **School Code**, **Roll Number**, and the **6-digit Security PIN** you received from your school.
5.  **Verify Mobile**: Enter your 10-digit mobile number. You will receive a One-Time Password (OTP).
6.  **Complete Activation**: Enter the OTP and click on "Submit". Your account is now activated!

### **How to Download CBSE 12th Marksheet on Result Day**

Once the [CBSE 12th Result 2026 expected date](/blog/cbse-12th-result-2026-expected-date) arrives and the results are declared, follow these steps:

1.  **Log In**: Open the DigiLocker app or visit [digilocker.gov.in](https://www.digilocker.gov.in).
2.  **Issued Documents**: Go to the "Issued Documents" section on your dashboard.
3.  **Automatic Sync**: If you activated your account using the security PIN, your Class 12 marksheet and certificates will automatically appear here.
4.  **Manual Search (If not visible)**:
    *   Go to the "Education" category.
    *   Search for "Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)".
    *   Select "Class XII Marksheet".
    *   Enter your roll number and the year (2026) to fetch your document.
5.  **Download**: Save the PDF to your device. This digital document is legally valid under the IT Act.
