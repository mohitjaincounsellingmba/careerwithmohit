Bangalore, the "Silicon Valley" of India, is the primary destination for technology and management education. With hundreds of Fortune 500 companies in its backyard, Bangalore offers an unmatched environment for postgraduate studies. With **CUET PG 2026** becoming a standard entrance for management and technical courses, many of its premier private universities have integrated the CUET score into their admission process.

If you are planning to pursue **MBA, MCA, or MSc in Bangalore**, here is your detailed guide to institutions accepting CUET PG 2026 scores.

---

### **1. Alliance University (Bangalore)**
Alliance is one of Bangalore's top-tier private universities for management and law.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA (Marketing/Finance/HR/Business Analytics), MSc.
*   **Admission Process:** They consider various national scores including CUET PG for several of their postgraduate programs.
*   **Why Choose Alliance?** Global recognition and high-quality placement support.

### **2. RV University (Bangalore)**
RV University is highly respected for its technical and professional postgraduate education.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA (Modern History), MSc.
*   **Admission Process:** They consider CUET PG scores as part of their admission criteria for several PG programs.

### **3. CMR University (Bangalore)**
CMR is known for its multi-disciplinary campus and focus on creative and technical PG courses.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA, MSc.
*   **Why Choose CMR?** Strong industry-academic collaboration and vibrant campus life.

### **4. Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) (Bangalore)**
DSU is highly regarded for its management and technical departments in Bangalore.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA.
*   **Admission Process:** They admit students via multiple national-level scores including CUET PG.

### **5. Other Private Universities in Bangalore**
Many growing private universities in the city use CUET PG to diversify their student intake.

*   **Reva University (Bangalore)**
