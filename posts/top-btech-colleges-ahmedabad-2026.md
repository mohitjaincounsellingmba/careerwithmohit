## Tier 1 — IIT & Premier Autonomous Institutions

### 1. IIT Gandhinagar (IITGN)
IIT Gandhinagar is one of India's newer IITs with a distinctive liberal arts + engineering pedagogy. Its interdisciplinary approach and research environment make it a strong choice for students with both technical and humanistic interests.

- **Courses:** CSE, EE, ME, Civil, Chemical, Materials, Cognitive Science, BS-MS
- **Admission:** JEE Advanced → JoSAA
- **JEE Advanced Cutoff (CSE, General 2025):** ~4,500
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.5 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹16–22 LPA | Highest: ₹1 Cr+ (international)

### 2. DAIICT Gandhinagar (Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information & Communication Technology)
DAIICT is Ahmedabad's most prestigious autonomous private institution — funded by the Reliance/Ambani trust. Highly selective, IT-focused, and producing excellent software placements.

- **Courses:** B.Tech IT (specialisations: AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Networks)
- **Admission:** DAIICT own entrance + JEE Main merit
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.2 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹10–18 LPA | Highest: ₹60+ LPA (Goldman Sachs, Nutanix)
- **Top Recruiters:** Qualcomm, Goldman Sachs, Amazon, JP Morgan, Salesforce

### 3. PDEU — Pandit Deendayal Energy University, Gandhinagar
A Gujarat state-funded university specialising in energy and petroleum engineering, PDEU is excellent for students targeting oil & gas and renewable energy careers.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Chemical, Petroleum Engineering, Smart Infrastructure
- **Admission:** ACPC Gujarat Counselling / JEE Main
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.8 Lakhs
- **Avg Package:** ₹7–12 LPA | Industry Connections: ONGC, Reliance, Adani

## ACPC Counselling 2026 — Gujarat B.Tech Admissions

**ACPC (Admission Committee for Professional Courses)** manages B.Tech admissions in Gujarat for government and private engineering colleges.

- **Authority:** Government of Gujarat
- **Eligibility:** 10+2 with PCM, minimum 45% aggregate
- **Merit:** Based on JEE Main score (70%) + GUJCET score (30%)
- **ACPC 2026 Rounds:** 3 rounds, starting July 2026

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Ahmedabad 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Ahmedabad?**
DAIICT Gandhinagar is the top autonomous private institution in the Ahmedabad region for IT. IIT Gandhinagar is the top overall. Among government colleges, LD Engineering College is the best state-government option.

**Q2. What is ACPC counselling for Gujarat engineering?**
ACPC is Gujarat's state-level counselling process for B.Tech admissions to government and private engineering colleges. Ranks are based on JEE Main + GUJCET scores.

**Q3. Is DAIICT a government or private college?**
DAIICT is a private autonomous institute funded by the Reliance/Ambani trust. Its fees (~₹2.2 L/yr) are managed and it functions like a government-aided institution in terms of affordable costs.

**Q4. Can I get B.Tech in Ahmedabad without JEE Main?**
Yes. DAIICT has its own entrance test. Private colleges like Silver Oak, Parul, and GLS offer direct admission based on Class 12 marks without requiring JEE.

**Q5. What is the fee for B.Tech at Nirma University Ahmedabad?**
Nirma University charges approximately ₹2.5 Lakhs per year for engineering programmes. Total programme cost is approximately ₹10–11 Lakhs.
