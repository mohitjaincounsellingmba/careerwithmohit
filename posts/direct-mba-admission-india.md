## 🚀 How Direct Admission Works?

Direct admission doesn't mean "anyone can get in." Top colleges still follow a rigorous **profile evaluation** to ensure quality.

### The 3-Step Selection Process:
1. **Profile Screening**: Your 10th, 12th, and Graduation marks are reviewed (minimum 50% usually required).
2. **Personal Interview (PI)**: A discussion to check your communication skills, passion, and career goals.
3. **Seat Confirmation**: Once approved, you pay the admission fee to block your seat under the Management or Institutional Quota.

### Why Choose This Path?
- **Zero Gap Year**: Don't waste a year retaking exams if you already have a good profile.
- **Specialization Choice**: Easier to get into in-demand fields like **Business Analytics** or **FinTech**.
- **Scholarships**: Some colleges offer merit-based scholarships even for direct admission candidates.

> **Key Takeaway:** Focus on your Interview prep. In direct admission, your personality and confidence matter more than your entrance exam percentile.
