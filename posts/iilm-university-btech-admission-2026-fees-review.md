**IILM University**, with its prominent campuses in Greater Noida and Gurugram, is a rapidly growing private university known for its multidisciplinary approach and strong focus on management-integrated engineering education. The Greater Noida campus is particularly well-regarded for its B.Tech programs, functioning as a key hub for tech recruitment in the NCR.

### **IILM University Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **Entrance Route**: Based on scores in **JEE Main 2026** or **CUET UG**.
2.  **Counselling (Greater Noida)**: Participates in the **UPTAC** (Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counselling) process.
3.  **Merit Route**: Based on Class 12 marks (minimum 50% aggregate in PCM).
4.  **Specializations**: B.Tech in CSE (Core), with tracks in AI, Cyber Security, and Data Science.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
IILM is an accessible option for students with decent 10+2 marks or middle-range JEE percentiles.

| Admission Route | CSE (Core) Requirement | Other Core Engineering |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JEE Main Percentile** | 65 - 75+ | 55 - 65 |
| **Class 10+2 (PCM)** | 70% - 80%+ | 60% - 70% |
| **CUET Score** | 75th Percentile+ | 65th Percentile+ |

### **IILM University B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
IILM offers a middle-tier fee structure compared to high-end private universities, providing a strong ROI for budget-conscious students.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹2.25 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Tuition Fees**: **₹9.0 Lakhs - ₹10.0 Lakhs** (for the entire duration).
*   **Registration/Misc**: approx. ₹25,000 (one-time).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹1.2 Lakhs to ₹1.6 Lakhs per year (AC options available).

### **Placement Review: A Tech Destination**
IILM University has a dedicated placement cell that focuses on product-based and service-oriented companies.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹26.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech CSE)**: ₹8.00 LPA+.
*   **Average Package (Overall)**: ₹5.50 LPA to ₹6.5 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Apple, IBM, Larsen & Toubro, Reliance, BlackRock, Wipro, and Amazon.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 90% student for eligible engineering branches.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Global focus with numerous case studies and industry-ready projects, excellent location in Gurugram (proximity to MNCs), and affordable compared to premium private universities.
**Cons**: Smaller campus footprint in Gurugram compared to Greater Noida, relatively high competition for the newer AI tracks.
