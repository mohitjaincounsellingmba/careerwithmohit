The **Common Admission Test (CAT) 2026** is the most prestigious entrance exam for MBA aspirants in India. If you are aiming for the IIMs or other top B-schools like FMS, SPJIMR, or MDI, staying updated with the official timeline is crucial.

Based on historical trends and the academic calendar, the **CAT 2026 Exam Date** is widely expected to be the last Sunday of November.

## 2. CAT 2026 Registration Fee
The registration fee for CAT 2026 is expected to be:
*   **General/NC-OBC Candidates:** ₹2,400 - ₹2,500
*   **SC/ST/PwD Candidates:** ₹1,200 - ₹1,250

*Note: Fees are subject to change as per the official notification.*

---

## 3. Eligibility Criteria for CAT 2026
Before applying, ensure you meet the following requirements:
*   **Graduation:** A Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PwD) from a recognized university.
*   **Final Year Students:** Candidates in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply.
*   **No Age Limit:** There is no age restriction for appearing in the CAT exam.
