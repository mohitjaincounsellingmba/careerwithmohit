The Jaipuria Institute of Management is a highly recognized brand in Northern India, operating four major campuses (Lucknow, Noida, Jaipur, and Indore). Ranked consistently among top private B-Schools by NIRF, Jaipuria is known for its uncompromised academic rigor and centralized placement process.

Here is the comprehensive review focusing primarily on their flagship Lucknow and Noida campuses for 2026:

## Key Highlights

- **Establishment:** 1995 (Lucknow Campus), 2004 (Noida Campus)
- **Program Offered:** PGDM (AICTE Approved, NBA Accredited, AIU Equivalent pattern)
- **Legacy & USP:** Backed by the prominent Seth Anandram Jaipuria Education Society. The biggest USP is its "Un-campus" placement system—placements are centralized across all four campuses, giving every student equal access to top recruiters.

## Admission Details

- **Entrance Exams Accepted:** CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT, GMAT
- **Expected Cut-off (Percentile):**
  - **Lucknow & Noida Campuses:**
  - CAT/XAT: 65 - 75+
  - CMAT/MAT: 80 - 85+ (Admissions are highly competitive and profile-centric)
- **Total Fees:** 
  - Noida Campus: ₹14.50 Lakhs (Approx. for 2 years)
  - Lucknow Campus: ₹13.50 Lakhs (Approx. for 2 years)

## Specializations Offered

- Service Management (Unique to Jaipuria)
- Marketing
- Finance
- Human Resource
- Operations Management
- Business Analytics

## Placement Statistics (2025-2026)

Thanks to the centralized placement drive, Jaipuria manages to pull massive corporate names for mass and niche recruitment.

- **Highest Placement Package:** ₹22.00 LPA (International), ₹18.00 LPA (Domestic)
- **Average Placement Package:** ₹8.95 LPA (Noida), ₹8.50 LPA (Lucknow)
- **Lowest Placement Package:** ₹6.00 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** Deloitte, Amazon, HCL, EY, ICICI Bank, Pantaloons, Tata AIG, Modenik Lifestyle, and MRF.

## Student Reviews & Verdict

**What Students Say:**
- **Pros:** The centralized placement process ensures that no matter which campus you are on, you get a fair shot at the best companies. The faculty is extremely stringent about discipline, attendance, and academics. Good infrastructure across all campuses.
- **Cons:** The strictness can sometimes feel like a school environment. With four campuses combined, the student pool during placements is very large, leading to fierce internal competition.

**Our Verdict:** Jaipuria Institute of Management is a highly credible Tier-2 B-School brand. If you score between 70-85 percentile in your entrance exams and want a disciplined, rigorous academic environment that guarantees strong corporate exposure, both the Noida and Lucknow campuses are excellent choices with great ROI.
