The Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (RBSE) is all set to declare the **RBSE Class 12 Result 2026 for Science, Commerce, and Arts tomorrow, March 31, 2026, at 10:00 AM**. This follows the recent announcement by Rajasthan Education Minister **Madan Dilawar**, who confirmed the results will be available online for students across the state.

Over 9 lakh students appeared for the RBSE Class 12 board examinations held in February. For the first time in recent years, the Rajasthan Board is expected to release the results for all three streams—**Science, Commerce, and Arts**—on the same day.

### **How to Check RBSE 12th Result 2026 Online?**
Once the results are declared tomorrow, follow these steps to download your provisional marksheet:

1.  **Visit Official Portal**: Go to [rajresults.nic.in](http://rajresults.nic.in) or [rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in](http://rajeduboard.rajasthan.gov.in).
2.  **Select Stream**: Choose the link for "Senior Secondary (Science/Commerce/Arts) - 2026 Result".
3.  **Enter Credentials**: Provide your **Roll Number** mentioned on the RBSE admit card.
4.  **Submit**: Click on the 'Submit' or 'View Result' button.
5.  **Save Marksheet**: Your RBSE 12th result will appear. Download the PDF and take a printout for admission purposes.
