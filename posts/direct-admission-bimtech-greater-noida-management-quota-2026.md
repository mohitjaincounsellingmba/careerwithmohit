Birla Institute of Management Technology (BIMTECH), Greater Noida, is a premier AACSB-accredited B-school with a rich legacy of producing industry leaders. Backed by the Birla Group, it offers excellent corporate exposure and research-led education. For the 2026-28 batch, **Direct Admission in BIMTECH Greater Noida** is available through a transparent management quota and NRI category.

## Why BIMTECH is a High-Priority Choice?

- **Premium Accreditation**: One of the few schools in India with the prestigious AACSB accreditation.
- **Top Specializations**: PGDM (Flagship), PGDM-International Business, PGDM-Insurance Business, and PGDM-Retail.
- **Placements**: The average package for the 2025 batch was around **₹11.2 LPA**, with the highest package reaching over **₹22 LPA**.

## Understanding Direct Admission at BIMTECH

BIMTECH provides a dedicated pathway for institutional seats, ensuring that talented aspirants can secure their future even with moderate competitive scores.

### 1. Management / Institutional Quota
BIMTECH reserves a percentage of its seats (approx. 15%) for the management category.
- **Who can apply?**: Any student who meets the basic eligibility (min. 50% in graduation) and has a valid entrance score.
- **Flexibility**: While the general CAT/XAT cutoff is high (around 80 percentile), the management quota allows students with **MAT (600+), CMAT (85+), or ATMA** scores to secure a seat.

### 2. NRI / Corporate Sponsored Seats
Candidates applying under the NRI category or those sponsored by corporate entities follow a profile-based selection process. These are direct entry spots that prioritize the candidate's career goals and background.

### Fee Structure for 2026
The regular academic fee for the flagship PGDM program is approximately **₹14 Lakhs to ₹15 Lakhs**. For management quota or institutional seats, there is usually a higher institutional development fee.

## Selection Strategy for 2026
Even with direct admission, BIMTECH ensures quality through its selection criteria:
1. **Entrance Exam**: Ensure you have a score in CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, or GMAT.
2. **Personal Interview**: BIMTECH values communication skills and a clear understanding of your chosen specialization (Retail, Insurance, etc.).
3. **Diversity Weights**: Being a postgraduate program, extra points are given for female candidates and students from non-engineering backgrounds.
