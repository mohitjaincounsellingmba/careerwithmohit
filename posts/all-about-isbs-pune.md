### **College Review: ISBS Pune**
*   **Established Authority**: Part of the Indira Group, a trusted name in Indian management education for decades.
*   **Pune Advantage**: Located in the educational hub of India with access to IT and Manufacturing clusters.
*   **Holistic Growth**: Mixes academics with personality development programs (PDP).

### **Placement Review (2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently maintains a **90-100% placement record**.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹21 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: **₹6.62 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Deloitte, HDFC, TCS, and various giants from BFSI and FMCG sectors.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Smart Campus**: Air-conditioned classrooms with interactive smart boards.
*   **Tech Stack**: Modern computer labs and campus-wide high-speed Wi-Fi.
*   **Resources**: A well-stocked library that is frequently updated with the latest business literature.
*   **Amenities**: Includes decent hostels, gyms, canteens, and medical support on campus.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Knowledgeable Experts**: Highly qualified faculty members known for being supportive.
*   **Effective Methods**: Uses a blend of traditional teaching and modern industry simulations.
*   **Curriculum Focus**: Industry-aligned syllabus that is updated to meet 2026 market standards.

**Final Verdict**: A reliable choice for students aiming for BFSI or IT management roles with steady growth and a solid reputation in Western India.
