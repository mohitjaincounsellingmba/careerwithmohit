### **ISB&M Nande Pune PGDM Fees 2026**

The PGDM fee at ISB&M Nande is competitive, given its high ROI. For the upcoming 2026-28 batch:
*   **Total Tuition & Course Fee:** **₹14.29 Lakhs** (includes tuition, caution deposit, alumni fees, and placement training).
*   **Payment Details:** The admission confirmation requires a first installment of **₹1,10,000**. The remaining balance is spaced out into convenient installments over the two years.
*   *(Note: Hostel and mess charges are separate and updated yearly based on accommodation type).*

---

### **ISB&M Nande PGDM Admission Process 2026**

The admission process at ISB&M is thorough and aims to identify candidates with high leadership potential. 
1.  **Eligibility:** Minimum 50% aggregate in graduation from a recognized university (45% for reserved categories). Final year students are eligible.
2.  **Valid Entrance Score:** You need a valid score in CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, ATMA, or GMAT.
3.  **Application:** Apply online via the [official website](https://isbm.ac.in/). 

**Selection Criteria Weightage:**
*   Group Discussion & Personal Interview (GD & PI): **40%**
*   Entrance Exam Score: **25%**
*   Academic Performance (10th, 12th, Degree): **25%**
*   Work Experience: **10%**

### **Expected Cutoffs for ISB&M Nande 2026**

ISB&M operates on a profile-based selection. While exceptional interviews can override lower test scores, the general indicative cutoffs for shortlisting are:
*   **CAT / XAT**: ~60 to 65 Percentile
*   **MAT / ATMA**: ~80 Percentile
