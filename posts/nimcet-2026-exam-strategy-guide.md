## 📝 NIMCET 2026 Exam Pattern & Weightage

The NIMCET exam is a 2-hour online test with 120 questions. However, the **weightage** of the sections is the most critical part of your strategy:

| Section | Questions | Marks | Weightage Factor |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mathematics** | 50 | 200 | **3x** (Total 600) |
| **Logical Reasoning** | 40 | 160 | **1.5x** (Total 240) |
| **Computer Awareness** | 10 | 40 | **2x** (Total 80) |
| **General English** | 20 | 80 | **1x** (Total 80) |

**Crucial Insight:** Mathematics alone accounts for **60% of your final rank**. If you are not solving Mathematics at a JEE Main/Advanced level, cracking NIMCET is nearly impossible.

## 🚀 6-Month NIMCET 2026 Preparation Plan

- **Months 1-2: Mathematics Foundation**
    - Focus exclusively on **Calculus, Vectors, and Coordinate Geometry**.
    - Solve NCERT Class 11 and 12 completely, then move to R.D. Sharma (Objective).
- **Months 3-4: Logic & Speed Drills**
    - Master Syllogisms, Blood Relations, and Data Interpretation.
    - Start including 30 minutes of daily Basic Computer Awareness (Binary, Logic Gates).
- **Month 5: Mock Marathon**
    - Take 2 full-length Mock Tests per week.
    - Analyze the "Maths Accuracy"—your goal should be 35+ correct attempts in Maths.
- **Month 6: Revision & Previous Year Papers**
    - Solve papers from 2018 to 2025. NIMCET often repeats logic patterns.
    - Focus on time management (giving 70 minutes to Maths, 30 to Logic, 20 to others).

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is NIMCET harder than CUET PG?**
Yes. **NIMCET** is significantly harder because of its high-level Mathematics (JEE level). CUET PG is more balanced and broadly focused on all computer science subjects.

**Q2. What is a good score for NIT Trichy?**
Usually, a score of **500+ out of 1000** (calculated with weightage factors) is required to be in the Top 50 rankers for NIT Trichy.

**Q3. Are B.Sc. students eligible for NIMCET 2026?**
Yes, provided you have Mathematics or Statistics as one of your subjects in B.Sc. or at the 10+2 level.

**Q4. Can I clear NIMCET in 4 months?**
Yes, if you already have a strong mathematical foundation from your 12th standard. If you are starting from zero, 6-9 months is the recommended timeframe.

**Q5. When should I start solving Mock Tests?**
Ideally, 4 months before the exam. Don't wait to finish the syllabus. Start with "Sectional Mocks" in the second month itself.
