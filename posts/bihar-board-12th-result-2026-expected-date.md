### **How to Check Bihar Board 12th Result 2026?**
Follow these steps to access your scorecard instantly once the link is active:

1.  **Visit Official Portal**: Go to [results.biharboardonline.com](http://results.biharboardonline.com).
2.  **Locate Link**: Click on the 'Annual Intermediate Examination Result 2026' link.
3.  **Enter Logins**: You will need to enter your:
    *   **Roll Code** (as per admit card)
    *   **Roll Number**
4.  **Security Captcha**: Enter the sum of the digits shown on the screen.
5.  **View & Save**: Click on 'View' to see your result. Download the PDF marksheet immediately.
