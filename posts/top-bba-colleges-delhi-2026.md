Delhi, the national capital, is the heart of India's education and commerce. For BBA aspirants, Delhi offers the prestige of central universities and the massive network of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU).

Here are the **Top BBA Colleges in Delhi for 2026**.

---

## 🏛️ Top BBA Institutions in Delhi

### 1. Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies (SSCBS)
- **Rank:** Consistently #1 in India for BBA (India Today / Outlook)
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹15,000 - 25,000 (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹10 - 12 LPA (Highest ₹44 LPA+)
- **Entrance Exam:** CUET (Common University Entrance Test)
- **USP:** The "IIM of Undergraduate Management." Best ROI in India with placements matching top MBA colleges.

### 2. Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Management Studies (MAIMS)
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹1.0 - 1.2 Lakhs (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹4 - 6 LPA
- **Entrance Exam:** GGSIPU CET / CUET
- **USP:** Top-ranked college under IP University known for its discipline and academic results.

### 3. Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI), Delhi
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹13,000 (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹4 - 5 LPA
- **Entrance Exam:** JMI Entrance Exam
- **USP:** Highly prestigious central university with very low fees and great research culture.

## 🚀 Pro-Tip for 2026 Admissions
If you can clear the **CUET** with a high score, **SSCBS** is the best decision you will ever make for your career. For students who want a more diverse range of college options within Delhi, appearing for the **IPU CET** is highly recommended.
