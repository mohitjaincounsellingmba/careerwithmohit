The **Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT)** in Gandhinagar is arguably the most prestigious private engineering institute for Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in India. Backed by the Reliance Group's legacy, it has earned a reputation comparable to the top IIITs and NITs.

If you are an engineering aspirant aiming for the **DA-IICT B.Tech 2026 batch**, here is the complete breakdown of the admission process, cutoffs, and ROI.

### **DA-IICT Admission Process 2026**
DA-IICT does NOT participate in JoSAA. Instead, it follows a multi-channel admission process:
1.  **All India Category (50%)**: Based purely on the **JEE Main 2026** All India Rank (AIR). You must apply directly on the DA-IICT portal.
2.  **Gujarat State Category (35%)**: Based on **GUJCET 2026** scores. Counselling is handled by the **ACPC (Admission Committee for Professional Courses)**, Gujarat.
3.  **NRI / Foreign Category (15%)**: For international students and NRIs.

### **Expected Cutoff 2026 (For B.Tech ICT/CSE)**
The competition for DA-IICT is extremely high, with cutoffs often matching the top 3-4 NITs.

| Category | Entrance Exam | Target Rank / Percentile |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **All India (Gen)** | JEE Main 2026 | **98.5 - 99.2 Percentile** |
| **Gujarat State (Gen)** | GUJCET 2026 | **Opening < 150; Closing < 600** |
| **All India (EWS)** | JEE Main 2026 | **97.8 - 98.2 Percentile** |

### **DA-IICT Fee Structure 2026**
DA-IICT is a premium institute, and its fee structure reflects the high-quality infrastructure and research facilities.

| Fee Component | Amount (Approx.) |
| :--- | :--- |

### **Placement Review: Why it is a ROI King**
Despite the high fees, DA-IICT offers some of the best placement statistics for CSE and ICT branches in India.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026**: ₹82.00 LPA (International) / ₹57.78 LPA (Domestic).
*   **Average Package (UG)**: ₹19.40 LPA to ₹21.0 LPA.
*   **Median Package**: ₹18.00 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Adobe, Morgan Stanley, Qualcomm, and Flipkart.

### **Key Specializations Offered**
DA-IICT is focused on future-ready domains:
*   B.Tech in Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
*   B.Tech (Hons) in ICT with minor in Computational Science
*   B.Tech in Mathematics and Computing (MnC)
