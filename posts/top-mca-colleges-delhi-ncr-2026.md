## 🏛️ Government Powerhouses (Tier 1)

### 1. JNU & Delhi University (DU)
The undisputed ROI kings. While JNU focuses more on **Conceptual Computing and AI**, DU’s Department of Computer Science is legendary for its **Data Science and Algorithm** focus.
- **Admission Strategy:** Prepare exclusively for the **CUET PG 2026**.

### 2. GGSIPU (IP University)
Consisting of USICT (Campus) and top colleges like MSI and JIMS.
- **Important Note:** In 2026, admission is primarily through **NIMCET** scores. CUET PG is used only for vacant seats.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is MCA from Delhi University better than NITs?**
In terms of **Research and Teaching**, DU is fantastic. However, for **Core Software Placement**, Top NITs like Trichy or Surathkal still have a slight edge. At a package level, DU and NIT Delhi are comparable (₹10L+).

**Q2. Does JNU require Maths for MCA?**
Yes. CUET PG (SCQP09) which is used for JNU MCA admission has a significant mathematical component.

**Q3. What is the average starting salary for MCA in Noida?**
For top-tier colleges like KIET or IP University, students usually clear ₹5.5L to ₹9.0L. Management quota seats in private colleges often see packages around ₹4.2L to ₹5.5L.

**Q4. Can I get direct admission in MCA NCR colleges?**
Private universities like Amity and Galgotias offer merit-based direct admission. However, top government and semi-government colleges like MSI and JNU strictly require entrance scores.

**Q5. When is the NIMCET 2026 exam?**
Usually held in **June**. Registrations typically open in February or March of 2026.
