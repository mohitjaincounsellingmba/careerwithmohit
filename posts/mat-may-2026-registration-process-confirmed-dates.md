The **All India Management Association (AIMA)** has officially released the finalized schedule for the **MAT May 2026 session**. For MBA aspirants who missed the earlier sessions or want to improve their scores for the 2026-28 academic batch, this is the most critical window.

With the Paper-Based Test (PBT) and Computer-Based Test (CBT) dates now firm, it is time to complete your registration before the deadlines.

## Step-by-Step Registration Process for MAT May 2026

The application process is entirely online. Follow this 6-step guide to ensure your form is accepted without errors:

### 1. Account Creation
Visit the official AIMA MAT portal at **[mat.aima.in](https://mat.aima.in/)**. Click on the "Register" button and enter your basic details:
- Full Name
- Date of Birth
- Email ID & Mobile Number
- State/City
- Create a Password

You will receive an **OTP** to verify your email and mobile. This step is mandatory to generate your unique login ID.

### 2. Log In & Choose Test Mode
Use your credentials to log in. You must decide whether you want to take a **Single Mode** (PBT or CBT) or **Both Modes** (PBT + CBT). 
> **Tip:** Opting for "Both Modes" allows you to send your scores to **7 B-Schools** for free (instead of 5) and gives you two attempts to maximize your percentile.

### 3. Fill Personal & Academic Details
Fill in your parent's names, category, and address. In the academic section, you will need:
- Class 10 & 12 Marks/Percentage
- Graduation details (Year of passing, Degree, and Percentage)
- Work experience (if any)

### 4. B-School Selection
This is a crucial step. You can select **5 Management Institutes** (or more if you pay extra) to receive your scores directly. Choose colleges that align with your expected percentile. You can refer to our **[Top MBA Colleges Accepting MAT](/blog/top-mba-colleges-mat)** guide for help.

### 5. Upload Documents
Ensure your files are in the prescribed size and format (JPG/JPEG):
- **Photograph:** Recent color passport-size photo (50 KB to 100 KB).
- **Signature:** Scanned signature on white paper (10 KB to 40 KB).

### 6. Payment of Application Fee
Pay the fee using a Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.
- **For One Test Mode:** ₹2,300
- **For Two Test Modes:** ₹4,000

After payment, download the **Confirmation Page**. You will need this for future reference during counseling.
