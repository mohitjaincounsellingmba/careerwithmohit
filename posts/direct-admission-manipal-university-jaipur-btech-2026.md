### **Does Manipal University Jaipur Offer Direct Admission or Management Quota for B.Tech?**

This is the most commonly asked question — and the answer requires clarity:

**No traditional management quota exists at MUJ.** Unlike many state-level private engineering colleges, Manipal University Jaipur does NOT offer seats through:
- Donation-based or capitation fee seats
- Agent-facilitated "guaranteed admission" against payment

> **Beware of Fraud:** MUJ has officially warned students against agents or agencies promising seats in exchange for money. If you receive such offers, report them directly to `admissions@jaipur.manipal.edu`.

**However**, there are two legitimate pathways that allow students with moderate scores or no JEE rank to still secure admission:

1. **MET (Manipal Entrance Test)**: MUJ conducts its own national-level entrance exam. Students who appear in MET and secure a qualifying rank get direct offers from the university.
2. **JEE Main Score**: Students with a valid JEE Main score (any percentile above the cutoff) can apply directly without appearing for MET.
3. **NRI/International Quota**: Students under the NRI category can apply directly through the official portal. This is the closest equivalent to a "direct seat" without a competitive rank, but fees are significantly higher.

### **MUJ B.Tech Admission Process 2026 (Step-by-Step)**

1. **Apply Online**: Visit [jaipur.manipal.edu](https://www.jaipur.manipal.edu) and fill out the application form.
2. **Choose Your Exam**: Opt for MET (Manipal Entrance Test) or submit your JEE Main score.
3. **Appear for MET**: The MET exam is conducted online in multiple slots across India.
4. **Merit List & Seat Allocation**: Based on MET/JEE rank, seats are offered branch-wise.
5. **Counselling & Fee Payment**: Accept the seat offer and pay the initial semester fee to confirm admission.
6. **Document Verification**: Submit original 10th, 12th marksheets, category certificate (if applicable), and migration certificate.

*Note: Candidates under the NRI category skip the entrance exam requirement but must meet the eligibility criteria (Physics + Mathematics + English with 50%+ aggregate in 12th).*

### **Common Questions About Manipal Jaipur BTech Admission**

**Q: Can I get admission in MUJ without JEE Main?**
Yes. You can appear for MET (Manipal Entrance Test), which is MUJ's own entrance exam. It is far less competitive than JEE Main.

**Q: Is there a management quota in Manipal University Jaipur?**
No. MUJ does not have a traditional management quota or donation seat system. All admissions are merit-based via MET or JEE Main.

**Q: What is the minimum percentage required for MUJ BTech?**
You need a minimum of **50% aggregate in Physics, Mathematics, and English** in your 10+2 examination.

**Q: Can NRI students get direct admission in MUJ BTech?**
Yes. NRI category students can apply directly without a MET/JEE score, but fees are significantly higher (₹33–34 Lakhs for the full program).

**Q: What is the last date to apply for MUJ BTech 2026?**
MET 2026 applications are typically open till April–May 2026. Check the [official MUJ website](https://www.jaipur.manipal.edu) for the latest dates.
