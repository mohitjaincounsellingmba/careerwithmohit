## 📊 IPMAT 2026 Exam Pattern (Indore)

IIM Indore's exam is the gold standard and follows a unique three-section format:

| Section | Question Type | Duration | Marking |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Quant (SA)** | Short Answer | 40 Mins | +4 / 0 (No Negative) |
| **Quant (MCQ)** | Multiple Choice| 40 Mins | +4 / -1 |
| **Verbal (MCQ)**| Multiple Choice| 40 Mins | +4 / -1 |

*Note: There is a SECTIONAL CUTOFF. You must score above a certain threshold in all three sections to be called for the interview.*

## 🚀 12-Month Preparation Strategy for IPMAT 2026

### Phase 1: Foundation (6-8 Months)
- **Math:** Revisit Class 6-10 fundamentals. Focus on Arithmetic (Percentages, Profit & Loss, Ratio) and Number Systems. 
- **Verbal:** Start reading one quality editorial (The Hindu/Indian Express) daily to build reading speed and vocabulary.

### Phase 2: Advanced Math (2 Months)
- Unlike Rohtak, **IIM Indore** asks "Higher Math" (Class 11 & 12 topics) like Permutations, Combinations, Probability, and basic Integration. Do not skip these.

### Phase 3: Mock Season (Last 2 Months)
- Give at least 2 full-length mocks per week.
- Focus on **Quantitative Short Answer (SA)** section—it is the biggest hurdle for most students.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. can a commerce student crack IPMAT?**
Yes. In fact, many toppers are from Commerce/Humanities. However, you must put in extra effort to master the **Quantitative Ability** section, which is mathematically intensive.

**Q2. is IPMAT tougher than JEE?**
No. JEE is purely science-based. IPMAT is an **Aptitude Test**. While the Quant section is tough, the logic is different. However, the seat-to-candidate ratio is similar to IITs.

**Q3. What is the safe score for IIM Indore 2026?**
While cutoffs change every year, a safe target is usually **180-200+ marks** out of 400 for a General category student to ensure an interview call.

**Q4. does IIM Ranchi have a separate exam?**
No. IIM Ranchi uses the **IPMAT Indore scores** for its first-round selection.

**Q5. When is IPMAT 2026 scheduled?**
Typically held in **May 2026**, soon after the 12th standard board exams are over.
