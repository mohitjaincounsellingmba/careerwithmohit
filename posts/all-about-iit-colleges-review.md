The Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) are the most prestigious engineering institutions in India. Recognized globally for their rigorous academics, brilliant peer groups, and astronomical placement figures, getting into an older IIT (Bombay, Delhi, Madras, Kanpur, Kharagpur) is a dream for millions of students.

Here is a comprehensive overview of the IIT system focusing on the top-tier institutes for 2026 admissions:

## Key Highlights & Legacy

- **Establishment:** 1951 (Starting with IIT Kharagpur). There are currently 23 IITs across India.
- **Legacy & USP:** IITs are "Institutes of National Importance." They boast the strongest alumni networks globally, with graduates leading Fortune 500 companies (Google, Alphabet, IBM) and building billion-dollar unicorns in India (Flipkart, Ola, Zomato).
- **Top 5 Institutes (The "Old IITs"):**
  - IIT Madras (Consistent NIRF Rank #1)
  - IIT Delhi
  - IIT Bombay
  - IIT Kanpur
  - IIT Kharagpur

## Admission Details

- **Program Offered:** B.Tech, Dual Degree (B.Tech + M.Tech), Integrated M.Sc.
- **Entrance Exam Accepted:** JEE Advanced (Only the top ~2.5 lakh qualifiers of JEE Main are eligible to write this exam).
- **Expected Cut-off (JEE Advanced Rank for CSE in Top 5 IITs):**
  - General Category: Ranks under 100 to 300
  - *Note: To get any branch in the top 5 old IITs, a General Category rank under 5,000-6,000 is generally required.*
- **Total Fees (Approximate for 4 Years B.Tech):**
  - ₹8.50 Lakhs - ₹10.00 Lakhs (General Category without fee waivers).
  - *Note: 100% tuition fee waiver for SC/ST/PH and students with family income < ₹1 Lakh.*

## Key Specializations Offered (B.Tech)

- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- Electrical Engineering 
- Mechanical Engineering
- Civil Engineering
- Chemical Engineering
- Aerospace Engineering
- Mathematics and Computing

## Placement Statistics (2025-2026 for Top IITs)

IITs attract global tech giants, high-frequency trading (HFT) firms, and top-tier management consulting agencies on Day 1 of placements.

- **Highest Placement Package:** ₹1.50 Crores to ₹2.00 Crores+ (International HFTs and Tech Giants) / ₹60.00 LPA - ₹80.00 LPA (Domestic)
- **Average Placement Package (Old IITs):** ₹20.00 LPA - ₹25.00 LPA (Varies by branch; CSE averages often exceed ₹35.00 LPA)
- **Lowest Placement Package:** ₹8.00 LPA - ₹10.00 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** Jane Street, Optiver, Google, Apple, Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, Bain & Company, BCG, McKinsey, and domestic giants like Tata and Reliance.

## Student Reviews & Verdict

**What Students Say:**
- **Pros:** The peer group is the absolute best in the country. The brand value is permanent; the tag "IITian" opens doors globally. World-class research facilities, massive 500+ acre green campuses, and iconic cultural fests (like Mood Indigo at IIT Bombay).
- **Cons:** Extremely high pressure and a toxic competitive environment for grading. The curriculum in some older branches can feel outdated compared to the fast-paced tech industry.

**Our Verdict:** There is no debate. If you clear JEE Advanced with a rank good enough for an old IIT or even a core branch in a newer IIT (like Hyderabad, Indore, or BHU), you take it. The ROI, the networking, and the respect commanded by the IIT tag are unparalleled.
