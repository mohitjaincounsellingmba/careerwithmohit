Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is home to some of the country's most iconic educational institutions. With **CUET PG 2026** becoming a major gateway, aspirants can now target top-tier Mumbai colleges with a single national score.

If you are looking for **HRM, MBA, MA, or MSc programs in Mumbai**, here is your detailed guide to institutions accepting CUET PG scores.

---

### **1. Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS)**
TISS Mumbai is the dream destination for aspirants of HRM & LR, ODCL, and social sciences. As of current patterns, TISS has officially moved to CUET PG for its flagship flagship MA (HRM & LR) programs.
*   **Top Programs:** MA in Human Resource Management & Labour Relations, MA in Organisation Development, Change and Leadership.
*   **Admission Process:** CUET PG scores (COQP12) are mandatory for shortlisting, followed by a personal interview (TISS PI).
*   **Why Choose TISS?** Unmatched ROI and industry reputation in the HR domain.

### **2. Somaiya Vidyavihar University (Mumbai)**
Somaiya is one of Mumbai’s largest private universities with a multi-disciplinary campus in Vidyavihar and Sion.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MA (Philosophy), MSc (Journalism).
*   **Admission Process:** They consider various national scores including CUET PG for several of their postgraduate departments.

### **3. D.Y. Patil University (School of Management, Mumbai)**
Located in Navi Mumbai/Mumbai, D.Y. Patil is known for its specialized MBA programs and state-of-the-art infrastructure.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA in Health & Hospital Management, MBA in Pharmaceutical Management, MBA in Finance.
*   **Admission Process:** They accept CUET PG scores for admissions alongside other national management tests.

### **4. Institute of Chemical Technology (ICT) Mumbai**
While ICT has its own legacy for engineering, some of its interdisciplinary and PG programs occasionally use CUET PG scores for admissions (Always check the latest ICT notification).

### **5. Other Deemed and Private Universities**
Many growing private universities in the Mumbai metropolitan region (MMR) use CUET PG to diversify their student intake.
*   **Homi Bhabha National Institute (HBNI)**: A specialized research university that participates in the CUET ecosystem for certain courses.
