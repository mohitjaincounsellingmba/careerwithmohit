Delhi NCR is the epicenter of the **Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG** movement. With some of the most prestigious central and state universities in the country, the competition is fierce but rewarding.

If you are a 12th-grade student targeting **Delhi NCR for the 2026-27 academic session**, here are the top institutions you should consider based on your CUET UG scores.

---

### **1. University of Delhi (DU)**
The University of Delhi is the primary reason why CUET exists today. With over 70,000 seats across 90+ colleges, it is the dream of every Indian student.
*   **Popular Colleges:** SRCC, St. Stephens, Miranda House, Hindu College, LSR.
*   **Admission Process:** Entirely based on CUET scores followed by the **CSAS (Common Seat Allocation System)** portal.

### **2. Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)**
While famous for its PG courses, JNU offers exceptional BA (Hons.) in Foreign Languages for UG students through CUET.
*   **Languages Offered:** French, Spanish, German, Japanese, Chinese, Persian, and more.

### **3. Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI)**
JMI accepts CUET scores for several high-demand UG programs including BA (Hons.) Economics, History, and Foreign Languages.
*   **Note:** Not all programs in Jamia are under CUET; some still require their own entrance test.

### **4. Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU)**
IP University has integrated CUET scores for admissions into courses where seats remain vacant after their own common entrance test (CET). This includes BBA, BCA, and B.Com (Hons).

### **5. Dr. B.R. Ambedkar University Delhi (AUD)**
A state university known for its deep focus on social sciences and humanities. AUD is a preferred choice for students looking for high-quality liberal arts education.

---

### **Why Choose Delhi NCR for UG?**
*   **Legacy:** From researchers to CEOs, Delhi’s universities have produced global leaders.
*   **Exposure:** Being in the capital provides unmatched access to internships, political exposure, and cultural diversity.
*   **ROI:** Central universities offer extremely low fees with some of the highest placement packages in India.
