**Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology (MAIT)** is widely considered the top private engineering college affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). Located in Rohini, North Delhi, MAIT is a preferred destination for JEE Main aspirants who want to stay in Delhi while enjoying a high ROI and decent campus placements.

### **MAIT Admission Process 2026**
1.  **Entrance Exam**: You must appear for **JEE Main 2026 (Paper 1)**.
2.  **IPU Registration**: Apply for GGSIPU (IPU) online counselling (last date typically in March/April).
3.  **Counselling**: Seats are allotted based on your JEE Main AIR. 85% of seats are reserved for **Delhi Region** students.
4.  **Management Quota**: 10% seats are available for direct admission through merit.

### **MAIT Fee Structure 2026**
MAIT offers one of the most cost-effective B.Tech programs in Delhi.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹1.17 Lakhs.
*   **Total 4-Year Cost**: **₹7.0 Lakhs - ₹8.0 Lakhs** (Course fees).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹2.1 Lakhs to ₹2.7 Lakhs per year (depending on AC/Non-AC).

### **Placement Review: The MAIT Success Story**
MAIT is famous for sending students to top-tier product companies like Amazon and Google.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹64.00 LPA (reached in 2024, expected higher for 2026).
*   **Average Package (CSE/IT)**: ₹12.0 LPA to ₹14.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, Deloitte, TCS, and ZS Associates.
*   **Placement Rate**: Above 92% for eligible circuit branch students.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Excellent ROI, liberal attendance policy (good for off-campus preparation), respected brand in Delhi.
**Cons**: Small campus compared to DTU/NSUT, limited extracurricular facilities, located on the outskirts of North Delhi.
