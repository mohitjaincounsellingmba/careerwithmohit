## 📍 Why Target Central Universities through CUET PG?

While most students run after private B-schools with ₹25 Lakh fees, smart aspirants choose CUET PG for these reasons:

- **Massive ROI**: Get an MBA from BHU or JNU for less than ₹2 Lakhs total fees while getting average placements of ₹11-13 LPA.
- **Brand Value**: Degrees from Central Universities carry huge weightage in PSU jobs and government sectors.
- **Diverse Specializations**: From MBA in Agribusiness at BHU to HRM at TISS, the variety is unparalleled.
