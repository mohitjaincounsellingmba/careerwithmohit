### **College Review: Maharaja Agrasen Business School**
*   **Heritage Brand**: Part of the Maharaja Agrasen group, which is a highly trusted name in education across Delhi NCR.
*   **Strong Ethics**: Known for a value-based management education approach combined with modern technical skills.
*   **Career Blueprint**: Focuses on an "integrated career development framework" for all its PGDM students.

### **Placement Review (2024-2026 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: Up to **₹13.96 LPA** (Recent) | Premium domestic/global claims reach higher for tech roles.
*   **Average Package**: **₹12.25 LPA** (Reported for top-tier tracks).
*   **Placement Rate**: Maintains a high placement success rate of **91%**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Adobe, Amazon, IBM, Deloitte, Google, and Reliance.
*   **CRC Strength**: A very active Corporate Resource Center (CRC) that provides intensive job preparation.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Professional Design**: Modern classrooms equipped with high-end digital learning tools.
*   **Resources**: Extensive library and digital archive for research in management and economics.
*   **Tech Stack**: Access to high-speed campus Wi-Fi and well-equipped computer labs.
*   **Amenities**: Proximity to local transport and high-quality student recreational zones.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Mentorship Intensive**: Small batch sizes allow for regular one-on-one sessions between faculty and students.
*   **Industry Immersion**: Curriculum incorporates case studies from top consultancies and actual business challenges.
*   **Prep Focus**: Faculty members are dedicated to building student portfolios that appeal to top-tier MNCs.

**Final Verdict**: A solid option for students in Delhi tracking high salary packages and a premium, corporate-focused learning environment.
