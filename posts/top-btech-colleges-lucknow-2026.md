## Tier 1 — Government Institutions in Lucknow

### 1. IET Lucknow — Institute of Engineering and Technology (HBTU)
IET Lucknow (now part of Harcourt Butler Technical University — HBTU) is Uttar Pradesh's most prestigious government engineering institution outside of IIT (BHU) Varanasi. The IET brand carries massive respect in UP's public and private sector hiring.

- **Courses:** CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, Leather Technology
- **Admission:** JEE Main / UPTAC State Counselling
- **UPTAC State Rank Cutoff (IET CSE, General):** ~2,000
- **Annual Fee:** ~₹70,000
- **Avg Package:** ₹5–9 LPA | Highest: ₹30+ LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** TCS, Wipro, Infosys, HCL, Capgemini, L&T

### 2. BBAU — Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Lucknow
BBAU is a central government university (like JNU) offering B.Tech in CSE and allied disciplines with a research focus and affordable fees.

- **Admission:** CUET UG / Board Merit
- **Annual Fee:** ~₹80,000 | **Avg Package:** ₹4–7 LPA

## Tier 2 — Top Private Engineering Colleges in Lucknow

### 3. Amity University, Lucknow Campus
Amity's Lucknow campus is a smaller version of the Noida campus with similar branding and curriculum. Good option for students wanting the Amity degree at Lucknow.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil
- **Admission:** JEE Main / Boards / AUCET
- **Annual Fee:** ₹1.5 L/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹5–8 LPA

### 4. Integral University, Lucknow
Lucknow's largest private university with 10,000+ students across engineering, management, and law. NAAC A accredited.

- **Courses:** CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, Biotechnology
- **Admission:** UPTAC / Board Merit / Direct
- **Annual Fee:** ₹90,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹4–6 LPA

### 5. Shri Ramswaroop College of Engineering & Management (SRCEM)
AKTU-affiliated college with a solid track record and affordable fees for budget-conscious students.

- **Admission:** AKTU UPTAC | **Fee:** ₹80,000/yr | **Avg Package:** ₹3.5–6 LPA

## AKTU UPTAC Counselling 2026 — Lucknow Colleges

All AKTU-affiliated private colleges in Lucknow fill B.Tech seats through UPTAC after JEE Main results.

- **UPTAC Registration:** June 2026 (expected)
- **Eligibility:** 10+2 with PCM, minimum 45%
- **Rounds:** 3 rounds of seat allotment in July–August 2026

## Direct Admission in Lucknow Engineering Colleges 2026

After UPTAC counselling is complete, unsold/leftover seats at private AKTU colleges are filled via direct admission (management quota):
- **Available at:** Integral University, SRCEM, FIMT, Amity Lucknow
- **Requirement:** 12th PCM with 45%+ 
- **No JEE Score required** for management quota

## FAQs — B.Tech Colleges in Lucknow 2026

**Q1. Which is the best B.Tech college in Lucknow?**
IET Lucknow (HBTU) is Lucknow's top engineering institution for government education. Among private colleges, Amity University Lucknow is the most recognised brand.

**Q2. What UPTAC rank is needed for IET Lucknow CSE?**
Approximately a UP State Merit Rank of 2,000 or below (General category) is needed for Computer Science at IET Lucknow (HBTU).

**Q3. Is B.Tech from Lucknow good for placement?**
Yes, especially from IET (HBTU). IT companies like TCS, Wipro, Infosys, HCL actively recruit from Lucknow colleges. Lucknow's growing IT sector also creates local opportunities.

**Q4. Can I get B.Tech in Lucknow without JEE Main?**
Yes. BBAU accepts CUET UG scores. Private colleges accept students via UPTAC (based on 12th marks) and management quota without requiring a JEE Main score.

**Q5. What is the fee for B.Tech at IET Lucknow?**
IET Lucknow charges approximately ₹70,000 per year — making it one of the most affordable quality engineering colleges in UP.
