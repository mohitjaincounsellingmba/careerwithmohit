## 🏛️ Tier 1: The Kolkata Establishments

### 1. St. Xavier's College, Kolkata
Consistently ranked India’s elite. Known for its rigorous academic environment and premium brand name in the East.
- **Selection:** High cut-offs based on 10+2 marks (especially Mathematics).
- **Top Recruiters:** Google, Deloitte, PwC, Infosys.

### 2. IEM Kolkata (Institute of Engineering & Management)
Located in Sector V, the heart of Kolkata’s IT hub. IEM is famous for its "Corporate Training" approach and massive placement drives.
- **Advantage:** Unbeatable proximity to IT giants.
- **Admission:** IEMJEE or merit-based interview.

### 3. Heritage Institute of Technology
One of the most trusted names under MAKAUT affiliation. Heritage offers a world-class campus in Anandapur and has a dedicated Cell for computer application placements.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is BCA from Kolkata better than Bangalore?**
For **Product Roles**, Bangalore wins. For **IT Services & Consulting**, Kolkata is equally strong and much more affordable. If you want a career in Big 4 (PwC, Deloitte, KPMG), Kolkata is a massive hub.

**Q2. Does Heritage Kolkata require Maths for BCA?**
Yes. A background in Mathematics at the 10+2 level is mandatory for most MAKAUT-affiliated colleges in West Bengal.

**Q3. What is the average fee for BCA in Kolkata?**
Government-aided colleges cost around ₹40,000 to ₹70,000 per year. Top private autonomous institutes range from ₹90,000 to ₹1.5 Lakhs per year.

**Q4. Can BCA graduates work in PwC or Deloitte Kolkata?**
Yes. Kolkata is one of the global HQs for the Big 4. They hire BCA graduates for technical analyst and support roles.

**Q5. When is the Kolkata BCA admission 2026 starting?**
Usually in **May/June**, immediately after the WBHSE and CBSE Board results are declared.
