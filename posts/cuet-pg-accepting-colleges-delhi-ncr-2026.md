Delhi NCR is the hub of higher education in India, offering a mix of prestigious central universities and high-growth private institutions. With the **CUET PG 2026** becoming the standard entrance for postgraduate studies, students have a unified gateway to enter some of the best campuses in the region.

If you are planning to pursue **MBA, MCA, MA, or MSc in Delhi NCR**, here is the most detailed guide to colleges accepting CUET PG 2026 scores.

---

### **1. University of Delhi (DU)**
The University of Delhi is the top choice for CUET PG aspirants. It offers a wide range of PG programs across various departments.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA (Financial Management), MBA (Business Economics), MCA, MA (Economics), MA (Political Science).
*   **Admission Process:** Entirely based on CUET PG scores followed by the **CSAS PG portal** registration.
*   **Why Choose DU?** Exceptionally high ROI, central university tag, and unmatched exposure.

### **2. Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)**
JNU is globally renowned for its research and social sciences programs. Its **Atal Bihari Vajpayee School of Management and Entrepreneurship (ABVSME)** also accepts CUET PG for its MBA program.
*   **Top Programs:** MA (International Relations), MSc, MBA.
*   **Admission Process:** CUET PG score (weightage varies by department).

### **3. Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI)**
While Jamia has its own entrance for many courses, it has integrated CUET PG for several specific postgraduate programs.
*   **Programs:** MA Persian, MA Sanskrit, MSc Disaster Management, etc. (Always check the latest list as it expands annually).

### **4. Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU)**
IP University is a state university that accepts CUET PG scores for many of its PG programs, especially when seats are left after their own CET and national exam (CAT/CMAT/GATE) counseling.
*   **Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA (English).

### **5. Dr. B.R. Ambedkar University Delhi (AUD)**
A state university focused on social sciences, humanities, and management. It is a premium choice for students looking for a liberal arts approach at the PG level.
*   **Programs:** MBA (Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Venture Development), MA Psychology, MA Sociology.

### **6. Top Private Universities in Delhi NCR**
Several top-tier private universities in Noida, Greater Noida, and Gurugram also accept CUET PG scores for admissions into their MBA and other PG programs.
*   **Amity University (Noida/Gurugram)**
*   **Galgotias University (Greater Noida)**
*   **Sharda University (Greater Noida)**
*   **BML Munjal University (Gurugram)**
*   **K.R. Mangalam University (Gurugram)**
