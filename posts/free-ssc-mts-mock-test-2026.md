## Section-Wise Strategy

- **Numerical Ability (20 Q, Session 1):** Class 10 level — Percentage, Ratio, Fractions, Profit-Loss, Simple Interest, Time & Work. Attempt all 20 questions since no negative marking applies.
- **Reasoning (20 Q, Session 1):** Coding-Decoding, Odd One Out, Figure Series, Directions, and Analogy. Simple puzzles and series problems. Attempt all 20.
- **General Awareness (25 Q, Session 2):** India GK, Capital-Currency, Scientific inventions, Freedom Movement, Sports, and current affairs. Cover NCERT Science (Class 8–10) and basic history.
- **English (25 Q, Session 2):** Reading Comprehension (short passages), grammar corrections, fill in the blanks. Basic Class 10 English level.

## Pro Tips for SSC MTS 2026

- **Master Session 1 Fully:** No negative marking in Session 1 means you MUST attempt all 40 questions. If not practised, this is a free marks opportunity you'll miss.
- **Current Affairs — Last 3 Months:** GA questions in MTS focus on very recent events — sports, appointments, schemes, and national events from the last 3 months.
- **Score 200+ for Safety:** While cutoffs vary by category and state, scoring 200+ out of 270 keeps you comfortably above most cutoffs.
- **Practice Speed:** 90 questions in 90 minutes means exactly 1 minute per question. Practice reading questions fast and eliminating wrong options quickly.
