Ghaziabad, part of the National Capital Region (NCR), is a major industrial and educational hub. With several universities and institutes affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), the **CUET PG 2026** score has become a key requirement for postgraduate admissions in the city.

If you are a PG aspirant targeting **Ghaziabad for the 2026-27 academic session**, here is the most detailed guide to colleges accepting CUET PG scores.

---

### **1. ITS Engineering College (Ghaziabad)**
ITS Group is one of the most recognized names in Ghaziabad's education sector. They accept CUET PG scores for their MBA and MCA programs.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA (Marketing/Finance/HR/IT), MCA.
*   **Admission Process:** They consider CUET PG scores as part of the AKTU-led counseling process or direct admission rounds.

### **2. IPEM Group of Institutions (Ghaziabad)**
IPEM is known for its focus on management and information technology in the NCR region.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA.
*   **Why Choose IPEM?** Strong focus on corporate exposure and high placement support.

### **3. HIET (Hi-Tech Institute of Engineering and Technology)**
HIET is a prominent AKTU-affiliated institute that uses CUET PG scores for its postgraduate admissions.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA.

### **4. RD Engineering College (Ghaziabad)**
As a technical institute, RD Engineering College offers specialized MBA programs that are accessible through CUET PG scores.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA (Finance/Marketing/HR).

### **5. SRM University (NCR Campus, Ghaziabad/Modinagar)**
SRM Delhi-NCR campus is a major private university that considers several national entrance scores, including CUET PG, for its postgraduate intake.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MA (English).

---

### **Why Ghaziabad for PG?**
*   **Proximity to Industries:** Being in the NCR, Ghaziabad offers easy access to industrial hubs in Noida and Delhi, which is ideal for internships and final placements.
*   **AKTU Affiliation:** Many Ghaziabad colleges are affiliated with AKTU, a prestigious state university, providing a recognized degree and a robust curriculum.
*   **Affordability:** Compared to Delhi or Noida, living costs and hostel fees in Ghaziabad are often more affordable for students from across India.
