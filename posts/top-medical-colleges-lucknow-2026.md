## 🏛️ The Lucknow Pride: KGMU & RMLIMS

### 1. King George’s Medical University (KGMU)
Commonly known as the "Heritage of Medicine" in India. KGMU is famous for its intensive clinical rounds and a hospital capacity that rivals global centers.
- **Top Choice:** For students who want to be part of an 100-year-old academic legacy.

### 2. Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Institute of Medical Sciences (RMLIMS)
A premium government institute that has rapidly climbed the rankings. It provides a more modern infrastructure compared to KGMU while maintaining high patient standards.

## 🚀 The Lucknow Advantage (UP NEET & Open State)

For a 2026 medical student, Lucknow offers:
- **Massive Patient Load:** Being the capital, Lucknow’s hospitals receive patients from all over UP and Bihar, ensuring you see rare medical conditions early in your training.
- **Affordable Private Medical:** Uttar Pradesh is one of the most affordable "Open States" for private medical education, with fees being more manageable than in Bangalore or Mumbai.
- **Academic Ecosystem:** Proximity to premier research institutes like the Sanjay Gandhi PGI (SGPGI) for specialized post-graduate inspiration.

---

## 🏗️ UP NEET Counselling 2026

The **Directorate General of Medical Education (DGME)** UP conducts the admissions.
- **85% State Quota:** For UP domicile students in government colleges.
- **Open Private Seats:** UP allows students from anywhere in India to apply for private medical colleges (Management Quota) through the official UP NEET website.

---

## 🏗️ Direct MBBS Admission in Lucknow 2026

Private Medical seats in Lucknow (like Hind, Era, and TSM) are allocated through the state counselling.
- **Process:** Must qualify for NEET. Even for management quota, you MUST register for UP NEET counselling. No college can legally give you a seat without the official allotment letter.
- **Fees:** Total first-year costs (including hostel and security) usually range from ₹15 Lakhs to ₹20 Lakhs.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is KGMU better than MAMC Delhi?**
KGMU is on par with MAMC in terms of **Clinical Training**. However, MAMC has a slight advantage for **Delhi-specific PG Quota**, which is highly valued.

**Q2. Does Era Medical College have different fees for Minority quota?**
Era is a Muslim Minority institution. While they have a minority quota, the fees are usually higher than regular private medical colleges, but the infrastructure is exceptional.

**Q3. What is the fee for MBBS in UP Government colleges?**
For government seats through UP NEET, the annual tuition fee is around ₹30,000 to ₹50,000.

**Q4. Can a student from Rajasthan/Delhi get a seat in Lucknow’s private colleges?**
YES. Uttar Pradesh is an **Open State**. You can apply for seats in Lucknow private colleges through the UP NEET portal even without a UP domicile.

**Q5. When is the UP NEET 2026 counselling?**
Usually starts late **June/July** after the NEET results.
