Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) is one of the most sought-after universities for MBA in Delhi NCR due to its excellent ROI and centralized counseling. However, unlike other universities, IPU follows a unique **Priority System** for MBA admissions.

In this guide, we provide the **expected IPU MBA cutoffs for 2025-2026**, covering CAT, CMAT, and IPU CET ranks for the top affiliated colleges.

---

## IPU MBA Admission Priority System — How it Works

IP University does not conduct its own exam as the primary gateway. Instead, it follows this hierarchy:

1. **First Priority: CAT** — All seats are first offered to students who appeared for the CAT exam.
2. **Second Priority: CMAT** — If seats remain vacant after CAT counseling, they are offered to CMAT-qualified candidates.
3. **Third Priority: IPU CET / CUET** — If seats are still available (which is rare for top colleges), the university considers IPU CET or CUET scores.

> **Crucial Tip:** Even if you have a 99 percentile in CMAT, a student with a 50 percentile in CAT will be given preference during the first phase of counseling.

## IPU MBA Counseling Process 2026

To get into these colleges, you must follow these steps:

1. **Register Online:** Visit [ipu.admissions.nic.in](https://ipu.admissions.nic.in/) and register for MBA (Code 101).
2. **Upload Scores:** Upload your CAT 2025 or CMAT 2026 scorecard.
3. **Pay Counseling Fee:** Pay the mandatory ₹1,000 - ₹1,500 counseling fee.
4. **Choice Filling:** Lock your preferences (Put USMS at #1, MAIT at #2, etc.).
5. **Seat Allotment:** Based on your rank and priority, a seat will be allotted in Round 1, 2, or 3.

## Delhi vs. Outside Delhi Quota

- **Delhi Region (85%):** For students who passed their qualifying exam (Graduation) from a college located in Delhi.
- **Outside Delhi (15%):** For students who graduated from colleges outside Delhi.

*Cutoffs for Outside Delhi candidates are significantly higher (usually 5–10 percentile more) due to fewer seats.*

## FAQs — IPU MBA Admissions 2026

**Q1. Can I get USMS Dwarka with a CMAT score?**
Yes, but only if seats remain vacant after the CAT merit list is exhausted. Usually, USMS Dwarka fills up 90% of its seats in the CAT round itself.

**Q2. Is there a Management Quota in IPU MBA?**
Yes, private affiliated colleges like MAIT, JIMS, and MSIT have a 10% Management Quota. However, USMS (Main Campus) does not have any management quota.

**Q3. What is the difference between USMS and other affiliated colleges?**
USMS is the university's on-campus department. It has the lowest fees and the highest brand value. Affiliated colleges are private but follow the same curriculum and degree.
