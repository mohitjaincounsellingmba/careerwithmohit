Located in Greater Noida with easy connectivity to the entire Delhi NCR, **GL Bajaj Institute of Management and Research (GLBIMR)** is one of the most popular PGDM destinations for management aspirants in North India. Known for its budget-friendly fee structure, dual specialization options, and strong corporate connections, GLBIMR offers a compelling package for students seeking quality education with genuine ROI.

Here is a complete, bulleted breakdown of everything you need to know about GLBIMR for the 2025-2026 session.

---

## 🏛️ Establishment & Legacy
* **Established:** 2007 (as an institute for management), under the umbrella of the GL Bajaj Group of Institutions (founded 1997).
* **Location:** Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh — strategically placed in the NCR corporate corridor.
* **Accreditations:** AICTE-approved, accredited by NAAC and NBA.
* **Unique Feature:** The total PGDM fee **includes a laptop and course materials**, making it exceptional value.

## 📚 Academic Programs Offered
* **PGDM (General):** 2-year, full-time flagship program.
* **Dual Specialization Model:** Students get to select two specializations to increase their career versatility.

## 🎓 Specializations Available
GLBIMR offers an extensive list of modern and traditional specializations:
* Marketing Management
* Financial Management
* Human Resource Management (HRM)
* Operations Management & Supply Chain
* Information Technology (IT)
* International Business
* Data Analytics
* Entrepreneurship
* Strategy & Consultancy

## 💰 Fee Structure (2025-2026 / 2026-2028)
* **Total PGDM Fee (2026-28 Batch):** ₹7,95,000 including a **laptop and all course materials**.
* **Earlier Batch (2025):** ~₹7.85 Lakhs.
* **Hostel Fees:** ₹1,40,000 to ₹1,80,000 per year (optional, based on room type).
* **Fee Payment:** Available in convenient installments.

## 📝 Entrance Exams Accepted
GLBIMR follows a holistic admissions policy, evaluating candidates holistically via GD/PI and past academics alongside entrance test scores:
* **CAT**
* **MAT**
* **CMAT**
* **XAT**
* **ATMA**

## 🎯 Expected Cut-Offs (2025/2026 Admissions)
Admission at GLBIMR is relatively accessible compared to top-10 PGDM institutes:
* **CAT:** Approximately 25+ Percentile.
* **MAT / CMAT / ATMA:** Competitive scores with a profile-based assessment.

## 💼 Placement Records & Recruiters (Recent Batches)
GLBIMR has a dedicated **Corporate Resource Centre** that maintains active industrial linkages for placements.

### Salary Statistics (2023/2024 Data)
* **Highest Package:** ₹23.00 LPA.
* **Average Package:** ₹8.5 LPA (2023) | Projected ~₹10.5 LPA (2025).
* **Minimum Package:** Officially reported at ₹6.00 LPA (though student reviews suggest base roles can start ~₹4-5 LPA).

### Top Recruiters
GLBIMR attracts a diverse set of domestic and multinational companies spanning multiple sectors:
* **IT & Consulting:** Accenture, Deloitte, KPMG, Infosys, Wipro, Tech Mahindra, Sopra Steria.
* **BFSI:** HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Deutsche Bank, ANZ Bank, American Express, Anand Rathi.
* **FMCG & Retail:** ITC, HUL, Asian Paints, Godrej, Bata, PUMA, Marks & Spencer, Bosch.
* **E-Commerce & Others:** Amazon, Flipkart, Oyo Rooms, Yamaha Motors, Microsoft, Capital IQ, Radisson.

## 🗣️ Student Reviews & Verdict
Here's what current students and alumni say about the GLBIMR experience:

### What Students Love:
* **Fee + Laptop Package:** Exceptional cost-effectiveness — the PGDM under ₹8 Lakhs that includes a laptop is virtually unmatched in the NCR.
* **Dual Specialization Advantage:** The ability to combine two specializations (e.g., Finance + Data Analytics) makes students much more versatile in placement interviews.
* **Good Infrastructure:** Rated 4.4/5 for infrastructure, including spacious classrooms, digital labs, and sports facilities.
* **Placement Support:** The Corporate Resource Centre is highly active, rated 4.2/5 for placement support.

### Things to Consider:
* **Average Package Variability:** While the official average is impressive, some students report that actual outcomes can be closer to ₹5-7 LPA, especially for those not from marketing specializations.
* **Location:** Greater Noida is well-connected but may feel remote compared to city-centre institutes.

**Final Verdict:** GLBIMR is one of the **best value-for-money PGDM colleges in Delhi NCR**. For students targeting a budget-conscious management education with access to top-tier recruiters and the dual specialization advantage — all under ₹8 Lakhs — GLBIMR is a very strong option.
