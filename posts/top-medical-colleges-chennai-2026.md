## 🏛️ The Chennai Icons: MMC & Stanley

### 1. Madras Medical College (MMC)
The oldest medical college in India. It is connected to the Rajiv Gandhi Government General Hospital (RGGGH), which receives over 10,000 OPD patients daily.
- **Top Choice:** For students who want an elite medical legacy and massive clinical diversity.

### 2. Stanley Medical College (SMC)
Famous for its "Hand Rehabilitation and Plastic Surgery" department, which is ranked among the best globally. Stanley has a unique "student-first" academic culture.

### 3. Kilpauk Medical College (KMC)
Known for its specialized "Burns and Trauma" care, KMC provides students with intensive frontline medical skills.

## 🏗️ Tamil Nadu NEET Counselling (Selection Committee) 2026

The **Selection Committee (Directorate of Medical Education)** TN conducts the admissions.
- **85% State Quota:** Available for Tamil Nadu domicile students.
- **Deemed Prowess:** Chennai has some of India's best Deemed Medical Universities (SRM, Ramachandra). Admission to these is done via the **National MCC Counselling**.

---

## 🏗️ Direct MBBS Admission in Chennai 2026

Direct admission in Chennai's Deemed universities is legally done through the MCC portal.
- **Process:** You MUST be NEET-qualified. Register under the "Deemed University" category.
- **Fees:** Chennai Deemed universities are known for their high fee structures (total package often crossing ₹1.2 Cr).

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is MMC Chennai better than AIIMS?**
In terms of **Clinical Patient Volume**, MMC is often superior to many new AIIMS. However, AIIMS Delhi retains the lead for national brand prestige and research funding.

**Q2. Are government medical seats in Tamil Nadu open to outside students?**
Only the **15% All India Quota (AIQ)** seats in state government colleges are open to students from other states. The 85% state quota is strictly for TN candidates.

**Q3. What is the fee for MBBS in Sri Ramachandra Chennai?**
Sri Ramachandra is a premium Deemed university. The annual tuition fee is around ₹25 Lakhs to ₹28 Lakhs per year.

**Q4. Does SRM Chennai have good medical placements?**
"Placements" for doctors are different from engineering. As an SRM graduate, you get incredible clinical experience and their hospital is very well-connected, helping you secure residency or join top hospital chains.

**Q5. When is the Tamil Nadu Medical admission 2026 starting?**
Counselling registrations typically open in **July** after the NEET results.
