### **Is There Direct Admission or Management Quota at SKIT Jaipur?**

The clear answer: **Yes — SKIT Jaipur has a Management Quota for B.Tech.**

As per Rajasthan state engineering admission norms:
- **85% of seats** are filled via **REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process)** centralized counselling.
- **15% of seats** fall under **Management Quota**, filled directly by the institute.

There is **no standard "direct admission" without eligibility criteria** at SKIT. The management quota seats are legitimate but still require meeting minimum marks criteria. The process for these seats runs through the institute's own admissions office.

> **⚠️ Caution**: SKIT is a government-norms compliant institution. Any third-party agent claiming to guarantee a seat via backdoor channels is likely running a scam. Always contact SKIT's official admissions office directly.

### **Admission Process 2026 — Step-by-Step**

**Route 1: REAP Counselling (85% Seats)**
1. Register on the REAP 2026 portal (typically opens June–July 2026).
2. Enter your JEE Main rank or 12th percentage.
3. Fill preferences — select SKIT and desired branch.
4. Seat allocated based on merit; report to college with documents.

**Route 2: Management Quota (15% Seats)**
1. Contact SKIT admissions office directly via [skit.ac.in](https://www.skit.ac.in).
2. Submit application with 12th marksheet, Transfer Certificate, and ID.
3. Eligibility verified; fee structure communicated.
4. Pay the admission fee to confirm the seat.

### **Eligibility Criteria for SKIT Jaipur B.Tech 2026**

- Passed 10+2 with **minimum 45% aggregate** (General) / **40%** (SC/ST/OBC from Rajasthan).
- Compulsory subjects: **Physics and Mathematics**.
- One additional subject: Chemistry / Biology / Computer Science / Technical Vocational.
- JEE Main score is preferred but not mandatory for management quota seats.

### **Is SKIT Worth It for B.Tech?**

**Pros:**
- One of the top-ranked private engineering colleges in Rajasthan
- All-time highest package of ₹47 LPA shows placement potential
- Legitimate management quota seats available through institute
- Very affordable total course cost of ₹4.6–5.2 Lakhs
- Strong industry connect in Rajasthan, especially for Jaipur-based tech companies

**Cons:**
- RTU affiliation means degree is less recognized outside Rajasthan compared to deemed/private universities
- Average placement packages are moderate — top packages are rare, not the norm
- Management quota process lacks public clarity — always get written documents

**Verdict**: SKIT Jaipur is arguably the best private engineering college in Jaipur for students on a budget. Strong local brand, legitimate management quota seats, solid training and placement support — all at a very affordable fee. For CSE students targeting Rajasthan's job market or MS/MBA after B.Tech, SKIT is a smart choice.
