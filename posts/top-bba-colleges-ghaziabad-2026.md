Ghaziabad, a key industrial city in the National Capital Region (NCR), has emerged as a hub for affordable yet high-quality management education. It offers students the dual advantage of living in a growing city while being just a short commute away from Delhi's corporate center.

Here are the **Top BBA Colleges in Ghaziabad for 2026**.

---

## 🏛️ Top BBA Institutions in Ghaziabad

### 1. IMS Ghaziabad (University Courses Campus)
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹1.4 Lakhs (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹11 LPA (Highest ₹41 LPA)
- **Entrance Exam:** Merit-based / JET
- **USP:** Known for its stellar ROI and one of the highest packages in the entire NCR for undergraduate courses.

### 2. ITS - Institute of Technology and Science, Ghaziabad
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹1.6 Lakhs (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹4 - 6 LPA (Highest ₹14 LPA)
- **Entrance Exam:** Merit-based
- **USP:** Strong focus on student activities, industrial visits, and personality development programs.

### 3. Christ University (Delhi NCR Campus), Ghaziabad
- **Approx. Fees:** ₹2.5 - 3.5 Lakhs (Annual)
- **Average Placement:** ₹5.16 LPA
- **Entrance Exam:** Christ University Entrance Test (CUET)
- **USP:** The legendary Christ brand name with a modern campus in the NCR region.
