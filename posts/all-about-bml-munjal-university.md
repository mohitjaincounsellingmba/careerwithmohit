Founded by the promoters of the Hero Group, BML Munjal University (BMU) in Gurgaon is rapidly emerging as a preferred destination for management education. Known for its strong industry integration—mentored by Imperial College London—BMU offers a highly experiential and practical MBA program.

Here is the complete review of the BML Munjal University MBA program for 2026:

## Key Highlights

- **Establishment:** 2014 (Founded by the Hero Group)
- **Program Offered:** MBA (UGC Recognized)
- **Legacy & USP:** The immense backing of the Hero Group provides unparalleled industry access. The curriculum is co-designed and mentored by Imperial College London (ICL), offering students a world-class academic framework.

## Admission Details

- **Entrance Exams Accepted:** CAT, XAT, NMAT, GMAT, MAT, CMAT (and BMU-MAT)
- **Expected Cut-off (Percentile/Score):**
  - CAT/XAT: 65 - 70+
  - NMAT: 160 - 180+
  - MAT/CMAT: 75+
- **Total Fees:** ₹13.70 Lakhs (Approx. for the 2-year MBA, including tuition and hostel)

## Specializations Offered

- Business Analytics
- Human Resources
- Finance
- Marketing
- Operations & Supply Chain
- Entrepreneurship

## Placement Statistics (2025-2026)

BMU’s placement cell leverages the extensive Hero Group network and its corporate partners in the NCR region.

- **Highest Placement Package:** ₹32.21 LPA
- **Average Placement Package:** ₹9.40 LPA
- **Lowest Placement Package:** ₹6.00 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** Hero MotoCorp, KPMG, Amazon, TVS, Hyundai, Deloitte, Wipro, and HCL.

## Student Reviews & Verdict

**What Students Say:**
- **Pros:** The campus in Sidhrawali (Gurgaon outskirts) is beautiful, sprawling, and lush green. The "Practice School" initiative, which mandates a long-duration industry internship, is highly beneficial for placements. The Imperial College London module is a fantastic global exposure opportunity.
- **Cons:** It is located on the highway, quite far from the main city of Gurgaon, making daily commuting difficult. The fees are slightly on the higher side.

**Our Verdict:** BML Munjal University is an excellent choice for students looking for an alternative to traditional PGDM colleges in Delhi NCR. If you value a strong corporate backing (Hero Group), excellent campus life, and a curriculum validated by an international university, BMU provides a very solid foundation.
