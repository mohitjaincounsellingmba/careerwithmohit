## 🏛️ The Bangalore Legend: NLSIU

### 1. National Law School of India University (NLSIU)
Commonly known as the "Harvard of the East," NLSIU is where the 5-year integrated law course in India was born.
- **Placement Prowess:** It consistently sets the record for the highest average package in the legal sector.
- **Admissions:** 100% focus on securing a top-50 rank in **CLAT UG**.

---

## 🚀 The Private Powerhouses: Christ & Symbiosis

### 2. Christ (Deemed to be University) School of Law
One of the most respected private law hubs in South India. Known for its rigorous academic schedule and excellent networking with Bangalore-based law firms.
- **Top Pick:** BA LLB (Hons) and BBA LLB (Hons) are highly sought after.
- **Admission Strategy:** Prepare for the **CUET** (Christ entrance) followed by an interview that tests your general knowledge and legal aptitude.

### 3. Symbiosis Law School, Bangalore (SLS-B)
Located in Electronic City, SLS-B offers a corporate-heavy curriculum that is ideal for students who want to enter the IT and Tech Law space.
- **Admission:** SLAT Entrance Test.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is Bangalore better than Delhi for Law?**
**Bangalore** is superior for **Corporate Law and Tech Law**. **Delhi** is superior for **Litigation and Judiciary** careers. Choose based on your long-term goal.

**Q2. Does Christ University Law have management quota?**
Christ University traditionally fills seats via its own entrance (CUET). However, a small percentage of seats in non-flagship programs may be available through management quota—always check with an official counsellor.

**Q3. What is the average fee for BA LLB in Bangalore?**
For top-tier private universities, the fees range from ₹2 Lakhs to ₹4.5 Lakhs per year. Government-aided colleges may be much cheaper (around ₹40k - 60k per year).

**Q4. Can I work in an IT company after Law?**
Yes. Every major IT firm in Bangalore has a dedicated "In-House Legal Team" that handles contracts, IPs, and employment laws.

**Q5. When is the Bangalore Law admission 2026 starting?**
Entrance-based admissions close by **March-April**. Direct merit-based admissions open in **May** after 12th results.
