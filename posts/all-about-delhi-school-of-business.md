### **College Review: Delhi School of Business**
*   **Quality Focus**: A premium B-school located in the VIPS campus (Pitampura), known for high academic standards.
*   **Strategic Location**: Well-connected via Metro and situated in a hub of North Delhi.
*   **Holistic Growth**: Mixes curriculum with regular seminars, workshops, and student clubs for all-round development.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Highest Package**: **₹19.44 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: **₹7.60 - 8.50 LPA**.
*   **Median Package**: **₹6.90 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Asian Paints, Deloitte, Nestle, Wipro, HDFC Bank, and Amazon.
*   **Placement Cell**: Highly active in preparing students with mock interviews and career planning.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Modern Campus**: Well-maintained infra with air-conditioned smart classrooms.
*   **Resources**: A well-stocked library and high-speed Wi-Fi across the management block.
*   **Campus Vibe**: Clean, organized, and professional environment with dedicated student lounges.
*   **Nearby Accommodation**: While on-campus hostels are limited, high-quality PG options are available in the vicinity.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Elite Pool**: Faculty members include graduates from IIMs and veterans from top-tier corporate firms.
*   **Industry Focus**: Heavy use of case studies, industrial visits, and guest lectures to ensure market readiness.
*   **Personalized Support**: Known for approachable teachers who provide mentorship beyond the syllabus.

**Final Verdict**: DSB is an excellent option for students targeting corporate roles in North Delhi who value a premium, academically-rigorous environment.
