The Common University Entrance Test (CUET PG) has emerged as one of the most significant entrance exams for MBA aspirants in India. With over 150+ universities participating, it offers a streamlined path to some of the most prestigious and affordable management programs in the country.

In this guide, we highlight the top-tier MBA colleges accepting CUET PG 2025 scores, evaluating them on the parameters that matter most: **ROI, Academic Excellence, and Career Prospects.**

---

### 1. Banaras Hindu University (BHU) - FMS & IRER
BHU remains a top choice for its legacy and exceptionally high Return on Investment (ROI).

| Parameter | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Entrance Exam** | CUET PG |
| **Expected Cutoff** | 210 - 230 (General) |

### Why Choose CUET PG for MBA?

1. **Low Competition compared to CAT:** While the syllabus is rigorous, the competition density is slightly lower, providing a better chance at top central universities.
2. **Extreme ROI:** Most government universities under CUET PG have fees under ₹2 Lakhs for the entire program, ensuring you start your career debt-free.
3. **Diversity of Programs:** From Agribusiness to Sustainability and Core Finance, the range of specializations is vast.
