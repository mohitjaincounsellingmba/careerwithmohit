2026 is set to be a landmark year for government job aspirants in India. With major recruitment drives announced by the **SSC, IBPS, UPSC, and Ministry of Railways**, over 2 lakh central government vacancies are expected to be filled. 

Whether you are a graduate, a 12th-pass student, or looking for technical roles, keeping track of the official calendars is the first step toward success. Here is the definitive guide to all major upcoming government jobs for 2026-27.

---

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection has released a tentative schedule for Public Sector Banks.

### **2. Staff Selection Commission (SSC) 2026 Exams**
The SSC is the largest recruiter for Group B and C posts. Notifications for 2026 are scheduled to start as early as March.

*   **SSC CGL 2026**: Notification expected in June 2026; Tier-1 exam in Sept-Oct.
*   **SSC CHSL 2026**: Notification in April 2026; Exam in July.
*   **SSC GD Constable**: Mega recruitment with 40,000+ vacancies expected.
*   **SSC MTS & Havildar**: High volume intake for 10th-pass candidates.

---

### **3. Railway Recruitment (RRB) 2026 Mega Drive**
The Ministry of Railways has institutionalized an annual calendar to ensure regular intake.

*   **RRB Group D (Level-1)**: ~22,000 vacancies. Application started Jan-Feb 2026. 
*   **RRB ALP (Assistant Loco Pilot)**: 11,000+ vacancies. Apply from May 15 to June 14, 2026.
*   **RRB NTPC & JE**: Notifications for Junior Engineers and Non-Technical Popular Categories are expected between **July and September 2026**.
*   **RRB Paramedical**: Recruitment for nurses and technicians expected in June 2026.

---

### **4. UPSC 2026 Civil Services Calendar**
For those aiming for IAS, IPS, and IFS roles, the timeline is strictly fixed:

*   **UPSC Prelims 2026**: May 24, 2026
*   **UPSC Mains 2026**: Starts August 21, 2026 (5-day schedule)
*   **Other Exams**: NDA & CDS notifications are released twice a year (January and June/July).
