*   **Highest Package**: ₹26.00 LPA

### **What Makes PIBM Stand Out?**
*   **Profile-Based Training**: They train students for specific domains like Fintech, Digital Marketing, and Supply Chain from Day 1.
*   **Corporate Tie-ups**: Strong partnerships with 500+ corporate leaders ensure the curriculum stays updated.
*   **Intensive Pedagogy**: Known for long hours and rigorous mock interviews, ensuring students are "corporate-ready" by the end of Year 1.

### **Fee Structure:**
The total fee for the MBA/PGDM program is approx **₹9.45 Lakhs**. For students who are ready to work hard and want a guaranteed industry-ready profile, PIBM is an excellent value-for-money choice.
