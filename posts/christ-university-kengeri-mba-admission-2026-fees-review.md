The **Kengeri Campus** of Christ (Deemed to be University) in Bangalore is a beautiful, green 75-acre campus known for its serene learning environment. While slightly away from the urban chaos of the main campus, Kengeri houses some of the most specialized MBA tracks and executive programs, sharing a common centralized placement pool with the Central Campus.

### **Christ Kengeri MBA Admission Process 2026**
1.  **Entrance Exam**: Valid scores in **MAT (Composite 600+)**, **CAT (60+ percentile)**, **CMAT**, **XAT**, or **GMAT**.
2.  **Selection Path**: Mandatory **Micro Presentation (MP)**, **Group Discussion (GD)**, and a **Personal Interview (PI)**.
3.  **Application Deadline**: Round 1 applications typically close in late January 2026.

### **Expected Merit Requirements 2026**
Kengeri shares its cutoff pool with the central campus.

| Entrance Exam | Expected Cutoff (Score/Percentile) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **MAT** | 600+ Composite Score |
| **CAT** | 60 - 70 Percentile |
| **CMAT / XAT**| 60+ Percentile |
| **GMAT / GRE** | 450+ / 295+ |

### **Christ University Kengeri MBA Fee Structure 2026**
Fees for the Kengeri campus are comparable to the main campus, providing a high-value residential experience.
*   **Annual Tuition Fee**: approx. ₹5.7 Lakhs - ₹5.9 Lakhs.
*   **Total 2-Year Fees**: **₹11.4 Lakhs - ₹11.8 Lakhs**.
*   **Registration Fee**: ₹8,000 (non-refundable).
*   **Hostel Fee**: ₹80,000 to ₹1.2 Lakhs per year (separate on-campus hostels).

### **Placement Review: Common Pool Success**
The best feature of the Kengeri campus is its participation in the common centralized placement pool of Christ University.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹32.00 LPA (Overall Christ Univ Bangalore).
*   **Average Package (MBA Overall)**: ₹7.00 LPA to ₹9.5 LPA.
*   **Median Salary (2024)**: ₹9.86 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Deloitte, Bosch, Oracle, KPMG, Accenture, and Dell.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 90% for eligible graduates.

### **Review & Verdict**
**Pros**: Sprawling green campus (perfect for student life), modern infrastructure, same placement pool as the central campus, and excellent residential facilities.
**Cons**: Located slightly away from the city center, which means fewer local weekend hangout options compared to Hosur Road.

### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**
**1. Is Kengeri Campus good for MBA?**
Yes, it is excellent for those who prefer a campus-style experience with lush greenery and less city noise, while maintaining access to the same high-tier placement pool.

**2. Are placements in Kengeri different from the Central Campus?**
No, placements for the MBA program are centralized across the three Bangalore campuses.

**3. What is the dress code for MBA at Christ Kengeri?**
Exactly the same as other campuses; formal attire and professional grooming are strictly monitored.
