The **Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG 2026** is just around the corner, and millions of aspirants are eagerly waiting for the official subject-wise schedule. As per the latest trends and NTA notifications, the exam is set to be a marathon event spanning over two weeks.

If you are a Class 12 student aiming for top central universities like DU, BHU, or JNU, here is everything you need to know about the **CUET UG 2026 expected dates and schedule**.

## 1. CUET UG 2026 Official Exam Dates
The National Testing Agency (NTA) has already indicated that the CUET UG 2026 examination will be conducted between **May 11, 2026, and May 31, 2026**.

The exam will be held in multiple shifts (Morning, Afternoon, and Evening) depending on the number of candidates and subject combinations.

| **CUET UG Exam Window** | May 11 to May 31, 2026 |
| **City Intimation Slip** | Last Week of April 2026 |
| **Admit Card Release** | 2-3 Days Before Exam |
| **Answer Key Challenge** | Second Week of June 2026 |
| **CUET UG 2026 Result** | First Week of July 2026 |

## 2. When will the Subject-wise Datesheet be Released?
While the overall window is known, the **subject-wise datesheet**—which tells you exactly which day your History, Physics, or Commerce paper is scheduled—is typically released **10-15 days before the first exam**.

*   **Expected Date for Datesheet:** Between April 20 and April 25, 2026.
*   **Where to Check:** Official website [cuet.nta.nic.in](https://cuet.nta.nic.in).

The NTA generates a dynamic schedule to avoid clashes for students appearing in multiple domains.

---

## 3. CUET UG 2026 City Intimation Slip
The City Intimation Slip is a crucial document that is released before the admit card. It informs candidates about the city where their exam center will be located, allowing them to make travel arrangements in advance.

*   **Release Window:** April 25 to April 30, 2026.
*   **Note:** This is *not* the admit card. It only mentions the city, not the specific center name.

---

## 4. Admit Card Download Update
The **CUET 2026 Admit Card** will be released in phases. If your exam is on May 15, your admit card might be available by May 12 or 13.

### Steps to Download:
1. Visit the official NTA CUET portal.
2. Login with your **Application Number** and **Date of Birth**.
3. Click on 'Download Admit Card'.
4. Verify your exam center details and reporting time.

## 5. Important Instructions for Exam Day
*   **Self-Declaration Form:** Ensure you fill the self-declaration part on the admit card before reaching the center.
*   **Valid ID Proof:** Carry an original Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Passport.
*   **Reporting Time:** Reach the center at least 60 minutes before the gate closing time.
