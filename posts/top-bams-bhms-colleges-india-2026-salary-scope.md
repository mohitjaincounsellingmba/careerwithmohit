## 🚀 Career Scope & Salary in 2026

### 1. The BAMS Edge
BAMS doctors can now work as **Medical Officers** in many state government hospitals. Additionally, the private wellness industry (Patanjali, Dabur, Himalaya) hires BAMS graduates for R&D and clinical consulting.
- **New Trend:** BAMS graduates joining high-end Wellness Spas in the Middle East and Europe with packages crossing ₹20L+.

### 2. The BHMS Edge
Homeopathy remains the second most popular system of medicine in the world. BHMS doctors excel in long-term chronic disease management (Skin, Migraine, Auto-immune).
- **New Trend:** Specialized Homeopathic clinic chains (like Dr. Batra's) offer structured growth paths for fresh graduates.

## 🏗️ Admission Process 2026 (AACCC)

In 2026, admission to all BAMS/BHMS/BUMS courses is handled by the **AACCC (Ayush Admissions Central Counseling Committee)**.
- **15% All India Quota:** Handled by AACCC for Central Universities and National Institutes.
- **85% State Quota:** Handled by respective state AYUSH departments.
- **Mandatory:** NEET qualification is essential even for private BAMS/BHMS colleges.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. can a BAMS doctor perform surgery?**
Yes. In 2024-25, the Indian government officially permitted BAMS post-graduates (MS Shalya Tantra) to perform around 58 types of surgeries. However, they must be specialized in that specific field.

**Q2. is NEET required for BAMS in private colleges?**
YES. Since 2018, NEET has been mandatory for all AYUSH courses in India, including management quota seats in private colleges.

**Q3. What is the fee for BAMS in a private college?**
Private BAMS fees typically range from ₹2 Lakhs to ₹5 Lakhs per year. Government colleges are much cheaper, around ₹20,000 to ₹60,000 per year.

**Q4. can I practice Allopathy after BHMS?**
Strictly, NO. A BHMS doctor is trained in Homeopathy. However, some states allow AYUSH doctors to prescribe a limited set of allopathic medicines during emergencies (always check state-specific laws).

**Q5. When is the BAMS/BHMS counselling 2026?**
It usually starts in **September/October**, following the completion of the main MBBS/BDS counselling rounds.
