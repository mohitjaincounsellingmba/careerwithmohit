The Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP) is a highly specialized management entrance exam conducted exclusively for admission into the 16 MBA institutes operating under the Symbiosis International (Deemed) University (SIU). 

If you are aiming for prestigious campuses like SIBM Pune or SCMHRD Pune, cracking SNAP is your only way in.

Here is the ultimate guide to the SNAP exam for 2026 admissions:

## Exam Overview & Conducting Body

- **Exam Name:** Symbiosis National Aptitude Test (SNAP)
- **Conducting Body:** Symbiosis International (Deemed) University (SIU)
- **Frequency:** Once a year (Conducted across 3 completely independent test dates in December. Candidates can attempt the exam twice or thrice, and their best score will be considered).
- **Mode of Exam:** Computer Based Test (CBT)

## Exam Pattern & Total Marks

SNAP is famously known as a "test of speed." It gives you the least amount of time per question compared to any other MBA entrance exam in India.

- **Total Duration:** 60 Minutes (1 Hour)
  - *Note: There are no sectional time limits. You can switch between sections freely.*
- **Total Questions:** 60 Questions
- **Total Marks:** 60 Marks
- **Question Types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
- **Marking Scheme:**
  - +1 mark for every correct answer
  - -0.25 marks for every incorrect answer

## Sectional Breakdown

1. **General English (Reading Comprehension, Verbal Reasoning, Verbal Ability):** 15 Questions
2. **Analytical & Logical Reasoning (ALR):** 25 Questions (The primary make-or-break section)
3. **Quantitative, Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency (QA, DI-DS):** 20 Questions

*Note: SNAP has completely completely removed the General Knowledge/Current Affairs section from its structure.*

## Expected Cut-offs (Percentile)

Symbiosis institutes rely heavily on extremely high percentile groupings due to the 60-mark structure.

- **SIBM Pune (Flagship MBA):** 98.0+ to 98.5+ Percentile (~40 to 44 Marks)
- **SCMHRD Pune (Flagship MBA & HR):** 97.0+ to 97.5+ Percentile (~38 to 41 Marks)
- **SIIB Pune, SIBM Bangalore:** 90.0+ to 93.0+ Percentile (~35 Marks)
- **SSBF, SIOM Nashik, SICSR:** 80.0+ to 85.0+ Percentile

## Top Colleges Accepting SNAP Scores

SNAP scores are primarily accepted by the institutes under the Symbiosis umbrella. The top-ranked ones include:

- SIBM (Symbiosis Institute of Business Management), Pune
- SCMHRD (Symbiosis Centre for Management & Human Resource Development), Pune
- SIIB (Symbiosis Institute of International Business), Pune
- SIBM (Symbiosis Institute of Business Management), Bangalore
- SIOM (Symbiosis Institute of Operations Management), Nashik
- SSBF (Symbiosis School of Banking & Finance), Pune
- SIBM (Symbiosis Institute of Business Management), Hyderabad
