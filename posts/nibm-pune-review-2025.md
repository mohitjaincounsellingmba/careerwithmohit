The National Institute of Bank Management (NIBM) Pune, established by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), is the apex institution for research, training, and consultancy in banking. Its PGDM in Banking & Financial Services (B&FS) is a highly specialized program for aspiring finance professionals.

### **Quick Highlights (2025 Batch):**
*   **Avg Placement**: ₹14.10 LPA
*   **Highest Package**: ₹26.60 LPA
*   **Placement Rate**: 100% (Batch of 2025)
*   **Median Salary**: ₹15.00 LPA
*   **Entrance Exam**: CAT (80+) / CMAT (95+)

### **Why NIBM is Special:**
*   **Industry Recognition**: Since it's backed by the RBI, every major bank (public, private, and international) recognizes NIBM as a core talent source.
*   **Specialized Focus**: Unlike general MBAs, the entire curriculum is designed around the nuances of Banking, Risk Management, and Fintech.
*   **Top Recruiters**: KPMG, Axis Bank, Federal Bank, Bank of America, and HDFC Bank are regular visitors.

### **Fee Structure:**
The total academic fee (including hostel and alumni charges) is approximately **₹17.64 Lakhs**. With a guaranteed 100% placement track and a median salary of ₹15 LPA, it is a gold standard for Finance.
