## UPPSC PCS — The Scale of Opportunity

Unlike many state civil services, UPPSC PCS encompasses an extraordinarily wide range of posts. A single PCS notification often covers 300–500+ vacancies across:

- **PPS (Provincial Police Service):** Deputy Superintendent of Police
- **APS (Accounts):** Accounts Officers
- **BDO (Block Development Officer):** Rural Development Administration
- **SDM (Sub-Divisional Magistrate):** Revenue Administration
- **ACMO (Assistant Chief Medical Officer)**
- **District Supply Officer**
- **UP Cooperative Service**
- **District Education Officer**

This makes UPPSC PCS one of the richest in post variety among all state PSC exams.

## The UP Special GK Component — 30 Marks of Pure Advantage

The **UP Special GK** section (30 Q, 30 marks) is your secret weapon if you know it — and a deadly weakness if you don't:

### UP History (8–10 Q)
- Ancient UP: Hastinapur, Mathura (Buddhist/Jain sacred site), Sarnath (Buddhism), Varanasi's ancient importance
- Medieval UP: Kannauj (Pratiharas), Lucknow as Nawabs' capital, Awadhi culture
- Modern UP: 1857 revolt (Meerut, Kanpur's Bibighar massacre), Freedom fighters (Chandrashekhar Azad, Ram Prasad Bismil)
- Post-Independence: Formation of UP, CM list, key developmental milestones

### UP Economy & Development (8–10 Q)
- UP's agricultural profile: Sugarcane (India's largest producer), wheat, rice
- Industrial corridors: UP Defence Corridor (Lucknow–Agra), Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development Authority
- MSME and handicrafts: Chikankari embroidery (Lucknow), Brass items (Moradabad = "Pitaliya Nagar"), Carpet weaving (Bhadohi)
- Infrastructure: Purvanchal Expressway, Jewar International Airport, Ganga Expressway

### UP Culture & Administration (8–12 Q)
- Classical dances: Kathak (UP origin, Lucknow and Jaipur gharanas)
- Folk art: Ramleela, Nautanki (recognized by UNESCO), Birha folk singing
- UP Government: CM, Cabinet, Legislative Assembly, Legislative Council (UP is one of few states with bicameral legislature)
- UP Districts: 75 districts, 18 Divisions, 540 Tehsils
- UP Panchayati Raj: 3-tier system (Zila Panchayat, Kshetra Panchayat, Gram Panchayat)
