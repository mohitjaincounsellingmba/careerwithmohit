## 🏛️ The Icons: Anna Univ & Loyola

### 1. Anna University, (CEG Campus)
The College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG) is one of the oldest and most prestigious technical campuses in Asia. Their MCA program is treats as equivalent to many NITs.
- **Top Recruiters:** Zoho, PayPal, Visa, Caterpillar.
- **Admission Strategy:** You need a **99+ percentile in TANCET** to secure a seat here.

### 2. Loyola College, Chennai
Known for its academic rigor and its "Loyola Brand" in the corporate world. It is the preferred choice for students targeting management roles within tech companies.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is Anna University better than NITs for MCA?**
For **Placements in the South Indian Tech Corridor**, CEG Guindy is as good as any NIT. For **National placements**, NIT Trichy still holds the top spot.

**Q2. Does SRM Chennai require TANCET for MCA?**
No. SRM has its own entrance system, though they do consider TANCET scores for certain scholarships. Admission is primarily through its own portal.

**Q3. What is the average fee for MCA in government colleges in Chennai?**
Subsidized seats in Anna University cost around ₹30,000 to ₹45,000 per year. Private colleges range from ₹1.2 Lakhs to ₹3.5 Lakhs per year.

**Q4. Can I get a job at Zoho after doing MCA from Chennai?**
Yes. Zoho is one of the biggest recruiters at Anna University and PSG Tech for their development roles.

**Q5. When is the TANCET 2026 exam?**
Usually held in **March**. Registrations typically open in January/February.
