### **🔍 Step-by-Step Guide: How to Download MHCET MBA 2026 Answer Key**

Follow these exact steps to access your MAH MBA CET response sheet once the link is active on the official portal:

1.  **Visit the Official Website**: Go to the State CET Cell Maharashtra homepage: [cetcell.mahacet.org](https://cetcell.mahacet.org).
2.  **Access the Candidate Portal**: Click on the tab that says **'Candidate Registration A.Y 2026-27'** or look for the **'MAH-MBA/MMS-CET 2026'** specific link.
3.  **Login to Your Account**: Enter your **Application Number** and **Password** (or Date of Birth as set during registration).
4.  **Find Objection Tracking**: Once logged in, look for a section named **'Objection Tracking'** or **'View Response Sheet'**.
5.  **Download PDF**: Click on the 'Display' button next to your exam shift. Your response sheet will open in a new tab. Save it as a PDF for future reference.

### **📊 MAH MBA CET 2026 Marking Scheme**

Calculating your MHCET score is simpler than CAT or XAT because there is **no negative marking**. 

| Action | Marks Awarded |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Correct Answer** | +1 |
| **Incorrect Answer** | 0 |
| **Unattempted Question** | 0 |

### **🚩 How to Challenge the MHCET MBA Answer Key?**

If you believe a question in the official answer key is wrong, you can raise an objection through the portal.

*   **Challenge Fee**: Usually ₹1,000 per question (Non-refundable in most cases).
*   **Evidence**: You must provide a valid justification (textbook screenshot or solved logic) for your challenge.
*   **Deadline**: The objection window typically stays open for only 2-3 days.
