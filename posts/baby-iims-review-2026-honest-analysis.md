The term **"Baby IIMs"** has become one of the most searched and most debated topics in the MBA world. These are the newest generation of Indian Institutes of Management — established primarily between 2015 and 2016 — and they come with both the prestigious IIM tag and some very real limitations that aspirants must understand before making a life-changing decision.

This is a **complete, honest review** of all Baby IIMs — no hype, no sugarcoating.

---

### **Who Are the "Baby IIMs"? Understanding the Three Generations**

The 21 IIMs in India are broadly divided into three generations:

| Generation | IIMs | Avg. CAT Cutoff |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Old IIMs (BLACKI)** | IIM A, B, C, L, K, I | 98+ Percentile |
| **New IIMs** | IIM Shillong, Udaipur, Rohtak, Ranchi, Raipur, Trichy, Kashipur | 94–97 Percentile |
| **Baby IIMs** | IIM Amritsar, Bodh Gaya, Jammu, Nagpur, Sambalpur, Sirmaur, Visakhapatnam | 90–94 Percentile |

*IIM Mumbai (formerly NITIE) is a separate case — often treated as a New IIM due to its NITIE legacy.*

**Baby IIMs are the 7 IIMs established in 2015–2016**. They are currently in their 8th–10th batch and rapidly maturing.

### **The CAP (Common Admission Process) — What You Must Know**

Most Baby IIMs participate in the **CAP (Common Admission Process)**:

- After shortlisting by each IIM separately, you may receive CAP interview calls from multiple Baby IIMs simultaneously.
- **One CAP interview** is valid for all participating IIMs — you don't need to travel to each campus separately.
- CAP interviews are typically held in 5–6 cities across India (Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad).
- Each IIM then uses your CAP interview performance, CAT score, academics, and diversity parameters to create its own final merit list.

*IIM Nagpur and IIM Visakhapatnam typically run their own separate PI processes outside CAP.*

### **Pros and Cons of Baby IIMs — The Honest Truth**

**✅ Pros:**
- **The IIM brand tag** — this is the biggest asset. Recruiters, LinkedIn, clients, and senior managers recognise "IIM" immediately.
- **Government institution** — secure, regulated, no capitation fees, UGC recognised
- **Improving placements** — each batch typically sees better numbers than the previous one
- **Small batches** — better faculty-to-student ratio, more personalised learning
- **Alumni network growth** — young but rapidly expanding as older batches move into senior roles
- **CAP process** — single interview for multiple IIM calls = low effort, high potential

**❌ Cons:**
- **Location disadvantage** — IIM Sirmaur, Bodh Gaya are in low-corporate-density areas, limiting recruiter footfall
- **Infrastructure still developing** — some campuses still on temporary/transit campuses
- **Alumni clout gap** — can't match decades of OLD IIM alumni network yet
- **Placement volatility** — small batch sizes mean one bad year can skew averages significantly
- **Limited peer learning** — younger alumni means fewer "batch senior" connections in top roles

### **Is a Baby IIM Worth It? — The Verdict**

| Situation | Verdict |
| :--- | :--- |
| Your alternative is a good private B-school (MDI, SPJIMR, TAPMI, FORE) | **Depends on the specific IIM — compare packages carefully** |
| Your alternative is a lower-tier private B-school | **Baby IIM wins clearly** |
| You have a 96+ CAT percentile | **Retake CAT or target New/Old IIMs instead** |
| You are a fresher seeking MBA brand value | **Baby IIM is a very good choice** |
| You are an experienced professional (5+ years) | **Carefully evaluate ROI — an exec program may be better** |
| You are targeting IIM Nagpur or IIM Visakhapatnam | **Strong yes — mentored by IIM-A and IIM-B respectively** |
| You are targeting IIM Sirmaur | **Think carefully — lowest placement stats in the group** |

**Bottom line**: A Baby IIM degree is a gateway to a ₹14–17 LPA starting salary with the lifelong credibility of an IIM tag. If your CAT percentile sits in the 90–95 range and a top private B-school isn't in reach, a Baby IIM — especially IIM Nagpur or IIM Visakhapatnam — is an excellent decision.

### **How to Pick the Right Baby IIM for You**

1. **Check mentor IIM** — IIM-A mentored (Nagpur) and IIM-B mentored (Vizag) are top picks.
2. **Location matters** — pick metros or growing industrial cities for better recruiter access.
3. **Specialisation value** — IIM Amritsar (HR/BA), IIM Bodh Gaya (IPM), IIM Sirmaur (Tourism) have niche offerings worth evaluating.
4. **Read the latest placement report** — always download the official annual report, not third-party summaries.
5. **Attend GDPI prep seriously** — with CAT cutoffs at 90–94%, the WAT-PI round often differentiates the final selection.
