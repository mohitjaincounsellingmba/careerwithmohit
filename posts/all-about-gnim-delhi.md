### **College Review: GNIM Delhi**
*   **Gursikh Traditions**: Founded by the Delhi Sikh Gurdwara Management Committee, focusing on ethical and value-based education.
*   **Strategic Location**: Well-placed in Punjabi Bagh, West Delhi, with excellent accessibility.
*   **Academic Legacy**: A steady performer among the IPU-affiliated management colleges in Delhi.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Reported a strong **90% placement record** for the 2024 batch.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹15 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: **₹6 - 7 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: KPMG, Deloitte, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Wipro, and Infosys.
*   **Support Cell**: Proactive in organizing placement drives and student training sessions.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Quality Block**: Functional labs, air-conditioned classrooms, and a well-stocked library.
*   **Campus Life**: Includes an open-air theatre and clean canteens for student interaction.
*   **Technology**: Campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity and updated management workshops.
*   **Environment**: While lacking a large playground, the campus is clean and professionally managed.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **PhD Holders**: Large number of faculty members are highly qualified Ph.D. scholars.
*   **Supportive Staff**: Known for assisting students with practical knowledge and case research.
*   **Ethical Focus**: Incorporates values and ethics into the core management teaching methodology.

**Final Verdict**: A good value-for-money option for students in Delhi tracking stable placements and an ethical learning environment.
