## Top Central Universities Accepting CUET UG 2026

Central Universities are funded by the Ministry of Education and offer massive campus infrastructure, brilliant faculties, and extremely low tuition fees. These are the most sought-after **CUET UG colleges**.

### 1. Delhi University (DU)
DU is the prime target for 90% of CUET aspirants. It comprises prestigious colleges like:
- St. Stephen’s College
- Hindu College
- Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC)
- Miranda House
- Lady Shri Ram College (LSR)
- Hansraj College

### 2. Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi
BHU is one of the largest residential universities in Asia, famous for its B.A., B.Sc., B.Com, and B.A. LLB programs. It requires a combination of Language and General Test for most courses.

### 3. Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi
JNU is renowned globally for its specialized B.A. (Hons) in Foreign Languages and integrated programs.

### 4. Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI), New Delhi
JMI accepts CUET UG scores for selected undergraduate courses including B.A. (Hons) Economics, B.Sc. Biotechnology, and B.A. (Hons) History.

### 5. Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), Aligarh
AMU utilizes CUET for courses like B.A. (Hons) Sanskrit, Persian, Hindi, and B.Voc programs.

### Other Notable Central Universities:
- University of Allahabad
- Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University (BBAU), Lucknow
- Central University of Rajasthan
- Central University of Haryana
- Visva-Bharati University, West Bengal

## How to Apply to CUET UG Colleges?

**CRITICAL STEP:** Simply appearing for the CUET exam is **NOT** enough. You must apply individually to the universities.

1. **Fill the CUET Form:** Select your desired universities and courses while filling the NTA CUET application form. Ensure you select the correct subject mapping required by the university.
2. **Take the Exam:** Attempt the Language, Domain Subjects, and General Test as required.
3. **University Portals:** Once results are declared, you MUST visit the individual university websites (e.g., DU CSAS portal) and fill out their specific counseling application form.
4. **Counseling & Merit List:** Universities will release their merit lists based on your normalized CUET score.
