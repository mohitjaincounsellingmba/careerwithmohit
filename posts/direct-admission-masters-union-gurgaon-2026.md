Masters Union, located in the Cyber Park hub of Gurgaon, is disrupting management education with its "un-business school" model. Led by CXOs and industry veterans rather than traditional professors, it has become a top choice for students eyeing a career in startups and tech. For the 2026-28 batch, **Direct Admission in Masters Union** is centered around profile quality and entrepreneurship potential.

## Why Masters Union is Revolutionary?

- **Practitioner-Led**: Classes are taught by CEOs of top firms and unicorn founders.
- **Venture Fund**: Students manage a real venture fund, gaining hands-on investment experience.
- **Placements**: Known for sector-leading salaries, with an average package of **₹33+ LPA** for the latest batch.

## How Direct Admission Works at Masters Union

Masters Union does not follow the traditional CAT-only merit system. Instead, it offers a holistic **Profile-Based Selection**, which functions as a direct entry for high-potential candidates.

### 1. Comprehensive Profile Assessment
The school looks for diverse backgrounds—founders, creators, consultants, and artists. If you have built a business or have an exceptional achievement, you have a direct advantage.

### 2. Entrance Score Flexibility
While they accept CAT, GMAT, GRE, or NMAT, candidates can also take the **MU-SAT (Masters Union Scholarship & Admission Test)**. This is a direct institutional entrance that allows you to prove your aptitude even if you missed other exams.

### 3. Management & NRI Category
Masters Union reserves specialized seats for students from diverse geographies and backgrounds. This institutional quota ensures a mix of industry-sponsored candidates and international profile holders.

## Fee Structure for 2026
The total fees for the 16-month PGP program is approximately **₹22 Lakhs to ₹25 Lakhs**. For the 2026-28 batch, scholarships are heavily integrated for merit and need-based applicants.

## Selection Strategy for 2026

1. **Craft a Story**: Your application essays and video essays are more important than your scores. Mention your entrepreneurial ventures or side hustles.
2. **Focus on MU-SAT**: If your CAT score is below 80, prepare specifically for the MU-SAT to secure your admission.
3. **Early Rounds**: Apply in Round 1 or 2 (typically ends in January) as the seats fill up remarkably fast.
