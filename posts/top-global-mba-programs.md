## 2. Europe: Global Diversity & High ROI
European MBA programs are famously diverse and are known for their rapid, intense 10-15 month curriculums. This means you enter the workforce faster, significantly reducing your opportunity cost.

*   **INSEAD (France/Singapore):** The "Business School for the World," offering a top-ranked 10-month program.
*   **London Business School (LBS):** The ultimate gateway to London’s finance and consulting hubs.
*   **HEC Paris:** Renowned for luxury management and high-end strategy consulting.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Which is better: 1-year or 2-year MBA?
1-year MBAs (popular in Europe and at ISB) offer faster ROI and lower opportunity cost. 2-year MBAs (standard in the USA) provide more time for a summer internship, which is crucial if you are making a radical career pivot.

### 2. Can I get a full scholarship for a global MBA?
Yes, but they are highly competitive. Schools like Stanford and HBS offer need-based aid, while others provide merit-based fellowships. Strong GMAT scores (685+ Focus) and unique life experiences are key.

### 3. Does ISB count as a global MBA?
Absolutely. While located in India, ISB’s pedagogy, global faculty, and international exchange programs make it a world-class institution. It is consistently ranked among the top 30-40 B-schools globally by Financial Times.

---

**Decision Tip**: If your goal is to work in the USA, a 2-year US program is superior due to the **STEM-OPT** extension which allows for a 3-year work permit.
