## 🏛️ The Icons: PSG Tech & Amrita

### 1. PSG College of Technology
The undisputed leader in the region. PSG Tech is known for its legendary "Sandwich Courses" (Industry-Integrated) and has one of the strongest alumni networks in the manufacturing and mechanical world.
- **Top Recruiters:** Microsoft, Google, Qualcomm, Caterpillar, and L&T.
- **Selection:** You need a **99+ percentile in TNEA (195+ Cutoff)** to secure a seat here.

### 2. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham
Consistently ranked among the top 10 engineering universities in India by NIRF. Amrita’s Coimbatore campus is the flagship headquarters and is world-renowned for its Research hubs and AI/ML labs.
- **Admission Strategy:** Prepare for **AEEE 2026** or use your **JEE Main 2026** score for the all-India quota.

### 3. Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT)
A heritage institute supported by a massive industrial group. CIT is famous for its rigorous academic curriculum and high placement percentage in core engineering roles.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. is PSG Tech better than some NITs?**
In terms of **Mechanical and Production Engineering**, PSG Tech is considered on par with Top-5 NITs. For **CSE/IT**, Top-3 NITs still hold a higher national brand.

**Q2. Does Coimbatore have good B.Tech CSE colleges?**
Yes. Amrita and PSG iTech are exceptional for CSE. Specialized institutes like **Sri Krishna (SKCET)** also have very strong ties with IT service giants like Amazon and TCS.

**Q3. What is the fee for B.Tech in Government College of Technology (GCT)?**
GCT is a pure government college. The fees are incredibly low, often staying below **₹10,000 to ₹15,000 per year**, making it one of the best ROI colleges in India.

**Q4. Can North Indian students get admission in Coimbatore via TNEA?**
Generally, TNEA is for Tamil Nadu domicile students. However, private universities like **Amrita and Karunya** are open to all-India students through their own entrance exams or JEE Main.

**Q5. When is the TNEA 2026 counselling?**
Usually starts in **June/July** after the 12th board results are announced.
