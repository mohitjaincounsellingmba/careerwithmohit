Jaipur, the Pink City, is not just a tourist hub but also a thriving center for higher education in North India. With the **CUET PG 2026** score becoming a standard for PG admissions, students in Jaipur can now access some of the best management, technical, and arts programs.

If you are a student targeting **Jaipur for the 2026-27 academic session**, here is the most comprehensive guide to colleges accepting CUET PG 2026 scores.

---

### **1. Central University of Rajasthan (Near Jaipur)**
Technically located in Kishangarh (near Jaipur), CURAJ is the top choice for any CUET PG aspirant in Rajasthan.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MSc (Physics/Chemistry), MA (Economics).
*   **Admission Process:** Entirely based on CUET PG scores followed by their own counseling portal.
*   **Why Choose CURAJ?** Low fees, central university tag, and excellent research facilities.

### **2. Jagan Nath University (Jaipur)**
A well-established private university in Jaipur known for its industry-oriented programs.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA.
*   **Admission Process:** They accept CUET PG scores for admissions to their postgraduate programs alongside other national entrance tests.

### **3. Vivekananda Global University (VGU) (Jaipur)**
VGU is famous for its modern infrastructure and diverse course offerings.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MCA, MA (Design), MSc.
*   **Why Choose VGU?** Strong placement record and vibrant campus life.

### **4. Suresh Gyan Vihar University (SGVU) (Jaipur)**
One of the first private universities in Jaipur to get NAAC 'A+' accreditation.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA in Digital Marketing, MCA, MA.
*   **Admission Process:** SGVU considers CUET PG scores as part of their eligibility criteria for several PG programs.

### **5. Mody University (Jaipur/Laxmangarh)**
While its main campus is in Laxmangarh, Mody University is a preferred choice for female aspirants in the Jaipur region.
*   **Top Programs:** MBA, MSc, MA.

### **6. Mewar University**
Located near Jaipur/Chittorgarh, Mewar University is a large private institution that participates extensively in the CUET PG framework.

---

### **Why Jaipur for PG?**
*   **Affordability:** Compared to Delhi NCR or Mumbai, living costs in Jaipur are significantly lower, making it a budget-friendly option for students.
*   **Emerging Economy:** With several IT parks and startups moving to Jaipur, the demand for MBA and MCA graduates is on the rise.
*   **Rich Culture:** Studying in a city with such a deep historical and cultural heritage provides a unique learning environment.
