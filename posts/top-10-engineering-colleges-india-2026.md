## #1 — IIT Madras, Chennai

**NIRF Rank: #1 (2019–2024, 6 consecutive years)**

IIT Madras is India's most consistently top-ranked institution. Located in a unique forest campus in Chennai, it has world-class research output, the most spin-off startups of any Indian university, and stellar international placement packages.

- **Founded:** 1959 | **Campus:** 617 acres
- **JEE Advanced CSE Cutoff:** ~150 CRL
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.5 Lakhs
- **Avg CSE Package:** ₹20–30 LPA | **Highest:** ₹1.8 Cr (international)
- **Notable Alumni:** Sundar Pichai (Google CEO), N. Chandrasekaran (Tata Group Chairman)
- **Research Output:** 4,000+ research papers/year; 50+ active startups via IITM Incubation Cell

## #2 — IIT Delhi, New Delhi

**NIRF Rank: #2 (consistent)**

IIT Delhi's location in India's capital gives it unparalleled access to government policy, corporate headquarters, and international research collaborations. It is the most sought-after IIT for students targeting consulting, finance, and policy careers.

- **Founded:** 1961 | **Campus:** 320 acres (Hauz Khas)
- **JEE Advanced CSE Cutoff:** ~120 CRL
- **Annual Fee:** ₹2.5 Lakhs
- **Avg CSE Package:** ₹20–28 LPA | **Highest:** ₹2.8 Cr (Google, Uber international)
- **Notable Alumni:** Raghuram Rajan (Former RBI Governor), Vinod Khosla (Sun Microsystems Founder)

## #3 — IIT Bombay, Mumbai

**NIRF Rank: #3 — but #1 for placements and entrepreneurship**

IIT Bombay has India's most active startup ecosystem (connects to Mumbai's fintech and VC ecosystem) and consistently records India's highest placement packages. Microsoft Japan's ₹3.67 Crore offer came to an IIT Bombay student in 2024.

- **Founded:** 1958 | **Campus:** 550 acres (Powai Lake campus)
- **JEE Advanced CSE Cutoff:** ~100 CRL
- **Avg CSE Package:** ₹22–35 LPA | **Highest:** ₹3.67 Cr
- **Notable Alumni:** Nandan Nilekani (Infosys), Kumar Mangalam Birla (Aditya Birla Group)

## #4 — IIT Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh

**NIRF Rank: #4 — India's best for Computer Science research**

IIT Kanpur pioneered Computer Science education in India (India's first CS programme, 1963) and remains among the top globally for CS theory, algorithms, and AI research.

- **Founded:** 1959 | **JEE CSE Cutoff:** ~210 CRL
- **Avg CSE Package:** ₹18–28 LPA
- **Research Strength:** AI, Quantum Computing, Cybersecurity, Aerospace

## #5 — IIT Kharagpur, West Bengal

**NIRF Rank: #5 — India's largest and oldest IIT**

IIT Kharagpur (est. 1951) is India's first IIT and largest campus (2,100+ acres). It offers the most diverse range of B.Tech programmes including Naval Architecture, Ocean Engineering, and Agricultural Engineering.

- **JEE CSE Cutoff:** ~350 CRL
- **Avg CSE Package:** ₹16–25 LPA
- **Notable Alumni:** Sundar Pichai (Google), Vinod Gupta (InfoGroup Founder)

## #6 — IIT Roorkee, Uttarakhand

**NIRF Rank: #6 — India's best Civil Engineering + Heritage campus**

IIT Roorkee is the oldest technical university in Asia (est. 1847 as Thomason College). Its Civil Engineering, Architecture, and Earthquake Engineering departments are world-class.

- **JEE CSE Cutoff:** ~450 CRL
- **Avg CSE Package:** ₹16–22 LPA | **Civil Engineering:** India's NIRF #1

## FAQs — Top Engineering Colleges India 2026

**Q1. Which is the #1 engineering college in India in 2026?**
IIT Madras has been ranked #1 by NIRF for 6 consecutive years. For placements, IIT Bombay is often considered #1. For pure computer science prestige, IIT Delhi is #1 among employers.

**Q2. Is BITS Pilani better than NITs for engineering?**
BITS Pilani's placements for CSE and ECE are generally comparable or better than the top NITs (Trichy, Warangal). Its Practice School gives it a significant advantage for industry exposure.

**Q3. What is the total fee for B.Tech at IITs?**
All IITs charge ₹2.5 Lakhs per year (₹10 Lakhs total for 4 years). Students with parental income below ₹5 Lakhs get full tuition waiver. These are among the lowest fees for a world-class engineering education anywhere.

**Q4. Can I get into top 10 engineering colleges without JEE?**
No — the top 9 (IITs and NIT Trichy) require JEE Advanced or JEE Main. Only BITS Pilani (#10) has its own entrance exam (BITSAT) — making it the only top-10 college accessible without a JEE score.

**Q5. Which IIT should I prefer if I have rank 1,000 in JEE Advanced?**
With a JEE Advanced rank of ~1,000, you can target IIT Kharagpur CSE, IIT Roorkee CSE, or IIT Guwahati CSE. Check the latest JoSAA opening/closing ranks for the most accurate guidance.
