The Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT) is widely considered one of the toughest management entrance exams in India, often perceived as more rigorous than CAT due to its unique "Decision Making" section. It serves as the exclusive gateway to XLRI Jamshedpur, a Mecca for HR and Business Management.

Here is the complete breakdown of the XAT exam for 2026 admissions:

## Exam Overview & Conducting Body

- **Exam Name:** Xavier Aptitude Test (XAT)
- **Conducting Body:** XLRI (Xavier School of Management), Jamshedpur on behalf of XAMI (Xavier Association of Management Institutes).
- **Frequency:** Once a year (Typically the first Sunday of January)
- **Mode of Exam:** Computer Based Test (CBT)

## Exam Pattern & Total Marks

XAT is known for its lengthy reading passages and its unique inclusion of an essay writing component.

- **Total Duration:** 210 Minutes (3.5 Hours)
- **Total Questions:** 100 to 105 Questions (varies slightly each year)
- **Total Marks:** Approximately 100 - 105 Marks (1 mark per question for initial sections)
- **Question Types:** Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) and one descriptive Essay.
- **Marking Scheme:**
  - +1 mark for every correct answer
  - -0.25 marks for every incorrect answer
  - -0.10 marks for consecutive unattempted questions beyond 8 skips

## Sectional Breakdown

**Part 1 (Time Limit: 175 Minutes - Negative Marking Applies):**
1. **Verbal and Logical Ability (VALR):** ~26 Questions
2. **Decision Making (DM):** ~21 Questions (The defining feature of XAT)
3. **Quantitative Ability & Data Interpretation (QADI):** ~28 Questions

**Part 2 (Time Limit: 30 Minutes - No Negative Marking):**
1. **General Knowledge (GK):** ~25 Questions
2. **Analytical Essay Writing (AEW):** 1 Question (Evaluated only if you clear Part 1 cutoffs)

## Expected Cut-offs (Percentile)

- **XLRI Jamshedpur (Business Management):** 96.0+ Percentile (Male) / 91.0+ Percentile (Female)
- **XLRI Jamshedpur (Human Resource Management):** 93.0+ Percentile (Male) / 90.0+ Percentile (Female)
- **XIMB, IMT Ghaziabad, IMI New Delhi:** 90.0+ Percentile
- **GIM Goa, Great Lakes Chennai, TAPMI:** 80.0+ to 85.0+ Percentile

## Top Colleges Accepting XAT Scores

- XLRI Jamshedpur & XLRI Delhi-NCR
- XIMB (Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar)
- IMT (Institute of Management Technology), Ghaziabad
- IMI (International Management Institute), New Delhi
- GIM (Goa Institute of Management)
- TAPMI (T. A. Pai Management Institute), Manipal
- Great Lakes Institute of Management, Chennai
- K. J. Somaiya Institute of Management, Mumbai
- FORE School of Management, New Delhi
