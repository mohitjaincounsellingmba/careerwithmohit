FOSTIIMA Business School, located in New Delhi, stands out for a very specific reason: it was founded by the alumni of **IIM Ahmedabad**. The name itself stands for "Friends Of Seventy Three batch of IIMA." If you are looking for a top-tier PGDM program in Delhi NCR with strong corporate networking, FOSTIIMA is a serious contender.

Here is a detailed breakdown of everything you need to know about FOSTIIMA Business School for 2026 admissions:

## Key Highlights

- **Establishment:** 2007 (Founded by IIM Ahmedabad Alumni)
- **Program Offered:** PGDM (AICTE Approved)
- **Legacy & USP:** The core faculty and management predominantly consist of IIM Ahmedabad alumni, bringing IIM-level pedagogy to the classroom.

## Admission Details

- **Entrance Exams Accepted:** CAT, XAT, CMAT, MAT
- **Expected Cut-off (Percentile):**
  - CAT/XAT: 60 - 65+ 
  - CMAT/MAT: 70 - 75+
- **Total Fees:** ₹11.25 Lakhs (for the 2-year PGDM program)

## Specializations Offered

FOSTIIMA offers core industry-relevant dual specializations:

- Marketing Management
- Financial Management
- Human Resource Management
- Operations Management
- International Business

## Placement Statistics (2025-2026)

FOSTIIMA has a strong track record of placements, heavily leveraged by its pan-India IIMA alumni network.

- **Highest Placement Package:** ₹25.00 LPA
- **Average Placement Package:** ₹10.76 LPA
- **Lowest Placement Package:** ₹7.00 LPA
- **Top Recruiters:** Deloitte, ICICI Bank, Asian Paints, Bajaj Capital, ITC, HDFC Bank, Naukri.com, Wipro, and Kotak Mahindra Bank.

## Student Reviews & Verdict

**What Students Say:**
- **Pros:** Excellent faculty (many from IIMs and IITs), strong focus on practical corporate exposure, and highly dedicated placement cell. The location in Delhi provides good industry interaction.
- **Cons:** The campus is relatively compact compared to older, more established university campuses. 

**Our Verdict:** FOSTIIMA is a high-ROI B-school in Delhi NCR. If you have an average percentile in CAT/CMAT and want to study under IIM alumni with an assured package of around ₹10 LPA, FOSTIIMA is an excellent choice.
