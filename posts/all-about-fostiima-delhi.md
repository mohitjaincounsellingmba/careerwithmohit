### **College Review: FOSTIIMA Delhi**
*   **Unique Identity**: Founded and managed by IIM alumni, focusing on delivering IIM-style curriculum at an affordable cost.
*   **Academic Rigor**: High focus on case studies and practical management drills.
*   **Connectivity**: Located in Dwarka, offering excellent Metro connectivity for local students.

### **Placement Review (2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Reported **100% placements** for the recent PGDM batch.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹30 LPA**.
*   **Average Package**: **₹11.15 LPA**.
*   **Median Package**: **₹11.6 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: ICICI Bank, Deloitte, Asian Paints, BlackRock, and Khimji Ramdas.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Modern Campus**: Compact but well-designed campus with a corporate feel.
*   **Classrooms**: Fully air-conditioned halls with smart-learning equipment.
*   **Labs & Library**: Well-equipped computer labs and a focused library for management research.
*   **Auditoriums**: Used for regular guest lectures and corporate workshops.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **IIM Legacy**: Majority of the faculty members are PhD holders from IIMs and other premier global institutions.
*   **Case-Based Teaching**: Heavy emphasis on Harvard/IIM case study methodologies.
*   **Mentorship**: Personalized grooming by senior industry leaders and alumni.

**Final Verdict**: FOSTIIMA is an excellent choice for students looking for high-quality, IIM-style education and superior placements without the massive fees of top-tier schools.
