Here is a full review of the MUJ MBA program covering the latest 2026 fee structure, placement stats, and cutoffs.

---

### **Admission Process & Cutoffs**

Admission to the MUJ MBA program is transparent and depends heavily on entrance scores and subsequent assessment rounds.

1.  **Entrance Exam**: Candidates must have a valid score in [CAT](/posts/all-about-cat-exam), [MAT](/posts/all-about-mat-exam), [CMAT](/posts/all-about-cmat-exam), XAT, or GMAT.
2.  **Cutoffs (Expected)**:
    *   **CAT**: ~25+ Percentile
    *   **MAT**: ~35-40+ Percentile
3.  **Further Rounds**: English Comprehension Test followed by a Personal Interview (PI).
4.  **Eligibility**: Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% aggregate.
