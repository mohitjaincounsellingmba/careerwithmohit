**MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU)**, located in the prestigious Kothrud area of Pune, is one of the most popular private engineering universities in India. Formerly known as MIT Pune, it rebranded into a university and is renowned for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, high-quality faculty, and strong corporate connections.

If you are an engineering aspirant looking at **MIT-WPU B.Tech 2026 admission**, here is everything you need to know about the process, cutoffs, and ROI.

### **MIT-WPU Admission Channels 2026**
MIT-WPU does not participate in the common CAP rounds of DTE Maharashtra. Instead, you must apply directly:
1.  **JEE Main 2026**: High priority for All India seats.
2.  **MHT-CET 2026**: Primary route for Maharashtra domicile students.
3.  **MIT-WPU CET**: The university's own entrance exam for direct admission.

### **Expected Cutoffs 2026 (General Merit)**
The competition for Computer Science (CSE) at MIT-WPU is very high.

| Branch | JEE Main Percentile (Expected) | MHT-CET Percentile (Expected) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computer Science (CSE)** | 92.0 - 94.5 | 94.0 - 96.5 |
| **Information Tech (IT)** | 90.0 - 92.5 | 92.0 - 94.5 |
| **Electronics (ECE)** | 85.0 - 88.0 | 88.0 - 91.0 |
| **Mechanical Engineering** | 70.0 - 75.0 | 75.0 - 80.0 |

### **MIT-WPU B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
The fees at MIT-WPU vary slightly based on the specialization.

| Fee Component | Approx. Annual Amount |
| :--- | :--- |
| **CSE Tuition Fee** | ₹3.5 Lakhs - ₹3.75 Lakhs |
| **Other Core Branches** | ₹2.5 Lakhs - ₹3.0 Lakhs |
| **Total 4-Year Cost (Approx.)** | **₹13.5 Lakhs - ₹15.5 Lakhs** |

*Hostel and mess fees are extra, with premium residential options starting from ₹1.5 Lakhs per year.*

### **Placement Review: A Leading Career Hub**
MIT-WPU is known for its strong placement cell, which provides 100% placement assistance.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026 Batch**: ₹51.36 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹7.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (CSE/IT)**: ₹9.5 LPA to ₹11.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: IBM, Microsoft, Amazon, Deloitte, Capgemini, TCS, and Wipro.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 85% for eligible graduates.

### **Why Choose MIT-WPU?**
*   **Prime Location**: Located in Kothrud, Pune, which is one of the best areas for students and young professionals.
*   **Autonomous Curriculum**: The university updates its syllabus annually based on the latest industry trends.
*   **Global Exposure**: MIT-WPU has tie-ups with numerous international universities for research and exchange programs.
