## 🛣️ The Pathway to a Bangalore MBA (Without Exam Stress)

Direct admission doesn't mean "any student." Colleges look for candidates who will represent their brand well in the corporate world of Bangalore.

### Eligibility Checklist:
- **Graduation**: Minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).
- **Entrance Presence**: Even if your score is low, a valid MAT/ATMA/CAT/KMAT scorecard is often mandatory for verification.
- **Statement of Purpose (SOP)**: In Bangalore, your "Why MBA?" matters more than your "What is 2+2?".
