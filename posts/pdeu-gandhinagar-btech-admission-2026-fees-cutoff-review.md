**Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU)**, formerly known as **PDPU**, is a world-class institution located in the capital city of Gujarat, Gandhinagar. Recognized as a "Centre of Excellence" by the Government of Gujarat and accredited with an **NAAC A++ grade**, it is one of the top choices for students interested in Energy, Petroleum, and Information Technology.

If you are aiming for the **PDEU B.Tech 2026 batch**, here is the complete breakdown of the admission process, expected cutoffs, and fees.

### **PDEU Admission Process 2026**
Like its neighbor DA-IICT, PDEU does not participate in JoSAA. The seats are allocated as follows:
1.  **All India Seats (60%)**: Based on **JEE Main 2026** All India Rank. Applications must be submitted directly on the [PDEU admission portal](https://www.pdpu.ac.in).
2.  **Gujarat State Seats (25%)**: Based on **GUJCET 2026** performance. Counselling is managed by the **ACPC (Gujarat)**.
3.  **NRI / NRI Sponsored Seats (15%)**: For international and NRI-supported candidates.

### **Expected JEE Main Cutoffs 2026 (All India Rank)**
Based on the 2025 results, here are the target ranks for the major B.Tech branches at PDEU:

| Branch Name | Expected JEE Main Closing Rank |

| **Chemical Engineering** | 32,000 - 40,000 |
| **Petroleum Engineering** | 45,000 - 55,000 |

### **PDEU Fee Structure 2026**
PDEU offers a premium learning environment, and its fees are structured on a per-semester basis.

| Fee Component | Amount (Approx.) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tuition Fee (Per Semester)** | ₹1,36,000 |
| **Other Charges (Exam/Library etc.)** | ₹15,000 |
| **Total Annual Tuition Fee** | **₹2.8 Lakhs - ₹3.0 Lakhs** |
| **Hostel & Mess (Annual)** | ₹1.0 Lakhs - ₹1.2 Lakhs |

*Total course cost over 4 years is approximately ₹11.5 Lakhs to ₹12.5 Lakhs.*

### **Placement Review: A Hub for Energy & Tech**
PDEU consistently demonstrates strong placement records, especially in its core energy sectors and booming IT departments.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026**: ₹42.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (Computer/ICT)**: ₹9.01 LPA to ₹10.5 LPA.
*   **Average Package (Overall)**: ₹7.50 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Shell, ONGC, GSPC, Reliance, JP Morgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, and Google.

### **What Makes PDEU Unique?**
*   **Petroleum Focus**: It is one of the few institutes in India with a dedicated School of Petroleum Technology (SPT), offering world-class research in upstream and downstream oil sectors.
*   **Strategic Location**: Located in Gandhinagar, adjacent to the GIFT City, providing students with immense networking and internship opportunities.
*   **Research Infrastructure**: The university has spent over ₹100 Crores on R&D labs and renewable energy research centers.
