**D.Y. Patil International University (DYPIU)**, part of the legendary D.Y. Patil Group, is a modern, research-oriented university in Pune (Akurdi). Known for its forward-thinking curriculum and industry collaborations, DYPIU has quickly become a center for students who want to specialize in high-growth tech areas like Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Bioengineering.

If you are an engineering aspirant looking at **DYPIU Pune B.Tech 2026 admission**, here is the complete breakdown of the process, fees, and placements.

### **DYPIU Admission Channels 2026**
DYPIU conducts its own multi-entrance admission process:
1.  **JEE Main / MHT-CET**: Valid for all B.Tech seats.
2.  **PERA CET**: Common entrance for private universities in Maharashtra.
3.  **DYPIU Entrance Test**: For direct admission and scholarship.
4.  **CUET-UG**: Accepted for specific specializations.

### **B.Tech Specializations at DYPIU**
The university is uniquely focused on modern technological tracks:
*   B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Core)
*   B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML)
*   B.Tech in Data Science (ICT)
*   B.Tech in Bioengineering
*   B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (Robotics)

### **DYPIU B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
The fees at DYPIU vary based on the specialization. **Computer Science (CSE)** is the most sought-after.

| Fee Component | Approx. Annual Amount |
| :--- | :--- |
| **CSE Tuition Fee** | ₹2,78,000 |
| **Bioengineering / Mechanical** | ₹2,35,000 |
| **Total 4-Year Cost (Approx.)** | **₹10.7 Lakhs - ₹11.5 Lakhs** |

*Hostel fees at DY Patil Akurdi range from ₹1.2 Lakhs to ₹1.6 Lakhs per year.*

### **Placement Review: A Hub for Future Talent**
DYPIU has a strong placement division that focuses on grooming students for product-based companies.
*   **Highest Package (2025/2026 Batch)**: ₹33.00 LPA.
*   **Average Package (B.Tech Overall)**: ₹7.50 LPA.
*   **Average Package (CSE/IT)**: ₹8.5 LPA to ₹10.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Infosys, Wipro, TCS, IBM, FIS Global, Bosch, Capgemini, and Amazon.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 80% for eligible graduates.

### **Why Choose DYPIU Pune?**
*   **Holistic Evaluation**: Admission isn't just about marks; it includes a **Personal Interaction (PI)** to assess student potential.
*   **Research Culture**: The university encourages students to start research projects as early as their 2nd year.
*   **Akurdi Campus**: Centrally located in Pune's industrial hub, providing easy access to internships.
