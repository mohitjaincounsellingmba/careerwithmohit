## How to Check SRMJEEE 2026 Result

Once the result is declared, follow these steps:

1. Go to the official website: **srmist.edu.in**
2. Click on **"Admissions"** → **"SRMJEEE 2026 Result"**
3. Enter your **Application Number** and **Password / Date of Birth**
4. Click **Submit**
5. Your **Rank Card / Scorecard** will appear on screen
6. Download and save your rank card as a PDF

> 📧 SRM also sends result notifications to your **registered email ID** — check your inbox and spam folder.

## What Details Will the SRMJEEE Rank Card Show?

Your rank card will contain:
- **Overall Rank** (All India)
- **Subject-wise marks** (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Aptitude, English)
- **Total Score** (out of 130)
- **Category Rank** (if applicable)
- Candidate's personal details and photo

## Score vs Campus — What Rank Gets You Where?

Use this as a general guide (based on previous years):

| Score Range | Likely Campus Allotment |
|---|---|
| **100+** | SRM Kattankulathur (KTR), Chennai — CSE / ECE |
| **85–99** | SRM Ramapuram / Vadapalani |
| **70–84** | SRM Delhi-NCR / Other branches at KTR |
| **Below 70** | Consider Direct Admission / Management Quota |

> **Note:** Cutoffs vary by branch. CSE at KTR is the most competitive. Branches like Civil, Mechanical have lower cutoffs.

## What to Do After SRMJEEE 2026 Result?

### If Your Rank Is Good:
1. **Register for SRM Counselling** — log in to the admission portal immediately after result.
2. **Pay the Counselling Fee** (non-refundable seat booking amount).
3. **Choose your campus and branch** in order of preference.
4. **Submit documents** — Class 10 & 12 marksheets, ID proof, passport-size photos.
5. **Pay the first semester fee** to confirm your seat.

### If Your Rank Is Lower Than Expected:
- You can **apply for Phase 2** of SRMJEEE if you appeared in Phase 1.
- Explore **Direct/Management Quota Admission** at SRM — seats are available without exam score.
- Consider other top private universities like VIT, Manipal, or Bennett.
