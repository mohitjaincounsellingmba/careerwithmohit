### **College Review: ITS Ghaziabad**
*   **Educational Legacy**: One of the oldest and most established management institutes in Ghaziabad.
*   **ROI Focus**: Known for providing high-value management education at an affordable cost with strong salary returns.
*   **Strategic Location**: Situated on GT Road, making it very accessible for students from Delhi, Noida, and UP.

### **Placement Review (2024-2025 Batch)**
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently aiming for and achieving the **100% placement mark**.
*   **Highest Package**: **₹12 - 15 LPA** (Domestic).
*   **Average Package**: **₹5 - 7 LPA**.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Major MNCs, FMCG giants, and BFSI leaders approach the campus annually.
*   **Supportive Cell**: The placement team is highly recognized for helping students find roles in their desired specializations.

### **Infrastructure & Facilities**
*   **Comprehensive Campus**: Features air-conditioned classrooms, multiple auditoriums, and seminar halls.
*   **Resources**: Large library with a significant collection of books and computer labs with high-speed internet.
*   **Sports & Wellness**: A dedicated sports complex is available for student recreation.
*   **Residential**: Safe and separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with on-campus medical support.

### **Faculty & Pedagogy**
*   **Experienced Educators**: Faculty members with long tenures in academia and industrial research.
*   **Knowledge Intensive**: Teachers are capable of providing deep theoretical insights backed by practical assignments.
*   **Accessible Mentors**: Known for being dedicated to guiding students through their two-year PGDM/MBA journey.

**Final Verdict**: Best suited for students looking for a reliable, well-established institution with strong regional corporate links and a proven track record.
