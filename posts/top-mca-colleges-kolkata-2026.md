## 🏛️ The Icons: Jadavpur & Heritage

### 1. Jadavpur University (JU)
The pride of West Bengal and one of the best ROI institutes in the world. Its MCA program is legenday for producing Silicon Valley architects.
- **Top Recruiters:** Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Texas Instruments.
- **Admission Strategy:** You need a **Top 50 rank in WB JECA** to guarantee a seat here.

### 2. Heritage Institute of Technology (HIT)
Located in Anandapur, Heritage is the most trusted private choice for MCA in West Bengal. It is known for its discipline and its massive alumni network in Salt Lake Sector V.

## 🚀 The Kolkata Advantage (Sector V & Rajarhat)

For an MCA student, Kolkata offers distinct benefits:
- **Salt Lake Sector V:** One of India's biggest IT delivery hubs. From TCS and Cognizant to small-scale product boutiques, Sector V is where your career will likely start.
- **Low Cost of Living:** Kolkata remains the most student-friendly metro city in terms of rent and food, allowing you to focus on your coding skills.
- **Academic Rigor:** West Bengal is known for its strong focus on mathematics and theoretical computer science, which helps students clear the complex algorithmic rounds of top tech firms.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. Is Jadavpur University better than NITs?**
For **Software Engineering and Research**, JU is on par with Top 3 NITs. For **Placements**, JU graduates often get higher domestic packages due to their strong presence in the Kolkata and Bangalore tech hubs.

**Q2. Does University of Calcutta accept WB JECA?**
Previously CU had its separate entrance, but for many MCA colleges in West Bengal, **WB JECA** remains the primary gateway. Always check the official MAKAUT and CU portals in March for the 2026 intake.

**Q3. What is the average fee for MCA in Heritage Kolkata?**
Heritage is a private institute, so the fees are around ₹1.2 Lakhs to ₹1.6 Lakhs per year.

**Q4. Can I get a job at Google después de doing MCA from JU?**
Yes. Google and other global tech giants regularly visit Jadavpur University for their flagship software engineering roles.

**Q5. When is the WB JECA 2026 exam?**
Usually held in **July**. Registrations typically open in May.
