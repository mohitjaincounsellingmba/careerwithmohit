**MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology (MSRIT)**, established in 1962, is widely considered the "Gold Standard" of private engineering education in Bangalore. Ranking consistently among the top 100 engineering institutes in NIRF, MSRIT is the second-most preferred choice after the iconic **RVCE** in the COMEDK and KCET counselling rounds.

### **MSRIT Admission Channels 2026**
1.  **KCET (45% Seats)**: For Karnataka domicile students only.
2.  **COMEDK (30% Seats)**: For All India candidates.
3.  **Management Quota (25% Seats)**: Direct entry based on merit and donation.

### **Expected Cutoffs 2026 (General Merit)**
The competition for **Computer Science (CSE)** and **AI & Machine Learning** at MSRIT is extremely fierce. 

| Branch | KCET Rank (Expected) | COMEDK Rank (Expected) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Computer Science (CSE)** | 500 - 1,100 | 400 - 1,400 |
| **AI & Machine Learning** | 1,000 - 1,600 | 900 - 1,900 |
| **Information Science (ISE)** | 1,200 - 2,000 | 1,100 - 2,100 |
| **Electronics (ECE)** | 1,800 - 2,800 | 2,000 - 3,200 |

### **MSRIT B.Tech Fee Structure 2026**
Fees at MSRIT depend on the admission quota:
*   **KCET Fee**: approx. ₹1 Lakh per year.
*   **COMEDK Fee**: approx. ₹2.72 Lakhs per year.
*   **Management Quota**: Total 4-year cost for CSE can range from **₹15 Lakhs to ₹35 Lakhs** depending on demand.

### **Placement Review: Why MSRIT is a Dream Choice**
MSRIT is one of the most industry-oriented institutes in the country, with over 300+ companies visiting the campus annually.
*   **Highest Package for 2025/2026**: ₹58.00 LPA (International) / ₹50.00 LPA (Domestic).
*   **Average Package (CSE)**: ₹12.00 LPA+.
*   **Average Package (Overall)**: ₹7.66 LPA to ₹10.0 LPA.
*   **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, Apple, Cisco, Adobe, Microsoft, Wipro, Infosys, Deloitte, and Capgemini.
*   **Placement Rate**: Consistently above 95% for all eligible circuit branch students.
