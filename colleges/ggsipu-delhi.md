### About GGSIPU
Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University is a public state university located in Delhi. It is known for its vast number of affiliated colleges and professional courses in management, engineering, and law.

### Key Highlights
- **Affordable Quality**: Low fee structure compared to private universities.
- **Wide Network**: One of the largest universities in Delhi with numerous professional institutes.
- **Diverse Programs**: Options from undergraduate to doctoral levels.
