### About Institute of Marketing & Management (IMM)
Institute of Marketing & Management (IMM), founded in 1969, is one of the premier business schools in Delhi. Located in the Qutub Institutional Area, it offers a dual specialization PGDM program with a strong focus on industry exposure and corporate networking. With over 50 years of legacy, IMM has a vast alumni network and consistent placement records in top multinationals.
