### About FMS Delhi
The Faculty of Management Studies (FMS), University of Delhi, is often called the "Red Building of Dreams." It is famous globally for its unparalleled ROI (Return on Investment) and for producing some of the finest business leaders in India.

### Key Highlights
- **Unbeatable ROI**: Total fees for 2 years is less than ₹2.5 Lakhs.
- **Top Brand**: Part of the prestigious Delhi University.
- **Elite Placements**: Average CTC consistently crosses ₹34 LPA.

### Admission
Admission is strictly based on CAT scores followed by an Extempore and Personal Interview.
