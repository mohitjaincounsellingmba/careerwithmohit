### About NDIM Delhi
New Delhi Institute of Management (NDIM) is a globally recognized management institute, accredited by the NBA and AIU. It has been rated as the "Best B-School in India for Industry Linkages" for three consecutive years by the CII and AICTE. NDIM is known for its excellent placements and strong focus on entrepreneurship.

### PGDM Programs
- **PGDM (General)**: Flagship program with 7+ specializations.
- **PGDM (Marketing)**: Strong focus on digital and traditional marketing.
- **PGDM (Finance)**: Comprehensive coverage of financial markets and tools.

### Placements 2024
- **Highest Package**: ₹24.00 LPA
- **Average Package**: ₹9.50 LPA
- **Placement Rate**: 100% since inception.
- **Top Recruiters**: Deloitte, KPMG, EY, Infosys, HDFC Bank, Amazon, Tata Motors.

1. **Entrance Exam**: Valid score in any national level management exam.
2. **Interview Round**: Conducted by senior faculty and industry mentors.
