### About IIFT Delhi
The Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) was established in 1963 by the Government of India to help professionalize the country's foreign trade management. It is the premier institute in India for International Business.

### Key Highlights
- **Specialization**: Known for its MBA in International Business (IB).
- **Global Presence**: Strong alumni network across international trade and shipping.
- **Top Academics**: Rigorous curriculum focusing on global trade policies and finance.
