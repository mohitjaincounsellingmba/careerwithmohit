### About IBA Bangalore
Indus Business Academy (IBA) is one of the few B-schools in India with international IACBE accreditation. The campus, known as "IBA Bangalore - The 3D School", focuses on Discovery, Design, and Delivery of management education in a sprawling residential campus.
