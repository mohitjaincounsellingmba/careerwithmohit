### About BIMTECH
Birla Institute of Management Technology (BIMTECH) was established in 1988 under the aegis of the Birla Academy of Art and Culture. It is one of India's most prestigious private B-schools, known for its strong industry connection and global perspective.

### Courses & Specializations
- **PGDM**: Flagship program with specializations in Marketing, Finance, Operations, and HR.
- **PGDM-IB**: Focus on International Business and global trade.
- **PGDM-RM**: Specialized in Retail Management.
- **PGDM-IBM**: Focus on Insurance Business Management.

### Placements 2024
BIMTECH has a stellar placement record with 100% placement for the 2022-24 batch.
- **Average Package**: ₹11.20 LPA
- **Highest Package**: ₹24.40 LPA
- **Top Recruiters**: Deloitte, KPMG, EY, Infosys, Wipro, and many more.

### Admission Process
1. **Entrance Exam**: Submit scores for CAT, XAT, MAT, or CMAT.
2. **Shortlisting**: Based on entrance scores and academic profile.
3. **Selection**: Personal Interview (PI) and group discussions.
4. **Final Offer**: Based on composite score.
