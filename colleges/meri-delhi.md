### About MERI Delhi
Janakpuri
MERI Janakpuri is one of the premier institutes under Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). It has a legacy of over 30 years in providing quality management and technical education. The institute is known for its affordable fee structure and excellent value-for-money, making it a popular choice in Delhi.

### MBA Programs
- **MBA (General)**: Affiliated to GGSIPU with specializations in Marketing, Finance, HR.
- **MBA + PGPM**: Dual program for enhanced industry readiness.
- **Global Exposure**: Strong track record of international student exchange.

### Placements 2024

### Admission Process
1. **University Entrance**: IPU CET or national level exam scores (CAT/MAT).
2. **Counselling**: Participation in GGSIPU centralized counselling.
3. **Interview**: Internal evaluation for the dual program.
