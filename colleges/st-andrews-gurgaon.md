### About St. Andrews Gurgaon
St. Andrews Institute of Technology and Management (SAITM) is a leading educational institution in Gurgaon, known for its focus on technology and management. The campus is spread over 22 acres and provides a conducive environment for learning and development. The MBA program is designed to create future-ready managers with a global mindset.

### MBA Program
- **Specializations**: Marketing, Finance, HR, and IT.
- **Industry Partners**: Collaboration with top MNCs for workshops and training.
- **Campus Life**: State-of-the-art labs, library, and sports facilities.

### Placements 2024
- **Highest Package**: ₹40.00 LPA
- **Average Package**: ₹8.00 LPA
- **Placement Rate**: 64% with 173+ students placed in top firms.
- **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Paytm, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Sony.

### Admission Process
1. **Entrance Exam**: Valid score in CAT/MAT or SAITM Entrance Test.
2. **Profile Review**: Assessment of academic and extracurricular achievements.
3. **Personal Interview**: Interactive session with faculty and industry experts.
