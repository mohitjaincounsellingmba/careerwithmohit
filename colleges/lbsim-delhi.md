### About LBSIM
Lal Bahadur Shastri Institute of Management (LBSIM) is a premier institute for management and information technology education in Delhi. It was established by the Lal Bahadur Shastri Educational Society.

### Key Highlights
- **Finance Focus**: Highly regarded for its PGDM in Financial Services.
- **Bloomberg Lab**: State-of-the-art terminal for financial research and training.
- **Value Based Education**: Follows the ideals of Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri.
