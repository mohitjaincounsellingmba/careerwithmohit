### About GNIOT
GNIOT Group of Institutions is a giant in the educational landscape of Greater Noida. Its management wing is known for providing quality education with a strong emphasis on practical exposure. The campus is massive with modern industry-alighed labs, a world-class library, and a dedicated career development center.

### MBA/PGDM Programs
- **PGDM (Global)**: Premium program with focus on international business.
- **MBA**: Affiliated to AKTU, focusing on core management principles.
- **Specializations**: Marketing, Finance, IT, and International Business.

### Placements 2025
- **Highest International Package**: ₹70.00 LPA
- **Highest Domestic Package**: ₹23.00 LPA
- **Average Package**: ₹7.25 LPA
- **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, TCS, Wipro, Airtel, PwC, Google, HCL.

### Admission Process
1. **Entrance**: Merit in national level management entrance tests.
2. **Counseling**: AKTU counseling for MBA or direct selection for PGDM.
3. **Merit Scholarship**: Substantial scholarships for top academic achievers.
