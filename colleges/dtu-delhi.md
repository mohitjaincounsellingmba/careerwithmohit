### About DTU
Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering) is a premier state university in Delhi. It is known for its strong technical education and has produced numerous industry leaders and successful entrepreneurs.

### Placements 2024-25
- **Highest International CTC**: ₹1.8 Crore Per Annum
- **Highest Domestic CTC**: ₹85.3 LPA
- **Average CTC**: ₹15.75 LPA
- **Top Recruiters**: Microsoft, Amazon, Google, Adobe, Qualcomm, Goldman Sachs, Uber.

### Admission Process
Admission to B.Tech is through JAC Delhi based on JEE Main All India Ranks.
