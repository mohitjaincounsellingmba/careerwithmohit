### About JIMS Kalkaji
Jagan International Management School (JIMS) Kalkaji is a leading management institute located in the heart of South Delhi. Recognized for its academic excellence, the institute is accredited by NBA and NAAC. It offers an environment that encourages innovation, research, and critical thinking, preparing students for senior leadership roles in the corporate world.

### PGDM Programs
- **PGDM (General)**: AICTE approved with dual specialization.
- **PGDM (International Business)**: Specialized in international trade and marketing.
- **Corporate Branding**: Strong industry-academia interface with frequent CXO talks.

### Placements 2024-25
- **Highest Package**: ₹35.00 LPA
- **Average Package**: ₹10.50 LPA (2025 Placement Drive)
- **Placement Rate**: 96%+
- **Top Recruiters**: Amazon, Deloitte, ICICI Bank, Tata Capital, Wipro, Genpact, PWC.

### Admission Process
1. **National Exam**: Valid CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT score.
2. **Written Communication Test**: Assessment of writing and logical skills.
3. **Personal Interview**: In-depth interaction with admission panel.
