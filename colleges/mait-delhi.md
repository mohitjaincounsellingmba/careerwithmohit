### About MAIT
MAIT is a top-tier private engineering institute affiliated with GGSIPU (Indraprastha University). It consistently ranks as the best private engineering college in Delhi/NCR and is known for its excellent placement record in IT and Tech companies.

### Placements 2024
- **Highest Package**: ₹64 LPA (On-Campus), ₹1 CPA+ (Off-Campus)
- **Average Package**: ₹8.0 LPA (CSE/IT focus)
- **Top Recruiters**: Google, Microsoft, Adobe, Goldman Sachs, Amazon, Infosys.

Admission is based on JEE Main ranks through GGSIPU Centralized Counselling.
