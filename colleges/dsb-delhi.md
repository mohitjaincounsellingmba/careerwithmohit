### About DSB Delhi
Delhi School of Business (DSB) is the management wing of the Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies (VIPS). It is established with a vision to create a world-class management school that provides high-quality business education. The school is known for its rigorous training, global immersion programs, and state-of-the-art urban campus.

### PGDM Programs
- **PGDM (General)**: Flagship dual-specialization program.
- **PGDM (FinTech)**: One of the few institutions in Delhi offering a specialized track for Fintech.
- **International Immersion**: Optional global study tours to top business hubs.

### Placements 2025
- **Highest Package**: ₹23.90 LPA
- **Average Package**: ₹9.40 LPA
- **Placement Rate**: 100% for 3 consecutive years.
- **Top Recruiters**: Federal Bank, Black Rock, Deloitte, EY, Asian Paints, Amazon, S&P Global.

### Admission Process
1. **Entrance Exam**: CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT/GMAT.
2. **Group Task**: Innovative assessment of leadership and collaboration.
3. **Personal Interview**: Professional interaction with faculty.
