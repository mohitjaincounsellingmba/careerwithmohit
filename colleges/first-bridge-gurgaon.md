### About First Bridge Gurgaon
First Bridge Business School (formerly Bridge School of Management) is known for its industry-led curriculum and focus on practical learning. It was established to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry requirements. The school offers a unique learning model that prepares students for the dynamic corporate world.

### MBA/PGDM Program
- **Industry-Integrated**: Courses designed in collaboration with corporate leaders.
- **Global Exposure**: Opportunities for international immersion and internships.
- **Skill Focus**: Heavy emphasis on digital skills, data analytics, and soft skills.

### Placements
- **Highest Package**: ₹15.00 LPA
- **Average Package**: ₹6.00 LPA
- **Top Recruiters**: Luminous, Ceil HR, Deloitte, HDFC, Axis Bank.

### Admission Process
1. **Application**: Online application with academic details.
2. **Entrance Score**: Merit in CAT/MAT/CMAT.
3. **Interview**: Evaluation of professional goals and aptitude.
