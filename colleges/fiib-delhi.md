### About FIIB Delhi
Fortune Institute of International Business (FIIB) is a leading B-school in Delhi with a strong focus on global business and sustainability. It offers a futuristic PGDM program that integrates modern technologies like AI and Data Analytics with core management principles. FIIB is known for its strong community and career outcomes.

### PGDM Program
- **Global Perspective**: Integrated international immersion modules.
- **Sustainability Focus**: First B-school in India to integrate ESG into core curriculum.
- **Experiential Learning**: 100+ guest lectures and live projects per year.

- **Average Package**: ₹10.50 LPA

### Admission Process
1. **Entrance Exam**: Valid score in CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT/ATMA.
2. **Career Guidance Session**: A unique advisory session before the interview.
3. **Personal Interview**: Final selection based on attitude and aptitude.
